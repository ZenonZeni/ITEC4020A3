<a name="monkey"><p><img border=0 align=left hspace=15 vspace=3 src="images/04016.gif"></a>
In Borneo, Indonesia, PV is helping fight the extinction of orangutans.
Researchers at Camp Leakey were unable to count on a utility grid to supply their power, so they
looked to PV as a solution. That solution was coordinated by NREL. Scientists designed a
960-W lighting and battery-charging system and installed it at the camp--securely enough for a
harsh environment that included not only environmental elements but curious apes!
