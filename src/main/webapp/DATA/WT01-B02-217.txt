<TITLE>Conversion History</TITLE>
<H1>Conversion History</H1>
<P>Abstracts and papers for this electronic digest were prepared in the
following manner:
</P>
<UL>
<LI>Hardcopy abstracts were scanned via Optical Character Recognition
(OCR) technology and reviewed by IGARSS Committee Members.
</LI>
<LI>Papers that were submitted electronically in Postscript format were
converted to Adobe Acrobat(TM) Portable Document Format (PDF).
</LI>
<LI>Papers that were submitted electronically in text format were
converted to HyperText Markup Language (HTML), as time permitted.
</LI>
<LI>Additional files, such as images, supporting text, etc., were
placed on the CD in the same directory with their respective paper.
</LI>
<LI>Hardcopy papers were scanned as TIFF images and then converted to 
Adobe Acrobat(TM) Portable Document Format (PDF).  (There are a few
oversized papers that could not be scanned.)   These papers will
sometimes appear with black borders around the edges.</LI>  
</UL>
<H1>Problem Submissions</H1>
<P>Several papers were submitted electronically that could not be
processed. These included those submissions requiring BinHEX 4.0.  Other
papers were submitted in TeX and LaTeX formats which were not processed
further, but are included on the CD in those original formats.
</P>
