<p><font SIZE=+1>Indoor air pollutants come from a number of sources, and
tend to promote a number of physical ailments:</font></p>

<table border>
<tr>
<th>Pollutant</th>

<th>Source</th>

<th>Physical Symptoms</th>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Dust </td>

<td>Skin flakes, soil, pet components, carpet fibers </td>

<td>Eye irritation, allergies, eye-ear-nose-throat infections, asthma attacks,
fatigue </td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Bacteria </td>

<td>Heating &amp; cooling system/s, house pets, garbage, bathrooms </td>

<td>Colds, respiratory infections, eye infections... </td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Mold Spores </td>

<td>Heating &amp; cooling system/s, damp clothing, cleaning materials and
the moisture in ceilings, walls, carpets, drapes... </td>

<td>Allergies, sinus headaches, irritability, fatigue </td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Benzene </td>

<td>Paint, new carpets, new drapes, upholstery... </td>

<td>Headaches,eye/skin irritation, fatigue, cancer </td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Ammonia </td>

<td>Tobacco smoke, cleaning supplies </td>

<td>Eye/skin irritation, headaches, nose-bleeds, sinus problems </td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Chloroform </td>

<td>Paint, new drapes, upholstery, new carpeting </td>

<td>Headaches,asthma attacks, dizziness, eye irritation, skin irritation
</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Formaldehyde </td>

<td>Tobacco smoke, plywood, cabinets, furniture, particle board, office
dividers, new carpets, new drapes, wallpaper, panelling </td>

<td>Headaches, eye/skin irritation, drowsiness, fatigue, respiritory problems,
memory loss, depression, gynecological problems, cancer </td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Benzopyrene </td>

<td>Tobacco smoke </td>

<td>Asthma attacks, eye/skin irritation, sinus problems, lung cancer </td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Hydrocarbons </td>

<td>Tobacco smoke, gas burners, furnaces </td>

<td>Headaches, fatigue, nausea, dizziness, breathing difficulty </td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Trichlorethylene </td>

<td>Paints, glues, furniture, wallpaper </td>

<td>Headaches, eye/skin irritation, respiratory irritation </td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Carbon Tetrachloride </td>

<td>Paints, new drapes, new carpets, cleaning supplies </td>

<td>Headaches, dizziness, fatigue </td>
</tr>
