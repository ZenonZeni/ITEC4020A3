<A NAME="objective"><STRONG>Investment Objective</FONT></STRONG></A>
<BR>
To seek to provide investment results that correspond to the total return performance
of fixed income securities in the aggregate, as represented by the Salomon Brothers Broad
Investment Grade Bond Index.
<P>
<BR>


<A NAME="f_profile"><STRONG>Fund Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>The Indexed Bond Fund invests primarily in investment-grade bonds that are included in the 
Salomon Brothers Broad Investment Grade Index, and unmanaged index generally considered representative of the U.S. Bond Market. 

<LI>Unlike other funds,which may seek to beat market averages, often with unpredictable results, index
funds seek to match their respective indices.
</UL>
<P>
<BR>




<A NAME="i_profile"><STRONG>Investor Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>Investors seeking to match the returns of the Salomon Brothers Broad Investment Grade Index.

<LI>Investors desiring a diversified portfolio of U.S. Government and corporate bonds,
mortgage-backed and asset-backed securities.

<LI>Investors seeking to invest in debt securities without having to select and monitor an
active bond manager.

</UL>

Before you invest...
<BR>
Shares of the fund are not guaranteed.  Like other bond funds, your investment return and
principal value will fluctuate  with interest rates and market conditions.
<P>
