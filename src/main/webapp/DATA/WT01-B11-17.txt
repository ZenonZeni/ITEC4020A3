<CENTER><H1> The New Mazda RX-01 </H1></CENTER>

Click on the pictures with green borders to get to the sites with the
full size images.

<P><IMG SRC="../Images/mazda-rx-01-h063.gif" ALT="Mazda RX-01 Image"
	ALIGN=MIDDLE WIDTH=119 HEIGHT=63>
<A HREF =
"http://teleparc.infoweb.or.jp/tms/hall/center/mazda-p/mazda-p-e.htm">
Mazda booth at 31st Tokyo Motor Show</A>

<P>The <A HREF="mazda.html">Mazda</A> RX-01 is a new concept or
prototype vehicle with a new multi-sideport rotary engine (MSPRE) in a
more central position.  It has an integrated front wing that doesn't
look like a wing and it retains the double bubble top of the <A HREF =
"rx-7.html#3rdgen">third generation RX-7</A>.  Structurally it could be
cropped to a convertible.

<H2> Specifications </H2>
<PRE>
                  RX-01                         RX-7 (3rd gen.)

Engine            MSPRE 2 rotor non-turbo       13B twin turbo
HP		  217, 220@8500 rpm, or 227     255
Torque            159 ftlb at 6500 rpm          --
Weight            1100 kg (2425 lb)             2825 lb
Air Efficiency    +30%                          --
Eng Location      8 in. back, 2.2 in. lower     --
Lubrication	  Dry Sump                      Wet Sump
Transmission      5 spd manual                  5 spd manual
Suspension        unequal length A-arms         --
Len x Wd x Ht	  4.055 x 1.73 x 1.245 m        4.28 x ? x ? m
Len x Wd x Ht     159.6 x 68.1 x 49.0 in.
Wheelbase	  2.345 m (92.3 in.)            2.425 m
Headlights        Cntrl Discharge Fibre Optic   Halogen
Seating           Two + 2                       Two
Cabin Space       Same as RX-7                  --
Trunk Space       Increased over RX-7           --
</PRE>

Notes: Polar moment of inertia is 30% lower in the RX-01 because the
engine, fuel tank, and battery are located inside the wheelbase for
even greater agility.  Headlights are below American and Japanese
legal heights.
