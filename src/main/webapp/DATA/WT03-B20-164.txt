<H1>Biotechnology</H1>
<HR SIZE=1 NOSHADE>




<P>RSSL&#39;s biotechnology unit can isolate and grow algae under carefully controlled conditions to supply the pharmaceutical industry with biomass or extracts for screening programmes to identify novel bioactive compounds. </P>

<P>RSSL also has a unique culture collection and can offer samples to clients on an exclusive basis. The unit has fermentation facilities which enable it to optimise and scale up the production of potentially novel metabolites. </P>

<P>Biomass can also be produced from other micro-organisms including bacteria, fungi, and yeasts. </P>
