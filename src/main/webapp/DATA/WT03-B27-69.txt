<h1>Florinsa Adopts New Web Presence</h1>
<h3><em>Changes allow for better information</em></h3>
<p><strong>Sept. 26, 1996</strong> -- Florinsa Farms, which has
been on the Internet since 1993, has adopted a new format and
layout for its Internet Web Site. This new web site will allow
for greater contact with the public and more concise information.</p>
<p>Included on the new site:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Product infromation complete with specifications and
        pictures of the varieties</li>
    <li>More contact information </li>
    <li>Greater information on the entire Florinsa family and
        network of offices globally.</li>
</ul>
<p>Florinsa has been dedicated to using the latest technologies
for producution and operations. We are happy to begin utilizing
the Internet in more creative manners to better inform our
customers.</p>
