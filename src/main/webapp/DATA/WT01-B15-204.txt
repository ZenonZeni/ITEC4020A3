<BODY><PRE><H2>
    Conference of Radiation Control Program Directors, Inc. (CRCPD)</H2><H3>
                           Contract DE-FC01-93RW00284 (Since 1989)</H3>
<P>
The Conference of Radiation Control Program Directors (CRCPD) is a non-profit organization
made up of state and local government representatives who regulate and control the use of
radiation sources.  The Conference is comprised of radiation control program directors in all states,
New York City, the California counties of Los Angeles, Orange and San Diego, the District of
Columbia, and the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico. The Conference is active in the  review of
emergency response procedures for shipment of radioactive material, and in the establishment of
standards for radiological inspections.
