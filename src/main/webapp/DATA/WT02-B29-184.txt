<A NAME="objective"><STRONG>Investment Objective</FONT></STRONG></A>
<BR>
To seek maximum long-term total return from a combination of capital growth and income.  The Fund is not
designed or managed primarily to produce current income.
<P>
<BR>




<A NAME="f_profile"><STRONG>Fund Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>The Fund seeks to achieve its objective by following investment policies emphasizing investment in
common stocks which the Fund's investment adviser considers undervalued at the time of purchase.
<LI>The Fund will normally invest primarily in common stocks that are listed on national securities
exchanges or traded in the OTC market.
</UL>
<P>
<BR>




<A NAME="i_profile"><STRONG>Investor Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>Investors seeking total returns through growth and income.
<LI>Investors pursuing long-range goals.
<LI>Investors seeking to diversify an income-oriented portfolio.
</UL>
