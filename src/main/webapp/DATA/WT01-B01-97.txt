<img src="../pictures/cpjlogo.gif" HEIGHT=90 WIDTH=90 ALIGN=LEFT><Center><BR><BR><h1>How CPJ Investigates and <BR>
Classifies Attacks on the Press</h1></center>
<hr>
<BR>
<BLOCKQUOTE>

The cases of press freedom violations described on this website were investigated and verified by CPJ's research staff. Each account was corroborated by more than one source for accuracy and to confirm that the attacks were against journalists* or news organizations and that silencing them was the probable motive.<P>
<hr>

<H3><CENTER>Classifications
</CENTER></H3>
<B>Killed</B><BR>
Killed or missing and presumed dead with evidence that retribution for news coverage or commentary was involved. Includes journalists killed unintentionally in the line of duty.<P>

<B>Missing</B><BR>
Missing and, in some instances, feared dead.<P>

<B>Imprisoned</B><BR>
Arrested or held against one&#146;s will, including kidnapped.<P>

<B>Attacked</B><BR>
Wounded; assaulted; a news facility physically attacked, raided, or searched; non-journalist employees attacked in any way because of news coverage or commentary.<P>

<B>Threatened</B><BR>
Threatened with physical harm or some other type of retribution.<P>

<B>Harassed</B><BR>
Access denied or limited; materials confiscated; materials damaged; entry or exit denied; family members attacked or threatened; dismissed or demoted (when it is clearly the result of political or outside pressure); harassed in some other way.<P>

<B>Legal Action</B><BR>
Credentials denied or suspended; fined; passage of a restrictive law; libel suit intended to inhibit coverage; sentenced to prison; visas denied or canceled.<P>

<B>Expelled</B><BR>
Expelled or forced to leave a country because of news coverage or commentary.<P>

<B>Censored</B><BR>
Officially censored or banned; editions confiscated; news outlet closed.<P>

<!-- Font Tag --><FONT SIZE=2><I>*Journalists are defined as people who cover news or write commentary on a regular basis. Only attacks that relate to the journalists&#146; professional work are included.</I></FONT>
