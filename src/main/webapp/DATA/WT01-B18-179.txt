<H2><I>B. </I>Energy-Efficient Retrofits Are a Source of Energy Savings 
Waiting to be Mined<BR></H2>

<BR>
Retrofits of existing buildings are a source of efficiency waiting to be 
mined. Eighty percent of U.S. commercial buildings were built prior to 
1979 and contain obsolete energy systems.<a href="#163" name="r163"> [ 163 ] </a>
 A full-scale retrofit 
generally calls for replacing lighting and HVAC system components, 
improving maintenance procedures, and installing computerized controls. 
Windows may be either replaced or coated with low-emissivity films, and 
sensors can be placed to monitor temperature and humidity, with the 
information fed into microcomputers to control the indoor climate.<BR>
