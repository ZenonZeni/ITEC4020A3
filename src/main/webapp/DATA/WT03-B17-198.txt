       <H1>Kandalaksia</H1>  <IMG SRC="../../grafikk/russia.gif" align="right"><br>   <BR><p>
</center><strong>
At the end of the journety we visited the city of Kandalaksia, which is located at the inner end of
the White Sea.<p>
Main event here: we went to a pro-casino and lost heaps of dollars. Got guided to the hotel by the police.<p>
On the second day we visited the local fairs and painting galleries, before heading up north.<p>
General impression: very nice city, clean, friendly and cheap hotel with good standard.<P>
