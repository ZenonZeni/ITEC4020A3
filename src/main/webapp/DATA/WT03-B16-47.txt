<H1>RADARSAT</H1> 
</center> 
<HR>
RADARSAT uses Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR), 
an active microwave sensor, allowing 24 hour data collection independent
 of weather conditions and illumination. The SAR sensor has selective viewing
 angles that allow a wide range of terrain conditions, applications 
and ground coverage requirements to be accommodated.Imaging modes for
 RADARSAT include Fine, Standard, Wide, ScanSAR (narrow and wide), 
and Extended Beam (high and low incidence angles).</P>
