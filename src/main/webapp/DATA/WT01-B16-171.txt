<TITLE>Diabetes Mellitis</TITLE>
</HEAD><BODY><P>Diabetes is a common disease of glucose metabolism. Glucose
levels in the blood are abnormally high due to lack of insulin production
from the pancreas or from resistance to insulin. This disease can cause disease
of the cardiovascular system, including strokes and heart attacks. Kidney
failure and blindness can also develope.
<P>Diabetes is treated with a low calorie diet and frequently with medication.
Patients who do not produce insulin on their own need insulin injections
daily.
