<h2>Ecuador and Roses</h2>
<p>.<img src="images/mountain.jpg" width="346" height="202"> The
Andean Mountains </p>
<p>The most beautiful roses in the world are being harvested
today, like everyday, on the farms of Florinsa Farms in Ecuador.
Nestled in the Andean mountains outside of Quito, the farms of
Florinsa Farms provide ideal conditions for the production of
exceptional roses.</p>
<p>The water is nature's purest, from the snow melt and the
temperature is stable year-round. The days are long here and the
special brilliance of sunlight at the equator is consistent
throughout the year.</p>
<p>Beginning with these great natural blessing, Florinsa Farms
has also developed the post-harvesting technology that defines
state-of-the-art in the industry. From our breeding laboratories
in the Netherlands to the most modern computerized irrigation and
fertilization systems on our farms, we've found the perfect blend
of nature and science. It's a marriage that produces roses with
unusually big heads, strong straight stems, healthy leaves, and
an intensity of color you simply have to see to believe.</p>
