<HR>

<P><H5>

<B>* No students will be allowed to register or attend classes until the tuition and 

all indebtedness is paid in full to the College.</B>

<P>

* Tuition is paid on a quarterly basis in advance of instruction for that quarter.  

International students' first year tuition is payable in full upon acceptance to 

the College.

<P>

* Tuition is due and payable in full at the time of registration.  A student's 

registration is finalized by the payment of tuition and any applicable fees.

<P>

No late registration is accepted.

<P>

No grades or documents will be released if there is an outstanding balance owed to the College.
