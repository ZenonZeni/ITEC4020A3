<title>Wind Plants of Northern Ireland</title>
<head><b><h1>Wind Plants of Northern Ireland</h1></b></head><p>
<body>
There are three wind plants operating in Northern Ireland providing a 
combined capacity of 15 MW.<p>

<center><img align=bottom src="ukelhl.gif"><p>
<h3>Vestas V39 on Elliott's Hill</h3></center><p>

B9 energy services operates the ten 39-meter wind turbines on 
Elliott's Hill. 15 miles (25 kilometers) north of Belfast. The site 
offers a grand view of the countryside in County Antrim, including 
Slemish Mountain, a nationally significant topographic feature. (The 
volcanic plug can be seen in the background at lower right.)<p>
