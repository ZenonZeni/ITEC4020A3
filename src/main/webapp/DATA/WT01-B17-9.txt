  <!-- --[ End title ]-- -->

<hr color="#ff0000" size="1" width="80%" align="left">
<p>

<blockquote>
<P>



  <!-- --[ Insert the article text here ]-- -->

The effects of fish oil on many metabolic processes were investigated in 12
healthy volunteers.
<p>
In addition to a controlled diet (that is, both groups eating the same foods), the
test subjects received either fish oil or corn oil supplements for two 4-week
periods.  The results support the hypothesis that dietary fish oil may protect
against colon cancer.

<p>
<i>Source:  </I>
Gastroenterology, 
<I>1993 Nov, 105(5): 1317-22</i>

<p>
Fish oil capsules are usually prepared in one-gram size, with instructions to take
three capsules a day.
