<H3>Courier EV Battery Exchange 
</H3>


<P>Stockholm Energi, local energy manufacturer and distributor, manages a project in co-operation with City of Stockholm vehicle operators MFO and courier companies Svenska Milj&ouml;bud AB and Elbudet AB. Ten Elcat light distribution EVs operate from a central garage offering battery exchange. </P>

See<!-- Link Tag --><A HREF="http://www.iea.lth.se/~niklas/work/evdatabase/carsproj.html#Elcat-B1"> listing</A> of the battery exchange vehicles.<BR>
