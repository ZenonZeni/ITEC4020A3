<h1>Engineering Advancement Office</h1>
The Engineering Advancement Office was established in 1990.
Responsibilities of the office include but are not restricted to:
<ul>
<li>fundraising,
<li>preparation of newsletters for staff, students, alumni and the community, 
<li>alumni relations
<li>fostering staff and student relations with former staff and former
students, and
<li>contacting industry.
</ul>
