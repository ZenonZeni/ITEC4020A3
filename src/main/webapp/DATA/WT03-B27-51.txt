<font size=+2>
First, Some Important Facts to Know</font>
<p>
<blockquote>
<LI>Completed registration forms (with fees included) must be sent via postal mail only (no e-mail or online registering) and have a postmark of April 1 or later.
<P>
<LI>Refunds will be sent to those whose written cancellation is received by 5:00 P.M. Thursday, August 1, 1996.  <b>A $50 cancellation fee will be assessed.</b>
<P>
<LI>No faxed cancellations will be accepted.  No refunds for any reason, medical or otherwise, will be allowed after August 1, 1996.
<P>
<li>One person per form
<p>
<li>Registration form must be signed - it is incomplete without a signature.
<P>
<LI>If you wish to apply with other cyclists as a group, applications must be submitted in the same envelope.  Each envelope may contain up to six individual applications.
<P>
<LI>Registrations are not transferable.
<p>
<li>Riders under age 18 must be accompanied by an adult over 21.
<p>
<li>You will receive notification of your acceptance, non-acceptance or waiting list status within 4-6 weeks from our receiving your application.  If accepted, you will receive a Rider handbook with full details by June 1.
</blockquote></blockquote>
