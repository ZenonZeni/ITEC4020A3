<H1><STRONG>Ways You Can Help AMI of Maryland</STRONG></H1>
<IMG SRC="gif/line_blu.gif"><BR>
</CENTER>


<P>There are many ways that caring people can help AMI of Maryland and persons with serious mental illness. Some suggestions: </P>

<UL>
<LH></LH>
<LI><STRONG>If you are a family or person directly affected by mental illness</STRONG>,  join your local AMI affiliate.  See our list of  <A HREF="helpyou.htm">AMI of Maryland affiliates</A>  You can particpate in support groups and advocate with us on behalf of mentally ill persons.<BR>

<LI><STRONG>If you are interested in our cause</STRONG>, you are invited to become a member of AMI. You will receive our newsletter and can participate in education and advocacy activities.
<BR>
<LI><STRONG>If you know of families with this problem</STRONG>, tell them about AMI.
<BR>
<LI><STRONG>Help combat stigma</STRONG> by objecting to negative stereotyping of persons with mental illness.  Tactifully correct misunderstandings about mental illness.
<BR>
<LI><STRONG>If you are a mental health professional</STRONG>, display and distribute AMI brochures.  Brochures can be obtained from the AMI of MD office or your local affiliate. See <A HREF="amiorg.htm">About AMI of Maryland</A> for our phone numbers and address, or contact  <A HREF="helpyou.htm">your local affiliate</A>.
<BR>
<LI><STRONG>If you are an employer</STRONG>, hire persons with mental illness for low stress jobs.  There can be tax advantages for private employers.
<BR>
<LI><STRONG>As a taxpayer</STRONG>, support funds for a good community-based support system and research to further understanding of the cause and treatment of mental illness.
<BR>
<LI><STRONG>If you are a homeowner</STRONG>, welcome  supervised housing or a group home in your neighborhood.  People with mental illness can be good neighbors.
<BR>
<LI><STRONG>If you want to make a donation</STRONG>, could you help us to obtain the following items or donate cash gifts towards the purchase of these items? Call 410-467-7100 and make our wishes come true!
<UL>
<LH> AMI OF MD WISH LIST</LH>
<LI>HP Laser Printer IV or V
<LI>Updated Phone System
<LI>Traveling/Portable Display Board
<LI>Additions to our Video library
<LI>Easel for Flip Chart
<LI>Baltimore Sun Newspaper subscription
<LI>Avery Copier Labels - #5351
</UL>




</UL>
