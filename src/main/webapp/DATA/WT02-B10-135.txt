<H1>LAUGHLAN (LOCH) CLARK</H1><P>LAUGHLAN (LOCH) CLARK'S practice includes
representation of: lenders, suppliers, and contractors in collection matters,
(including lien, bond, and retainage actions, foreclosures, workouts, and
bankruptcy claims); commercial landlords in unlawful detainer and collection, and
businesses in real estate transactions, and contract litigation. Loch has served
on the Executive Committee of, and is a member of the Creditor-Debtor Section,
and the Real Property Probate and Trust Section of the Washington State Bar
Association. A graduate of Western Washington University (1976) and University of
Puget Sound Law School (1980), Loch has practiced law in Bellingham since 1981.
