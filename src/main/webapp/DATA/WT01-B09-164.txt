<B>Project Summary:</B>
<P>
Develop and manufacture two 30 ft hybrid electric buses; one for
revenue service in transit operations, the other for shuttle service
at USAF base. El Dorado National provided the body and chassis,
Hughes Power Control Systems provided two 50 kW AC motors and
controllers for each bus; APS provided the APU, powered by propane,
and assisted with the integration; Trojan Battery Company provided
the batteries.
<P>
<B>Project Deliverables:</B>
<P>

Two 1994 El Dorado National Corporation RE29E Hybrid/Electric
Buses;<UL>
<P>
<LI> One for operation by Honolulu Public Transit Authority
<P>
<LI> One for operation at Hickam Air Force Base.
<P>
</UL>

<B>Project Schedule:</B><UL>
<P>
<LI> Buses arrived in Honolulu on 1/14/96.
<P>
<LI> Demonstration and Evaluation of buses continues through
July 1996.
</UL>
