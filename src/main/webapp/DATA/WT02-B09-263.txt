<H2>Labour Relations Assistance
BC Federation of Labour	430-1421</H2>
4279 Canada Way<BR>
Burnaby  V5G 1H1<BR>
Fax: 430-5917<BR><BR>

The B.C. Federation of Labour is the central labour organization for more<BR>
 than a quarter of a million workers who belong to 750 local unions in BC.<BR>
  For over 35 years, have represented the interests and concerns of working<BR>
 people and their families to governments, in communities, or to employers and <BR>
with other like-minded organizations.<BR><BR>

<H2>Employment Standards Branch	660-4000</H2>
Ministry Of Labour and Consumer Services <BR>
210-4946 Canada Way<BR>
Burnaby  V5G 4J6<BR>
Fax: 660-7047<BR><BR>

This branch deals with issues on wage protection, hours of work, overtime,<BR>
 special apparel, annual vacations, termination severance pay, child employment,<BR>
 maternity leave, parental leave, employee protection, and wage complaints. <BR>
 They also license employment agencies and farm labour contractors.<BR><BR>

<H2>Residential Tenancy Branch	660-3456</H2>

<H2>Ministry of Housing Recreation & Consumer Services </H2>

Burnaby  V5H 4A5<BR>

<H2>Estate / Probate Assistance</H2>
<H2>Deputy Official Administrator </H2>
<H2>Lower Mainland Region	775-1811</H2>
<H2>Ministry of Attorney General </H2>
204-6446 Nelson Avenue<BR>
Burnaby  V5H 3J5<BR>
Fax: 775-1813<BR><BR>

The deputy official administrator provides estate administration services<BR>
 when someone dies, either with a will, but has no executor or beneficiary willing <BR>
and able to administer the estate or, when someone dies without a will and without <BR>
any heirs who will administer the estate. The Lower Mainland Region includes<BR>
 Burnaby, New Westminster, Surrey, Delta, Coquitlam and all other areas west of Hope.<BR>

<H2>Probate Division of the Supreme Court of BC	660-8504</H2>
<H2>Law Courts, Begbie Square </H2>
651 Carnarvon Street<BR>
New Westminster<BR>
  V3M 1C9<BR><BR>

Receives and processes applications for the grant of letters of probate<BR>
 and administration.  Open for search of documents to establish if letters<BR>
 of probate and administration have been issued in New Westminster.<BR><BR>

<H2>Public Trustee	660-4444</H2>
<H2>Ministry of the Attorney General </H2>
600-808 West Hastings Street<BR>
Vancouver  V6C 3L3<BR>
Fax: 660-9498<BR><BR>

To protect the financial interests and legal rights of children, adults unable<BR>
 to manage their financial and legal affairs, and to administer the estates of<BR>
 deceased or missing persons where no one else is willing or able to act.<BR>
 For children and adults, this protection includes monitoring of trusts and<BR>
 investigating situations where financial abuse is suspected.<BR><BR>

<H2>Tax Assistance </H2>
<H2>Income Tax Workshops	Ron Zang: 224-0698</H2>
Provincial Liaison Volunteer Coordinator<BR>
Workshops for volunteers held in the Spring. Income tax forms completed for <BR>
seniors at senior recreation centres during March.  Phone closest centre for an<BR>
 appointment. Free service for seniors and for those whose annual income is $18,000 or less.<BR>
 Volunteer will go to client's home if very elderly or disabled.<BR><BR>

<H2>Seniors' Bureau Tax Clinic	524-0516</H2>
Western Society/Seniors' Bureau 330-555-6th Street<BR>
New Westminster<BR>
  V3L 5H1<BR><BR>

A clinic to assist in completing income tax returns is established in a shopping<BR>
 mall in New Westminster annually, February 15 to April 30 from 9:30 am to 2:30 pm.<BR>
 Drop in basis, no appointments. Phone for specific location. Centrally located with<BR> 
good access by public transit, good parking, wheelchair accessible. Home visits and <BR>
off-season assistance available if needed. For seniors and low-income adults, whose<BR>
 income is $18,000 per year or less. No charge.<BR><BR>
