<IMG ALIGN=RIGHT SRC="icedive.jpg"><H1>ICE DIVING<BR>
<FONT SIZE=-1><B>Certification Program:</B> $245.00</FONT></H1>
<P>
Join us for one of our New England ice diving weekends.  This is the perfect opportunity to prepare yourself for our Arctic or Antarctic dive expeditions.  Participants will become certified ice divers, pending completion of three dives.  You need to have a minimum of advanced open water or equivalent and should be drysuit trained.  Price includes training and certification.
