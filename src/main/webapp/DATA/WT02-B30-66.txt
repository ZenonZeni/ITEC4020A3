<A NAME="objective"><STRONG>Investment Objective</FONT></STRONG></A>
<BR>
To seek to provide investment results that correspond to the total return performance
(reflecting reinvestment of dividends) of common stocks in the aggregate, as represented by the
Standard & Poor's 500 Composite Stock Price Index. 
<P>
(Unlike funds that seek to "beat" the market, index funds seek to "match" their respective indexes.  S&P 500 Stock Index is a registered trademark of Standard & Poor's.  S&P 500 Stock Index is an unmanaged index that is considered representative of the U.S. stock market.  Inclusion of a security in the S&P 500 in no way implies an opinion by Standard and Poor's as to its attractiveness as an investment.  The Fund is neither sponsored by nor affiliated with Standard & Poor's.)
<P>
<BR>




<A NAME="f_profile"><STRONG>Fund Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>The Fund seeks to replicate the investment results of the S&P 500 Composite Stock Price Index, a broad-based
measure of stock market conditions composed of 500 common stocks selected by Standard & Poor's.
<LI>The Fund uses the S&P 500 Composite Stock Price Index as the standard performance because it represents
approximately two-thirds of the total market value of all U.S. common stocks, and is well-known
to investors.
<LI>Normally, at least 80% of the Fund's net assets will be invested in stocks listed in the
Index.
</UL>
<P>

<A NAME="i_profile"><STRONG>Investor Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>Investors seeking long-term capital growth.
<LI>Investors who seek to reduce risk by investing in a broad selection of common stocks.
</UL>

Before you invest...
<BR>
The Fund's broad diversification across issuers and industries helps to reduce the risks of
investing in individual securities or market sectors.  The values of common stocks of major U.S. corporations tend to
fluctuate based on a variety of
market and general economic conditions.
