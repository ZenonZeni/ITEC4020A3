<H2>Bipolar Risk</H2></center>

 <HR>



	According to an article in the fall 1995 NARSAD Research Newsletter ("Advances in Genetics of Psychiatric Disorders" by Anne Brown), "For bipolar disorder, if an individual has one affected parent, the risk of developing the disorder is 27%, and if both parents are affected, the risk increases to 74%.  For schizophrenia, the corresponding risks are 9% and 46%."
