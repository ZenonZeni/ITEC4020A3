<H1><A HREF="STD37.s.gif">Sea Surface Height Variability</A></H1><P>
</CENTER>

The picture shows the Ocean Sea Surface Height Variability as observed by TOPEX/POSEIDON during
its first year of operation (September 23, 1992 - September 24, 1993), in color 
coding. <P>

The Code Name for this image is: STD37. It was completed in May 1994. It is
filed at the JPL Photo Lab under number P-44075.<P>

The actual final product was a 24-bit, 5100 x 3500 image, which was printed on
Ektachrome film.<P>

The version provided here is an 8-bit, 1024 x  702 GIF image.
