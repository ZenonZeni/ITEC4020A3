<FONT FACE="Arial"><H2>January 11, 1997</H2>

<P>SCIENCE.ORG is now a member of the <A HREF="http://www.linkexchange.com/">Internet
Link Exchange</A>.</P>

<P>The <A HREF="http://computers.science.org/internet/site/setup/">setting
up an Internet site</A> resource in the <A HREF="http://computers.science.org/">SCIENCE.ORG
Computer Lab</A> has a new look.</P>

<H2>December 29, 1996 </H2>

<P>SCIENCE.ORG has eliminated its Java-only Web sites after months of experimentation
and seemingly endless problems. We apologize to the thousands of visitors
who were unable to view our sites during this time. We remain convinced
that Java is an important technology for operating-system and hardware
architecture-independent computing but have been forced to conclude that
Java has a long way to go before it is a viable platform upon which to
build and deploy Internet content. Watch for research papers and additional
Web content that will present our experimental results in more detail.</P>
