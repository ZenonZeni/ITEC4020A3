<FONT SIZE="+1"><STRONG>The MainStay Funds Make Investing Easy </STRONG></FONT><P>



<STRONG>Low minimum investment</STRONG><BR>
You can invest in The MainStay Funds for as little as $500. The MainStay Money Market and
Equity
Index Funds
require an initial investment of $1,000.
<P>
<STRONG>Systematic Investment Plan</STRONG><BR>
You can invest a set amount at regular intervals with automatic withdrawals from
your
checking
account. In most Funds, you can start with as little as $100 and make regular
investments in
modest
amounts. <SUP><FONT SIZE="-1"><A HREF="#1">1</A></FONT></SUP> Different
minimums apply to
the Money Market and Equity Index Funds. See The MainStay Funds' <A
HREF="mfpr.html">prospectus</A>
for details. 
<P>
<STRONG>Automatic reinvestment</STRONG><BR>
You may reinvest all Fund distributions - dividends and capital gains - in
additional shares
at net
asset value without a sales charge. 
<P>
<STRONG>Free exchanges - by phone or mail</STRONG><BR>
You may exchange shares of a single class among any of The MainStay Funds offering that
class of
shares without a sales charge.<SUP><FONT SIZE="-1"><A HREF="#2">2</A></FONT></SUP> 
<P>
<STRONG>Easy access to your money</STRONG><BR>
Fund shares may be redeemed upon written request at their current net asset value,
less
any
applicable Contingent Deferred Sales Charge, on any day the New York Stock Exchange
is
open for
business. Keep in mind that due to market conditions, when your shares are
redeemed, they
may be
worth more or less than their original cost. 

<P>
<STRONG>Systematic Withdrawal Plan</STRONG><BR>
If your Fund account is valued at $10,000 or more, you may elect to receive
monthly checks
in fixed
amounts of $100 or more.
<P>
<STRONG>Complete recordkeeping</STRONG><BR>
As a shareholder, you will receive confirmation of all purchases, transfers,
exchanges, and
redemptions. You will also receive regular reports from the Fund, including
investment
results, changes
in the portfolio holdings, and audited financial statements.
<P>
<STRONG>Two classes of shares</STRONG><BR>
With most of The MainStay Funds you can choose to pay sales charges when you buy your
shares
- with
Class A shares - or when you redeem - with Class B shares. When deciding whether to
purchase a
particular class of shares, you should consider the investment amount, anticipated
holding
period, and
other important factors. The prospectus contains more complete information about
advisory
fees, other
expenses, and share classes. Please read the <A HREF="mfpr.html">prospectus</A>
carefully before you
invest or
send money. 

<P>
<FONT SIZE="-1"><SUP><FONT SIZE="-2"><A NAME="1"><B>1</B></A></FONT></SUP>
Investors
should consider their ability to continue to make purchases through periods of
varying price
levels.
<P>
<SUP><FONT SIZE="-2"><A NAME="2"><B>2</B></A></FONT></SUP> Like all redemptions
from
the Equity
Index Fund, exchange redemptions may affect the guarantee. The exchange privilege
may
not be
materially modified or terminated except upon notice. Shares must be offered in the
investor's state of
residence.  Certain exchanges may be subject to a sales charge.  Please see the prospectus for details.
<P>
