<H1>Decompression Diving</H1>
<UL><H2>Advanced Training: Nitrox & Trimix</H2>
<P>Advanced divers who want to learn about decompression diving or enhance the safety of both their decompression and no decompression dives should obtain training in the use of mixed gases such as NITROX and TRIMIX.  The advantages of using these gases are now widely accepted and considered essential for technical diving.  We offer training from basic NITROX through TRIMIX in conjunction with our cave and wreck expeditions.  <B>Our New York clients will also be intrerested in hearing that we have recentley established Manhattan's first and only Nitrox and mixed gas filling station and training facility.</B> On certain trips we will also offer an introduction to rebreather use.  As with the wreck diving, these courses are tailored to individual needs and prices vary according to options chosen. <I><A HREF="tech.html">Technical training</A> is through IANTD and Recreational training is through PADI.</I>
