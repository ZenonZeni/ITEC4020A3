<h2>Where to get help in Victoria: </h2>




<table border=3 width = 100%>
<tr><td width=10%></td><td><b>Self Help Legal Information: <br>
<br>
<a href="http://www.vicnet.net.au/%7Eeclc/eclckr00.htm"><b>Know your Rights in Victoria</b></a> Most of us only think about the law when something goes wrong, and when it does, we are, quite naturally, anxious to know where we stand . It is important that you know your rights as well as your responsibilities. 
<br>
<a href="http://www.vicnet.net.au/%7Eeclc/eclcpp00.htm"><b>Police Powers - Your Rights</b></a> An in depth guide to police powers with regard to arresting, questioning, obtaining samples, etc. 
<br>
<a href="http://www.vicnet.net.au/%7Eeclc/eclcwc00.htm"><b>Where to Complain in Victoria</b></a> You've got a complaint but you don't know who to see about it. This guide lists some organisations where you can make complaints or get help about a range of problems. 
<br>
<a href="http://www.vicnet.net.au/~eclc/indechil.htm.htm"><b>Child Support</b></a> Information on the financial support of children after separation of their parents. 
<br>
<a href="http://www.vicnet.net.au/%7Eeclc/eclcpubs.htm"><b>Educational and Resource Material</b></a> Here you can check out a number of kits produced by ECLC. These include a do-it-yourself motor vehicle collision kit, a wills kit, and power of attorney kit. 
<br>
</b><a href="flspubs.htm#FLS.00"><img src="lhbook.gif" alt=" " border = 0 align = "right"><font color=#ff0000>The Law Handbook</font></a> <br>
<br>
<b>Welfare Services and Community Agencies:</b> 
<p>
<a href="http://www.vicnet.net.au/vicnet/community/cafca/fin.htm" >Financial Counselling Services in Victoria</a>
<br>
<a href="http://www.vicnet.net.au">Victoria's Network</a> <br>
<a href="http://www.vicnet.net.au/vicnet/COM.HTM#hpage">Victorian Community Information:</a> A huge list via [Vicnet] 
<br>
<a href="http://www.infoxchange.net.au/ixssd.htm" >The Info Xchange Online Support Services</a>   Search engine to a huge list of welfare and support agencies throughout Victoria. 
<br>
<a href="http://www.vicnet.net.au/vicnet/COM.HTM#hpage">Victorian Community Groups and Community Services</a> Home page listing of Community groups and Community services throughout Victoria. Also includes links to overseas community initiatives. 
<br>
<a href="http://michell.maths.adelaide.edu.au/Applied/WRC/wrc.html">Welfare Rights Safety Net (South Australia)</a> Access &quot;Social Security information&quot;
