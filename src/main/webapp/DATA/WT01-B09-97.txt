<h3>Southern Coalition for Advanced Transportation (SCAT)</h3><p>

<blockquote>
<B>Project Title:  Hybrid Electric - CNG Pickup Conversion<p>
Performing Organization:  Florida Power & Light; FACT; Saminco; Solar Car Corporation, Fisher Electric Motor Technology</B><p>

<P>POC: Bob Suggs, Telephone (305) 522­4133</P>

<P><B>Program Summary: </B></P> 

<P>A 1994 Chevy S­10 electric pickup (for commercial use) will be converted using CNG to fuel the hybrid
motor as well as advanced A/C drive systems and climate control system Training and O & M manuals
will be provided</P>

<P>The benefit derived will be range extension while also increasing fuel efficiency </P>
