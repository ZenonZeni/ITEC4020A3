<P><H2> Catalog Description </H2>
<BLOCKQUOTE>
Dynamics, and control of robotic manipulators;
emphasis on fundamental concepts and analytical methods
for analysis and design of robotic systems; laboratory
experiments and computer simulation complement the
theoretical development.
</BLOCKQUOTE>
<P><H2>  Textbook </H2>
M.W. Spong, and M. Vidyasagar, Robot Dynamics and Control, John Wiley & Sons, Inc., New York, NY, 1989.
