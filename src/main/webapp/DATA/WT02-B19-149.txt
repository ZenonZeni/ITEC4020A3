<title> Robotics, Dynamic Systems and Controls</title>
<h1> Robotics, Dynamic Systems and Controls</h1>
<img align=middle src="/icons/line.blue.gif" alt="">
<p>

<b>MIE1001H - Dynamics II </b> - <a href="../../staff/profiles/zu.html"> J.W. Zu </a>
<br>
Variational principles and Lagrange's Equations, Hamilton's 
principle.  Kinematics of rigid body motion, Euler angles, rigid 
body equations of motion. Hamilton's equations, cyclic 
coordinates, Legendre transformations. Canonical 
transformations, Hamilton-Jacobi theory.<p>

<b>MIE1005H - Theory of Vibrations I</b> - <a href="../../staff/profiles/cleghorn.html"> W.L. Cleghorn  </a>
<br>
Multi-degree of freedom systems, using both analytical and 
approximate methods.  Vibrations of continuous systems, 
including strings, bars and membranes.  Natural modes of 
plate vibration - approximate methods such as Rayleigh's 
Energy Methods, Rayleigh-Ritz Method, Galerkin's Method, 
and assumed mode method.  Introduction to finite element 
analysis.<p>

<b>MIE1009H - Applied Acoustics - D.L. Allen </b>
<br>
Behaviour and measurement of sound waves.  Sound 
propagation in enclosures of various sizes.  The transmission 
through solid and porous materials.  Reactive and dissipative 
mufflers, criteria for acceptable noise levels in occupied areas.  
Auditory effects of noise.  Hearing loss.  Methods and materials 
commonly used for controlling noise.<p>

<b>MIE1043H - Control Systems</b> - <a href="../../staff/profiles/vandevegte.html"> J. Van de Vegte </a>
<br>
Digital control of dynamic systems.  State space description 
and transfer function matrix description of the dynamics of 
multivariable systems. The Control of multivariable systems, 
such as power plants and paper mills.  Multivariable control 
system design techniques, and their digital implementation.<p>

<b>MIE1062H - Robot Kinematics and Dynamics</b> - <a href="../../staff/profiles/goldenberg.html> A.A. Goldenberg</a>
<br>
Displacement of rigid body. Rigid body kinematics. Motion of 
kinematic chains. Differential kinematics.  Statics.  Dynamics of 
robot manipulators.<p>

<b>MIE1063H - Introduction to Theoretical Kinematics</b> - <a href="../../staff/profiles/goldenberg.html"> A.A. Goldenberg </a>
<br>
Displacements. Motion of rigid body. Screw theory. 
Quaternions. Structure equations.  Constraint manifolds.<p>

<b>MIE1064H - Control Analysis Methods with Applications to Robotics</b> - <a href="../../staff/profiles/mills.html"> J.K. Mills </a>
<br>
Robotic control problem formulation, advance dynamic 
formulation for control application, dynamic model 
formulations, linear, nonlinear, stability definitions, local and 
global stability methods, integration of manipulator dynamic 
equations of motion, differential-algebraic systems. <p>

<b>MIE1066H - Advanced Robot Mechanics and Control</b> - <a href="../../staff/profiles/goldenberg.html"> A.A. Goldenberg</a>
<br>
Position control:  joint & Cartesian spaces.  Force control.  
Constrained motion control.  Grasping & manipulation.  
Stability of grasp.  Dynamics of grasping. Control of grasping. 
Special topics: coordinated arms, variable structure, 
telerobotics. Prerequisite: MIE1062H. <p>
