<H1>DANIEL ZENDER</H1><P>DANIEL ZENDER practices in the areas of land use, natural resources, real estate and business
law.  He is a licensed land surveyor, with a degree in forest engineering.  He represents developers,
property owners and businesses.  He is especially active in forest practices, represents many major
forest land owners, and is active at the federal, state and regional levels in forestry issues.  He is a
lifetime Whatcom County resident, graduated from the University of Washington (1967) and from
Gonzaga School of Law (1973, magna cum laude).<P>
