<H1>Sensory evaluation</H1>
<HR SIZE=1 NOSHADE>



<P><IMG ALIGN=RIGHT SRC="booth200.gif" alt=testing booth align=bottom>The taste, smell, touch, and feel of consumer goods are ultimately the factors that influence repeat purchases. RSSL&#39;s sensory evaluation studies are conducted using trained analysts who are able to detect and describe accurately the sensory characteristics of products. These methods may be used to compare one product against its competitors, and further, to understand the factors that drive consumer preference for one product over another. This enables companies to reformulate products having due regard for those characterisitics that will make the product preferred by a majority of consumers.</P>

<P>Sensory evaluation is also used in <A HREF=taint.html>taint and off-flavour</A> investigations giving analysts the vital first clues that allow them to direct chemical investigations at the most likely cause of the problem.</P>
