<title>Wind Plants of Cumbria, England</title>

<head><b><h1>Wind Plants of Cumbria, England</h1></b></head><p>

<body>

The British firm Windcluster is attempting to develop wind plants on 

unused land near harbors or old industrial sites. In their first 

project, Windcluster installed five Vestas V27 turbines on an 

abandoned air field. The airport at Haverigg on the Cumbrian coast had 

not been used since World War II when it was an army air base.<p>
