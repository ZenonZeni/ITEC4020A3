<P><H4>
The American College of Traditional Chinese Medicine seeks, as applicants 
to its Masters degree program, mature students with demonstrated academic 
ability who are motivated toward a career in the healing arts. 
<P>
Part-time degree students are currently accepted in any quarter.  Students are
admitted to the full-time degree program usually in the Fall quarter. 
Applicants for admission to the part-time Non-degree  program 
are accepted at any time.</H4>
