<B>NHERI In the News: Recent Contacts NHERI Has Had With the Media</B><P>Recently, the NHERI researchers (Dr. Brian Ray in Oregon, and Bill Lloyd in Washington DC) have had a number of contacts with local and national media concerning home schooling.<P>Here is a list of whom we have spoken to in the past few months.<P><B>Interviews/Events with Dr. Brian Ray</B><P>3/96: NHERI supplied information to a parent in California who is in a court case.<P>3/96: NHERI supplied information to a parent in Massachusetts who is in a court case.<P>3/7/96: Marguerite Kelly, a syndicated reporter with the <B>Washington Post</B>, interviewed Dr. Brian Ray.<P>3/15/96: Dr. Brian Ray was one of keynote speakers at Indiana Association of Home Educators' 11th Annual Home Educators' Convention in Indianapolis IN.<P>3/19/96: Joanna Natale interviewed Dr. Brian Ray for <B>Executive Educator</B> magazine (published by National School Board Association) regarding home education &amp; wants/needs of home educators.<P>3/25/96: Dr. Brian Ray was invited speaker at Third Annual Global Village Schools Conference in San Francisco on March 25-27, gave a presentation as part of the plenary session panel entitled &quot;Shaking the Foundations: The Rise of Alternative Educational Structures - Home Schooling&quot; and Dr. Ray was organizer/presiding panelist of the breakout panel entitled &quot;The Home Schooling Phenomenon and Traditional Schools: Adversaries or Partners?&quot;<P>3/26/96: Lynn Schnaiberg of <B>&quot;Education Week&quot;</B>, a weekly publication interviewed Dr. Brian Ray.<P>4/96: NHERI supplied information to a parent in Minnesota who is in a court case.<P>4/18/96: Kathy Prentice, a freelance writer, interviewed Dr. Brian Ray for story that will be in August issue &quot;React&quot; weekly insert in newspapers and is published by <B>Parade Magazine</B>.<P>4/18/96: Alison Hashimoto of CNN News interviewed Dr. Brian Ray for <B>&quot;Parenting Today&quot;</B> Saturday show.<P>5/96: NHERI supplied information to a parent in Washington who is in a court case.<P>5/2/96: Marko C. Leavitt of Associated Press in Salt Lake City, UT interviewed Dr. Brian Ray on home education.<P>5/9/96: Kathy Prentice, a freelance writer, interviewed Dr. Brian Ray again for story that will be in August issue &quot;React&quot; weekly insert in newspapers published by <B>Parade Magazine</B>.<P><B>Interviews/Events with Bill Lloyd</B><P>2/20/96: Bill Lloyd was interviewed by Walter Jones, a newspaper writer for the Griffin, GA Daily News concerning home schooling.<P>2/21/96: Bill Lloyd was interviewed by Brian Barber, a newspaper writer for the Waterloo, IA Courier concerning home schooling.<P>3/4/96: Bill Lloyd was interviewed by Kate Confair, a newspaper writer for the Coral Springs, FL Forum concerning home schooling high-school-aged students.<P>3/8/96: Bill Lloyd was interviewed by Jason Method, a newspaper writer for the Asbury Park, NJ Press concerning home schooling.<P>3/15/96: Bill Lloyd appeared on Lifestyle Magazine, a nationally-syndicated talk show program, as the research expert on home schooling issues.<P>4/1/96: Bill Lloyd was contacted by Maria Fitzpatrick of <B>Zoobooks</B>, an educational magazine for children, about distributing thematic curriculums for the Zoobooks series to home-schooling parents.<P>4/1/96: Bill Lloyd was interviewed by Pam Ramsey, a freelance reporter, concerning home schooling; her article will appear in the magazine <B>Family Life</B> later this year.<P>5/96: Bill Lloyd is scheduled to speak at the following home schooling conferences:<P>the Home Educators Association of Virginia (HEAV) in June 1996<P>the Christian Home Educators Network of Maryland (CHEN) conference in June 1996<P>5/1/96: Bill Lloyd was interviewed by Lisa Applegate, a newspaper writer in Lynchburg, VA concerning home schooling.<P>5/7/96: Bill Lloyd was interviewed on WJHU Radio in Baltimore, MD for a program about home schooling.<P>5/21/96: Bill Lloyd was interviewed by Kevin Kilbane, a newspaper writer for the Fort Wayne, IN News-Sentinel concerning home schooling.<P>5/28/96: Bill Lloyd and his home-schooling family were the subject of a program to be aired by Maryland Public Television on home schooling. Bill was interviewed concerning his knowledge of home-schooling research, and his wife and children were asked about their experiencesas a home schooling family.<P><B>Ongoing events</B><P>NHERI continues its nationwide and longitudinal study of home education across the United States.<P>NHERI continues its study of home education in Illinois.<P>NHERI continues its study of home education in Massachusetts.<P>If you know of reporters in your area who are doing articles on home schooling, let them know about NHERI, and have them contact Brian Ray at the Oregon home office, or Bill Lloyd in the Washington DC NHERI branch office.
