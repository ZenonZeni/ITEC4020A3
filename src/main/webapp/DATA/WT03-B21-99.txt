The Conceptus Soft Seal Cervical Catheter is designed for atraumatic, transcervical access to the uterus.  During use, the latex balloon is inflated with an air filled syringe to seal the cervical canal.  A two-way stopcock located on the inflation port maintains the balloon volume.  The Soft Seal Cervical Catheter has a luer lock port at the proximal end for easy injection of dye.</TD>
