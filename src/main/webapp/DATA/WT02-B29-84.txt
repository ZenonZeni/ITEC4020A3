<FONT SIZE="+1"><STRONG>What's Available?</STRONG></FONT><P>



<STRONG>Is plan document assistance available?</STRONG><BR>
Many of our clients use our plan design and document preparation services.  We
currently offer plan
sponsors the choice between a prototype, volume submitter, or individually
designed
plan document.
We have the ability to administer a variety of plan document types, including
customized plan
documents.  We encourage use of our volume submitter document; however, plan
sponsors have the
option to maintain their current plan documents subject to our administrative
review and approval.
<P>
	<STRONG>Does NYLBS provide a draft summary plan description?</STRONG><BR>
Our legal staff reviews all plan documentation, including the summary plan
description, and
recommends updates to these documents.  If you so elect, we will also draft and
update the documents
for you.<P>

	<STRONG>Are actuarial services available as needed?</STRONG><BR>
We provide substantial value-added expertise to our clients because of our
full-service professional
capabilities.  In addition to our investment, administrative and communications
services, we provide
plan design and regulatory compliance consulting, defined benefit administration
and actuarial
services, regulatory audit and determination letter services, health and welfare
consulting and support
services, and non-qualified deferred compensation programs.  We also have a
full-service bundled
defined benefit program called DB Complete<FONT SIZE="-1"><SUP>SM</SUP></FONT>.  
<P>
	<STRONG>Are there special management reports available upon
request?</STRONG><BR>
We provide a variety of standard administrative/management reports to the plan
sponsor, including:
<P>


Management Reports:
<UL>
<EM><LI>Administrative Report</EM> - details periodic participant
activity by fund, source
and transaction type (contribution, withdrawal, etc.).  The frequency of this
report is mutually agreed
upon.
</UL>
<P>
Trust Reports:
<UL TYPE=DISC>
<LI><EM>Consolidated Trust Reports</EM> - report the plan activity
on the trust level
for a specified period (generally monthly). 
<P>
<LI><EM>Monthly Reporting:</EM>
<UL>
<LI><EM>Trust Fund Summary</EM> - reconciles plan assets on a monthly
basis.
<LI><EM>Investment Fund Transaction Summaries</EM> - these individual cash basis
asset
statements are provided monthly.
<LI><EM>Plan Trial Balance</EM> - details the activity posted to the
plan during the month. 
The activity is broken down by fund and money source and includes the total plan
holdings by fund.
</UL></UL>
