<b>T<small>HE</small> V<small>IETNAM</small> B<small>USINESS</small> J<small>OURNAL</small></b> accepts display and classified advertising in both the print and online editions. Special packages are available for advertisers who wish to take advantage of the opportunities created by using both mediums to communicate to The VBJ's readers.<p>

The VBJ in print is circulated throughout the world and has a readership of more than 20,000. The magazine is read by senior executives and key decision makers and is available by subscription and on newsstands worldwide. The VBJ can also be found at embassies, consulates and missions, as well as at most Vietnam-related associations and organizations.<p>

The VBJ has offices in both Hanoi and New York City. Additional advertising sales offices are located in Singapore, Thailand, Japan and Hong Kong.<p><br><br>

<center><font size=+2><b>Display and Classified Advertising Online</b></font></center><p>

Advertising on this site is available as a button, banner or classified listing. Buttons are found at the top of the "Homepage" and the "Current Issue" pages; banners are found at the top of the "Cover Story," "Company Profile," "FYI," "Street Talk," "Table of Contents" and "Editor's Letter" pages; and classified listing are found on the Classified Advertising page. For special opportunities for print advertisers, please send an e-mail to <a href="mailto:onlinead@viam.com">onlinead@viam.com</a>.<p>
