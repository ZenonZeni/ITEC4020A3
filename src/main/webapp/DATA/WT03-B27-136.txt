<H3>INVESTMENT (IV)</H3>

<B>APS Review--IV27</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/iv27.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Arab Press Service, Nicosia, Cyprus.<BR>
Distributed by Input Solutions<BR>
$5.00/article; $50.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly; current issue retained<BR>
(Also in EY, IT, Mideast)<BR>
Divided into three sections: Oil Market Trends, Gas Market Trends, Downstream
Trends.<P>

<B>Buyouts--IV70</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/iv70.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Securities Data Publishing<BR>
$3.00/article; $50.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 11/11/96<BR>
(Also in FI)<BR>
This biweekly newsletter provides news, data analysis, and interpretation of 
trends in the buyout industry. It reports on new funds, existing and completed transactions, exits, and fund closes, as well as reports on general partners, 
deal makers, and intermediaries.<P>

<B>FirstList: Companies Available for Acquisition--IV18</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/iv18.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Vision Quest Publishing Inc.<BR>
$1.00/article; $60.00/issue<BR>
Retains current issue only<BR>
(Also in BusOps)<BR>
Provides profiles of businesses available for acquisition, those seeking
acquisition, sources of financing, ventures seeking financing, and joint venture
and licensing opportunities. Most of the listings are placed by business brokers
and intermediaries working on a contingency fee basis for the companies and,
periodically, directly for the owners.<P>

<B>International BusinessMan News Report--IV51</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/iv51.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
International BusinessMan<BR>
$2.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly; retained one year plus current year<BR>
(Also in FI, IT)<BR>
Gives full contact information on and direct phone numbers to international
moneymen and banks. Useful for developing new business and new money sources.<P>

<B>International Stocks Database--IV50</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/iv50.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Vision Information Inc.<BR>
$2.00/article<BR>
Updated monthly; retained one year plus current year<BR>
(Also in IT)<BR>
Publishes current business data on corporations publicly traded on many worldwide
securities exchanges. The directories are grouped according to country and
represent all major exchanges within the country. All data is edited monthly and,
where applicable, updated.<P>

<B>Investor Relations Business--IV74</B><BR>
Securities Data Publishing<BR>
$3.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 11/11/96<BR>
(Also in GB)<BR>
This biweekly title covers changes in securities laws, corporate government, 
proxy developments, technology and the Internet, disclosure and board elections, career guidance and practice management, and consulting services. It also
offers consulting and public relations firms insights how to promote companies,
updates on the competition, and profiles of investment.<P>

<B>Investors Chronicle--IV17</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/iv17.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Financial Times Ltd.<BR>
$3.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 3/11/94<BR>
(Also in FT)<BR>
Provides company information and investment news. Useful for anyone interested in
investments, from CEOs to individuals planning their retirement.<P>

<B>The IPO Aftermarket--IV75</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/iv75.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Securities Data Publishing<BR>
$3.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 11/11/96<BR>
(Also in FI)<BR>
This weekly title provides news and analysis of the initial public offering
market. It follows companies through the IPO process, offering data and news on
filings and pricings, plus a complete calendar of upcoming issues. This weekly
newsletter also covers investment information on the IPO aftermarket, including
analysts' estimates and earnings projections from the 25 day quiet.<P>

<B>RateGram Online--IV61</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/iv61.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
The Bradshaw Group<BR>
$1.00/article; $15.00/issue<BR>
Available from 8/21/95--4/23/96<BR>
(Also in FI, GB)<BR>
Provides an up-to-date directory of the nation's highest federally insured yields
offered by banks and thrifts. Covers the gamut of fixed net asset value
investments, including CDs of all maturities, and money market funds.<P>

<B>SEC Today--IV16</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/iv16.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Washington Service Bureau<BR>
$2.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Available from 4/19/94--2/19/96<BR>
(Also in GT, Day)<BR>
Provides articles featuring SEC rule-making activities, legislative developments,
significant no-action letters, and other regulatory and industry issues. Also
included is the SEC News Digest, the SEC's own daily summary of proceedings, and a
daily filings guide listing 8-Ks, registrations, Williams Act filings, and other
filings as they are made public.<P>

<B>Trendvest Ratings--IV34</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/iv34.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Trendvest Corp.<BR>
$2.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly; 16-week retention<BR>
(Also in FI, GB)<BR>
Fully integrated market-timing and stock-selection system which provides a single
numerical rating to summarize relative attractiveness of investment possibilities.
No investment advice is given.<P>
