<P>
<A NAME="objective"><STRONG>Investment Objective</FONT></STRONG></A>
<BR>Seeks to provide long-term growth of capital commensurate with an acceptable level of risk by investing in a portfolio consisting primarily of non-U.S. equity securities. Current income is a secondary objective.

<P>
<BR>
<A NAME="f_profile"><STRONG>Fund Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>

<UL>
<LI>The Fund takes a "top-down" approach, emphasizing country selection which the investment adviser believes to be the single most important decision in managing an international portfolio.
<LI>In selecting countries, the adviser considers overall economic strength and  fundamental value. Each country is ranked by value and a decision is made as to how much to invest in each.
<LI>The Fund's portfolio is broadly diversified emphasizing investment in large, well-established companies, seeking to own a cross-section of the leading companies in each target country.
<LI>For additional risk control, the portfolio managers hedge foreign currencies against  unfavorable changes.
</UL>


<P><A NAME="i_profile"><STRONG>Investor Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>Investors seeking the potential for higher returns and willing to assume higher risks
<LI>Long-term investors pursuing capital growth and willing to assume higher risks
<LI>Investors seeking to invest in equities worldwide and willing to assume higher risks
<LI>Long-term investors seeking to broaden their investment horizon
</UL>
Before you invest...
<BR>Investments in foreign securities may be subject to greater risks than domestic investments. These risks include fluctuations of currencies, changes in U.S. or foreign tax or currency laws, and changes in monetary policies and economic and political conditions in foreign countries.
