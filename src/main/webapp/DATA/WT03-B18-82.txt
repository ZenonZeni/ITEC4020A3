<CENTER><P><FONT COLOR="#FFFFFF">Mars appears reddish in our sky, even

reddish dust. Mars has two small moons - Phobos is the larger and zips
around Mars in only 7 1/2 hours. Deimos, the smaller moon, takes 30 hours
to orbit. </FONT></P></CENTER>
