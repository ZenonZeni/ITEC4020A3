<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>Training</STRONG></FONT><P>                                                                                                
                                                                                                                   
                    Rixan Associates offers complete on site training as well as training at our Dayton, Ohio facility.
  Classes are four days in duration       and cover every aspect from "Set Up" to "Programming" and                
  "Preventative Maintenance". In addition, a two day class is offered for "M" series maintenance. Training
  at Rixan's facility is available for     1996 in the months of February, April, June, August, October and        
  December. For additional information on Training please select the "FOR MORE INFORMATION" icon. <P>       
                                                                                                                   
<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>  Software Programs       </STRONG></FONT><P>                                                                                          
                                                                                                                   
        Rixan Associates offers  custom robot programs to meet customers specific robot integration needs.
  In addition, Rixan also offers a RoboWare<SUP><FONT SIZE=1>tm</FONT></SUP>;
 programming/control software. This software is a Window<SUP><FONT SIZE=1>tm</FONT></SUP>;     
  based software programming environment with a collection of utilities to assist user interface between
  the robot and a PC compatible computer. This downloads the latest version and bug fixes. <P>                
                                                                                                                   
<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>  Design Services      </STRONG></FONT> <P>                                                                                         
                                                                                                                   
        Rixan Associates offers a        variety of design services to its customers. The design services offered     
  include robot integration (hardware I/0 integration), end of arm tooling, and AUTOCAD<SUP><FONT SIZE=1>tm</FONT></SUP>; drawings.            
                   <P>
                                                                                                
<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>Feasibility Studies  </STRONG></FONT><P>                                                                                            
                                                                                                                   
       Rixan Associates offers feasibility studies which provide customers with suggestions for robot type
  end of arm tooling and I/0 requirements to meet the customer's specifications. A documentation of the     
  study  along with  a video  tape are  provided to the customer after the study is completed. <P>                  

<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>  Consulting Services  </STRONG></FONT><P>                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                        
                      Rixan Associates offers   a    variety of consulting services. These services include engineering
  consulting, programming consulting, and integration consulting. Engineers and sales people are available
  to answer any questions over the phone or set up appointments to meet and discuss applications. <P>            
                                                                                                                        
<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>End-of-Arm Tooling (E.A.T) </STRONG></FONT><P>                                                                               
                                                                                                                        
      Rixan Associates offers end-of-arm tooling. Our engineers are skilled in the design and manufacturing of end-of-arm tooling with over 40 
years of experience in this area.<P>                      
                                                                                                                        
<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>  Update Bulletins</STRONG></FONT> <P>                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                        
        Rixan Associates offers              update bulletins to its customers so that they are aware of any new product
  or service available to them. These bulletins include the latest RoboWare upgrades, new robots            
  available, new services available,           training class schedules, and engineering specification upgrades. Rixan

  best performance possible from              their robots. <P>
