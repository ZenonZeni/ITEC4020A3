<h4 align=center>Helpline Fact Sheet </h4>
<p align=center><font color="#000080"><b>Parity for Mental Illnesses under Health Care Reform</b></font></p>
<hr>
<p><strong>Parity means that under health care reform, mental illnesses would be given the same 
coverage &amp; benefits of physical illnesses </strong></p>
<p><font color="#FF0000">How to make a difference:</font></p>
<blockquote>
<p>1 - Senators Peter Domenici &amp; Paul Wellstone introduced an amendment to the 
Health Care Reform Bill to make sure mental illnesses were treated the same as 
other physical illnesses (it was passed) </p>
<p>2 - call your representative in Congress to voice your opinion about parity 
under the Domenici-Wellstone amendment </p>
<p>3 - call Capitol Hill for the number of your congressperson at 202-224-3121 or 
1-800-972-3524</p>
