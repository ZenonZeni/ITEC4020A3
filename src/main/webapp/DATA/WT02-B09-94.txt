<p><b><font SIZE=+1>To place an order:</font></b> 
<br></p>

<ol>
<li><font SIZE=+1>Find the model you want by scrolling through the models
in the selection box under the &quot;Model&quot; column heading below (by
clicking on the box's up/down scrolling arrows).</font></li>

<li><font SIZE=+1>Once the name of the model you want appears in the box,
highlight it by clicking on it.</font></li>

<li><font SIZE=+1>Then, type a number in the corresponding &quot;Quantity&quot;
box to the right indicating how many you would like to purchase.</font></li>

<li><font size=+1>If you need a voltage and/or plug different from those used in the United 
States, type this information in the "Country/Voltage" column. Otherwise, leave it blank.</li>

<li><font size=+1>Finally, fill in the information at the bottom of this form (e.g., name,
address, etc.) and press the "send order" button. We will use this latter information to
contact you (most likely by phone) and complete the order.</li>
</ol>

<p><font SIZE=+1>Up to three <i>different</i> models can be ordered at
one time (one model per &quot;item&quot; line). If you need
