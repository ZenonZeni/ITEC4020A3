<TITLE>CIS590: Computers, Ethics, and Society</TITLE>
<H1><A HREF="big_brother_inside_logo.gif"><IMG SRC="big_brother_inside_logo.small.gif" ALT=""></A>CIS590: Computers, Ethics, and Society</H1>

<BLOCKQUOTE>
<I>  This course will investigate the various issues and facets of
     Computer Crime and Computer Ethics. Some topics to be researched
     include: general ethics (utilitarian, kantian, nicomachean),
     privacy, data protection, employee privacy, data regulation,
     artificial intelligence, information technology and the third world,
     the "look-and-feel" issue, computer malfunction liability, computer
     crime (electronic trespassing), and the Professional Responsibility
     of the Computer User.
</I></BLOCKQUOTE>
