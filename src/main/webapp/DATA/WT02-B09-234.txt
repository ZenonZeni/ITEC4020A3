<H4>Introduction</H4>
As teenagers in the Burnaby/New Westminster area, you may be faced with various questions and decisions.  <B><I>The Youth Pages</B></I> is a resource directory written especially for you.  This directory focuses on critical topics and offers accessible information and contact numbers to develop your awareness of services in your area.
