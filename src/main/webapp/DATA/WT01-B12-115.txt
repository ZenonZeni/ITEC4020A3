<BLOCKQUOTE>

At Kemper Funds, we believe that establishing a relationship with
a professional financial representative is the best way to take
advantage of today's investment alternatives. Mutual funds are excellent
building blocks for your portfolio, with their built-in diversification,
professional management and convenience. And, with over 6,000 funds
available today, we believe the best way to choose among them is with
the guidance and advice of a professional financial advisor.
