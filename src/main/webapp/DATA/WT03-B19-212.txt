<H1 ALIGN=CENTER><b>Victoria's  Key  Industrial  Strengths</b></H1>
<P ALIGN=CENTER><BR><BR>
<img src="../gifs/line.colorbar.gif">
<p><br><p>

Victoria offers internationally competitive advantages for companies in established and newly emerging services and manufacturing sectors.  <P>

The State's strong export growth shows that Victoria is the place for companies to achieve sustained growth and profitability.
<P>

The Victorian Government has identified a range of key industry sectors 
in which it is  concentrating its efforts to generate the greatest rate 
of sustainable economic growth for Victoria and at the same time enhancing 
the State's international competitiveness.
<P>
The key industry sectors selected have the following characteristics:
<P>
They enjoy a growing share of world trade and form a significant part 
of Victoria's industrial, educational and research base.  
These sectors are:
<P>
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="aerospace.html">aerospace</A>
<LI><A HREF="aviation.html">aviation</A>
<LI><A HREF="chemplas.html">chemicals, plastics and rubber</A>
<LI><A HREF="education.html">education services</A>
<LI><A HREF="infotech.html">information technology and telecommunications</A>
<LI><A HREF="pharma.html">pharmaceuticals</A>
<LI><A HREF="scimedequ.html">scientific and medical equipment</A>
<LI><A HREF="tourism.html">tourism</A>
<LI><A HREF="wasterecycle.html">waste management and recycling</A>
</UL>
<P>
Each sector adds value to Victoria's existing natural resource advantages.  These sectors are:
<P>
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="foodproc.html">food processing</A>
<LI><A HREF="forest.html">forest products</A>
<LI><A HREF="textiles.html">textiles, clothing and footwear</A>
</UL>
<P>
They are industries in which Victoria has a strong industrial base and which have strong linkages with sectors in the above two categories.  Examples of these sectors are:
<P>
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="auto.html">automotive</A>
<LI><A HREF="engineering.html">engineering</A>
<LI><A HREF="transport.html">transport and storage</A>
<LI><A HREF="packaging.html">packaging</A>
</UL>
<BR>
In aggregate, the key sectors account for about 30% of Victoria's 
economic output.  The 11 key sectors within the manufacturing sector 
have combined sales of $35 billion and employ 80% of Victoria's total 
manufacturing workforce.  Exports of education services and tourism are 
worth $2 billion and waste management sales are around $200 million.
