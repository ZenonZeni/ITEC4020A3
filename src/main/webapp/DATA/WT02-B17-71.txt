<H2>Impact of DSM Programs</H2>

<P>Assessments of DSM program
impacts are largely non-existent, as most DSM activities in most
countries are in the initial stages of implementation. The <A HREF="http://www.eia.doe.gov/"
>U.S. Energy Information Agency</A> prepares an annual comprehensive
assessment of U.S. utility DSM activities. The <A HREF="http://www.lbl.gov/"
>Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory</A> maintains a U.S. and
International database on the measured performance of energyefficiency
DSM programs. The <A HREF="http://www.epri.com/">Electric Power
Research Institute</A> conducts annual surveys of individual DSM program
descriptions.</P>
