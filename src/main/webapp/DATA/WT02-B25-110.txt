<p>If people with mental illnesses are to receive the treatment, respect,

and economic opportunities they deserve, we will have to change

the behaviors and public policies of lawmakers and public

officials. We will have to challenge the corporate policies of our nation&#146;s businesses

and the attitudes of our nation&#146;s opinion leaders towards

people with brain disorders. </p>



<p><strong>Mission Statement:</strong> To improve the quality of

life for people with brain disorders by working to ensure dignity

and by securing non-discriminatory access to quality health care, housing,

education, and all economic opportunities. </p>
