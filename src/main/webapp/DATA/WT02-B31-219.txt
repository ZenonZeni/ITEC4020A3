<CENTER><H2>Coporate Profile... what we do!</H2></CENTER>
<P>Merlan Scientific Ltd. (Merlan) is a major Canadian distributor and manufacturer of <B>Science and Technology</B> Teaching Solutions for education.  Our products are widely used in high schools, colleges and universities throughout North America.
<P>Merlan was established as a science equipment distributor in Mississauga, Ontario in 1970.  Since that time, our business has grown to include a broad range of technology education products.  In addition to our present office in Georgetown, Ontario (50 km northwest of Toronto), we now have sales representatives throughout Canada.
<P><B>Training</B><BR>In a time of shrinking budgets, it is important to get the most out of your equipment, proper training is the key.  We offer regular training sessions to Merlan customers at locations across the country.  Cost-effective on-site training is also available.
<P><B>CSA Approval and Computer Configuration</B><BR>All equipment comes to you CSA approved.  In addition, when your purchase includes a computer, our technicians can install your Merlan application software for you, so that you are "ready to run" when you take it out of the box.
<P><B>Installation</B><BR>Our technical service team provides the accurate and timely assistance you need to ensure that your equipment is installed as smoothly and quickly as possible.  Whether you have purchased a computer with a printer or a full Manufacturing Technology Lab, our experts are ready to help.
<P><B>Services</B><BR>At Merlan, our goal is to provide you with the highest quality equipment.  We take pride in offering a full range of services designed to put the right equipment in your hands, and keep it working for you.
<P><B>Product Demonstrations</B><BR>With today's rapidly changing technologies, it is sometimes difficult to keep up to date.  To help you make informed decisions, we invite you to visit our classrooms in Georgetown to view the latest in Teaching Systems.
<P><B>Quotations</B><BR>Our product specialists are happy to assist you in developing equipment lists, designing the layout of your facility, and providing timely quotations.
