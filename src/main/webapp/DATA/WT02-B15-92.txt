<h1>Awarding Partnership in Controlling Costs and Building for
the Future</h1>

<hr>

<p>Congratulations to our award winners. These SIS clients have
proven that they are actively controlling workers&#146;
compensation losses, saving money and building better futures.
The Award of Excellence goes to facilities that have achieved the
lowest experience modification factor. The Chairman&#146;s Award
is based on overall performance, significant recent improvement
and commitment to partnership.</p>
