<H1>Engineering Faculty computer centre</H1>
<BODY>
The Engineering Faculty computer centre provides network and computing
resources, advice and support to the other members of the Engineering Faculty
of Sydney University.
<P>
We have a staff of four:
<OL>
<LI> Kevin Rosolen

</OL>
<P>
We are located variously within the Link Building and the Electrical Engineering
Building at Sydney University.
<P>
We also provide a workstation Laboratory, made up of Alpha workstations
running Digital Unix (OSF/1) as a teaching resource.
