<STRONG>Spectra 2</STRONG><BR>
The insurance protection of Spectra 2 consists of a base plan of 50% Whole Life insurance
with 50%
Five Year Term rider. This equal mix allows you to keep the premiums within your budget,
while adding
the advantage of cash value accumulation.
<P>
<STRONG>The Features of Spectra 2 Include:</STRONG><BR>
<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>A guaranteed death benefit, generally free from federal income tax
	<LI>A lower initial premium than a comparable policy of 100% Whole Life insurance
	<LI>The privilege to convert the term portion into permanent insurance, generally for 10
years
	<LI>Tax-deferred cash value accumulation
	<LI>The ability to borrow from cash value
	<LI>Eligibility to earn dividends on the Whole Life base plan, as declared by the
Company
	<LI>Generally includes the Waiver of Premium rider, which waives premiums should
you become
totally disabled as defined by the rider
	<LI>The ability to customize the plan by adding optional riders
</UL>
