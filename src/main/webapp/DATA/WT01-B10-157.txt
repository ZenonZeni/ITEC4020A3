<pre>		MATERIALS ACCEPTABLE FOR COMPOSTING

	Type			Specific Examples

Food Processing Wastes		Hulls, rejects, and by-
				products resulting from
				food processing activities

Food Preparation/		Plate scrapings, coffee
Service Wastes			grounds, egg shells, or
				other scrapings or peelings
				from food preparation
				or service

Wet or Soiled Paper		Cardboard, paper towels,
				paper food liners, coffee
				filters, fast-food wrappings,
				or other compostable but
				readily recyclable materials

Yard Waste			Leaves, grass clippings,
				brush, or wood wastes
				from residential and
				commercial sources

Unrecyclable Paper		Fiber materials separated
				by the generator for
				recycling but subsequently
				rejected or determined
				by the recycler to be
				unrecyclable

Garden Wastes			Plants, floral waste, trimmings,
				overripe vegetables, and other
				compostable discards from
				gardening and horticultural
				activities, or from florists
				or garden markets</pre>
