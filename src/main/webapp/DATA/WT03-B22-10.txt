<CENTER><H2>BUILDING INTERPERSONAL RELATIONSHIPS THROUGH TLC (1994)</H2></CENTER><P>
<CENTER><EM>Bormaster, Jeffrey and Carol Lou Treat</EM></CENTER><P>
<BR>The full title of this text is: Building Interpersonal Relationships Through Talking, Listening and Communicating. Many activities and exercises are presented to build an effective working group and to make the reader a more effective group leader. Blackline masters are provided for group activities suitable for professional development seminars or for use by staff/management trainers. Activities develop a positive regard for interpersonal differences, build a sense of belonging, and foster horizontal "non-authoritative" relationships.<BR>
Contents include: skills for group leaders, building effective groups, sharing self, sharing values, improving personal communications, relating to others, developing creativity, decision making by consensus, solving problems as a group, ending a TLC group.<BR>
