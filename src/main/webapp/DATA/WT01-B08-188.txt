<FONT SIZE=5><B><I>Fact Sheet:</I></B></FONT><BR>
<FONT SIZE=4><B><I>How do I make sure I am not dealing with an unauthorized party?</B></I></FONT>
<P>
<IMG SRC="rainbwln.gif" height=14 width=600></CENTER><BR> 

<FONT SIZE=3><B>There are several steps you should take.</B> <P> 

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif"> The first is to read and understand the <A HREF="knowcust.htm"><B><I>"Know Your Customer Guidance"</I></B></A> provided in the Export Administration Regulations and as part of this website.  It will help you recognize and avoid prohibited transactions.<P>

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif"> The second is to be familiar with the <A HREF="redflags.htm"><B><I>"Red Flag Indicators"</I></B></A> that can help you recognize illegal, or potentially illegal, transactions.<P>

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif"> The third is to check the parties to your transaction (including freight forwarders, intermediate consignees, and the ultimate consignee) against the most recent <A HREF="ftp://ftp.fedworld.gov/pub/bxa/1_denial.txt"><I><B> "Denied Persons List."</B></I></FONT></A><FONT SIZE=4>*</FONT><FONT SIZE=3>   Although you are only required to make this check for export or reexport transactions, it may also be useful to do so for domestic transactions as well.  Click <A HREF="franco.htm"><B><I>here</I></B></A> for an example of why this may be important.<BR>
</FONT><FONT SIZE=2>[</FONT><FONT SIZE=4>*</FONT><FONT SIZE=2>Note:<i>  This link will transfer you to FedWorld.  You may want to add our address to your "bookmarks" or "favorites" prior to  leaving our website.]</i></FONT><FONT SIZE=3><P>

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif"> If you are being asked to participate in an export transaction that you believe may be illegal, or if you have information that such an illegal transaction may be about to occur, you are encouraged to contact BXA's <A HREF="report.htm"><B><I>Office of Export Enforcement immediately</I></B></A>. <P>

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif">You may also wish to check the parties to your transaction against the <A HREF="http://www.ustreas.gov/treasury/services/fac/fac.html"><B><I>"List of Specially Designated Nationals"</I></B></A> maintained by the Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control. </FONT><BR>
<FONT SIZE=2>[Note:<i>  This link will transfer you to the website at the Department of the Treasury.  You may want to add our address to your "bookmarks" or "favorites" prior to  leaving our website.]</i></FONT><FONT SIZE=3><P>

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif">Finally, you may want to review our web page on <A HREF="report.htm"><B><I>"Recognizing and reporting possible violations."</I></B></A> <P>
