<font size=4><B>The Economic Analysis Division:</B> </font><font size=3>The Economic Analysis Division (EAD) was established for the purpose of ensuring that U.S. economic interests are considered in export control decisions, equally with national security and foreign policy concerns, and are addressed as early as possible in the export control policy-making process. <P>

EAD analyzes the impact of existing or proposed export controls on the U.S. economy, identifying potential export disincentives that might be modified, reduced, or eliminated in order to enhance the competitiveness of U.S. industry.  EAD also analyzes the potential economic impact of export license application decisions on U.S. companies.<P>

In addition, EAD has replaced the former Office of Foreign Availability as the organization within BXA that is responsible for conducting foreign availability assessments of national security controlled items.<p></font> <br>
