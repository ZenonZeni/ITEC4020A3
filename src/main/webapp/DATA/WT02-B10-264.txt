<B>JOB POSTING</B>

<B>
POSITION:</B>          Research Scientist II - Grade 22
                   Salary: $43,470 ($21,735-$43,470)  50%-100% + $768 Location Pay
                   Depending on availability
<B>
LOCATION:</B>          New York State Psychiatric Institute
                   Therapeutics/Anxiety Disorders Clinic
<B>
DUTIES:</B>            The incumbent will be responsible for behavioral 
                   assessments of patients which include cognitive testing 
                   and administration of structured interviews. He/she will 
                   also be responsible for individual intensive behavior 
                   therapy treatment of patients - must be willing to travel 
                   to patients' homes for assessments, as well as to satellite 
                   clinic for treatment of off-site patients.
                   Candidate must also be able to spend 4 weeks in 
                   Philadelphia for supervison and reliability meetings.
                   Independent evaluations of other anxiety clinic patients 
                   will also be required.
<B>
EDUCATION:</B>         Bachelor's degree and 3 years of professional research 
                   experience in appropriate field; or a master's and 2 years 
                   experience; or a doctorate.

<B>TRAINING & </B>
<B>EXPERIENCE:</B>        Previous experience in anxiety disorders and structured 
                   clinical interviewing, as well as research experience 
                   preferred.  Prior training in behavior therapy also 
                   preferred.

<B>SPECIAL</B>
<B>REQUIREMENTS:</B>       Bilingual in English/Spanish a plus.  Must be flexible as
                   treatment hours are determined by patients availability.  
                   Weekends may be required.
