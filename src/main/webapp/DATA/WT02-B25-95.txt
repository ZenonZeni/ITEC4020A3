<h4 align="center">Helpline Fact Sheet </h4>

<h4 align="center"><font color="#000080"><b>Prozac--Treatment for </b></font><a
href="ocd.htm"><font color="#000080"><b>OCD</b></font></a><font
color="#000080"><b> and </b></font><a href="depress.htm"><font
color="#000080"><b>Depression</b></font></a></h4>

<hr>

<p><font color="#FF0000">What is prozac? </font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - a new antidepressant</p>
    <p>2 - one of the most widely prescribed medications on the
    market</p>
    <p>3 - used primarily to treat serious depressive disorders
    with specific symptoms</p>
    <blockquote>
        <p>a. problems in thinking, concentrating, &amp; decision
        making</p>
        <p>b. excessive fatigue</p>
        <p>c. loss of self-esteem</p>
        <p>d. inappropriate feelings of unworthiness &amp; guilt</p>
        <p>e. loss of interest &amp; pleasure in usual activities</p>
        <p>f. sleep &amp; appetite disturbances</p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>4 - treats Chronic Depressive Disorder (dysthymia)</p>
    <p>5 - treats Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD)</p>
    <p>6 - treats Bulimia Nervosa</p>
    <p>7 - treats Panic/Agoraphobia Syndrome</p>
    <p>8 - usual dosage is 1 to 2 20mg capsules each day (those
    with OCD may need 4)</p>
    <p>9 - more expensive than other antidepressants/$1.00 to
    $2.00 per capsule</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000">Benefits </font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - causes fewer &amp; milder side effects than other
    antidepressants</p>
    <p>2 - a special diet is not necessary as with other
    antidepressants </p>
    <p>3 - a substantial overdose is not likely to be fatal</p>
    <p>4 - especially suitable for depressed elderly</p>
    <p>5 - less likely than other antidepressants to cause
    sleepiness, irregular heartbeat, or low blood pressure</p>
    <p>6 - somewhat effective within a few days (improved
    concentration &amp; energy level)</p>
    <p>7 - fully effective within 3 to 6 weeks (use for 6 weeks
    before evaluating efficacy)</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000">Risks or Possible Side Effects</font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - slight weight loss</p>
    <p>2 - anxiety/nervousness (subsides within 48 hours)</p>
    <p>3 - insomnia (avoided by taking medication before 3:00pm)</p>
    <p>4 - drowsiness</p>
    <p>5 - headache</p>
    <p>6 - diarrhea</p>
    <p>7 - rash</p>
    <p>8 - may inhibit orgasm</p>
    <p>9 - may cause craving for alcohol</p>
    <p>10 - triggers manic episodes in those predisposed to
    Bipolar Disorder</p>
</blockquote>
