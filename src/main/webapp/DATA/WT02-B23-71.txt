<h1>Franchises</h1>
</center>

On the sports franchise front, The
<a href="http://www.sacsports.com/">Sacramento Sports Commission</a>,
<a href=http://www.sna.com/cvb/>Sacramento Convention &amp; Visitors Bureau</a>,
government and community business leaders are assisting in the acquisition and
location of Triple A and/or Major League Baseball as well as the International
Hockey League to the Sacramento region. This would be in addition to these
professional sports franchises already attracted to the area:

<ul>

<li>Sacramento Capitals Team Tennis charter franchise, drawing summertime
capacity crowds for over 10 years.

<p><li><a href=http://www.nba.com/kings/>Sacramento Kings</a>,
NBA franchise, with over 425 sell-out home games since
their arrival in 1985 in their current home, 17,000 seat
<a href=arco_arena.html>ARCO Arena</a>.

<p><li>Sacramento Knights, Indoor Soccer franchise, playing to an average of
7,500 fans during the first two years of the league's inception.

<p><li><a href=http://www.rollerhockey.com/riverrats.html>
Sacramento River Rats</a>, Indoor Roller Hockey franchise.

</ul>
