<HR SIZE=5><P>


<A NAME="Features"></A>
<IMG ALIGN=ABSBOTTOM SRC="http://www.rixan.com/unixicon.gif" WIDTH=22 HEIGHT=22> <STRONG><FONT SIZE=+1> Features</FONT></STRONG><P>
<ul>
<li><li>High maximum resultant speed (3500mm/sec.).<br>
<li>High repeatability (+/-0.04mm).<br>
<li>No origin setting required due to the use of absolute encoders with reliable AC servo motors.<br>
<li>Programming can be easily accomplished through the teach box without the use of a separate computer.<br>
<li>Insert/Delete commands are included for simple correction, addition or change to the robot positional data.<br>
<li>Shift command allows transfer of a robot motion to another coordinate without re-teaching<br>
</ul>

<P>

<A NAME="Applications"></A>
<IMG ALIGN=ABSBOTTOM SRC="http://www.rixan.com/unixicon.gif" WIDTH=22 HEIGHT=22> <STRONG><FONT SIZE=+1>  Applications</FONT></STRONG><P>
The UNIX-531 E III can solder:<br>
<ul>
<li>PC boards slanted on conveyors.<br>
<li>minute and delicate components which are not usually washable or solderable with reflow or a solder bath.<br>
<li>Intricately laid points on densely populated boards.<br>
</ul>
<P>

</ul>
