<h2 align="center">The Office of Building Technology, <br>
State and Community Programs</h2>

<p align="center"><font color="#F76C1E" size="5"><strong>- Under
Construction -</strong></font> </p>

<p>The Office of Building Technology, State and Community
Programs (BTS) is part of the Department of Energy's Office of Energy
Efficiency and Renewable Energy. BTS' mission is to provide
technology leadership, promote energy efficiency, and stimulate
collaborative activities to make U.S. buildings more efficient,
comfortable, affordable and sustainable. The goal of this website
is to provide our customers with information about advanced
building technology systems and partnership opportunities that
promote energy efficiency and pollution prevention. </p>
