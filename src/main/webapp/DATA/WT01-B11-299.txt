<p><font size="5">Meeting Special Needs&#133;</font></p>

<p><font size="4">The ability to meet special needs and provide
solutions for unique medical problems are <img
src="images/chem1.jpg" align="right" hspace="0" vspace="3"
width="300" height="204"></font> <font size="4">some of the
advantages offered by professional compounding. Compounding
medications can utilize alternative routes of administration that
may not be possible with commercially available products.
Customization of dosage and dosage form gives the patient many
significant benefits. We work closely with the physician and
patient to optimize each patient's health care. For an example of
what type of custom compounding we do at Professional Compounding
Pharmacy check out </font><a href="mortar.htm"><font size="4">The
Mortar and Pestle© Industry Newsletter</font></a><font size="4">.</font></p>

<p align="center"><img src="images/triam.gif" width="300"
height="173"><em><br>
This triad has always been the basis of the profession of
pharmacy.</em></p>

<p><font size="4">Each year, the physician-pharmacist-patient
relationship becomes more significant. Our pharmacist
continuously attend educational seminars to stay abreast of the
latest advances in compounding and pharmaceutical delivery
systems. Additionally, we have and extensive support network that
generates a constant exchange of ideas and innovations.<br>
</font></p>

<p><font size="4">We make the medication to the exact strength
that your pet may need. This is true wether your pet is a snake,
snail, dog, cat, horse, fish, bird or kangaroo.<br>
</font></p>

<p><font size="4">We will make the medication in a Flavor that
your pet will like. No longer will you have to stuff a capsule or
pill down your pet's <img src="images/vetsign.gif" align="right"
hspace="5" vspace="5" width="100" height="174"></font><font
size="4">throat.<br>
</font></p>

<p><font size="4">We can make the Strength and the Dosage Form
that will best solve the needs of your pet and make life happier
for you and your pet. We have may Flavoring Agents for your pets,
such as BEEF, LIVER, CARROT, TRIPLE FISH, TUNA, SARDINE, SALMON,
and APPLE to name a few.<br>
</font></p>

<p><font size="4">Our capsule sizes range from the very very
small to the very very large. We customize the medication and the
delivery system to create happiness and better health for all
creatures, great and small.<br>
</font></p>
