Where you might have read about Kemper Funds or Kemper Funds investment
experts recently
<P>

<I>And The Contrarian column in </I>Forbes<I> is written by David Dreman, 
a pioneer of the philosophy of contrarian investing and portfolio manager
of <A HREF="../funds/high_return.html">Kemper-Dreman High Return Fund.</A></I>
<P>





<B>"Back On Track,"</B> <I>Barron's</I>, October 14, 1996<BR>
An interview with President, Chief Investment and Executive Officer <A HREF="../curious/letter.html">Stephen B. Timbers</A> outlines Kemper's "ambitious growth plans and generally solid performance."

<P>
<B>"Zurich Kemper Adds Products, Expands Staff,"</B> <I>Pensions &#38; Investments</I>, September 20, 1996<BR>
<A HREF="aboutzki.html">Zurich Kemper Investments Inc.'s</A> efforts to build on its sales and marketing structure are described.

<P>
<B>"Zurich-Kemper's Blue Chip Fund Makes Comeback,"</B> <I>Dow Jones News Service</I>, September 11, 1996<BR>
The strategy of <A HREF="../funds/bluechip.html">Kemper Blue Chip Fund</A> Portfolio Manager Tracy McCormick Chester is profiled.

<P>
<B>"Sowing Seeds At Kemper Growth,"</B> <I>Business Week</I>, September 30, 1996<BR>
The efforts of Portfolio Manager Patrick S. Adams to revive <A HREF="../funds/growth.html">Kemper Growth Fund</A> are described.

<P>
<A  HREF="../funds/international.html">Kemper International Fund</A> Portfolio Manager Dennis Ferro
is recognized by <I>Barron's (July 22, 1996)</I> for excellence in fund management.

<P>
<B>"The road ahead," </B><I>Financial Planning,</I> June 1996
<BR>
<A  HREF="../funds/high_return.html">Kemper-Dreman High Return Fund</A> Portfolio Manager David Dreman is
included as one of four managers who share their management philosophies, investment strategies and market outlooks.

<P>

<B>"New Swiss owner funding Kemper in return to banks,"</B> <I>American Banker</I>, June 13, 1996
<BR>
<A  HREF="aboutzki.html">Zurich Kemper Investments, Inc.</A> is profiled as placing a renewed emphasis on reclaiming
its position as a dominant seller of mutual funds through banks.

<P>

<B>"Nintendo capitalism: Zapping the markets,"</B> <I>The New York Times</I>, May 28, 1996
<BR>
<A  HREF="aboutzki.html">Zurich Kemper Investments, Inc.</A> is profiled as an example of how traders use sophisticated economic models and powerful computers to make quick decisions.

<P>

<B>"The Outliers,"</B> <I>Barron's</I>, May 20, 1996
<BR>
<A HREF="../funds/high_return.html">Kemper-Dreman High Return Fund</A>, <A HREF="../funds/contrarian.html">Kemper-Dreman Contrarian Fund</A> and <A HREF="../funds/smallcap.html">Kemper-Dreman Small Cap Value Fund</A> are cited as funds that refuse to run with the herd.

<P>

<B>"Oldest muni fund rides rates and risks into third decade,"</B> <I>The Bond Buyer</I>, April 22, 1996
<BR>
<A  HREF="../funds/muni_bond.html">Kemper Municipal Bond Fund</A> Portfolio Manager Christopher Mier is profiled.

<P>

<B>"Two players dominate DC market,"</B> <I>Pensions &amp; Investments</I>, April 1, 1996
<BR>
<A  HREF="aboutzki.html">Zurich Kemper Investments, Inc.</A> ranks third among defined contribution service providers, based on total number of plan sponsors.

<P>

<B>"It's all in the families,"</B> <I>Barron's</I>, February 5, 1996
<BR>
A 1995 performance survey of mutual fund families. <I>Barron's</I> noted
Kemper's strength in its bond funds and overseas stock funds.
