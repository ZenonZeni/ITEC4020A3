<h3>Search Engine Behavior</h3>
<ol>
<li> Searches are performed throughout the SEMA Site:
<li> The engine can take as many keywords as you can fit into the box.  Separate keywords
with a space.
<li> If a match of any of your keywords occurs with any of the database entries, the engine
will display the hit.
<li> Matches are not restricted to whole-word matches.  For example, if you use
"<strong>wis</strong>" as your keyword, you'll pull up matches with "<strong>Wis</strong>dom"
and "Le<strong>wis</strong>."
<li> The engine is not case-sensitive.
</ol>
<h3>Tips for Searching</h3>
<ol>
<li> Try to use one or two keywords that are unique to the company you are searching for.
<li> "Less is More"&#151 The fewer characters you use in the search, the more hits you'll get.
</ol>
