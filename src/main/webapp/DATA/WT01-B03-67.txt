<HR WIDTH="95%" SIZE="7">FAQs</H3>
<B><BR>
What is <I><A HREF="gymnemaherb.html#anchor1678457">Gymnema sylvestre </A>
</I>?</B><BR>
It is an extract of ayurvedic herbs used widely in traditional Eastern medication
practice that has attracted interest and recognition from Western medical
practitioners.<BR>
<BR>
<B>What is ayurvedic medicine ?</B><BR>
It is Eastern traditional medicine using herb extracts from various plants
to treat such conditions such as hypertension, inflammation, obesity and
diabetes.<BR>
<BR>
<B>What does <I>Gymnema sylvestre</I>  <A HREF="gymnemaherb.html#anchor1668145">effect</A>
?</B><BR>
The active ingredient in <I>Gymnema sylvestre</I> is &quot;gymnemic acid&quot;
which effects the : 
<UL>
<LI>taste buds in the oral cavity, the acid prevents the taste buds from
being activated by any sugar molecules in the food. 
<LI>absorptive surface of the intestines, the acid prevents the intestine
from absorbing sugar molecules. 
</UL>
<BR>
<B><A HREF="gymnemaherb.html#anchor1627478">Practical uses</A> of <I>Gymnema
sylvestre</I> ?</B> 
<UL>
<LI>a reduced appetite for sweet tasting food 
<LI>reduces metabolic effects of sugar by reducing it's digestion by the
intestines thus reducing the blood sugar level. 
</UL>
<BR>
<B>In <A HREF="gymnemaherb.html#anchor1633521">Treating Diabetes</A> :</B><BR>
In experimental and clinical trials, <I>Gymnema sylvestre</I> has been successful
in treating both insulin dependent and non-insulin dependent diabetes without
reducing the blood sugar level to below the normal blood sugar levels, an
effect seen with the use of insulin or oral hypoglycemic sulphonylurea compounds.<BR>
<BR>
<B><A HREF="gymnemaherb.html#anchor1637678">Other uses</A> of <I>Gymnema
sylvestre </I>?</B><BR>
In treating snakebites, fever and swollen glands.<BR>
<BR>
<B>Why <I>Gymnema sylvestre</I> ?</B><BR>
Due to it's non-toxic nature and sweetness-suppression activity, <I>Gymnema
sylvestre</I> can play a role in treating conditions caused by excessive
sugar intakes. Not only diabetics benefit from it, conditions like obesity,
hypoglycemia, anemia and osteoporosis can also be treated using <I>Gymnema
Sylvestre.</I>
