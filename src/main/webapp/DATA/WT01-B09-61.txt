<h3>Mid Atlantic Alternative Vehicle Consortium</h3><p>

<blockquote>

<B>Project Title: Innovative Fixed Speed Ratio SYNKdrive™<br>
Transmission for EV/HEV and Evaluation
Support  <p>

Performing Organization: Synkinetics, Inc. <p></B>

Technical POC: James E. Maslow<br>

Phone: (617) 271-0220 <br>

Fax: (617) 271-9362 <p>

<B>Project Summary:</B><p>

SYNKdrive technology promises to improve drivetrains for on/off road and
amphibious/marine vehicles. The purpose of this project is to demonstrate SYNKdrive aplicability for
EV/HEV vehicles in a commercially useful format that addresses military missions and functions. <p>

The program is dedicated to parametric evaluation of SYNKdrive technology via exhaustive inhouse
testing on dynamometer. Based on test results, SYNKINETICS will design, fabricate, assemble, and
analyze, a 5 HP SYNKdrive transmission.<p>

<B>Project Deliverables:</B> <p> 5 HP SYNKdrive transmission and final report. <p>

<B>Project Schedule:</B> <p><ul><li> The project was initiated in September 1995 and will be completed 

in late 1996.</ul>
