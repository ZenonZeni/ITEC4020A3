<P ALIGN=Left>






<B>By signing up as a member, you will receive the 4th edition of <I>Made
in
the USA: The Complete Guide to America's Finest Products</I>. This book is
an essential resource for anybody sick of scouring through labels in stores,
or tired of buying goods by so-called 'American' comapanies, only to discover


that these companies have shipped production overseas. In addition, this
November you will receive the 5th edition of <I>Made in the USA</I>, completely


updated for 1997, with special markings next to union-made products. The
4th edition retails for $12.95 and the 5th edition will cost $16.95, but
the annual individual membership of $25 includes both these books. You will
also receive our quarterly


<A HREF="http://www.madeusa.org/report.htm">newsletter</A> which addresses
the most pressing concerns for American industry and your favorite selection
from our 'Buy American' <A HREF="http://www.madeusa.org/bumper.htm">bumper


stickers</A>.&nbsp;
</B>

<P ALIGN=Left><B>To become a member, please choose the form below that applies
to your group's category. &nbsp;There



are three separate forms for business, labor union, and individuals. &nbsp;After






you print the applicable page and fill out the necessary information, you
can fax the form to us at 1-202-822-6062, or mail it to us at Made in the
USA Foundation, c/o Joel Joseph, 1925 K St., NW, Suite 100, Washington, DC
20006. You can also cut and paste the page to our
e-mail address at















</B><A href="mailto:MadeUSAFdn@aol.com"><B><FONT SIZE=+1>joel@madeusa.org</FONT></B></A><B>.


We are now equipped to process credit cards.
