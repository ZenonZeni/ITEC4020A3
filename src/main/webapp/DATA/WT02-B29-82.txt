<STRONG><A NAME="1">What kinds of participant services does NYLBS
offer?</A></STRONG><BR>

Plan participants can choose how they access account information with
Benefits
Complete&reg; plan services... from registered
representatives
in our Participant Service Center,  over our automated telephone
voice response system, or
the Internet. 
These integrated services provide participants with consistent,
up-to-date
information when they want it.
<P>


Our voice response system is user-friendly and
accessible 24 hours a
day.  Participants can model loans, view account statements and more
--
conveniently from work or
home.
<P>
NYLBS' responsive Participant Service Center is open from 9:00 a.m.
to 8:00 p.m. Eastern
Time, Monday through Friday, with representatives available to answer
general plan
questions, initiate
transactions and provide account and investment information.
<P>


	<STRONG><A NAME="2">What are the 401(k) services that NYLBS
provides?</A></STRONG><BR>
NYLBS provides a full-service solution to plan sponsors' 401(k) and
defined contribution
plan needs:

<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI><A HREF="#record">Recordkeeping</A>
	<LI><A HREF="#admin">Administration</A>
	<LI><A HREF="#trustee">Plan trustee services</A>
	<LI><A HREF="#invest">Investment management</A>
	<LI><A HREF="#legislative">Legislative regulatory and compliance
assistance</A>
	<LI><A HREF="#consult">Consulting</A>
</UL>
	<P>
	<DL>
	<DD>
	<STRONG><A NAME="record">a.	Recordkeeping</A></STRONG><BR>
NYLBS' proprietary recordkeeping system performs all recordkeeping
functions.  Our
system maintains all
plan, participant, transaction and account information indefinitely
and performs
all transactions and
reporting associated with qualified plan administration.  We also have
the
flexibility to provide you with
information in addition to our standard reporting to suit your
specific needs.<P>
	<STRONG><A NAME="admin">b.	Administration</A></STRONG><BR>
NYLBS provides comprehensive defined contribution plan administration
to our clients. 
All implementation
and conversion services are provided as part of our standard offering,
including
conversion meetings,
documentation and plan review, preparation of Administration Manual
and forms,
preparation and
implementation of employee communications and education programs,
enrollment
meetings, record
conversion and reconciliation and coordination of asset transfer to
New York Life Trust
Company.  Our
recordkeeping and administration services include daily valuation,
daily check
processing, periodic
plan level reporting, access to Benefits Complete&reg; Voice
Response System and Participant Service
Center services, and participant statements (periodic and Statement on
Request&reg;). We
also provide
ongoing employee communications and education programs and
materials.<P>
	<STRONG><A NAME="trustee">c.	Plan trustee
services</A></STRONG><BR>
New York Life Trust Company, a wholly-owned, indirect subsidiary of New York
Life Insurance
Company, provides
comprehensive directed trustee services including:<P>
<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>Custody of plan assets
	<LI>Preparation of all benefit withdrawal checks
	<LI>Preparation of all loan funding checks
	<LI>Investment fund purchases and liquidations
	<LI>IRS Form 1099-R
	<LI>Form 945 Federal tax reporting
	<LI>Report of independent accountants pursuant to SAS 70
</UL>
<P>
	<STRONG><A NAME="invest">d.	Investment management</A></STRONG><BR>
New York Life and its investment advisor subsidiaries, MacKay Shields
Financial
Corporation and
Monitor Capital Advisors, Inc., will provide the investment advisory
services for
our clients' retirement plans, coordinated through NYLBS as part of
our bundled program.
<P>
The MainStay Funds are advised by New York Life, MacKay Shields and
Monitor Capital.  We also offer investments from Fidelity Advisor,
Strong, Twentieth
Century, Warburg Pincus, GAM, Dodge & Cox, Scudder, INVESCO and AIM.  All of
these fund families are offered through NYLIFE Securities Inc. (Member NASD). 
We continually seek
to improve our service offering
and
expand our investment options from outside fund families to include
new
selections across the risk
spectrum.<P>

	<STRONG><A NAME="legislative">e.	Legislative, regulatory and
compliance
assistance</A></STRONG><BR>
NYLBS performs all statutory testing as part of our standard
compliance services,
including:
<P>
<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>401(k) nondiscrimination testing
	<LI>401(m) nondiscrimination testing for matching (and after-tax)
contributions
	<LI>410(b) coverage analysis
	<LI>415 maximum annual addition limitation testing
	<LI>416 top-heavy testing
</UL>
<P>

We prepare Form 1099-Rs and signature-ready Form 5500s in-house as
part of our
compliance
services.  We also prepare and file the federal tax reporting Form 945
and remit
all taxes withheld.
<P>
<STRONG><A NAME="consult">g.	Consulting</A></STRONG><BR>
Supporting NYLBS' 401(k) service teams is a fully integrated
consulting practice. 
NYLBS provides a
multidisciplinary approach to consulting projects that can involve our
consultants,
attorneys, compliance
analysts, systems analysts and communications professionals.
Consequently, our
clients receive
substantial value-added services because of our one-stop professional
capabilities. 
We provide the
following consulting services:
<P>
<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>Plan design consultation
	<LI>Plan document preparation and filing
	<LI>Consultation on federal regulatory compliance
	<LI>Continuous review and monitoring of IRS and regulatory changes
</UL>
<P>
Consulting services for defined benefit pension plans, health and
welfare benefits,
supplemental
executive compensation plans and other employee benefit plans are also
available.
<P>
</DL>

	<STRONG><A NAME="3">How many defined contribution plans does NYLBS
now
service?</A></STRONG><BR>
As of June 30 1996, NYLBS provides recordkeeping and administrative services for
approximately 300 plans
with a total of
approximately 175,000 plan participants.  The net asset value of these
plans is
approximately $4.5
billion.
<DL>
<DD>
<P>
	<STRONG>a.	Average size of plan (by number of
participants)</STRONG><BR>
	Our average plan size is approximately 600 participants and $15
million in
assets.
	<P>
	<STRONG>b.	Smallest plan currently serviced</STRONG><BR>
	Our smallest plan is approximately 10 participants and $100,000
in assets.
	<P>
	<STRONG>c.	Largest plan currently serviced</STRONG><BR>
	Our largest plan is approximately 15,000 participants and $83
million in
assets.
	<P>
</DL>

<STRONG><A NAME="4">What are NYLBS' daily valuation
capabilities?</A></STRONG><BR>

We offer full daily valuation capabilities including:
<P>
<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>Participant account values
	<LI>Rollover contribution processing
	<LI>Lump-sum loan repayment processing
	<LI>Loan processing
	<LI>In-house withdrawal checks
	<LI>In-house distribution checks
	<LI>Investment transfers
	<LI>New investment elections
	<LI>Payroll contribution processing
</UL>
<P>
As of June 30 1996, we currently provide daily recordkeeping to approximately 53% of our
plans and 71%
of our
participants, quarterly recordkeeping to approximately 11% of our
plans and 13% of
our participants,
semi-annual recordkeeping to approximately 6% of our plans and 2% of
our
participants, and annual
recordkeeping to approximately 30% of our plans and 14% of our
participants.
