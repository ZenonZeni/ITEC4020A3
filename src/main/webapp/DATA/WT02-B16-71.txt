<p><font size=+2>W</font>elcome to the new Federal Energy Management
Program (FEMP) website.  FEMP's mission is to reduce the cost of
government by advancing energy efficiency, water conservation and
the use of solar and other renewable energy.  FEMP accomplishes its
mission by creating partnerships, leveraging resources, transferring
technology and providing training and support.

Collectively, FEMP and Federal Agencies will <em><a href="bsbmtc.html">Save
Billions in Federal Energy Management.</a></em>
