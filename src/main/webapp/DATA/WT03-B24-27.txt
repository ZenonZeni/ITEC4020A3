<H1>Special Issue on the <BR>
Intelligent Integration of Information</H1>
<H2>Journal of Intelligent Information Systems, <BR>
Volume 6, Numbers 2-3</H2>
<BR>
This special issue provides an overview of several projects that try to
add value to traditional database and web access. It contains the following
papers :
<OL>
  <LI>Editor's Foreword; G. Wiederhold.
  <LI>Query Reformulation for Dynamic Information Integration; <I>Y. Arens.</I>
  <LI>Information Mediation in Cyberspace: Scalable Methods for Declarative
Information Networks; <I>S. Dao, B. Perry.</I> 
  <LI>An Approach to Information Mediation in the Industrial Domain; <I>A.
Goldschmidt</I>. 
  <LI>NCL: A Common Language for Achieving Rule-Based Interoperability Among
Heterogeneous Systems; <I>S.W. Su, et al. </I>
  <LI>Generating Data Integration Mediators that Use Materialization; <I>G.
Zhou, et al.</I> 
  <LI>CoBase: A Scalable and Extensible Cooperative Information System;
<I>W.W. Chu, et al.</I> 
  <LI>Integrating Information via Matchmaking; <I>D. Kuokka, L. Harada.
</I>
  <LI>Glossary; G. Wiederhold. * * available at: <A HREF="http://www-db.stanford.edu/pub/gio">http://www-db.stanford.edu/pub/gio</A>
</OL>
