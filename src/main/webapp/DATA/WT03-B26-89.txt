<H3></H3>

</CENTER>

<P>Participants at Seattle meeting on global observing strategy. Seated
(L&shyp;R): Dr Tillmann Mohr, Director, EUMETSAT; Mr Karuo Mamiya, Deputy
Director-General, Research and Development Bureau, Science and Technology
Agency of Japan; Dr Brian Embleton, CSIRO, Chair of CEOS; Dr Michel Jarraud,
Deputy Secretary General, World Meteorological Organization; Dr Charles
Kennel, Associate Administrator, Office of Mission to Planet Earth, NASA;
and Mr Robert Winokur, Assistant Administrator for Satellite and Information
Services, NOAA.<BR>
Amongst other items, the meeting paid special attention to how user requirements
might be specified and incorporated into the Earth observation activities
of CEOS members; the potential role of CEOS in a future Integrated Global
Observing Strategy; and the interdependencies of satellite operators; service
users; and space-based and local measurement.<BR>
