Coverage includes appropriations, budget, taxes, energy, the environment, health
care, trade, communications, education, labor, and transportation.<P>

<B>Congressional Activities--GT20</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/gt20.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Oliphant Washington News Service<BR>
$1.00/article; $35.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly; retained one <BR>
year plus current year<BR>
(Also in EV, EY, WashDC)<BR>

including a schedule of hearings, historical items, list of bills introduced,
bills passed, laws enacted, and Congressional Record references.<P>

<B>Congressional Research Report--GT46</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/gt46.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Penny Hill Press<BR>
$1.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 6/1/92<BR>
(No cross-referencing)<BR>
Contains abstracts of all the latest reports produced by the Congressional
Research Service (CRS), a research arm of the United States Congress. The
abstracts are taken from a section of the monthly publication, CRS Update, for
which distribution is limited to members of Congress.<P>

<B>Drug Policy Report--GT05</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/gt05.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
The Drug Policy Research Institute<BR>
$3.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Available from 1/1/94--3/1/96<BR>
(Also in LA)<BR>
Reports on all aspects of America's war against drugs. Coverage includes federal
drug and crime control initiatives by Congress, the White House, and key agencies
including the DEA, FBI CAI Border Patrol and the Department of Justice. Special
features include exclusive reports on upcoming government actions; highlights of
major narcotics-related news; and a high-tech focus that discusses the ways in
which new technology is affecting drug trafficking and drug control.<P>

<B>Federal Grants and Contracts Weekly--GT37</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/gt37.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Capitol Publishing Group, Capitol <BR>
Publications Inc.<BR>
$3.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly; retained for 26 weeks<BR>
(Also in ED, CPG, BusOps)<BR>
Provides timely and complete record of new federal grants and contracts in
research services and training. Also includes news, analysis, profiles of key
agencies, and updates on new legislation, regulations, budget developments,
publications and foundations.<P>

<B>Federal/Industry Watchdog--GT44</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/gt44.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>

government contractor's activities.<P>
