<H1>CAVE DIVING</H1>
<IMG ALIGN=right SRC="../PHOTO-1-96/cavebro.gif" WIDTH="291" HEIGHT="246">
<B>Certification: NSS CDS Cavern Diver</B><BR>
Over the summer, our expeditions took people to the cave systems of Mexco and Northern Florida.  Team members became certified cave divers while exporing the world's finest known underwater caves. One team of experienced cave divers accomplished a 6,000 foot penetration of Mexco's Dos Ojos system.  That's over a mile underwater and in a cave! Even with the help of a siphon current, the traverse took nearly two hours to complete.
