<H3>Malm&ouml;
</H3>


<P>&#148;Electric Vehicles in Malm&ouml;&#148; is a co-operation project between the City of Malm&ouml;, the national energy producer and distributor Sydkraft AB and KFB. The project started in 1995, including the development of a strategy for city charging infrastructure. Vehicle demonstrations will encompass over 50 passenger and light distribution vehicles in 1997. </P>





<P>&#148;Electric Vehicles in Malm&ouml;&#148; is one of the two principal projects of the KFB program.</P>
See<!-- Link Tag --><A HREF="http://www.iea.lth.se/~niklas/work/evdatabase/carsproj.html#Clio-M4"> listing</A> of the Malm&ouml; vehicles.<BR>
