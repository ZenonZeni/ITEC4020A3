<A NAME="objective"><STRONG>Investment Objective</FONT></STRONG></A>
<BR>
To seek to provide total return by investing primarily in a portfolio of non-U.S. (primarily
government) debt securities.<P>

<P>
<BR>




<A NAME="f_profile"><STRONG>Fund Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>When selecting countries in which to invest, the investment adviser looks at fundamentals
including economic growth and
inflation, interest rates, and fiscal policies.
<LI>Under normal conditions, the Fund invests primarily in foreign bonds of a number of
countries (a minimum of 5) exclusive of the United States.
</UL>
<P>
<BR>




<A NAME="i_profile"><STRONG>Investor Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>Investors seeking the potential for higher yields overseas and who are willing to
accept the risks of foreign investing
<LI>Investors seeking to reduce their overall risk through diversification
<LI>Long-term investors seeking to broaden their investment time horizon
</UL>
Before you invest...<BR>
Investments in foreign securities may be subject to greater risks than domestic investments. 
These risks include fluctuations of
currencies, changes in U.S. or foreign tax or currency laws, and changes in monetary policies
and economic and political
conditions in foreign countries.
<P>
