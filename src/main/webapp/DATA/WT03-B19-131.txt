<H2>Purpose</H2>
<P>
To assist <A HREF="crc/index.html">Victorian-based Cooperative Research Centres (CRCs)</A>
to commercialise and export their research and development, and
to assist small to medium sized enterprises to access CRCs facilities
and services.
<H2>Background</H2>
<P>
Commercialisation of research and development is a key objective
of the Victorian Government's innovation policy,
<P>
This initiative will assist Cooperative Research Centres in bringing
their research and development closer to potential beneficiaries.
<H2>Description</H2>
<P>
Assistance will be available to Cooperative Research Centres headquartered
in Victoria or Victorian organisations that are major core participants
in Cooperative Research Centres headquartered outside Victoria.
<P>
Assistance, of up to $25,000 per project, is designed to increase
the probability of success of proposed research and development.
<P>
To be eligible for assistance:
<UL>
<LI>Cooperative Research Centres will be in key strategic industry
sectors identified by Government, and preferably trade-exposed.
<LI>Private-sector support for the research and development will
be clearly demonstrated.
<LI>Research and development project is likely to be completed on time.
<LI>A significant level of economic benefit will flow to Victoria
from the research and development, particularly in export potential
or import substitution.
<LI>The financial viability of the core participants to the research
and development is demonstrated.
</UL>
<P>
Preference will be given to projects which involve small to medium
sized enterprises.
<P>
Projects will gain access to Business Victoria's suite of business
services, including enterprise improvement and export promotion.
<H2>Users</H2>
<P>
CRCs headquartered in Victoria; and other eligible organisations
that are major core participants in CRCs based outside Victoria.
<H2>Outcomes</H2>
<P>
<UL>
<LI>Commercialisation of the research and development products and
services of CRCS, and enhanced export performance.
<LI>Increased level of investment in CRCs by small and medium sized
enterprises.
</UL>
