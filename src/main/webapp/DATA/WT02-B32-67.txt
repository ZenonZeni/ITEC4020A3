<b>Research Interests</b><br>
Mass, heat transfer and mixing in gas-liquid systems including e.g.
aeration/oxygenation and jet mixing in waste water treatment and fermentations;
direct steam injection sterilisation.  Foam separation for recovery of proteins
in downstream processing and waste water treatment. Characterisation of foam
formation/behaviour in fermentation systems. Integrated mathematical modelling
and simulation of mass, heat transfer and mixing processes.<p>

<b>Teaching Acitivities</b><br>
Process engineering and design.
