<H2>TOPEX Oscillator Drift Correction<img src="gifs/new.gif"></H2>
<P>
<A HREF=wff_OscDrift.html> Click here</A> to view the cycle-by-cycle
correction table provided by NASA
Wallops and description of the nature of the oscillator drift algorithm change.
The table shows that, the 1 mm level, a single number per cycle is adequate.
Note that the oscillator drift error has not affected past analyses of TOPEX
data other than the absolute bias and the determination of long-term mean global
sea level changes.
