<P>
<STRONG><A NAME="objective">Investment Objective</A></FONT></STRONG>
<BR>
Seeks to provide competitive overall return commensurate with an acceptable level of risk by investing primarily in a portfolio of non-U.S. (primarily government) debt securities.
<P>
<BR>

<STRONG><A NAME="f_profile">Fund Profile</A></FONT></STRONG><BR>

<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>When selecting countries in which to invest, the investment adviser looks at fundamentals including economic growth and inflation, interest rates, and fiscal policies. Only countries which may offer the potential for relatively high incentives to investors are selected for the Fund.  
	<LI>The investment advisor looks for countries offering strong values
	<LI>The portfolio seeks to maintain a good mix of issuers, rates and maturities to help position the Fund for consistent returns.
	<LI>Under normal conditions, the Fund intends to invest in securities of issuers of a number of countries (minimum of five) exclusive of the United States. 
</UL>
<P>

<STRONG><A NAME="i_profile">Investor Profile</A></FONT></STRONG>
<BR>

<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>Investors seeking the potential for higher current yields overseas
	<LI>Investors seeking to help reduce their overall risk through diversification
	<LI>Long-term investors seeking to broaden their investment time horizon
</UL>

Before you invest...<BR>
Investments in foreign securities may be subject to greater risks than domestic investments. These risks include fluctuations of currencies, changes in U.S. or foreign tax or currency laws, and changes in monetary policies and economic and political conditions in foreign countries.
