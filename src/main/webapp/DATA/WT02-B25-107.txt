<U><STRONG>Who we are!! What we do!!</STRONG></U><br>
We are the parents, children, spouses, brothers and sisters, and friends of people with serious mental illnesses-as well as those persons themselves.  We are a grass roots, self-help advocacy organization for family and friends of persons with serious mental illness, a biological brain disorder.  
<br>
<i><b>AMI-KC</b> offers <b>HELP</b> and <b>HOPE</b></i>
<p>

<b><u>How can AMI-KC Membership benefit you?</u></b>
<br>
<UL>
<LI>AMI-KC Monthly <u><b>News and Views</b></u> Newspaper - with the most current research, medications, treatment and local information
<LI><b>Educational Seminars</b> - which enhance members' knkowledge about support, education, advocacy, and research
<LI><b>Free Help-line</b> - offering information, support, and referral services
<LI><b>Resource Library</b> - books, brochures, fact sheets, videos and audiotapes in the AMI-KC Library
<LI><b>Staff Members</b> - dedicated to the special concerns of families and consumers
</UL>

<u><b>How can You become a Member?</b></u>
<br>
<u><b>Your Membership</b></u> enables AMI to help <u><i><b>all</b></i></u> citizens of Greater Kansas City, and <u><i><b> not turn anyone away</b></i></u> - even those who cannot afford membership.  Please help AMI-KC continue reaching out to those in need.  Get involved by:
<ul>
<li>Becoming a member:  $15 Limited Income, $25 Individual/Family, $25 Professional, $100 Corporate, $50 Donor, $100 Benefactor, $500 Patron
<li>Donate in-kind items:  Printing, 35 mm film, computer equipment, office supplies
<li>Volunteer:  Office help, photographer, professional expertise, marketing, creativity and consultation
</ul>
