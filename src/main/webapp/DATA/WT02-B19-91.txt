<h3> Research Activities  </h3>
- Flow behaviour of complex fluids (eg. paint, synovial fluid, molten plastic, pusher fluids
for oil recovery, ketchup, tailings). <br>
- Characterization of fluids in shear (steady, oscillatory). <br>
- Characterization of resistance to extensional motion (novel instrumentation available). <br>
- Friction reduction in fluid flow using polymer additives (application to oil pipelines). <br>
- Flow in porous media (applications to oil recovery and paper making). <p>
<h3> Research Facilities </h3>
- Rheometrics Fluid Rheometer<br>
- Brookfield Viscometer<br>
- Sangamo Extensional Rheometer<br>
- Filament Extensional Rheometer<br>
- Converging Channel Extensional Rheometer<p>
