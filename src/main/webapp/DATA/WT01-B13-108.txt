For Kemper Funds' shareholders who believe that there's no time like the
present, we offer a series of shareholder account service forms. If
there's an action that you'd like to take now, just click on the
appropriate form, print it, complete and sign it and return it by mail to:
<DL><DD>
	Kemper Service Company <BR>
	P.O. Box 419415 <BR>
	Kansas City, MO 64141-6415 <BR>
</DL>

<I>(This address also appears on each form.)</I>
<BR><BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/prospectuses/accappli.pdf">
Kemper Funds account application</A> (445k)
<BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/forms/syswithd.pdf">
Systematic Withdrawals</A> (231k)
<BR>
To receive regular cash withdrawals from your account.
<BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/forms/checkwri.pdf">
Checkwriting</A> (for Kemper Money Funds only) (170k)
<BR>
You may write as many checks as you like for amounts of $500 or more up
to the available balance in your account.
<BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/forms/bankdd.pdf">
Bank Direct Deposit</A> (203k)
<BR>
For automatic investing directly from your financial institution.
<BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/forms/dividen.pdf">
Dividend Payment</A> (223k)
<BR>
To change dividend payment options on the Kemper account identified in
the Authorization Agreement.
<BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/forms/expwire.pdf">
Expedited Wire Redemptions</A> (143k)
<BR>
To request quick redemptions by phone from the Kemper account identified
in the Authorization Agreement to your financial institution.
<BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/forms/exptrans.pdf">
EXPRESS-Transfer</A> (196k)
<BR>
To request quick cash transfers by phone to and from the Kemper account
identified in the Authorization Agreement.
<BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/forms/sysexch.pdf">
Systematic Exchanges</A> (243k)
<BR>
To invest automatically, by periodic exchange, from the Kemper 
account identified in the Authorization Agreement into another existing
Kemper account(s).
<BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/forms/paydd.pdf">
Payroll Direct Deposit</A> (189k)
<BR>
For investing into your account directly from your employer's payroll.
<BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/forms/govtdd.pdf">
Government Direct Deposit</A> (116k)
<BR>
To invest all or a portion of your government check directly into your 
mutual fund account.
<BR><BR>

<A HREF="../pdfs/forms/statcust.pdf">
Statement Customization Request Form</A> (170k)
<BR>
To customize your Kemper account statement mailings by consolidating additional accounts on one statement or by combining additional statements in one envelope.
