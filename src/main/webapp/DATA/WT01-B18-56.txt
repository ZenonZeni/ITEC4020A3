<H1><CENTER>Deadlines and Dates</CENTER></H1>
<HR>

<H3>DEADLINES: ADMISSION</H3>

<P>
The Graduate Admission Bulletin, which contains your application
materials, will be mailed to you under separate cover. In addition
to these materials, the Department of Marine Science requires
the enclosed &quot;Applicant's Academic Background - Course Work
Summary&quot; and &quot;Supplemental Information&quot; sheets.
All of these materials must be received
<P>
NO LATER THAN
<P>
OCTOBER 1,  for admission in the SPRING SEMESTER
<P>
FEBRUARY 1, for admission in the SUMMER SESSION
<P>
FEBRUARY 1, for admission in FALL SEMESTER
<P>
Please mail your Application form, official transcripts, GRE test
scores, and Application Fee to GRADUATE AND INTERNATIONAL ADMISSIONS
CENTER, P.O. BOX 7608, THE UNIVERSITY OF TEXAS AT AUSTIN, AUSTIN,
TEXAS 78713-7608. Your statement of objectives, supplemental information
sheets, and letters of reference may be mailed directly to GRADUATE
ADVISER, DEPARTMENT OF MARINE SCIENCE, MARINE SCIENCE INSTITUTE,
THE UNIVERSITY OF TEXAS, 750 CHANNELVIEW DRIVE, PORT ARANSAS,
TEXAS 78373.
<H3>DEADLINES: TEACHING ASSISTANTSHIPS</H3>

<P>
To be considered for a teaching assistantship for the academic year, all of the above materials must be <STRONG>received</STRONG>
by <B>JANUARY 1</B>. Applications completed after that date
will be considered for an alternate list.
<P>

You must register to take the GRE approximately five to six weeks before the specific examination
date. Please contact the EDUCATIONAL TESTING SERVICES, GRADUATE RECORDS
EXAMINATIONS, CN 6000, PRINCETON, NJ 08541-6000 for the exact registration deadlines.
<P>
Please be aware that a minimum of six weeks will pass between the time you take the GRE test and the
time your score will be sent to The University of Texas and it is not uncommon for the transcripts of
your previous college course work to arrive four to eight weeks after you order them from your former
school.
<P>
THE APPLICATION PROCESS IS EXPENSIVE AND TIME CONSUMING,
<P>
AN EARLY START IS TO YOUR ADVANTAGE
