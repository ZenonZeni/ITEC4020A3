<p>
<center><H3>Services</H3></center>
<p>
All Rhodes students are welcome to visit the Health Center.  We provide primary care health services, preventive health services, referrals for medical services in the Memphis community, and educational information. Immunizations, injections, select laboratory tests, and medical consultations are available by appointment.  Students are also invited to visit the <B>Self-Care</B> center for complementary over-the-counter medications such as non-prescription pain relievers, decongestants, throat lozenges, etc.  The Self-Care center is available on a walk-in basis.
<p>
To make an appointment with a Nurse or Physician, call ext. 3895 (726-3895 from off-campus).
<p>
<center><H3>Insurance</H3></center>
It is a College policy that all students must have health insurance.  When you have an appointment to see a <u>physician</U> at the Health Center, please bring your insurance card or information with you.  Physicians' services are billed to your insurance; you are not required to make any co-payments.  Services provided by a Nurse are not billed to you or your insurance. 
<p>
If you seek medical services at a local Minor Medical Center or a hospital emergency room, you <B><U>must</U></B> have your insurance information with you.
