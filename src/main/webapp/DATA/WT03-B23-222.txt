<HR SIZE=5><P>


<A NAME="Features"></A>
<IMG ALIGN=ABSbottom SRC="http://www.rixan.com/unixicon.gif" WIDTH=22 HEIGHT=22> <STRONG><FONT SIZE=+1> ROBOSOL FRIEND Features</FONT></STRONG><P>
<ul>
<li><li>Capable of continuous soldering, the Unix-421 Soldering Robot features four-axis controls with theta adjustments for fine-pitch registration, as well as adjustments on the X, Y, and Z axes.<br>
<li>Soldering areas of up to 400x300mm can be accommodated within a desktop format.<br>
<li>Built-in, fully integrated soldering controller included.<br>
<li>Up to 99 sets of different soldering conditions can be programmed.
</ul>

<P>

<A NAME="Applications"></A>
<IMG ALIGN=ABSbottom SRC="http://www.rixan.com/unixicon.gif" WIDTH=22 HEIGHT=22> <STRONG><FONT SIZE=+1> ROBOSOL FRIEND Applications</FONT></STRONG><P>
The UNIX-421 can solder:<br>
<ul>
<li><li>minute or delicate components that cannot withstand reflow or solder bath processes.<br>
<li>many soldering points in parallel lines.<br>
<li>QFPICs and the tape automated bonding (TAB) terminals of LCDs.
</ul>
<P>

<A NAME="Configurations"></A>

</ul>
