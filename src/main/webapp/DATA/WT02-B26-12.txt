<A NAME="freev">Free Video On Schizophrenia</A>
<P>
 Because negative symptoms of schizophrenia are frequently not recognized by professionals and are difficult for families to understand, a 60-minute tape, "Negative Symptoms in Schizophrenia" by Nancy C. Andreasen, M.D., is now available to NAMI families free of charge.  The tape defines negative symptoms and discusses treatment strategies. 
<P>
For a free tape, send your name and address to Melissa Mandrell, MHCRC - 2911 JPP, University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics, 200 Hawkins Drive, Iowa City, IA 52242-1057.
<P>
<A NAME="wyeth">Wyeth Offers Assistance For Effexor</A>
<P>
Effexor is a new drug for depression offered by Wyeth-Ayerst Laboratories, which offers a program designed to provide the medication at little or no cost to uninsured outpatients when medically indicated and when patients meet certain financial criteria. 
<P>
For information, have your physician call 1-800/568-9938.
