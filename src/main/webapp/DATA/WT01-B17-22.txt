<p>
Sir Thomas Bazley of Gloucestershire, Great Britain, quotes Sir William 
Osler, famed professor who had a great influence on the teaching and direction 
of medicine: "It is more important to know what sort of person has a disease 
than to know what disease a person has."

<HR>
<p>

<center>"IT IS MORE IMPORTANT TO KNOW
<br>WHAT SORT OF PERSON HAS A
<br>DISEASE THAN TO KNOW WHAT
<br>DISEASE A PERSON HAS."</center>

<p>
Sir Bazley adds: "This philosophy is widely neglected in orthodox medicine."
