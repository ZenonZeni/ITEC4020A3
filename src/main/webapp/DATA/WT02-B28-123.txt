<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>Increasing Premium Term Insurance</STRONG></FONT><P>

Increasing Premium Term (IPT) offers temporary life insurance protection at a low initial
premium.  IPT is guaranteed renewable through the contract's expiry age and includes a
conversion privilege which is available until age 70.  The coverage it provides could help
families and business owners safeguard their financial future.
<P>
<STRONG>The Benefits of IPT</STRONG>

<UL>
<LI>The peace of mind of pure life insurance protecion
<LI>A death benefit that is, in most instances, free from federal income tax
<LI>An initial premium that is considerable lower than a comparable permanent plan
<LI>Guaranteed renewable coverage through the contract's expiry date (see"Product Specifics"
for variations)
<LI>The privilege to convert to a permanent policy that builds cash value (See "Conversion
Privilege")
<LI>A <EM>Conversion Credit</EM> that's applied against the new policy's first year premium
<LI>The financial strength of New York Life
</UL>
<P>
<STRONG>The Uses of IPT</STRONG><BR>
There are many situations where IPT may be appropriate.  Here are just a few examples:
<UL>
<LI>Temporary Need for Protection
<DL>
	<DT>When coverage is needed for a relatively short period of time, IPT can be ideal.  A
common use is when a mortgage or student loan is taken.
<P>
<LI>Limited Funds
<DT>In situations where life insurance is essential but dollars are scarce, IPT could serve
as a stop-gap.  Young people starting their careers can choose IPT, then convert to a
permanent plan as their finances improve.
<P>
<LI>Supplemental Insurance
<DT>IPT can be added as a rider to some permanent plans to help boost the death benefit. 
It's an affordable way to increase total insurance coverage.
<P>
<LI>Provide Family Coverage
<DT>Optional riders such as <EMSpouse and Children's Insurance, and Children's Insurance</EM>
can be added to the policy to extend coverage to family members.  See "Riders" section for
more information.
<P>
<LI>Protection For Businessowners
<DT>IPT can be used as a hedge to cover outstanding loans, shielding partners or shareholders
from financial hardship.
</DL>
</UL>

<STRONG>Conversion Privilege:</STRONG>  <BR>IPT policies allow the owner to convert all or
part of the policy into permanent, cash value building life insurance without furnishing
evidence of insurability.  This valuable privilege is available until the insured is age 70.
