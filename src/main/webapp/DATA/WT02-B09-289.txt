<P>
To wend your way through our story, just click on the items below. For some background information on our cast of characters, try the &quot;Personnel&quot; files. 
<P>
<B>And now, to the business at hand . . . </B>
<P As our drama begins, Silicon Fusion's tyrannical, cigar-puffing,  suspender-wearing president has hired a new administrative assistant  to replace the last one he scared away.  <P>
Meanwhile, the Silicon Fusion brain trust (we use the term &quot;brain trust&quot; loosely) is struggling to find a way to squeeze enough work out of the company's under-motivated employees to get a new product out the door before the firm's financial backers pull the plug once and for all.
<P>
And, as if that wasn't enough, the results of a Justice Department investigation into Silicon Fusion's relationship with a group of culinary guerrillas in a certain small but strategically located former Soviet Republic are about to be revealed . . .
<P>
<B>So, without further ado, we present Silicon Fusion!</B>
