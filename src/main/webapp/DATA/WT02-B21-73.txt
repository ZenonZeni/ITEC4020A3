<P><B><FONT SIZE=-1><I>Note:</I> Data are for wage and salary employment

of Employment Projections. Unpublished raw data.</FONT></B></P>

<P><B><FONT SIZE=-1> The Bureau of Labor Statistics data for special educators,
occupational therapists, and physical therapists are similar to those reported
in the Department of Education's <I>Annual Reports to Congress (1992, 1994)</I>.
The BLS numbers for social workers, psychologists, and counselors are much
higher than those reported in the <I>Annual Reports to Congress (1994)</I>.
It is beyond the scope of this information sheet to examine differences
in methodology between the two data sets. Information on BLS data collection
methodology can be found in <I>BLS Handbook of Methods (1991)</I>. Information
on data collection methodology for the Department of Education<I> Reports</I>
can be found in the <I>OSEP Data Collection History</I> cited below.</FONT></B></P>
