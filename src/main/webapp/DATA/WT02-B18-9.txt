<H2>Conservation Enterprise</H2>
</CENTER>

<P><FONT SIZE="+2">E</FONT>conomics drives much of the world's rain forest destruction.  Local people clear forests to grow crops, raise cattle, cut timber, or pursue industrial development.  Conservation International's Conservation Enterprise Department helps create an alternative to deforestation by developing enterprises based upon sustainable use of natural resources.  These sustainable enterprises market "biodiversity products" such as tree oils, plant fibers, nuts, and latexes harvested in an ecologically sound manner from key biological areas.  In doing so, they demonstrate that sustainable <IMG SRC="../../../resource/facts/fmedical.gif" ALIGN="RIGHT" BORDER="0" vspace=15 hspace=15 width=250 height=60>enterprise can help local people earn their living by managing and harvesting biologically rich forests instead of destroying them for short-term economic gain.
