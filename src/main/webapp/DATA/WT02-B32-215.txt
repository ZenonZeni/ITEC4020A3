<H3>CHEMICAL (CH)</H3>

<B>Chemical Business Newsbase--CH24</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch24.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Asia Intelligence Wire<BR>
$3.00/article<BR>
Updated at least weekly, from 10/1/96<BR>
(Also in AIW)<BR>
This weekly title originates in Cambridge, England. It provides citations and 
abstracts, covering the chemical industry and all related sectors, including
agrochemicals, food chemicals, pharmaceuticals, electronics chemicals, and 
textiles. It also covers topics of general business news like mergers and 
acquisitions, legal and regulatory issues, pricing trends, production issues, 
and supply and demand matters.<P>

<B>Chemical Engineering--CH19</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch19.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
The McGraw-Hill Companies Publications Online<BR>
$4.00/article<BR>
Updated monthly, from 9/1/91<BR>
(Also in BT, EV, M-H)<BR>
Considered the leading magazine for authoritative information on technologies used
by the worldwide chemical process industries. Sample subjects include new
processes and engineering techniques, environmental protection, and
biotechnology.<P>


<B>The Chemical Monitor--CH15</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch15.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
The Chemical Monitor<BR>
$3.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 5/1/86<BR>
(Also in EC, RD)<BR>
Reviews technical and market information suggesting opportunities for new design 
and applications of chemical instrumentation, focuses on new developments in
transducer systems and opportunities for their applications to current problems.
Also reviews related patents and technical literature, commenting on current
market developments.<P>


<B>Comline Japan Daily: Chemicals--CH16</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch16.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Comline International Corp.<BR>
$4.00/article<BR>
Updated daily, from 1/5/90<BR>
(Also in IT, RD, Comline, Day, Japan, PacRim)<BR>
Reports on new materials such as ceramics, composites, engineering plastics,
resins, and electronic materials, and processes such as crystallization, composing, molding, sintering, vapor deposition, and
sputtering.<P>


<B>Hazardous Waste News--CH10</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch10.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Publishers Inc.<BR>
$5.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 1/4/82<BR>
(Also in EV, GT, BPI, HazMats, Wastes)<BR>
Provides coverage of federal and state hazardous-waste management activities, <BR>
including legislation, regulations, and litigation; business and technology news;
grants and contracts; and publications and meetings.<P>


<B>Hazmat Transport News--CH14</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch14.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Publishers Inc.<BR>
$5.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 3/26/85<BR>
(Also in EV, ML, TS, BPI, HazMats)<BR>
Covers international, national, state and local legislation, regulations,
investigations and litigation concerning the transportation of hazardous materials
shipped by truck, rail, air, or barge. Of special interest to shippers, carriers,
and packagers.<P>


<B>Modern Plastics--CH23</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch23.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
The McGraw-Hill Companies Publications Online<BR>
$4.00/article<BR>
Updated monthly, from 1/1/91<BR>
(Also in ML, RD, M-H)<BR>
Reports on the latest developments of the global plastics industry, from equipment
and processing technologies to materials, product design and applications.
Includes environmental issues and market and industry developments.<P>


<B>Pesticide & Toxic Chemical News--CH18</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch18.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
CRC Press Inc.<BR>
$3.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 12/25/91<BR>
(Also in FB, HazMats)<BR>
Covers pesticides, hazardous wastes, and toxic substances, including legislative 
activities, Supreme Court decisions, or regulatory actions behind the closed doors
of the EPA.<P>


<B>Platt's International Petrochemical Report--CH20</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch20.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
The McGraw-Hill Companies Publications Online<BR>
$5.00/article<BR>
Updated weekly, from 9/12/91<BR>
(Also in EY, M-H)<BR>
Written for executives who manufacture, market and distribute petroleum products.
Coverage includes olefins, solvents, fuels, fibers, polymers, feedstocks, and
important supply and demand trends.<P>


<B>Stereochemical Technology News--CH09</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch09.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 10/1/94<BR>
(Also in BT, Pharma)<BR>
Reports on stereoselective synthesis; separations; catalysts and transformation
agents; and new technology, including patents, tech transfer, raw materials and
intermediates, and reaction strategies.<P>


<B>Toxic Chemicals Litigation Reporter--CH22</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch22.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Andrews Publications<BR>
$4.00/article; $50.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 9/10/92<BR>
(Also in LA, Andrews)<BR>
Covers the latest developments in personal injury and property damage lawsuits
involving exposure to radon, asbestos, environmental tobacco, toxins and other
substances alleged to cause harm in the home and workplace.<P>


<B>Toxic Materials News--CH12</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ch12.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Publishers Inc.<BR>
$5.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Available from 1/6/82--4/25/94<BR>
(Also in EV, GT, ML, BPI, HazMats)<BR>
Provides coverage of legislation, regulations, and litigation pertaining to the
Toxic Substances Control Act, as well as state and local activities, research and
development, grants and contracts, and publications and meetings.<P>
