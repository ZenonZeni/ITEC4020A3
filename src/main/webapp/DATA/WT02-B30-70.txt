<A NAME="objective"><STRONG>Investment Objective</FONT></STRONG></A>
<BR>
To seek to maximize total return, consistent with certain percentage constraints on amounts allocated to each asset
class, from a combination of common stocks, fixed income securities and money market investments.
<P>
<BR>




<A NAME="f_profile"><STRONG>Fund Profile</FONT></STRONG></A><BR>
The Fund invests in a diversified portfolio of:
<UL>
<LI>Common stock indices of domestic and foreign markets. Investments in foreign securities could be more volatile and more difficult to sell than U.S. investments and involve additional risks.
<LI>Fixed income securities selected to attempt to parallel the performance of the investment grade U.S. broad market
<LI>Selected money market instruments
</UL>
The asset mix is determined by using a proprietary, qualitative asset allocation model.
<P>
<BR>




<A NAME="i_profile"><STRONG>Investor Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>Income investors seeking to enhance their total return from a combination of common stocks, fixed-income securities
and money market instruments.
</UL>

Before you invest...<BR>
Like any mutual fund that invests in stocks and bonds, the Fund's returns and net asset value will vary with market
conditions and changing interest rates.
