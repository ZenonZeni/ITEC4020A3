<h1 align=center>The ACLD Foundation:</h1>

<h2 align=center>A Source of Strength Through Commitment</h2>
<pre>
</pre>

<CENTER>
<TABLE BORDER=0 width=500 cellpadding=5>
<tr>
<th align=left>
<br>
The ACLD Foundation was established in 1979 to support and strengthen
the
activities of the Learning Disabilities Association of America. A Trust
Agreement exists between the Foundation and the Mellon Bank, Pittsburgh,
Pennsylvania, as Trustee.
<br>
<br>
This Trust is established for the support of charitable, scientific,
literacy identification, evaluation and education for LD children and
adults.
<br>
<br>
The ACLD Foundation provides funds for LDA, for research, education and
broadening the base of public awareness in the field of learning
disabilities.
