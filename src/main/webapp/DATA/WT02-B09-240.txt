<H2>Child Identification	525-5411</H2>
<H2>New Westminster Community Police </H2>
511 Royal Avenue<BR>
New Westminster  V3L 1H9<BR>
Fax: 525-7036<BR><BR>

Free program provides an official record of your children. The parent or guardian<BR>
 of the child completes an identification form for each child, and then the child is<BR>
 photographed and finger-printed. Records should be kept in a safe place and photo <BR>
updated each year. Operates at Royal City Centre, near the 6th Avenue entrance every <BR>
Saturday and is sponsored by Royal City Remax. Also, volunteers visit local child care<BR>
 facilities and schools.<BR><BR>

<H2>Combat Auto Theft (CAT)	268-5500</H2>
c/o B.C. Automobile Association <BR>
4567 Canada Way<BR>
Burnaby  V5G 4T1<BR>
Fax: 268-5560<BR><BR>

CAT is a yellow and black decal that tells police the driver doesn't usually drive between<BR>
 1:00 am - 5:00 am. Any car that is spotted on the roads between those early morning hours <BR>
with the decal will automatically be assumed by police to be stolen. The officer will then stop<BR>
 the car and question the driver about the car's ownership. To join CAT, pick up a registration<BR>
 form at any ICBC office, BCAA service centre, police station or RCMP detachment.<BR><BR>

<H2>Gang-Youth Against Violence/Contact Line	775-GANG</H2>
<H2>Burnaby/New Westminster</H2>

Wallet size cards with the Lower Mainland numbers are distributed to every<BR>
 grade 7-12 student in each target community. Available also through municipal<BR>
 parks and recreation and community police offices. Translations of contact line<BR>
 information is available in Vietnamese, Spanish, Cantonese and Punjabi. Messages <BR>
can be left in any language and translated. Not restricted for use by young people only;<BR>
 parents are urged to use it if they are being victimized or threatened. Funded through the<BR>
 Attorney General's Ministry, Solicitor General of Canada, Lions International, Sony and BC Transit.<BR><BR>

Dial-a-Law	687-4680<BR>

<H2>BC Branch, Canadian Bar Association </H2>
10th Floor-845 Cambie Street<BR>
Vancouver  V6B 5T3<BR>
Fax: 669-9601<BR><BR>

From a touch-tone phone, you can use Dial-A-Law  24 hours a day, but when calling<BR>
