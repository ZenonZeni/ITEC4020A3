<H4>We Acknowledge . . . </H4>

<P>
This publication describes the objectives, strategy, findings
and limitations of a continent-wide assessment of landcover disturbance
undertaken by CSIRO staff on behalf of the Biodiversity Unit,
Environmental Strategies Directorate, Department of the Environment,
Sport and Territories (DEST), Canberra.
<P>
A distilled version is available as a printed report entitled
'Landcover disturbance over the Australian Continent: A contemporary
assessment'. Biodiversity Series, Paper No. 7, available from
the Department of the Environment, Sport and Territories, Canberra,
Australia.
<P>
The research reported in this publication was supported in part
by the Biodiversity Unit of DEST, and by the CSIRO Climate Change
Research Program. <HR>
