<h1>Hemp (Marijuana) </h1>

<p><b>This page contains materials related to hemp (marijuana),
cannabis sativa.</b> </p>

<hr>

<p><img src="../graphics/golddot1.gif" width="22" height="22"><a
href="taxact/taxact.htm">Marihuana Tax Act of 1937</a> </p>

<p>Did you ever wonder why hemp was illegal? Here are some of the
actual transcripts from the hearings for the Marihuana Tax Act of
1937 -- an interesting piece of lunacy. It is not all there yet,
but it is coming soon. Please see the <a
href="../HISTORY/history.htm">Historical References</a> page for
the full history of the marijuana laws in the United States. </p>

relieved of duty. </p>
