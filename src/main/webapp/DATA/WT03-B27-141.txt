<H3>MANAGEMENT (MT)</H3>

<B>ADA Update--MT23</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt23.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Plain English Legal Publications<BR>
$6.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly; from 1/1/95<BR>
(Also in GT, HRes)<BR>
Provides coverage of significant court rulings on the Americans with Disabilities
Act. Reports and analyzes lawsuits regarding smoking, disability-related leave,
AIDS-infected employees, and more.<P>

<B>Affirmative Action/EEO Personnel Update--MT24</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt24.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Plain English Legal Publications<BR>
$3.00/article; $25.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 1/1/95<BR>
(Also in HRes)<BR>
Covers court rulings and governmental decisions affecting public and private
employers, including recent trends, lawsuits, the question of legal bias, and
more.<P>

<B>Corporate Security--MT14</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt14.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Research Publications<BR>
$6.00/article; $35.00/issue<BR>
Available from 4/15/94--11/3/95<BR>
(Also in LA, HRes)<BR>
Provides valuable guidance on how to safeguard assets and personnel from security
liability and lawsuits. Major topics include preventing violence in the workplace,
significant court and arbitration rulings relating to security, protecting
inventory, innovations in access control, sentry systems, and the latest
techniques in security training.<P>

<B>Drug Detection Report--MT03</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt03.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Pace Publications<BR>
$3.00/article; $45.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 4/5/91<BR>
(Also in HH, LA, ME)<BR>
An independent report on drug testing in the workplace, covering regulatory,
legislative, judicial and scientific developments. Provides detailed treatment of
topical subjects, such as "random" testing, "reasonable suspicion" testing, and
federal regulation of drug testing labs.<P>

<B>Drugs in the Workplace--MT15</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt15.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Research Publications<BR>
$4.00/article; $35.00/issue<BR>
Available from 5/1/94--10/1/95<BR>
(Also in HRes)<BR>
Provides comprehensive coverage of how federal government rules and guidelines
affect the lawful prevention, detection and treatment of substance abuse on the
job. Special areas of coverage focus on medical developments and breakthroughs,
employee assistance programs, and cutting-edge solutions to common jobsite
problems. Regular features include actual workplace cases, a monthly lab update
and legal review.<P>

<B>Employee Relations in Action--MT16</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt16.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Research Publications<BR>
$4.00/article; $25.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 5/1/94<BR>
(Also in Asia, LA)<BR>
Delivers vital news and information to the busy HR professional who needs to
handle employee complaints and manage potential repercussions to the organization.
Taken from true-life arbitration rulings over such issues as hiring and firing,
violence, ERA's attorney; editors pick scenarios that reflect the challenges
managers and supervisors face each day in dealing with tough employee problems.<P>

<B>Human Resource Banking & Insurance--MT21</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt21.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Lafferty Publications Ltd<BR>
$3.00/article $50.00/issue<BR>
Available from 1/1/95--1/1/96<BR>
(Also in IT, Lafferty)<BR>
Reports news and trends in the human resources arena, and presents the results of
surveys of interest to human resources managers. Also offers periodic case studies
of legal and general problems faced by human resource professionals working in a
global environment.<P>

<B>Journal of Business Strategy--MT26</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt26.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Faulkner & Gray Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article $35.00/issue<BR>
Updated bimonthly, from 2/1/95<BR>
(Also in GB, F-G)<BR>
Focuses primarily on industries in transition.  The journal spotlights 
these industries' strategies and progress, and provides insights from
and about top executives.<P>

<B>Labor Trends--MT13</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt13.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Research Publications<BR>
$4.00/article; $25.00/issue<BR>
Available from 1/15/94--12/16/95<BR>
(Also in LA, HRes)<BR>
Contains concise, insightful, timely reports of national and local labor relations
news, including pattern-setting collective bargaining agreements, court rulings,
management and union strategies, and political events from Washington and around
the country. Both widely read and respected, this newsletter has scooped the
competition since 1945 to bring readers the latest negotiations, regulations and
legislation affecting labor and management.<P>

<B>Management Consultant International--MT22</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt22.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Lafferty Publications Ltd.<BR>
$3.00/article; $50.00/issue<BR>
Updated Monthly, from 1/1/95<BR>
(Also in IT, Lafferty)<BR>
Tracks the successes and failures of the world's largest management consulting
firms, from a global perspective. Issues cover worldwide news and trends, offers
features on consulting overseas, and ranks the leading consulting firms by fee
income, number of consultants, and more.<P>

<B>Management Matters--MT11</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt11.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Merton Allen Associates/Infoteam Inc.<BR>
$3.00/article; $25.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 10/1/90<BR>
(Also in GB, LA,)<BR>
Provides updates on effective management, including sales and marketing, personnel
issues, purchasing, technology, and more. Reviews published reports, conferences,
and workshops relating to management.<P>

<B>Management Policies & Personnel Law--MT18</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt18.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Research Publications<BR>
$4.00/article; $35.00/issue<BR>
Available from 4/15/94--9/29/95<BR>
(Also in LA, HRes)<BR>
A leading source of information and ideas human resources executives turn to for
practical solutions to everyday problems. Each issue searches out and analyzes the
most thoughtful and creative personnel policies and procedures from the innovators
in the corporate world, and provides detailed coverage of important legal
decisions and arbitration rulings that can affect your workplace.<P>

<B>Substance Abuse Report--MT17</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mt17.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Research Publications<BR>
$4.00/article; $35.00/issue<BR>
Available from 1/1/94--12/1/95<BR>
(Also in HH, HRes)<BR>
Delivers news on funding availability, treatment success rates, the latest private
and federal research efforts, and updates on the health care reform debate as it
relates to substance abuse treatment. Regularly featured are report on federal,
state and local legislation and initiatives aimed at tackling substance abuse
issues. Coverage includes the entire range of drugs related to social problems,
from alcohol to "designer drugs" just hitting the streets.<P>
