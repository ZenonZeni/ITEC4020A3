<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>A Wide Range of Annuity Benefits</STRONG></FONT><P>


At the time you purchase your annuity, you will need to choose the type which best meets
your
needs.
There are a number of different options regarding when and for how long you will receive
income
payments. Your own personal situation will dictate which is most
appropriate for you.
<P>
Your choice of payment options may be affected by a number of factors, such as:
<P>
<UL>
<LI>Whether you are married
<LI>Whether you want payments to continue after your death for a spouse or other
beneficiary
<LI>Whether you want payments to continue for a specific minimum period of time
</UL>
<P>

As you will see, an immediate annuity from New York Life's Rollover IRA Program can be
quite
flexible
and may be able to offer you the following annuity types:
<P>

<STRONG>Annuity Payment Options</STRONG><BR>
Provides lifetime payments for payee:
<P>
<UL>
<LI><STRONG>Life Annuity</STRONG>  Monthly payments for your life only.
<LI><STRONG>Life Joint & Survivor Annuity</STRONG>  Provides payments for your life plus specified
payments to
your joint
annuitant for their lifetime, after your death.
</UL>
<P>

<STRONG>Insures Minimum Payments</STRONG>
<UL>
<LI><STRONG>Full Cash Refund</STRONG>  Monthly payments for your life with a guaranteed minimum
payout
equal
to
premium paid.  In the event of your death prior to receiving the minimum payout, the balance is paid
in
a
lump sum to
your beneficiary.
<LI><STRONG>Full Cash Refund Joint & Survivor Annuity</STRONG>  If both you and your joint annuitant
die
prior
to the
minimum payout, the remaining amount is paid in a lump sum to your beneficiary.
<LI><STRONG>Certain and Life Annuity </STRONG> Specified minimum number of monthly payments made
plus
payments
for your life thereafter.  If you die during the guarantee period, monthly payments continue to your
beneficiary
for the
remainder
of the guarantee period.
</UL>
<P>

<STRONG>Does Not Provide for Lifetime Payments</STRONG>
<UL>
<LI><STRONG>Period Certain Annuity </STRONG> Monthly payments for specified period only.  	If you
die
during the guarantee period, your beneficiary receives monthly payments for
the
remainder
of the guarantee period.
<P>
</UL>

<P>
All types may not be available to all individuals.
<P>
Remember, with New York Life's Rollover IRA Program, you can divide your distribution to
purchase a guaranteed annuity benefit and open an IRA.
