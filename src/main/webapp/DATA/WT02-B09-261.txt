<H4><u>Social Activities</u></H4>
It is important to put aside some time for yourself. Have fun, go out and meet others. Make new friends.
Volunteering is one way for you to join forces with people who make good things happen. It is also a way to learn new skills and explore possible careers.
<p>
A good track record of volunteer activities is valuable work experience. Become involved with your school, church, youth group or through your local volunteer centre.
Burnaby Volunteer Centre 	294-5533
Greater Coquitlam Volunteer Centre 	524-9808
<p>
The agencies listed below are good places to find adventure, hang out, meet people, dance, listen to music and talk. They have people there who are interested in helping kids grow and enjoy life.
