<P><CENTER><FONT SIZE=5 FACE="Arial">Purpose and History of the<br></FONT><FONT SIZE=5 FACE="Arial">National Home Education Research Institute<br><p></FONT></CENTER> <table border=0 width=600><tr><td width=70% align=left valign=bottom><i><b><FONT SIZE=6 FACE="Arial Narrow">NHERI'S PURPOSE</i></b></font></td><td width=30% align=right><img src="thinker2.gif" width=55 height=73 alt="The Thinker"</td></table><p><FONT FACE="Times">The mission of NHERI is three-fold:<br></FONT><ul><li><B><FONT FACE="Times">To produce high-quality research on home education.<br></FONT><li><FONT FACE="Times">To serve as a clearinghouse of research for home educators, researchers, policy makers, and the media.<br></FONT><li><FONT FACE="Times">To educate the public concerning the findings of all such research.<br></ul><p></FONT></B><FONT FACE="Times">The non-profit National Home Education Research Institute was founded by Dr. Brian Ray, who has been the editor of the journal <I><B>Home School Researcher</B></I> since 1985. <p></FONT><FONT FACE="Times">In fulfilling its mission, NHERI engages in professional research for various clients throughout the nation. In addition, Dr. Ray serves as a consultant to  the growing number of those presently engaged in research on home education topics.<p></FONT><FONT FACE="Times">NHERI, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, also makes available for public purchase many print, audio, and video productions ranging from basic bottom-line fact sheets about home schooling to more technically sophisticated academic reports.</FONT><p><table border=0 width=600><tr><td width=70% align=left valign=bottom><i><b><FONT SIZE=6 FACE="Arial Narrow">NHERI<br>IMPACTS - Education</i></b></font></td><td width=30% align=right><img src="thinker2.gif" width=55 height=73 alt="The Thinker"</td></table><p><FONT FACE="Times">NHERI impacts the world of education  worldwide. Primary and secondary school educators, undergraduate and graduate school educators, education reformers and administrators, and government officials to local board members to parents  all are interested in the most current and accurate research findings concerning the resurgence of the home education of children. Without doubt, the positive track record of successful home education has made an impact upon the American educational establishment. It has also brought attention from other countries around the world.<p></FONT><FONT FACE="Times">By being the leading center for research in home education, NHERI has been able to provide necessary leadership in this crucial arena of the education of future generations. <p></FONT><FONT FACE="Times">Dr. Brian Ray is a noted author of many scholarly publications in the realm of education. His works have been published or cited in such periodicals as <I>The Congressional Quarterly Researcher</I>, <I>The Wall Street Journal</I>, <I>The Washington Post</I>, <I>The American School Board Journal</I>, <I>Education and Urban Society</I> journal, <I>Library Journal</I>, and <I>Newsweek</I>. He is the editor of the refereed journal <I>Home School Researcher</I>. He is a conference and seminar speaker on topics such as education research, science education, and family issues.<p></FONT><table border=0 width=600><tr><td width=70% align=left valign=bottom><i><b><FONT SIZE=6 FACE="Arial Narrow">NHERI<br>IMPACTS - The Courts</i></b></font></td><td width=30% align=right><img src="thinker2.gif" width=55 height=73 alt="The Thinker"</td></table><p><FONT FACE="Dutch 801 Italic">&quot;Ongoing quality home-schooling research is essential for success in both courts and legislatures. Your participation with NHERI is vital.&quot;</FONT></I><FONT FACE="Arial Narrow">  Michael Farris, President, Home School Legal Defense Assoc.<p></FONT><FONT FACE="Times">The work of NHERI is valued by those engaged in court cases across America regarding the home education of children. The parties involved must have solid, verifiable statistics to present a true picture of home schooling.<p></FONT><FONT FACE="Times">Dr. Ray also appears as an expert witness in courtroom and legislative testimony concerning home education issues</FONT><FONT SIZE=2 FACE="Times">.<p></FONT><table border=0 width=600><tr><td width=70% align=left valign=bottom><i><b><FONT SIZE=6 FACE="Arial Narrow">NHERI<br>IMPACTS - Government Policymaking</i></b></font></td><td width=30% align=right><img src="thinker2.gif" width=55 height=73 alt="The Thinker"</td></table><p><FONT FACE="Dutch 801 Italic">&quot;Whoever has the data controls the policy.&quot;</FONT></I><FONT FACE="Arial Narrow">  Kay Coles James, Virginia State Cabinetmember<p></FONT><FONT FACE="Times">Whether in state or national legislative matters, research data are relied upon heavily by both lobbyists, for the persuasion of legislators, and by legislators, for the promotion of bills.<p></FONT><FONT FACE="Times">The work of NHERI in this important arena provides state-specific, national, and international research information to various educational  organizations. NHERI also offers more general materials to the citizen public for them to forward to their various state and national representatives.<p></FONT><table border=0 width=600><tr><td width=70% align=left valign=bottom><i><b><FONT SIZE=6 FACE="Arial Narrow">NHERI<br>IMPACTS - Home School Families</i></b></font></td><td width=30% align=right><img src="thinker2.gif" width=55 height=73 alt="The Thinker"</td></table><p><FONT FACE="Dutch 801 Italic">&quot;Truly objective quality research will be essential in defending home schoolers from the attack to come.&quot;  </FONT></I><FONT FACE="Arial Narrow">J. Richard Fugate, Author/Lecturer<p></FONT><FONT FACE="Times">Home educators desire that their children receive a superior education. They are willing to shoulder the corresponding responsibility to pursue excellence in their own teaching methods. NHERI provides research insights into the teaching of specific academic disciplines for the ease of teaching and the enhancement of learning.<p></FONT><FONT FACE="Times">The work of NHERI in offering print, audio, and video materials in non-technical language is also of great benefit to home schooling families who give these materials to relatives, friends, officials, or anyone else who may be either concerned about the decision or possibly interested in beginning a home school of their own. Solid, verifiable data goes a long way toward informing those who are unfamiliar with home education.<p></FONT><table border=0 width=600><tr><td width=70% align=left valign=bottom><i><b><FONT SIZE=6 FACE="Arial Narrow">NHERI<br>IMPACTS - Public and Private School Families</i></b></font></td><td width=30% align=right><img src="thinker2.gif" width=55 height=73 alt="The Thinker"</td></table><p><FONT FACE="Times">Families with children in institutional school settings are concerned about the quality of education that their tax dollars fund. Research showing the common characteristics and methods of successful home education can impact public and private school methodology and assist in the needed educational reform.</FONT>
