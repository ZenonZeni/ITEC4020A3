<p><IMG ALIGN="Right" SRC="new-gaw2.gif" ALT="Generator Associate" WIDTH="200"
    HEIGHT="145"><font size="4">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The accuracy and accessibility of information is of equal importance. The companies who possess a quick means of creating accurate forms and labels have a distinct advantage over companies who do not. How organized easily can your company create these documents? Accurate&nbsp;waste forms are important to state regulators and Forms Associate is the answer.
</p>
<br>
<center><img src="senhr.gif" height="5" width="536"></center>
<br>
<center><A NAME="1"><!-- --></A><B>Manifest Printing</B></center>
<P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;No need to create shipping documents from scratch. The system contains an extensive list of transporters, disposal facilities and waste streams to make manifesting quick. If you choose to manage your inventory, just point at the on-site inventory to ship and you're done. Even if you are shipping waste to many different states, the system includes all state specific manifests. We can recommend several different printers for printing through these 6 and 8 part forms.
</p>
<br>
<center><img src="senhr.gif" height="5" width="536"></center>
<br>
<center><B>Container Labeling</B></center>
<p><IMG BORDER="0" ALIGN="Right" SRC="cp3-75c.gif" ALT="Container Labels" WIDTH="124" HEIGHT="112">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;FMA is designed to make container labeling a side effect of your other work instead of a job unto itself. Simply select a manifest and FMA prints all the labels you will need in just about any format. Bar coding of waste turnsoutbound shipments into a painless task.
</p>
<P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In cases where you need a manifest quickly, simply choose a profile and a generator and you're done.
</p>
<br>
<center><img src="senhr.gif" height="5" width="536"></center>
<br>
<center><B>Custom Forms You Design</B></center>
<P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Forms Associate&#174 is flexible enough to handle just about any form you will need, even if they are specific to your company. The system contains a document editor which allows you to build upon the pre-designed forms. Should you need a special work order or other forms, simply start the editor, add your company logo and go from there!
</p>
<br>
<center><img src="senhr.gif" height="5" width="536"></center>
<br>
<center><B>Management Reports</B></center>
<P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Included are management reports which use your site's hazardous/non-hazardous waste disposal history. These reports can assist you with determining trends within your company.
</p>
<br>
<center><img src="senhr.gif" height="5" width="536"></center>
<br>
<center><B>Integrated Spreadsheets</B></center>
<P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Once you have accumulated historical data, Forms Associate&#174 allows you to quickly manipulated it using a built-in spreadsheet. With just a few easy menu choices you can manipulate your historical data using the system. For users who are already familiar with Excel, the system allows you to save the data in Excel spreadsheet format.
</p>
<br>
<center><img src="senhr.gif" height="5" width="536"></center>
<br>
<center><B>Easily Import from Legacy Systems</B></center>
<P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In some cases companies will have an existing system for tracking some part of their hazardous waste and/or material. Forms Associate&#174 has a user friendly import feature to smooth your transition. Importing can draw upon any DBF or comma-delimited format file.
</p>
<br>
<center><img src="senhr.gif" height="5" width="536"></center>
<br>
<center><B>Your Support and Training</B></center>
<P>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sentinel provides extensive support to get your company up and running with Forms Associate&#174 quickly. To keep your system up to date, we offer extended service which includes updates for new features, changes in manifest forms, container labels and regulatory reporting requirements. We include unlimited phone support for answering your questions. If needed, Sentinel can provide you with on-site training.
</p>
