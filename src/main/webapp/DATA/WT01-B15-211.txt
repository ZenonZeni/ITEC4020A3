<BODY><PRE><H2>
               Southern States Energy Board (SSEB)</H2><H3>
                     Contract DE-FC01-92RW00247 (Since 1987)</H3>
<P>
The Southern States Energy Board (SSEB) is a non-profit interstate compact organization
founded in 1960.  It serves sixteen states (Alabama, Arkansas, Florida, Georgia, Kentucky,
Louisiana, Maryland, Mississippi, Missouri, North Carolina, Oklahoma, South Carolina,
Tennessee, Texas, Virginia, and West Virginia), the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, and the
Virgin Islands. The SSEB provides technical assistance, program development, policy analysis,
and research support to members in a vast range of diverse energy and environmental areas.
