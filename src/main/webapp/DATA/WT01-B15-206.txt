<BODY><PRE><H2>
   Council of State Governments-Midwestern Office (CSG-MW)</H2><H3>
                   Contract DE-FC01-94RW00286 (Since 1989)
</H3><P>
The Council of State Governments is a non-profit, state supported and directed service
organization that provides research and creates a network for legislative, executive, and
judicial branch representatives. The role of the Midwestern Office is to foster
intergovernmental cooperation through the promotion of regional, as well as individual, state
responses to common issues and challenges. Twelve states are represented (Illinois, Indiana,
Iowa, Kansas, Michigan, Missouri, Minnesota, Nebraska, North Dakota, Ohio, South
Dakota, and Wisconsin).The High-Level Radioactive Waste Committee represents state
governors, legislatures, energy offices, and agencies active in state transportation issues.
