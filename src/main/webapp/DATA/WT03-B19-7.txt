Special Fare Stuff:<P>

Flextrains:  Tickets used between 0830 and 1530 and after 1855 are 25%
		off, 50% for youths.<P>
		
Kids under 6 ride free with a fare paying adult.<P>
	
Transfer Discount: $1.00 off One Way or Round Trip tickets when used
		with transfer from participating transit agency.<P>
	
Step Up Coupon Program:  Monthly pass holders on Ventura and Orange County
	Lines can ride on certain Amtrak trains at discount when used with 
	special coupons.  Coupons are $18.00 for 10.  On Ventura County Line, 
	coupons valid on Amtrak 576, 578, 786.  Origin or destination must be
	in Ventura County.  On Orange County Line, valid on Amtrak 773, 774, 
	777, 780, 783, 786.  Origin or destination must be in Orange County.
		<P>

Bicycles:  Riders must possess a valid ticket and bicycle permit.  For permit applications,
call 808-LINK.  Bicycles allowed only as space permits and at the discretion
of the conductor.<P>
