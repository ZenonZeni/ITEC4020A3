<p><h3>Instructions for Using Downloadable Forms: WordPerfect 6.0a</h3><P>

Where there is a "box" that needs to be"checked" or "x'd" and it is empty,  and you are using WordPerfect 6.0a:<br>

<ul><li>Delete the "box" that isn't "checked" or "x'd".
<li>Click on "Insert" at the top of your screen.
<li>Click on "Character" from the pull down menu.
<li>Make sure you have "Iconic Symbols" chosen as your Character Set.
<li>Find character #5,25 (a box "checked" or "x'd").
<li>Highlight that particular character, click on "Insert and Close".
<li>The font may need to be adjusted to enlarge this particular character.
</ul><P>

Where there is a "box" that needs to have a number inserted and is empty, and you are using WordPerfect 6.0a:<br>

<ul><li>Print the form out and hand-write in the number or letter that needs to be inserted into this particular box; OR
<li>Delete the blank box on the screen, and just type in the letter or number that is required to go in this space.</ul><P>


Some of the forms print duplex (on both sides), so try to use a printer that has duplex printing capabilities.  If your printer does not have duplex printing capabilities, the form will print two (2) pages.  The back pages of most of these forms are instructions on how to complete the form.
