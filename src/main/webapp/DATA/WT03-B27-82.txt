width="150" height="193"> Memphis</font></p>
<p>Brought to the market in November 1993, this exciting true hot
pink will not suffer from discoloring. The variety itself is
strong and resistant to diseases. It's known for its long vase
life, opening very slowly which is why it can bear an open cut
stage. This is important to realize that its bud actually
contrinues to grow while travelling.</p>
