<H2 ALIGN="CENTER">About Pierson &amp; Burnett</H2>
<P><B>Pierson &amp; Burnett llp</B> is a small, specialized firm which brings
together the legal training and business expertise of its professional staff to
provide regulatory, commercial, corporate and legislative services to
telecommunications, space, maritime, and emerging high technology companies for
domestic and international projects.  </P>
<P><B>The Founding Partners</B> are W. Theodore Pierson Jr. and Dennis James
Burnett.  Mr. Pierson has more than 30 years of experience in telecommunications
and mass media business and regulatory law.  Mr. Burnett has more than 20 years
experience in regulatory and commercial matters relating to aerospace
manufacturing, launch services, telecommunications and maritime industries. 
Stephen D.  Hayes, who has nearly 10 years of experience representing a diverse
cross-section of the global telecommunications industry as a lawyer and
management consultant, joined the firm as a partner in 1996.</P>
<P><B>The Firm</B> is staffed with a select group of experienced professionals
who have an in-depth knowledge of the industries they support and a history of
breaking ground for innovative services and new technologies.  As described in
the pages that follow, the firm's partners have been involved in the
establishment and development of numerous new businesses.  Mr. Pierson, for
example, co-founded Advanced Radio Technologies Corporation, a wireless
broadband local distribution company and Digital Satellite Broadcasting
Corporation, an U.S. applicant for the Satellite Digital Audio Radio Service.
The familiarity of the Pierson &amp; Burnett professional staff with the
industries we support enables the firm to provide effective, practical support
to address our clients' legal and business needs.</P>
<P><B>This Web Site</B> presents information about our professional team, and
capabilities, services and experience.  We also provide brief descriptions of
the industries we serve, some of the projects and programs in which we have been
involved and some selected case studies of our accomplishments. </P>
<P><B>Comments and Inquiries</B> are welcome as this space permits only
partial descriptions of experience and partial samples of our capabilities. We
welcome your questions about our firm and the information presented.</P>
<P><B>Thank you</B> for your interest in Pierson &amp; Burnett.</P>
