<h3>Southern Coalition for Advanced Transportation (SCAT)</h3><p>

<blockquote>
<B>Project Title:  Stealth Auxiliary Power Unit<p>
Performing Organization: Sustainable Power Systems </B><p>

<P>POINT OF CONTACT: Donald Alessi 
<BR>
Telephone:  (407) 863­4130<BR>


<P><B>Program Summary: </B></P> 


Develop a fuel cell power system based on H­Power metal platelet technology as a low signature,
range extender for an existing pickup truck.

<P><B>Benefits:</B></P>

<P><UL>
<LI>     Three times increase in fuel efficiency over fossil fueled combustion engine vehicle
<LI>     Stand­alone power source 
<LI>     Range extender </UL></P>
