<STRONG><FONT SIZE="+1">NYLCare Vision</FONT></STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>Helping People Feel at Ease About Health Care</STRONG><P>

Our goal at NYLCare is to help people feel at ease about health care. We have designed NYLCare to be user-friendly to
members
and employers alike, to offer members access to quality health care provider networks and to strive to help keep them healthy through
preventive health care and wellness programs. <P>

We want members, employers and providers to feel confident that when they deal with NYLCare, they will receive prompt,
courteous and professional service.  By choosing NYLCare, they gain a valuable health benefits partner actively involved in
managing the cost and quality of employee benefit coverage. <P>

We also maintain an entire spectrum of ancillary benefit programs, such as dental, vision, managed mental health and
substance
abuse care, managed prescription drug programs and disability and life insurance, as well as the management of flexible
benefits
plans and flexible spending accounts to round out any employee benefit package.<P>
