<PRE>
  THE  VEGETABLE GARDEN is a very important link between us and  the
earth.  As our garden grows we grow. When we grow our own food we  know
exactly what   has been used to grow this food. We know that the fruit,
the  vegetables, the herbs that we grow are rich and full of  nutrients
neccessary  for  our health. Our backyards can provide   us  with  good
homegrown  food._ Growing your own food is a very important  aspect  of
maintaining a healthy mind and body. Even more important is growing our
own  food  organically  without  the  use  of  chemicals  that  pollute
ourselves  and our environment. If our home grown food is to have  good
nutritional  value it must be grown in healthy live soil. This  becomes
even   more  important  when  you  realize  that  to  succeed   growing
organically, good healthly  soil is neccessary.
