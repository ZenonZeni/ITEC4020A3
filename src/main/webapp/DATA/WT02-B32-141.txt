<H2 ALIGN="CENTER">Projects &amp; Programs</H2>
<P>Listed below in alphabetical order are some examples of projects and
programs in which the partners at Pierson &amp; Burnett have participated.  For
more information, click on the name of the project or program.</P>
<P><B><A HREF="http://www.artelecom.com/">ART</A>  </B>  -  Wireless
broadband fixed telecommunications service.</P>
<P><B>ChinaStar</B>  -  Fixed satellite service for China Orient Satellite
Corporation</P>
<P><B>Columbus</B>  - European element for the Space Station</P>
<P><B><A HREF="cosmos.html">Cosmos (SL-8)</A></B> -  Russian launch services
of mid-sized payloads to LEO</P>
<P><B>DSBC </B>  -  Application for Digital Audio Radio Satellite license</P>
<P><B>DFH-3 </B> -  Chinese direct broadcast communications satellite</P>
<P><B><A HREF="earlybrd.html">EarlBird &amp; Quick Bird</A></B> - Commercial
3 meter and 1 meter IFOV land remote sensing satellite service</P>
<P><B>EarthStar</B>  -  initiative to privatize U.S. government owned land and
meteorological satellites</P>
<P><B>EUREKA</B> -  Commercialization of the European retrievable shuttle
carrier</P>
<P><B>FirstLine</B>  -  Insurance facility to meet requirements of Oil
Pollution Act of 1990</P>
<P><B>GALS </B> -  Russian direct broadcast satellites</P>
<P><B>GTE TSI   </B>- National long distance service</P>
<P><B>GTE Card Services - </B>National long distance credit card company</P>
<P><B>Home Box Office - </B>First national pay cable network.</P>
<P><B><A HREF="infomob.html">Infomobile</A></B> - French national radio
paging system using European Radio Message System (&quot;ERMES&quot;) standard</P>
<P><B>Nahuel</B> - Argentine Ku-band fixed satellite service.</P>
<P><B>Nicatelsat</B> - Nicaraguan teleport for Intelsat and InterSputnik.</P>
<P><B>NYNEX  - </B>National long distance service</P>
<P><B>Omninet</B> -  Land Mobile Satellite Service</P>
<H3>Omnitel-Pronto</H3>
<P><B><A HREF="omnitel.html">Italia</A></B> - Italian GSM cellular wireless
telephone service</P>
<P><B>OmniTracs</B>  - Ku-band frequency hopping spread spectrum messaging and
position location service</P>
<P><B>Satphone </B>  -  Mediterranean Basin GSM compatible land mobile radio
satellite service.</P>
<H3>Shinsegi </H3>
<P><B><A HREF="shinsegi.html">Telecomm</A></B>- CDMA cellular wireless
telephone service</P>
<P><B>SBS </B>   -  Ku-band Fixed Satellite Service for business applications</P>
<P><B>Sinosat</B>   -  Chinese Communications Satellites.</P>
<P><B>SpaceLab</B>   -  European/US Shuttle Compatible Laboratory</P>
<P><B>Space Station</B> - Earth orbiting laboratory, observatory and service
station.</P>
<P><B>SPARX </B> -  Commercialization of the SPAS and MOMS</P>
<P><B>Target Telecom -</B>National long distance telephone service (reseller)</P>
<P><B><A HREF="windstar.html">Windstar</A></B>   - Satellite sensed ocean
wind speed and direction demonstration and commercialization project</P>
