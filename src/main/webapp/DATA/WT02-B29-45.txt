<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>What Are Your Options?</STRONG></FONT><P>

There are, under current tax laws, generally five choices when it comes to taking
your
distribution, depending on your company's retirement savings plan. Only you can
decide
what
makes the most sense for you. Without planning ahead, you run the risk of making a
choice
that will not be in your best interest in the long run. It may seem as though the
choices are
simple, but the consequences of each differ a great deal. It's important,
therefore, to
become familiar with all options before you make a choice.<P>
Look at the following five options:
