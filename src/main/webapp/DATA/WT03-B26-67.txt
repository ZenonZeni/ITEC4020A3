<H1><CENTER>Land AVHRR Workshop<BR>
<HR></CENTER>
</H1>
<BR>
<B>2. PRESENTATIONS</B><BR>
<BR>
The presentations were broken down into four main sections. 
<BLOCKQUOTE>1. Generic issues of data quality and management.<BR>
Presentations by Wilson, Turner, Grant and Mitchell addressed this topic.<BR>
<BR>
2. Current operational usages of reflective AVHRR data.<BR>
Presentations by Smith, Allan and Dudgeon.<BR>
<BR>
3. Linking AVHRR measurements with non-remote sensing environmental models.
<BR>
Presentations by Danaher and McVicar.<BR>
<BR>
4. Introduction of the MODIS system, which will probably replace AVHRR data
for many applications later this decade.<BR>
Presentation by Strahler.</BLOCKQUOTE>
<BR>
Following and during the formal presentations there were discussions as
to what was required and more importantly as to how to progress the requirements
that were discussed. These are listed below the timetable.<BR>
