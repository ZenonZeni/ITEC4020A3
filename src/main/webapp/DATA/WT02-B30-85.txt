<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>Matching Your Needs and Objectives With the Right
Investments</STRONG></FONT><P>


There are three basic investment approaches that can help you "put your money to
work." These
three approaches are:<P>
<STRONG>Growth</STRONG><BR>  Do you want to "grow" your nest egg so that it becomes
bigger and perhaps put off receiving income from your investments
until a later date?<P>
<STRONG>Income</STRONG><BR>  Do you want your investments to provide you with
current,
ongoing
income, perhaps beginning immediately?<P>
<STRONG>A Combination of Growth and Income</STRONG><BR>  Do you want to receive
some
income
immediately, and continue to "grow" your nest egg to offset the effects of
inflation?<P>
