<h1><b><center>1997 CALENDAR - SATELLITE IMAGES OF ASIA</b></h1>
<i>jointly produced by</i><br>
<h3>Center for Remote Imaging, Sensing and Processing, National University of Singapore and<br>
Spot Asia Pte Ltd</h3></center>

<p>
Twelve of our best images of Southeast Asia, which were obtained mainly from the SPOT Satellite, are featured  in this calendar.  The calendar will feature different images of Malaysia, Hong Kong, The Philippines, Bangladesh, South China, Indonesia, Myanmar and Vietnam for every month of the year.
<p>
The 13-page full-coloured calendar with matching envelopes is on sale from CRSIP at S$15.00 (inclusive of 3% GST).  For more information, please send us a email at <a href="mailto:crisp@leonis.nus.sg">crisp@leonis.nus.sg</a>.
<p>
