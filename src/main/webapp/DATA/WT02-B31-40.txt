Health care benefits play a large role in providing for your individual ­ and your
dependents' ­  routine
and emergency needs.  A significant number of employers across the United States have
selected
NYLCare, a wholly owned subsidiary of New York Life, as their health benefits service
provider.  

"Members" - people just like you - are the employees of these organizations that are covered
by a
NYLCare health benefits plan.  Currently, there are over 3.5 million NYLCare members across
the
United States.  <P>

<Strong>Members: </Strong> If you are a NYLCare member, these links will
provide you with information to help you get the most from the NYLCare network.<P>

<Strong>Consumers: </Strong>  If you are not a NYLCare member, these links can provide you
with
valuable information concerning health benefits, in general.  Although the site is designed
primarily for our members, non-members will find much of the information useful and
interesting.<P>

<STRONG><A HREF="/viewer/wsh-nyl.hts/nylcare/mcpl.html">NYLCare Provider
List</A></STRONG><BR>
A comprehensive listing of NYLCare health service providers in the United States.<P>

<STRONG><A HREF="/viewer/wsh-nyl.hts/nylcare/mcif.html">Making an Informed Health
Care Decision</A></STRONG><BR>
Guidelines to making an informed decision about the type of health benefits coverage that is
right for you and your family.<P>

<STRONG><A HREF="/viewer/wsh-nyl.hts/nylcare/mcrp.html">NYLCare Research
Reports</A></STRONG><BR>
Abstracts of health care research reports commissioned by NYLCare.  The full text of
research reports is also available<P>

<STRONG><A HREF="/viewer/wsh-nyl.hts/nylcare/mcgl.html">Glossary of Health Care
Terms</A></STRONG><BR>
This glossary of health care terms will grow as the site evolves.<P>

<STRONG><A HREF="/viewer/wsh-nyl.hts/nylcare/mcfq.html">Frequently Asked Questions
(FAQ)</A></STRONG><BR>
If a question you have is not listed here, ask us!  We will add to this list as we receive
your questions.<P>
