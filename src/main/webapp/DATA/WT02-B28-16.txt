<H1>Action Points for Mensa as an Organisation</H1>

<H3>All growing organisations are beset with internal political problems
at a certain stage of their development. A scientific approach is called
for to minimize conflict. </H3>

<LI><B>Commission an independent business ethicist to develop a Code of
Ethics, in conjunction with the BMC and Mensa staff.</B><BR>
</LI>

<LI><B>Develop a Members' Charter to give weight to members' duties to
uphold the good name of Mensa.</B><BR>
</LI>

<LI><B>Develop leadership by training Mensa key figures in business ethics
so as to implement corporate strategy.</B><BR>
</LI>

<LI><B>Identify best practice in all areas of activity so as to encourage
minimum standards.</B><BR>
</LI>

<LI><B>Employ outside expertise, where necessary, to optimise conflict
management.</B></LI>
