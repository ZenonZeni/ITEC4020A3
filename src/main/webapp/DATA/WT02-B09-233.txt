<li><H1>Camping</H1>
The Burnaby Camping Bureau provides children from low
 income families,<BR> foster children and handicapped people with an opportunity to attend<BR>
 organized camps that they would not otherwise be able to afford.<BR>
<BR>

Send a kid to camp.  What fun a child could have for maybe the first time<BR>
 in their lives by taking part in camp activities, such as craft activities,<BR>
 canoeing, horseback riding, games and sports.<BR>
<BR>
For as little as $200 a child from an underprivileged family will have an<BR> 
opportunity of their life by attending an organized summer camp.<BR> 
To find out how you can financially help a child to have a fun time<BR>
 in their summer.<H2><UL>Call 299-5778 extension #22</UL></H2>

<li><H2>Meals on Wheels</H2>
The Burnaby Meals On Wheels Society provides nutritious home delivered meals<BR>
to persons who live alone and are unable to care for their own nutritional requirements.<BR>

Volunteer<BR>
Do you have two hours free a week?  Take part in your community and volunteer for a<BR>
very worthwhile service to our shut-in frail seniors.   Hot meals are delivered on Monday,<BR>
Wednesday and Friday during the noon hour.  Take responsibility and volunteer by contacting us at<BR>
<H2><UL>299-5778, extension #1</UL></H2>

 Volunteer drivers maintain this program.<BR>
