<h3 align=center><font color="#FF0000">MENTAL ILLNESS: MYTHS DISPELLED</font></h3>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">MYTH</font><font color="#000000">:	Mental illnesses do not affect the average person.</font></p>
<blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">FACT</font><font color="#000000">:	No one is immune to mental illness. The National Institute of Mental 
Health statistics show that 5 million adults and 7.5 million children in America 
have some form of mental illness in any given year.</font></p>
</blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">MYTH</font><font color="#000000">:	A person can recover from a mental illness by turning his or her thoughts positively 
and with prayer.</font></p>
<blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">FACT</font><font color="#000000">:	Mental illness is a physical, not a moral, condition. Recovery comes 
through receiving the necessary medical treatment and supportive services. 
Highly effective treatments allow many people to resume productive, satisfying 
lives.</font></p>
</blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">MYTH</font><font color="#000000">:	The cause of mental illness is either poor parenting or poor character.</font></p>
<blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">FACT</font><font color="#000000">:	Mental illnesses like major depression, manic depression, and 
schizophrenia are no-fault brain diseases, and can strike in any family.</font></p>
</blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">MYTH</font><font color="#000000">:	A person experiencing mental illness is possessed by an evil spirit.</font></p>
<blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">FACT</font><font color="#000000">:	Mental illnesses are caused by physical disorders or chemical 
imbalances, not supernatural forces.</font></p>
</blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">MYTH</font><font color="#000000">:	Mental illness is the same as mental retardation.</font></p>
<blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">FACT:</font><font color="#000000">	Mental retardation is an intellectual defect at birth, or a result of illness 
or injury during infancy or childhood. Mental illness are brain diseases, physical 
disorders that often strike between the ages of 16 and 25.</font></p>
</blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">MYTH</font><font color="#000000">:	People who have a mental illness are dangerous.</font></p>
<blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#0000FF">FACT</font><font color="#000000">:	Most people who have a mental illness are no more violent than 
someone suffering from cancer, diabetes or any other serious illness. More 
often, they are the victims of violence.</font></p>
</blockquote>
