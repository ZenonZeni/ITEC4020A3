<BLOCKQUOTE><CENTER>
<H2>Strategic Guidelines

</H2>
</CENTER>

<BR>


<p>CI has identified five strategic guidelines for protecting biodiversity:

<ol>
<p><li>Increasing <b>scientific understanding</b> of biodiversity in our target ecosystems;

<p><li>Determining economic forces at play and developing <b>economic alternatives</b> to ecologically destructive land-use practices;

<p><li>Assisting in the design and implementation of <b>conservation policies</b> and funding programs with multilateral institutions and national governments;

<p><li>Maintaining viable parks and <b>protected areas</b> in our target ecosystems; and,

<p><li>Building <b>conservation awareness</b>, commitment, and capacity in local communities, government, and the private sector.</ol>

</BLOCKQUOTE>
