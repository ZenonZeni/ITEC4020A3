<STRONG><FONT SIZE="+1">About Members & Consumers</FONT></STRONG><P>

A significant number of employers across the United States have selected NYLCare as their
health benefits service provider.  Currently,  there are over 3.5 million individuals served by NYLCare across the United States.  <P>

<Strong>Members: </Strong> If you are a NYLCare member, ("Members" are the enrollees in a

managed care plan) this site has been designed to provide you with information to help you 

get the most from your NYLCare network.<P>



<Strong>Consumers: </Strong>  If you are not a NYLCare member, this site can provide you with

valuable information concerning health benefits in general.  Although the site is designed

primarily for our members, non-members will find much of the information useful and

interesting.<P>



<STRONG><A HREF="mcpl.html"><STRONG>NYLCare Provider List</STRONG></A></STRONG><BR>

A comprehensive listing of NYLCare network providers in the United States.<P>



<STRONG><A HREF="mcif.html"><STRONG>Making an Informed Health Care Decision</STRONG></A></STRONG><BR>

Guidelines to making an informed decision about the type of health benefits coverage that is

right for you and your family.<P>



<STRONG><A HREF="mcrp.html"><STRONG>NYLCare Research Reports</STRONG></A></STRONG><BR>

Abstracts of health care research reports conducted by NYLCare.  The full text of

research reports is also available.<P>



<STRONG><A HREF="mcgl.html"><STRONG>Glossary of Health Care Terms</STRONG></A></STRONG><BR>

This glossary of health care terms will grow as the site evolves.<P>



<STRONG><A HREF="mcfq.html"><STRONG>Frequently Asked Questions</STRONG></A></STRONG><BR>

If a question you have is not listed here, ask us!  We will add to this list as we receive

your questions.<P>
