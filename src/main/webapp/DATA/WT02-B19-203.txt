more than 850,000 citations (60,000 citations on disabilities or gifted issues)<BR> <BR> <li>   Collect and review the educational literature (documents, journal articles, etc.) in the areas of special and gifted education, selecting the highest quality and most relevant material<BR> <BR> <li>     Catalog, abstract, and index the material we select for inclusion in the ERIC database<BR> <BR> <li>     Provide information in special/gifted education and publications on current research, programs, and practices<BR><BR>  <li>     Prepare publications such as ERIC Digests (brief overviews of current topics); provide information on topics such as ADD, gifted, behavior disorders, early childhood, inclusion, learning disabilities<BR> <BR> <li>     Respond to questions on disabilities and gifted education via the AskERIC service on the Internet<BR> <BR> <li>     Provide references to other relevant sources and organizations that address disabilities or  giftedness<BR> <BR> <li>     Provide general information on education;
