<B><I>Red Flag Indicators<br>
What to look for in an export transaction</I></B></FONT><br> <BR> <BR>

<IMG SRC="stripe.gif"></CENTER>

<p> 

<img src="blueball.gif"><FONT SIZE=4> <B>Use this as a check list to discover<I> possible</I> violations of the Export Administration Regulations. </font><FONT SIZE=3><B> You may also wish to visit our page that provides <A HREF="knowcust.htm"><I>"Know Your Customer Guidance"</I></B></A>.<P>


<UL><IL><img src="whitebal.gif"><B> The customer or its address is similar to one of the parties found on the Commerce Department's [BXA's] list of denied persons. </font><FONT SIZE=2><I><A HREF="franco.htm">Case example.</A></I><P></font><FONT SIZE=3>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"><B> The customer or purchasing agent is reluctant to offer information about the end-use of the item. <P>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> The product's capabilities do not fit the buyer's line of business, such as an order for sophisticated computers for a small bakery. <P>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> The item ordered is incompatible with the technical level of the country to which it is being shipped, such as semiconductor manufacturing equipment being shipped to a country that has no electronics industry.<P>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> The customer is willing to pay cash for a very expensive item when the terms of sale would normally call for financing.<P>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> The customer has little or no business background. <P>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> The customer is unfamiliar with the product's performance characteristics but still wants the product.  <p>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> Routine installation, training, or maintenance services are declined by the customer.   <P>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> Delivery dates are vague, or deliveries are planned for out of the way destinations.  <P>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> A freight forwarding firm is listed as the product's final destination.  <P>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> The shipping route is abnormal for the product and destination. <P>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> Packaging is inconsistent with the stated method of shipment or destination.  <P>


<IL><img src="whitebal.gif"> When questioned, the buyer is evasive and especially unclear about whether the purchased product is for domestic use, for export, or for reexport.</UL>  <P>
