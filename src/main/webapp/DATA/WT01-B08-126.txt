<CENTER><H3>Flower &amp Fruit Booster</H3>
<H4>(5-7-0)</H4></CENTER>
<IMG SRC="flower.JPG" ALT="Flower Food" LOWSRC="flower1.JPG" ALIGN=right>
<P><B>FLOWER &amp FRUIT BOOSTER</B> is a unique blend of Seabird Guano and Bat Guano.  Seabird Guano is a natural organic fertilizer derived from fossilized deposits in South American.  It is ideal for providing plants with a supply of phosphorous which encourages fruiting and blooming.  Seabird Guano is used extensively on fruits, citrus, and grapes in South America, and is suitable for use throughout the garden on all types of plants.  Bat Guano is an excellent source of readily available nitrogen and other nutrients, and was very popular with farmers and gardeners prior to the advent of chemical fertilizers.  We add it to our blend to ensure optimum performance in your garden, and to help support the nitrogen needs of your plants.</P>
