<H3>Peter L. Butt:</H3>
<P><IMG Align=Left SRC="petecave.gif" ALT="Photo of Peter Butt">Cave diving instructor Peter L. Butt:
Pete Butt specializes in cavern and cave diving instruction and guiding, has been an active instructor with the NSS-CDS since 1983, and presently lives, works and dives in North Central Florida.  He has been an instructor member of the NACD since 1987 and with NAUI since 1984.  Pete began his dive instruction career with PADI in 1977 and currently holds its Master Instructor rating.  In addition, he has served on both the NSS-CDS' board of directors and its training committee.  He has operated his own dive store and was manager of Ginnie Springs Resort from 1983 to 1987.  Since 1989, he has been vice-presidentr of Karst Environmental Services,Inc., a hydrogeological special services and consulting company.<BR>
