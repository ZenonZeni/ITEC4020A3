<h2>New Online Titles</h2>



<i>By Angie Felag<br>
Publisher Services Representative</i><P>


This month NewsNet introduces 23 new titles provided by Financial Times Information's Asia Intelligence Wire.  Twenty of the new titles are daily publications and three are weekly publications. The titles listed cover all facets of Asian / Pacific-Rim current events including national and international business issues, politics, and the economy.<P>

Also, these titles follow some of the important industries in this area of the world, like the computer technology industry.<P>
The following is a description of each new daily title provided through Asia Intelligence Wire. <P>
<B>Antara - The Indonesian National News Agency </b>(IT117) from Antara, Jakarta, Indonesia, is providing very broad coverage of news and current events in Indonesia.<P>

<b>Asahi Shimbun/Asahi Evening News </b>(IT118) from The Asahi Shimbun, Tokyo, Japan covers international, national, and local news reported in Japan, including such subjects as politics, the economy, social issues, science, international affairs, and culture.<P>
<b>Business Times (Malaysia) </b>(IT122)  from New Straits Times Press (Malaysia) Berhad, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, contains Malaysian news focusing on finance, trade, the economy, corporations, and capital markets. A  daily supplement covering shipping and transportation issues, called Shipping Times, is also included.<P>
<b>Business Times (Singapore Press)</b> (IT123) is a daily newspaper published by Singapore Press Holdings, Singapore. It provides articles focusing on the business news and information of China and the world.<P>
<b>BusinessWorld (Manila) </b>(IT124) from BusinessWorld, Quezon City, Philippines, reports on business, trade financial, economic, political, and regulatory news relevant to the Philippines. <P>
<b>China Business Information Network</b> (IT127) is a daily title published by China Business Information Network, Beijing, People's Republic of China. It brings to our readers business, trade, and the economic news of China.<P>
<b>China Daily </b>(IT128) is a national English-language newspaper published by China Daily Publications, China. It supplies political, economic and domestic news and comment for an international readership.<P>
<b>China News</b> (IT129) is Taiwan's major English-language daily publication, published by Federation of Hong Kong Industries, Kowloon, Hong Kong. It provides coverage on news and current events in Taiwan.<P>
<b>Central News Agency</b> (IT130) originates in New York City,  New York, and provides a Taiwanese perspective on domestic and international news.<P>
<b>The Jakarta Post</b> (IT137)  is a daily newspaper of Indonesia, that supplies general news, business news, sports news, editorials, and features.<P>
<b>The Korea Economic Daily</b> (IT138) from The Korea Economic Daily, Seoul, Republic of Korea, offers coverage on Korean business and industry, including markets, ventures, licensing, foreign investment, exports and imports, new factories and expansion, labor, national business issues, and local financial and currency markets.<P>
<b>The Korea Herald</b> (IT139) originates in Seoul, Republic of Korea. It provides news and current events, including business trade, political coverage and analysis, and economic coverage and analysis.<P>
<b>Mainichi Daily News</b> (IT140) is a newspaper published by The Mainichi Newspapers, Tokyo, Japan. It covers international and national news along with local news of the Tokyo region.<P>
<b>Mainichi Press Release Service,</b> (IT141) from The Mainichi Newspapers, Tokyo, Japan, provides press releases generated by Japanese- based companies.<P>
<b>New Straits Times</b> (IT142) is a newspaper published by New Straits Times Press (Malaysia) Berhad, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. It provides local news coverage of finance, trade, economics, culture, politics, and sports of Malaysia.<P>
<b>The Saigon Times Daily </b>(IT143) is a newspaper published by The Saigon Times Group, Vietnam. It reports on business, economic and financial news relevant to Vietnam.<P>
<b>The Statesman</b> (IT147) is a major daily English-language publication serving India. The publication originates in Calcutta and covers the business, political, and economic news of Bengal and the Northeast states.<P>
<b>The Daily Yomiuri</b> (IT152) is a newspaper from Yomiuri Shimbun-sha, Japan. It contains articles and editorials relative to Japan.<P>
<b>Business Line</b> (IT156) is a daily newspaper published by The Hindu, India. Articles focus on the financial and business news of India. Regular features include a corporate briefs section and a section devoted to Indian stocks and commodities.<P>
<h4>AIW Weekly Titles</h4>
The following are descriptions of the new weekly titles added to the NewsNet database.<P>
<b>Asiaweek </b>(IT119) is published in Causeway Bay, Hong Kong. It provides national, regional, political, and economic news and trends in the Far East.<P>
<b>Beijing Review</b> (IT120) is a weekly newspaper published in China. It covers politics, business, and general new stories relevant to China. The in-depth articles provide a glimpse into Chinese life and culture.<P>
<b>Asia Computer Weekly</b> (EC118) is published by Miller Freeman, Asia. An executive briefing of Pacific-Rim technology news, it reports on vital matters of the information technology industry from an Asian perspective.<P>
