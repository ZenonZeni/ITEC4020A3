<H1>Introduction</H1>
<P>
<BIG>
<b><i>
If you wish to expand your business in the Asia-Pacific region,
there are excellent reasons to locate it in Melbourne.
<P>
</b></i>
An extensive and independent study of international business costs,
recently completed for the Victorian Government by KPMG
Management Consulting, shows that Victoria is a particularly 
cost-competitive location for business.
<P>
Victoria's competitive advantages include its location in the
Asia-Pacific
and the strength of its trade within the region.
<P>
Its labour force is cost-competitive, skilled and well trained.
It is also adaptable and stable.
<P>
Business services are competitively priced and are readily available
to new investors.
<P>
Infrastructure and communications services are "state-of-the-art"
and competitively priced.
<P>
Serviced industrial sites for manufacturing are:
<UL>
<LI>readily available
<LI>moderately priced
<LI>close to general cargo ports
<LI>close to international and domestic air-freight terminals.
</UL>
<P>
The cost of living is low. The standard of living is high.
The living environment is extremely attractive.
<P>
Advantage Melbourne is an invaluable
tool in assisting you with plans to invest in Melbourne
and Victoria. 
<P>
