<H3>EDUCATION (ED)</H3>

<B>Education Daily--ED08</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ed08.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Capitol Publishing Group, Capitol <BR>
Publications Inc.<BR>
$2.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated daily, from 1/3/94<BR>
(Also in GT, SS, CPG, Day)<BR>
Provides accurate, up-to-the-minute reports every business day on national, state,
and local events pertinent to top-level education officials. Includes news from
Congress, the Education Department, the White House and the courts, and reports on
the latest education research, civil rights, special education and higher
education news.<P>

<B>Education Technology News--ED12</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ed12.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Publishers Inc.<BR>
$5.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 8/2/94<BR>
(Also in EC, TE, BPI, Software)<BR>
Delivers the latest information on new products, what programs are working in
other schools, regulatory information that affects planning, and the funding
sources that make it all possible. Covers the hottest topics, from the utilization
of the information highway to distance learning, and the newsmakers whose ideas
may be tomorrow's reality.<P>

<B>Heller Report on Educational Technology--ED11</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ed11.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Nelson B. Heller & Associates<BR>
$3.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 5/1/90<BR>
(Also in EC, TE, Multimedia)<BR>
Provides coverage and analysis of the <BR>
K-12, higher education, corporate/industrial training, and home education markets.
A wide range of technology and telecommunications products and services are
covered, including educational software, and integrated learning systems.<P>

<B>Report on Literacy Programs--ED10</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ed10.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Publishers Inc.<BR>
$5.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 12/7/89<BR>
(Also in GT, BPI)<BR>
A guide for education and business leaders to the legislation, funding, programs,
and resources in the vital field of adult education. Special emphasis is placed on
corporate literacy and basic-skills training programs.<P>
