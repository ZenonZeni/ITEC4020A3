On the island of Kauai at Princeville Resort you are surrounded by beauty no matter where you are or what you choose to do.  And at the heart of the Princeville experience is golf. Robert Trent Jones, Jr. has fashioned 45 of the most beautiful holes of golf in the world.  They range along green bluffs that skirt mountains and the bluest ocean, offering stunning views everywhere you look.
<P>
Golf Digest has honored the Prince Course with its highest accolade: number one among all golf courses in the State of Hawaii. Also ranked number 43 in "America's 100 Greatest Golf Courses."  And Golf Magazine has named Princeville a "Gold Medal Resort."  Golf Digest also rated the Makai Course number seven in Hawaii.
