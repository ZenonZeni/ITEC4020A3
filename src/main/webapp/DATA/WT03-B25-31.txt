<P>
<P>The following overview provides a first description of our work carried out in the area of Simulation. For further information please reply to us the form at the end of this document or contact us <A HREF="../ecm/lage_e.htm">directly</A>
<P>
<HR>
<P>The large variety of tasks in simulation and modelling is influenced by the developments and results of the other main activities like automation technology, robotics and mechatronics. Complex robot and process simulation systems are used, imroved and also developed. Those Systems are used for further verification and development of algorithms and modelling techniques in the field of process technology. The used methods include computer graphics, descrete event simulation, finite element, hybrid simulation techniques and AI. The output of those tasks is the verification, optimisation and visualisation of processes.
