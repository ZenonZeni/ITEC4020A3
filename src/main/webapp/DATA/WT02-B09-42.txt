<H1 ALIGN=center><I>Background</I></H1>

<center><IMG src="graphics/pat_circles.gif"></center><P>

<B>

Rutherford Publishing, Inc. specializes in designing customized mastheads and pages which meet clients' specifications and needs. This service includes copy editing and designing of materials. Rutherford Publishing, Inc. is available to provide telephone communication regarding customization of the newsletter and other matters of concern.<p>



In addition to <A HREF="totalwell.html">Total Wellness®</A>, Rutherford Publishing, Inc. produces four other monthly newsletters which focus on specific areas of the workplace, including leadership management, personal and professional development, time management, and Christian counseling. These newsletters are: 



<ul>

<LI><A HREF="lacurrnt.html"> The Leadership Advantage</A>, a monthly, eight-page newsletter, which provides executives, managers, supervisors, and other professionals with suggestions for ongoing leadership development. 



<LI><A HREF="securrnt.html"> The Slight Edge®</A>, a monthly, eight-page newsletter, which provides executives, middle managers, supervisors, professional business owners, and sales associates with tips and suggestions for ongoing personal development.



<LI><A HREF="tmcurrnt.html"> Tyme Management™</A>, a monthly, four-page newsletter, which provides executives, managers, supervisors, secretaries, assistants, owners, sales associates, and other people in the workforce with tips and suggestions on improving time management.



<LI><A HREF="cocurrnt.html"> Connections®</A>, a monthly, eight-page newsletter, which provides individuals with helpful tips, articles, self-help tests, and checklists that will enrich people's lives, increase their well-being, provide encouragment for living, and help improve their relationships.

</ul></B>
