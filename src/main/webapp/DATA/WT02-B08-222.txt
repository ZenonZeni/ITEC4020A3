<H1 align=center>SPRI LIBRARY: SERVICES TO REMOTE USERS</H1>

The best way to make use of SPRI Library is to become a 
<a href="visitors.htm">Visitor</a>.  
However, a number of services have been developed for those wishing to make 
use of our collections at a distance.  For more information about these, see 
Additional Services (WWW page under construction).
<p>

<H3>Remote access</H3>

<H4>INTERNET facilities:</H4><ul>
<li> <a href="http://www.muscat.co.uk/ccdp/spri/wdccg.html">ICE AND 
SNOW</a> glaciological database. 
<li> <a href="sprilib.htm">SPRILIB</a> monograph records. Currently 
18,000 monograph records 
may be searched on  the <b>Cambridge University Union Catalogue</b>.  To find 
them, follow these instructions:<br><p>
<pre>
Telnet ul.cam.ac.uk
Choose option 3 (Union catalogue)
Choose option 6 (Select preferred libraries...)
Type 50 (=Sco.) Scott Polar Res. Inst.
 
Carry out search.
Type END to leave system from any menu.
</pre>
</ul>


<H4>Retrospective bibliographies:</H4><p>
Subject searches on your chosen topic using the polar bibliographic 
databases:
<ul> <li> <a href="sprilib.htm">SPRILIB</a> 
<li> Arctic and Antarctic Regions, PolarPac and Wenger Eskimo Anthropological 
Database CD-ROMs.  Databases available include: Antarctic Bibliography, ASTIS, 
Bibliography of Alaska and Polar Regions, BOREAL, C-CORE, CRREL, INAC and 
WDC-A.
</ul>

<H4>Current awareness:</H4>
<ul>
<li> SPRILIST monthly personal bibliographies (lists of recently catalogued 
material selected on the basis of relevance to researcher's interests).
</ul>

<H4> Document supply</H4><ul>
<li> Photocopies of material available on request (subject to copyright). 
Many items in the collection are unavailable from any other library,
including the British Document Supply Centre.  This applies to both
historic and current material. 
</ul>

<H4>Publications</H4>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.cup.cam.ac.uk/Journals/JNLSCAT/pga/pga.html">
Polar and Glaciological Abstracts</a> is a quarterly compilation with
subject/geographic and author indexes listing all recent catalogued
material. 
</ul>


For further information on retrospective bibliographies, current awareness and document 
delivery, see Additional Services (WWW page under construction).
