<H1><CENTER>Arctic Ocean expedition 1996</CENTER></H1>
<P>
The Arctic Ocean expedition was organised by the <A HREF="url: www.polar.kva.se">Swedish Polar Secretariat </A>in
collaboration with the Alfred Wegener Institute, Germany, and
included the ice breaker Oden and the research vessel Polarstern.
<P>

<TR><TD WIDTH=411><CENTER><IMG SRC="Oden2.jpg"></CENTER></TD>
<TD WIDTH=225>The program for Remote Sensing and Sea Ice, RESSI, took place from the Swedish ice breaker Oden.
</TD></TR>
</TABLE>

<H2>Route</H2>
<P>
The expedition was split in two legs with exchange of personell
at the Lominosov Ridge on 25 August. The planned route for the
expedition is illustrated below:
<P>
<CENTER><IMG SRC="karta.gif"></CENTER>
<P>
The final route was changed during the expedition time and Oden
returned over the North Pole.
