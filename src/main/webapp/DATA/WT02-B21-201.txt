<CENTER><H2>Internet Shopping Habits Survey Results</H2></CENTER>
<CENTER><H5>Survey started: July 12, 1995</H5></CENTER>
<P>
<CENTER><H4>Total Respondents: 1521</H4></CENTER><BR><HR>
<UL>
</PRE><LI><B>Have you ever purchased a product via the Internet?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  771]  50.7% - <I>Yes</I>
[  706]  46.4% - <I>No</I>
[   43]   2.8% - <I>No Answer</I>
</PRE><LI><B>How many times have you made online purchases?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  690]  45.4% - <I>Never</I>
[  352]  23.1% - <I>2-3 times</I>
[  201]  13.2% - <I>Once</I>
[  128]   8.4% - <I>4-6 times</I>
[   59]   3.9% - <I>No Answer</I>
[   57]   3.7% - <I>7-15 times</I>
[   34]   2.2% - <I>More than 15 times</I>
</PRE><LI><B>If no, what is your reason for not ordering from the Internet?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  823]  54.1% - <I>No Answer</I>
[  348]  22.9% - <I>I do not want to submit personal billing information over the Internet.</I>
[  167]  11.0% - <I>In general I don't do alot of shopping.</I>
[  100]   6.6% - <I>I'm wary of ordering online due to unfamiliarity with the company.</I>
[   75]   4.9% - <I>It was too difficult finding what I was looking for.</I>
[    8]   0.5% - <I>If I don't deal with a person, I'm afraid my order will get lost.</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Was your purchase(s) for an item that you specifically were searching for?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  493]  32.4% - <I>I was specifically looking for the item.</I>
[  443]  29.1% - <I>No Answer</I>
[  303]  19.9% - <I>N/A - Never purchased anything online</I>
[  281]  18.5% - <I>I came across it and liked it, so I bought it.</I>
</PRE><LI><B>On the net, what is the primary factor which influences your decision to buy?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  402]  26.4% - <I>N/A - Never purchased anything online</I>
[  236]  15.5% - <I>The price</I>
[  208]  13.7% - <I>The convienence of shopping online.</I>
[  190]  12.5% - <I>No Answer</I>
[  160]  10.5% - <I>The explanation of the item and its features.</I>
[  103]   6.8% - <I>The unique nature of the item.</I>
[   76]   5.0% - <I>The 'ease' of finding the specific item.</I>
[   66]   4.3% - <I>The photo or drawing of the item that was presented.</I>
[   44]   2.9% - <I>The payment method available for purchasing the item.</I>
[   35]   2.3% - <I>The general quality of the web site.</I>
</PRE><LI><B>On average, are your purchases from a stand-alone site, or from a mall?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  524]  34.5% - <I>Standalone storefront</I>
[  411]  27.0% - <I>N/A - Never purchased anything online</I>
[  216]  14.2% - <I>No Answer</I>
[  209]  13.7% - <I>Mall</I>
[  160]  10.5% - <I>Not sure</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Would you make repeat purchases from the sites that you've patronized?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  606]  39.8% - <I>Yes</I>
[  429]  28.2% - <I>N/A - Never purchased anything online</I>
[  251]  16.5% - <I>Maybe</I>
[  207]  13.6% - <I>No Answer</I>
[   27]   1.8% - <I>No</I>
</PRE><LI><B>How did you pay for this online purchase (for the majority of your purchases)?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  451]  29.7% - <I>N/A - Never purchased anything online</I>
[  260]  17.1% - <I>No Answer</I>
[  244]  16.0% - <I>Sent credit card via secure form/method.</I>
[  154]  10.1% - <I>Called a telephone number and provided credit card info.</I>
[  104]   6.8% - <I>Sent credit card but not sure if secure or not.</I>
[   94]   6.2% - <I>Sent credit card via unsecure form/method.</I>
[   69]   4.5% - <I>Mailed a check or money order via postal service.</I>
[   43]   2.8% - <I>Other</I>
[   29]   1.9% - <I>Emailed my credit card number.</I>
[   23]   1.5% - <I>First Virtual account</I>
[   21]   1.4% - <I>COD</I>
[   11]   0.7% - <I>Terms</I>
[    5]   0.3% - <I>Another Internet-based ordering system</I>
[    5]   0.3% - <I>Cybercash</I>
[    4]   0.3% - <I>E-Cash</I>
[    3]   0.2% - <I>Digicash</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Have you ever had anyone use your credit card without approval?</B><BR>
<PRE>[ 1003]  65.9% - <I>No</I>
[  136]   8.9% - <I>No Answer</I>
[  123]   8.1% - <I>I don't have any credit cards</I>
[  119]   7.8% - <I>Yes - but I cleared it up without any liability</I>
[   33]   2.2% - <I>Yes - by a family member</I>
[   31]   2.0% - <I>Yes - and I ended up having to pay</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Are you set up or do you plan to become set up for online payment proecssing?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  714]  46.9% - <I>Not currently and I do not plan to</I>
[  386]  25.4% - <I>Not currently but I do plan to</I>
[  235]  15.5% - <I>No Answer</I>
[  123]   8.1% - <I>Yes, with one payment processor</I>
[   63]   4.1% - <I>Yes, with multiple payment processors</I>
</PRE><LI><B>What type of products have you purchased online?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  424]  27.9% - <I>Computer-related products & services</I>
[  373]  24.5% - <I>Books, Information and Magazines</I>
[  144]   9.5% - <I>Music and Videos</I>
[  139]   9.1% - <I>Internet Products & Services</I>
[  125]   8.2% - <I>Clothing</I>
[  108]   7.1% - <I>Flowers and Plants</I>
[   99]   6.5% - <I>Gifts</I>
[   93]   6.1% - <I>Electronics/Electrical</I>
[   91]   6.0% - <I>Adult Products/Services</I>
[   85]   5.6% - <I>Travel and Vacations</I>
[   78]   5.1% - <I>Communications</I>
[   70]   4.6% - <I>Toys and Games</I>
[   60]   3.9% - <I>Hobbies/Crafts</I>
[   52]   3.4% - <I>Business/Legal/Financial</I>
[   47]   3.1% - <I>Home Products</I>
[   46]   3.0% - <I>Food/Drink</I>
[   42]   2.8% - <I>Education</I>
[   41]   2.7% - <I>Information Resources/Help/Non-Profit Organizations</I>
[   36]   2.4% - <I>Art</I>
[   33]   2.2% - <I>Sports and Fitness</I>
[   31]   2.0% - <I>Business Opportunities</I>
[   26]   1.7% - <I>Other Services</I>
[   25]   1.6% - <I>Jewelry and Accessories</I>
[   21]   1.4% - <I>Cosmetics and Beauty Aids</I>
[   21]   1.4% - <I>New Age</I>
[   18]   1.2% - <I>College Services</I>
[   16]   1.1% - <I>Medical and Health Products</I>
[   14]   0.9% - <I>Real Estate-related</I>
[    9]   0.6% - <I>Mortgage Lending</I>
[    9]   0.6% - <I>Safety and Security</I>
[    8]   0.5% - <I>Infant and Toddler Products</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Have you been satisfied with the products that you have purchased online?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  586]  38.5% - <I>Very satisfied - no problems</I>
[  447]  29.4% - <I>N/A - Never purchased anything online</I>
[  277]  18.2% - <I>No Answer</I>
[  185]  12.2% - <I>Somewhat satisfied</I>
[   25]   1.6% - <I>Not satisfied - many problems</I>
</PRE><LI><B>How would you compare online ordering with mail ordering?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  549]  36.1% - <I>Online ordering is more appealing</I>
[  391]  25.7% - <I>N/A - Never purchased anything online</I>
[  284]  18.7% - <I>They're both about the same</I>
[  216]  14.2% - <I>No Answer</I>
[   80]   5.3% - <I>Mail-order is more appealing</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Are you worried about giving credit card information over the telephone?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  470]  30.9% - <I>Yes</I>
[  468]  30.8% - <I>Not much</I>
[  377]  24.8% - <I>No</I>
[  104]   6.8% - <I>I have no credit cards</I>
[  101]   6.6% - <I>No Answer</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Are you worried about giving credit card information over the internet (in a secured transaction)?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  535]  35.2% - <I>Yes</I>
[  400]  26.3% - <I>No</I>
[  389]  25.6% - <I>Not much</I>
[  107]   7.0% - <I>I have no credit cards</I>

</PRE><LI><B>Are you worried about giving credit card information over the internet (in a non-secured transaction)?</B><BR>
