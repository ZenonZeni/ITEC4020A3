<H2 ALIGN=Center>Domestic NetShade<SUP><FONT SIZE=-1>TM</FONT></SUP> - U.S. and Canada Only
</H2><P>
<P>In case you didn't know, the United States government considers
encryption software to be a military technology, and therefore regulates
its export as munitions. &nbsp;For this reason, NetShade is available for download to residents of the United States and Canada only.
<P>Fortunately, Michael Johnson has graciously allowed us use of his domestic FTP server for electronic distribution of our product. Once you have accessed the site, look in the "net/" directory. &nbsp;There you will find the file "NS95b10d.exe", which is a self-extracting archive of NetShade for Windows 95.
<P>Please note that all transfers are logged and provided to U.S. law enforcement officials. &nbsp;The entry point to the ftp site is <A HREF="ftp://ftp.csn.net/mpj/README">ftp://ftp.csn.net/mpj/README</A> (/net/ns95b10d.exe).
