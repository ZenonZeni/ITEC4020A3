<H1>Major National Poetry Awards</H1>

<P>The following is a current list of major American awards for poetry, along

with their recent recipients.</P>

<P><B>The Academy of American Poets Fellowship</B>.</P>

<P>Offered by The Academy of American Poets for distinguished poetic

achievement.</P>

<UL>

<LI> Jay Wright (1996) <I>Selected Poems of Jay Wright</I> (Princeton

University Press)

</LI>

<LI> Denise Levertov (1995) <I>Evening Train</I> (New Directions)

</LI>

<LI> David Ferry (1994) <I>Dwelling Places</I> (University of Chicago Press)

</LI>

<LI> Gerald Stern (1993) <I>Odd Mercy</I> (W.W. Norton)

</LI></UL>

<P>Offered by the Library of Congress for the best book of poetry published by

<UL>

<LI> Kenneth Koch (1996) <I>One Train</I> (Knopf)

</LI>
