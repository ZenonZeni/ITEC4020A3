<h1>The Arts</h1>
<p>
There is strong support for the arts, with national and international 
performers assured of large, appreciative audiences. Opera, ballet 
and theatre companies and orchestras perform at indoor and 
outdoor venues.

<p>
Melbourne is the home of the Australian Ballet, the Victoria State 
Opera, the National Gallery of Victoria and the Melbourne 
Symphony Orchestra, as well as several international art and 
literary festivals. 

<p>
The city has the largest range of theatre venues in Australia, 
including the Arts Centre, State Theatre and the historic Princess 
and Regent theatres.
