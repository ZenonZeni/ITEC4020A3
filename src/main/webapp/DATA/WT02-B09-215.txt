			<font size=+1 face="Times New Roman,Times">
LONDON, UK&#0151;<a href="http://www.game-land.com/games/interlotto/">Game-Land</a> (http://www.game-land.com), an internet site devoted to gaming, is getting into the Christmas spirit by hosting a huge lotto giveaway, sponsored by the first Internet Lottery, <a href="http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158">InterLotto</a>. What’s more the promotion will be around into the new year, so if you haven’t had a chance to play yet, you’ve still got plenty of time.




 
			</font>
			<br><br>
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">
				&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
					<a href="http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158">InterLotto</a> has sponsored Game-Land’s <a href="http://www.game-land.com/games/interlotto/">‘Lootery’</a> game, where you pick six numbers from forty and then watch a random draw take place, which is animated with Shockwave technology. If you match four, five or six balls then you can win up to SFr 500 (about US $410) instantly. All you have to do is click <a href="http://www.game-land.com/games/interlotto/">‘Go!’</a>
			</font>
			<br><br>
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">
				&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
					GameLand’s web site has been created to ‘promote and encourage internet gaming’. Their many exciting games includ ‘Pachinko’, ‘Roulette’, ‘Tiles’ and ‘Laser Tag’. These games are as addictive as they are visually exciting.  The games are divided up into three sections: ‘Games of chance’, ‘Games of thought’ and ‘Games of skill’. ‘Lootery’ is a game of chance where you must guess against the results of the random ball picker to win prizes.
 			</font>
			<br><br>
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">
				&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
					To win with <a href="http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158">InterLotto</a> ‘lootery’, you must first register as a player on the <a href="http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158">InterLotto</a> lottery system, although you do not need to deposit any funds. Once you are registered, any winnings you accumulate from the <a href="http://www.game-land.com/games/interlotto/">Game-Land</a> game are credited to your <a href="http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158">InterLotto</a> winnings account and can be used to play <a href="http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158">InterLotto games</a>. If you then win on <a href="http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158">InterLotto</a>, you can withdraw your winnings.
			</font>
			<br><br>	
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">
				&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
					For matching four balls you win 10 Swiss Francs (SFr). Match five balls to win SFr 50. And a six ball win gains you SFr 500 (about US $410). If you did win the maximum SFr 500, you would have enough funds to play <a href="http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158">InterLotto’s</a> massive SFr 1 million jackpot Big Basho game 5 times or enter 500 lines in <a href="http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158">InterLotto’s</a> weekly lottery which boasts a jackpot of SFr 1,200,000. Take your pick!
			</font>
			<br><br>	
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">

A spokesman at <a href="http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158">InterLotto</a> commented, 'Our Lootery promotion may end in January, but I wouldn't be surprised if it was followed up soon after with something just as exciting.'
