<P>
Our chapters and MOCAMI offer a wide range of services, from
Helplines, <a href="journey.html">Journey of Hope Family Education Classes</a>, family and patient
support groups, educational forums, speaker's bureau, public and professional
education, fighting discrimination, and up to date information about
legislation affecting the lives of persons with mental illness.
<P>
We have an 800#, answered in Jefferson City. 1 800 374-2138. If no one is
available, leave a message and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
Our staffing is sparce.
<P>


Membership fee includes membership in your local chapter, MOCAMI and NAMI.
 Fees range from $12 to $35, depending on
your chapter.  We are a service oriented, public benefit organization.<br>

Contributions to us are tax deductible.  
<p>
<img align=left src="jourlogo.gif">
<p>The <b>Journey of Hope </b>is a 12 week course for family members of persons with brain disorders/serious mental illness.  It is provided free of charge as a service of your MOCAMI, local AMI chapters and the Missouri Department of Mental Health.
