<BLOCKQUOTE>

<IMG SRC="gifs/how_kempergift_head.gif" ALT="How To Give The Gift Of A Kemper Fund">
<BR>

There are many gifts you can give a special youngster, but very few have
the potential to appreciate over time. A Kemper fund investment
provides the opportunity to grow with a child - without getting lost,
broken or forgotten as the child matures.
<P>

Whether your gift will help finance college or other future needs, a
mutual fund can be one of the most significant gifts a child can receive
because the more time it has to grow, the greater value a mutual fund
can potentially provide.
<P>

Kemper offers several mutual funds that could be well suited for a
child's portfolio because of their long-term investment objectives. And,
according to the Uniform Gift to Minors Act (UGMA) or Uniform Transfer
to Minors Act (UTMA), a mutual fund gift to a child can provide certain
tax benefits, based on current law in most states.
<P>

For more information about giving the gift of a Kemper fund, please call 
your financial representative or Kemper Shareholder Services 1-800-621-1048. 
We'll be happy to send you a gift kit, including  
<A HREF="../prospectus.html">prospectuses</A> containing more complete details 
about custodial accounts and an attractive gift card and envelope.
<P>
