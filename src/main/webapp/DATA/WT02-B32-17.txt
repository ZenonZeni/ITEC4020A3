<h2>Research Assessment Exercise 1996</h2>
</FONT>
The Department is very pleased to have received a Rating of <b>4</b> as a result of the Research Assessment Exercise conducted by the Higher Education Funding Council.  This is an improvement on our previous figure and demonstrates the Department's committment to maintain a strong reserach activity at both national and international level.
<p>
The Deprtment included 24 members of staff in the assessment - double the number of any other equivalent UK food department.
