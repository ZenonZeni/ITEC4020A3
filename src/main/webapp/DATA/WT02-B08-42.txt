<P ALIGN=left><BR><FONT SIZE="4"><B>MARINE DECK UNIT</B><BR>
The Marine Deck Unit logs and displays the output from a wide range of analogue instruments. Provides power (16.5 V DC @ 2A ) and accepts up to 4 analogue inputs. It is powered either externally (110-250VAC 50/60Hz or 12VDC) or from internal rechargeable batteries (1.8Ah). Real time display in engineering 
units and an RS232 output to a PC and analogue output for a chart recorder.
