<CENTER><B>TVX GOLD INC. ANNOUNCES 1994 RESULTS</B></CENTER>

<P>Toronto, Ontario, March 7, 1995 ... TVX Gold Inc. announced today that earnings for the year ended December 31, 1994 were a record US$22,779,000 or US$0.14 per share, which is an increase from US$15,725,000 or US$0.12 per share earned in 1993. For the fourth quarter of 1994, earnings were US$3,598,000 or US$ 0.02 per share compared with earnings of US$4,164,000 or US$ 0.03 per share in the fourth quarter of 1993.

<P>Gold and gold equivalent production was 436,500 ounces in 1994 compared to 438,800 ounces in 1993. In the fourth quarter of 1994, gold and gold equivalent production was 105,300 ounces, compared with 110,800 ounces in the fourth quarter of 1993. Average realized revenue on a per gold equivalent ounce basis was comparable at US$371 in 1994 and 1993.

<P>On a per gold equivalent ounce basis, average cash cost per ounce for 1994 was US$199, compared to US$172 in 1993. The increase was generally due to lower ore grades mined at Mineral Hill, Casa Berardi and La Coipa.

<P>Increased exploration and business development spending in 1994 was offset by reduced interest expense, favourable translation and foreign exchange gains and higher interest income earned on cash balances resulting from the issuance of common shares in January 1994.

<P>Eike Batista, Chairman and CEO of TVX Gold, said the results are indicative of the ability of the Company's management team to recognize and focus on global opportunities. Mr. Batista also commented that TVX is well positioned for continued growth in the future.
