<strong>Edoardo SPIRANDELLI</strong><br>
Technical Assistant</font></p>

<p><font size="3">Edoardo Spirandelli received his high-school
certificate in Industrial Electronics in 1987. In 1987/88 he
completed a specialization course in electronics and in 1988
attended a course on English and Computers at Berkeley University Extension
College of San Francisco. <br>
In 1989, he joined the Istituto Automazione Navale as a
technician. He has worked at the development of various scientific
hardware and software systems and in late 1994 was made assistant
systems administrator.<br>
He has participated in several oceanographic campaigns for
instrument testing.<br>
