<A NAME="objective"><STRONG>Investment Objective</FONT></STRONG></A>
<BR>
To seek to provide a high level of current income while preserving capital and maintaining
liquidity.
<P>
<BR>




<A NAME="f_profile"><STRONG>Fund Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>The Fund seeks to optimize current income and minimize investment risk through professional
management,
careful security selection, and diversification by issuer, sector, maturity and yield.
<LI>The Fund manager emphasizes high quality, short-term securities consistent with
preservation of capital,
maturing in thirteen months or less.
</UL>

<P>
<BR>




<A NAME="i_profile"><STRONG>Investor Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>Investors who seek a place to keep emergency funds while maintaining liquidity.
<LI>Investors who are more averse to the risk that may be associated with other types of investments.
</UL>
<BR>
<STRONG>Before you invest...</STRONG><BR>
You probably know that all investments involve some degree of risk.  Although the Fund seeks to
maintain a stable
$1.00 net asset value, there is no assurance that it will be able to do so.
<P>
