<H1>Consultancy</H1>
<HR SIZE=1 NOSHADE>

<IMG ALIGN=RIGHT SRC="rayhaccp.gif">


<P>RSSL provides a variety of consultancy services, particularly in the area of product safety and product acceptability. </P>

<P><IMG  SRC="squarebullet12.gif"><FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG> Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point (HACCP)</STRONG></FONT><BR>
HACCP is the internationally recognised system for ensuring product safety and widely accepted as forming the basis for a &#39;due diligence&#39; defence. RSSL is an internationally recognised authority in HACCP <A HREF=training.html">training</A> and consultancy.</P>
<BR><BR><BR>
<HR>
<P><IMG  SRC="squarebullet12.gif"><FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG> Emergency Response Service (ERS)</STRONG></FONT>
<BR>The <A HREF="ers.html">ERS</A> provides immediate analytical suport during product emergencies, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. RSSL also provides consultancy in helping companies avoid problems.</P>
<HR>
<P><IMG  SRC="squarebullet12.gif"><FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG> New product development</STRONG></FONT></P>
<HR>
<P><IMG  SRC="squarebullet12.gif"><FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG> Consumer and market research </STRONG></FONT><BR>RSSL uses <A HREF="sensory.html">sensory evaluation</A> methods to help you understand product characteristics, and demonstrate which characteristics drive consumer preferences.</P>
<HR>
<P><IMG  SRC="squarebullet12.gif"><FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG> Information services</STRONG></FONT><BR>Information is key to good decision making in industry, and RSSL can help through the <A HREF="fis.html">Food Intelligence Service</A></P>
<HR>
<P><IMG  SRC="squarebullet12.gif"><FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG> Quality systems </STRONG></FONT><BR> RSSL provides consultancy in setting up, and carrying out Supplier <A HREF="selectqa.html">Quality Assurance</A> and due diligence programmes, and offers advice on and support to laboratories seeking NAMAS accreditation. <A HREF="selectqa.html">Select QA</A> provides a range of third party supplier quality assurance services for both manufacturers and retailers.</P>
<HR>
<P><IMG  SRC="squarebullet12.gif"><FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG> Training</STRONG></FONT><BR>Through <A HREF=training.html>FITR</A> RSSL offers a number of courses in food safety/quality related topics</P>
