<H3>Safety Issues</H3>

<P>

Widespread use of hydrogen for transportation, and for lighting, 
heating, and 

cooling our homes requires new methods for detecting hydrogen leaks 
quickly and 

reliably. We also need to understand how to best store hydrogen in 
vehicles to 

prevent catastrophic tank failures in the event of an accident. 
Researchers have 

been working in these areas; select a project summary for more 
information:
