<h1 align=center>Learning Disabilities Association</h1>

<h3 align=center>ATTENTION: ALL SCHOOL PRINCIPALS</h3>

<table>
<tr>
<th COLSPAN=2>WIN SCHOLARSHIP TO LDA INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE
<br>Gain instructional and leadership strategies for meeting the
<br>diverse and special needs of students within your school
<br><br></th>
</tr>

<tr>
<td VALIGN=TOP><b>WHAT:</b></td>

<td>Award including $300 stipend, complimentary conference registration
and banquet ticket to the 1997 International Conference of the Learning
Disabilities Association of America. Recipients within fifty miles of Chicago
will receive one-half the stipend.<br><br></td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td VALIGN=TOP><b>WHEN:</b></td>

<td>February 19-22, 1997<br><br></td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td VALIGN=TOP><b>WHERE:</b></td>

<td>Palmer House, Chicago, Illinois<br><br></td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td VALIGN=TOP><b>WHY:</b></td>

<td>The leadership role of a principal is vital to the success of the educational
team. Serving students with special needs is a primary challenge for school
administrators. The LDA International Conference can provide invaluable
information for principals as they seek to serve all students.<br><br></td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td VALIGN=TOP><b>HOW:</b></td>

<td>Applicants must include: 
<ul>
<li>an essay (150 words or less), describing what you expect to gain from
this conference that will benefit all students, including those with special
needs</li>

<li>a brief biological sketch</li>

<li>your name, address, school, home/office telephone numbers</li>
</ul>

<p>Send to:
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;LDA Principals' Project
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;4156 Library Road
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Pittsburgh, PA 15234-1349
<br>
<br>Applications must be received by <b>November 1.</b> Recipients will
be notified by December 15.
</td>
</tr>
</table>
