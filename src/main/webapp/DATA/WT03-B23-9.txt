<CENTER><H2>EATING DISORDER INVENTORY - 2ND ED. (EDI-2)</H2></CENTER><P>
<CENTER><EM>Garner, David; M. Olstead and J. Polivy</EM></CENTER><P>
<BR>EDI-2 is a 91 item, self-report instrument designed to assess a broad range  of meaningful psychological and behavioral traits common in anorexia  nervosa and bulimia. It consist of 11 subscales: Drive for Thinness, Bulemia, Body Dissatisfaction, Ineffectiveness, Perfectionism, Interpersonal Distrust, Introceptive Awareness , Maturity Fears, Social Insecurity, Asceticism, and Impulse Regulation. EDI can be used to measure specific cognitive and behavioral dimensions that may differentiate subgroups of individuals with serious psychopathology from "normal" dieters, and help in the understanding and treatment of eating disorders.<BR>
