<FONT SIZE=2 FACE="Arial">AESOP has been widely accepted by the
nation's leading surgeons and has been integrated into many of
the training programs at these centers. These programs are comprised
of classroom lectures and hands-on training within a simulated
surgical environment. The learning surgeon is able to become familiar
with the key features and benefits of AESOP and gain hands-on
experience under the guidance of an experienced surgeon user.
