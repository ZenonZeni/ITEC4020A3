<h2 align="center"><i>U.S. Pet Dog Statistics </i></h2>
<ul>
    <li>U.S. pet owners have approximately 55.7 million pet dogs
        of which 7.2 million, or 13%, are 10 years of age or
        older </li>
    <li>Approximately 35.9 million U.S. households own at least
        one dog </li>
    <li>In 1995, U.S. consumers spent $5.61 billion on pet dog
        health care </li>
    <li>91.5% of dog owners consider veterinarians the best
        source of pet health care knowledge and advice </li>
    <li>93% of dog owners have a &quot;regular veterinarian&quot; </li>
</ul>

<h2 align="center"><i>U.S. Pet Cat Statistics </i></h2>
<ul>
    <li>U.S. pet owners have approximately 63.4 million pet cats
        of which 6.9 million, or 11% are 10 years of age or older </li>
    <li>Approximately 30.2 million U.S. households own at least
        one cat </li>
    <li>In 1995, U.S. consumers spent $2.89 billion on pet cat
        health care </li>
    <li>92.8% of cat owners consider veterinarians the best
        source of pet health care knowledge and advice </li>
    <li>71% of cat owners have a &quot;regular veterinarian&quot; </li>
</ul>
