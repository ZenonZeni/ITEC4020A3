<H2><CENTER><I><FONT SIZE=5 COLOR=#008000 FACE="Arial">DAIS</FONT></I><FONT SIZE=5 COLOR=#008000 FACE="Arial"> Products &amp; Pricing
</FONT></CENTER></H2>
<HR>
<P>
<FONT FACE="Arial">The </FONT><I><FONT COLOR=#008000 FACE="Arial"><B>DAIS Corporation</b></font></FONT></I><FONT FACE="Arial"> has invented a <FONT COLOR="#804040"><B>new</B></FONT>, <FONT COLOR="#804040"><B>low cost</B></FONT>, ionomeric material, a low cost electrode structure, and a novel interface polymer. These three materials combine to form a DAIS POG (or Membrane/Electrode Assembly). </FONT></B>
<P>
<B><FONT FACE="Arial">The text below outlines briefly the attributes of these materials.</FONT></B>
<BR>
</FONT>
<P>
<FONT FACE="Arial">Click here to review papers discussing the <A HREF="press.htm"><B><b><I>DAIS</I></b> ionomer</B></A> </FONT><FONT SIZE=2 FACE="Arial"><B>(the DAIS 585 hydrocarbon membrane material)</B>. 
<BR>
</FONT>
<P>
<FONT FACE="Arial">In the coming months the folks at DAIS will be introducing a line of application driven Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cells (PEM-FC) based on its novel materials and stack designs. 
</FONT>
<HR>
<P>
<CENTER><STRONG><FONT COLOR=#808000 FACE="Arial">Material Summary Fact Sheet</FONT></STRONG><FONT FACE="Arial"> 
<BR>
</FONT></CENTER>
<P>
<B><FONT FACE="Arial">The DAIS 585 Membrane:<BR>
</FONT></B>
<P>
<FONT FACE="Arial">The DAIS 585 is a sulfonated styrene/ethylene-butylene/styrene triblock copolymer. The material is an elastic, hydrocarbon-based hydrogel with cation exchange ability, and good optical transparency. <BR>
</FONT>
<P>
<FONT FACE="Arial">The block copolymer contains approximately 29wt% styrene units. Sulfonation levels (based on styrene units) range from ca.15-60%. Water uptake and extent of swelling depend on the sulfonation level (for more information on this please <A HREF="http://www.eng.rpi.edu:80/dept/chem-eng/Biotech-Environ/URP/URP.html">click here.</A>) 
<BR>
</FONT>
<P>
<FONT FACE="Arial">Cast films in the acid (H+) forms have ionic conductivity's (when fully hydrated) of greater than 9 x 10-2 S/cm, which are comparable to those of the fluoropolymer Nafion<SUP>&reg;</SUP>. Numerous derivatives with different cations, including many that are redox-active, are readily prepared from the parent polymer. <BR>
</FONT>
<P>
<FONT FACE="Arial">The material is available at sulfonation levels of 55%-60% as a pre-cast reinforced film (thickness' ca. 50 - 200 micron) or as a solution in alcohol's (either 5% or 10% solids). Casting instructions are provided.<BR>
</FONT>
<P>
<B><FONT FACE="Arial">The DAIS POG (</FONT><FONT SIZE=2 FACE="Arial">Membrane Electrode Assembly</FONT><FONT FACE="Arial">):
<BR>
</FONT></B>
<P>
<FONT FACE="Arial">The patent pending DAIS POG (Membrane/Electrode Assembly) contains ca. .5mg cm<SUP>2</SUP> of platinum, and has a 1/8&quot; shorter anode than cathode. This 'shorter' anode will accommodate many different sealing schemes. The MEA is flexible by construction. Each MEA is ca. 45/1000<SUP>th</SUP> inch thick. </P></FONT>
