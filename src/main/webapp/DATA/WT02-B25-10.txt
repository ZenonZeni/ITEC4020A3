<title>NetFortress - Specifications and Features</title>

<body background="grid.gif" bgcolor="#ffffff" text="#eeeeee" link="#ff0000" vlink="#ff9900">
<br>


<font size=+2 color=#ff2222>Features:</font><br><br>
<font size=+1>
The NetFortress is the only network security device to combine these
powerful features into a plug and play solution:
</font>
<p>

<ul>
<li><font size=+1 color=#ff2222><em>Impenetrable Physical Barrier Between Network and Computer: </em></font><br>
<font size=+1>
All communication must pass through the NetFortress, making it inaccessible to hackers.
</font>
<br>
<br>

<li><font size=+1 color=#ff2222><em>Network-layer Encryption: </em></font><br>
<font size=+1>
Encrypts the entire application layer and
transport layer headers, and modifies
portions of the network layer's header, leaving no useful information
exposed for the hacker.
</font>
<br>
<br>
	
<li><font size=+1 color=#ff2222><em>Automated Authentication: </em></font><br>
<font size=+1>
Communication is automatically authenticated at
both ends of the transmission eliminating the possibility of human error in
the verification process.
</font>
<br>
<br>

<li><font size=+1 color=#ff2222><em>High Speed Data Throughput: </em></font><br>
<font size=+1>
1.18mbs - 1.72mbs
</font>
<br>
<br>

</ul>

<table border=0 cellspacing=0 cellpadding=0>
<tr>
<td>
<ul>
<li><font size=+1 color=#ff2222><em>Automated Key Exchange: </em></font><br>
<font size=+1>
Public-key/private-key hierarchy that does not
require elaborate human intervention or user knowledge that could endanger
security. Encryption keys are automatically exchanged every 24 hours to
further protect against intrusions.
</font>
</ul>
</td>
<td><img valign=center src="expc.gif"></td>
</tr>
</table>

<ul>
<li><font size=+1 color=#ff2222><em>Self-configuring: </em></font><br>
<font size=+1>
Automatically finds and locks onto host or server's
IP/Ethernet address.  Appropriate for all Internet hosts, PCs, high-end
work stations, servers, and mainframes.
</font>		
<br>
<br>

<li><font size=+1 color=#ff2222><em>Hardware/Operating System Compatibility: </em></font><br>
<font size=+1>
Compatible with all hardware platforms and operating systems.
</font>
<br>
<br>

<li><font size=+1 color=#ff2222><em>Invisible Protection: </em></font><br>
<font size=+1>
Transparent to both users and erstwhile intruders.
</font>
<br>
<br>

<li><font size=+1 color=#ff2222><em>Firewall Compatibility: </em></font><br>
<font size=+1>
The NetFortress can be used to complement
virtually any existing firewall to increase
its effectiveness.
</font>
<br>
<br>

<li><font size=+1 color=#ff2222><em>Simple and Inexpensive to Install and Maintain: </em></font><br>
<font size=+1>
Compact, self-contained, and maintenance-free plug and play box priced at $4995 per unit.
</font>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<font size=+2 color=#ff2222>Specifications: </font><br><br>
<font size=+1>
The NetFortress measures 14" x 14" x 3" (19" rack mounts are also
available).  It operates at 120 VAC.  Connectors are BNC, 10-Base-T.  It's
capacity is 2048 simultaneous remotes.
<p>
The NetFortress can be configured or customized by DSN for specific corporate
or industry applications.
</font>
