Challenge</h1>

<p align="center"><img src="pvcrace.gif" width="743" height="176" align="bottom" >
<h6 align="center">During an Acceleration Run in the Rain at the 1996 Propane Vehicle Challenge</h6>

<p align="center">The 1997 Propane Vehicle Challenge (PVC) is a Student Competition between 24 United States
and Canadian Universities and will be held in Texas during the last part of April in 1997. The
competition is sponsored by Chrysler-Canada, the Texas Railroad Commission the United States
Department of Energy, Canadian Department of Natural Resources and Argonne National
Laboratory.  

<p align="center">The 1997 PVC participants will be competing in two classes, the Chrysler Minivan Class and the
new Dodge Dakota Class.
