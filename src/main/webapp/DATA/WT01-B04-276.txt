<TITLE>More About Style Advisor</TITLE></HEAD>
<BODY><IMG SRC="logo.gif" ALIGN="BOTTOM">
<P>Since it's debut in 1993, Style Advisor has been the style analysis package
of choice for the large institutional market place. Our Client List has grown to
include over 140 plan sponsors, consultants, and money managers. Below are a few
of the reasons why.</P>
<H3>Money Managers, Plan Sponsors and Consultants use StyleADVISOR for:<BR></H3>
<H4>Style and Attribution Analysis of Individual Managers and Total Funds</H4>
<UL>
<LI>Analyze the style of any manager. Select from over 6000 domestic equity,
international equity and fixed income managers or from over 7000 mutual funds.</LI>
<LI>Graphically display a manager's style.  Show how consistent they have been
and/or how their style has changed over time.</LI>
<LI>Build customized style benchmarks (normal portfolios) for any manager.</LI>
<LI>Display the manager's skill in stock selection, even if the manager's style
is out of favor.</LI></UL>
<H4>Performance Analysis and Reporting</H4>
<UL>
<LI>Construct peer group universes. Display a managers relative performance
using the risk/return scatter diagram or Style Advisor's  unique &quot;Manager
versus Universe&quot; comparison graph.</LI>
<LI>Prepare high quality, multi-color presentation materials.</LI></UL>
<H4>Product Analysis</H4>
<UL>
<LI>Money managers compare and contrast their products with those of their
competitors to determine strengths and weaknesses.</LI>
<LI>Plan Sponsors and Consultants compare the performance and style of multiple
managers in a single graph or table against their policy benchmark or a peer
group universe.</LI></UL>
<H4>Manager Searches</H4>
<UL>
<LI>Search for domestic equity, international equity, fixed income, global bond
or emerging market managers.  You determine the criteria - let StyleADVISOR do
the rest.</LI></UL>
