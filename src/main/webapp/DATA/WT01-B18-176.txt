<H2><I>A. </I>Energy-Efficient Design Is Demonstrating Dramatic Energy 
Savings</H2>
<BR>
Energy decisions made when constructing buildings are long-lasting. The 
median lifetime for commercial buildings is between 50 and 70 years, far 
longer than most power plants.<a href="#144" name="r144"> [ 144 ] </a>The potential for savings is high. A 
reasonably-attainable 30 percent improvement in U.S. building efficiency 
would reduce energy bills by $75 billion annually in 15 years.<a href="#145" name="r145"> [ 145 ] </a>
