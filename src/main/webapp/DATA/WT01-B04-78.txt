                    <p><font color="#000000" size="5"><strong><u>BACKGROUND</u></strong></font></p>
                </blockquote>

<blockquote>
    <blockquote>
        <blockquote>
            <blockquote>
                <blockquote>
                    <p><font color="#000000">The Persistent
                    famine situation in Ethiopia coupled with
                    civil war and economic stagnation has been
                    very devastating.</font></p>
                    <p><font color="#000000">Children who were
                    the most vulnerable group become easy victim
                    of various diseases, malnutrition, death and
                    untold misery and suffering.</font></p>
                    <p><font color="#000000">Alem Children
                    Support Organization was established as an
                    indigenous and humanitarian NGO, to
                    contribute its share towards alleviating the
                    plight of the children in the country.</font></p>
                    <p><font color="#000000">ASCO was founded by
                    Ethiopian married couple and was registered
                    with the Ethiopian government in 1994 to
                    undertake child focused rehabilitation and
                    development programs.</font></p>
                    <p><font color="#000000">The organization is
                    superintended by a board of directors whose
                    membership is drawn from government and
                    non-government organizations. I is managed by
                    Mrs. Alemnesh MOgessie with the help of small
                    but diligent staff. There are now a couple of
                    professional people who have joined the
                    organization as paid staff.</font></p>
                </blockquote>
            </blockquote>
        </blockquote>
    </blockquote>
</blockquote>

<p align="center"><font color="#000000" size="4"><strong><u>HIGHLIGHTS
OF ACTIVITIES</u></strong></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <blockquote>
        <blockquote>
            <blockquote>
                <blockquote>
                    <p><font color="#000000">Within the past
                    three years, efforts have been made to
                    provide basic and material support by the
                    founders and by few humanitarian individuals.</font></p>
                    <p><font color="#000000"></font>&nbsp;</p>
                </blockquote>
            </blockquote>
        </blockquote>
    </blockquote>
</blockquote>

<blockquote>
    <blockquote>
        <blockquote>
            <p><font color="#008080"><strong><u>BENEFICIARIES -
            STREET AND DESTITUTE CHILDREN</u></strong></font></p>
            <blockquote>
                <ul>
                    <li><font color="#000000">Street and
                        destitute children numbering 200 from low
                        income families of the neighborhood are
                        being supported at a drop-in center in
                        Addis Ababa with the following:</font></li>
                </ul>
            </blockquote>
            <blockquote>
                <ul>
                    <li><font color="#000000">Provision of
                        supervised informal education through the
                        day care center.</font></li>
                </ul>
            </blockquote>
            <blockquote>
                <ul>
                    <li><font color="#000000">Supplementary
                        feeding program, i.e. offering one mean a
                        day at the drop-in center through out the
                        year.</font></li>
                </ul>
            </blockquote>
            <blockquote>
                <ul>
                    <li><font color="#000000">Recreational
                        facilities.</font></li>
                </ul>
            </blockquote>
            <blockquote>
                <ul>
                    <li><font color="#000000">Health referral and
                        clothing support.</font></li>
                </ul>
            </blockquote>
            <blockquote>
                <ul>
                    <li><font color="#000000">School supplies
                        such as exercise books and pencils are
                        issued for pre-school children and a few
                        primary school children.</font></li>
                </ul>
            </blockquote>
            <blockquote>
                <ul>
                    <li><font color="#000000">Providing monthly
                        financial support to very poor families
                        through the child sponsorship program.</font></li>
                </ul>
            </blockquote>
        </blockquote>
    </blockquote>
</blockquote>

<blockquote>
    <blockquote>
        <blockquote>
            <p><font color="#008080"><strong><u>DESTITUTE MOTHERS</u></strong></font></p>
            <blockquote>
                <ul>
                    <li><font color="#000000">Provide cash loan
                        to 80 disadvantaged mothers of the
                        neighborhood to start small income
                        generating ventures.</font></li>
                    <li><font color="#000000">Assist another 60
                        poor women to earn income by involving
                        them in traditional cotton spinning
                        activity. The spun cotton will be used as
                        raw material to produce traditional
                        Ethiopian clothes. The weaving cottage
                        handicraft was set up within the compound
                        of the drop-in center as part of our
                        effort to reduce our dependence on
                        external assistance and promote self
                        reliance.</font></li>
                    <li><font color="#000000">Provision of basic
                        health education, including family
                        planning practice, child care and
                        nutrition.</font></li>
                </ul>
            </blockquote>
        </blockquote>
    </blockquote>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#000080"><strong><u>MAJOR CONSTRAINTS</u></strong></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <ul>
        <li><font color="#000080"><strong>Lack of direct access
            to internatonal donors to mobilize funds and other
            organizational support.</strong></font></li>
    </ul>
</blockquote>

<blockquote>
    <ul>
        <li><font color="#000080"><strong>Lack of transport is a
            major constraint for carrying out planned activities
            on time.</strong></font></li>
    </ul>
</blockquote>

<blockquote>
    <ul>
        <li><font color="#000080"><strong>Shortage of qualified
            staff, office supplies and equipment.</strong></font></li>
    </ul>
</blockquote>

<blockquote>
    <ul>
        <li><font color="#000080"><strong>Frequent changes of
            government structure and policies</strong>.</font></li>
    </ul>
</blockquote>
