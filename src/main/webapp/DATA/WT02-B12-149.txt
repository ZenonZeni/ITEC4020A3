Migration Dialogue promotes an informed discussion of the issues associated with

international migration by providing unbiased and timely information on immigration and

integration issues.  Four Migration Dialogue activities are summarized below:  the

newsletters Migration News and Rural Migration News, the work of University of

California researchers, and seminars for European and American opinion leaders.<p>
