<CENTER><P>
<HR>The following selection can never be complete. Most of the photos are
from snapshots, so the quality is often poor. <BR>
<BR>
<BR>
<BR>
</P></CENTER>

<LI><A HREF="admiraal.jpg">Pieter Admiraal</A> - pioneering euthanasist
in the Netherlands (now retired) and winner of the 1992 Humanist Award.
</LI>

<LI><A HREF="beloff.jpg">John Beloff</A> - academic psychologist and former
chairman of VESS (Scotland). </LI>

<LI><A HREF="collbwl.jpg">Buji Collabawalla</A> - medical doctor and President
of the Right to Die Society of India. </LI>

<LI><A HREF="l_crack.jpg">Lynda Cracknell</A> - president of the Northern&nbsp;Territory
(of Australia) euthanasia society, formed to support the world's first
euthanasia law, the Rights of the Terminally Ill Act.</LI>

<LI><A HREF="dghs-cgd.jpg">Chris Docker</A> (Scotland - VESS), with members
of the German society (DGHS), researching <I><A HREF="dd.html">Departing
Drugs.</A></I> </LI>

<LI><A HREF="goodheir.jpg">Phyllis Goodheir</A> with her husband Albert
- major independent distributor and campaigner in Scotland for living wills.
</LI>

<LI><A HREF="haggart.jpg">Alastair Haggart</A> - retired bishop and theologian,
and Convener of VESS (Scotland). </LI>

<LI><A HREF="dh.jpg">Derek Humphry</A> - journalist, and early campaigner
for the movement in the USA. </LI>

<LI>Representatives of the <A HREF="israel.jpg">Israel</A> Right to Die
Society. </LI>

<LI><A HREF="kennedy.jpg">Ludovic Kennedy</A>- well-known writer and broadcaster
and President of the English VES. </LI>

<LI><A HREF="kevorkia.jpg">Jack Kevorkian</A> - renowned American doctor
who has assisted many suicides. </LI>

<LI><A HREF="kuhse.jpg">Helga Kuhse</A> - philosopher working in bioethics,
especially in the area of death &amp; dying. </LI>

<LI><A HREF="little.jpg">Sheila Little</A> (deceased) - founder of the
Scottish Society (VESS). </LI>

<LI><A HREF="macardle.jpg">Meredith MacArdle</A> - editor and press officer
for the English society. </LI>

<LI><A HREF="marchig.jpg">Jeanne Marchig</A> - leader of the Swiss society
(ADMD). </LI>

<LI><A HREF="nitschke.jpg">Philip Nitschke</A> - first Australian doctor
to implement the NT euthanasia law.</LI>

<LI><A HREF="perron.jpg">Marshall Perron</A> - parliamentarian who launched
Australia's NT&nbsp;euthanasia law.</LI>

<LI><A HREF="rookhuiz.jpg">Marion Rookhuizen</A> of the Dutch society (NVVE).
</LI>

<LI><A HREF="cks.jpg">Cheryl Smith</A> - lawyer and VESS (Scotland) Research
Consultant in America. </LI>

<LI><A HREF="uribe.jpg">Clemencia Uribe</A> - nurse and representative
of the Colombian society. <BR>
