"Social Sciences in Forestry" is an annotated bibliography distributed by the Forestry Library, College of Natural Resources, University of Minnesota, St. Paul, MN.<P>

The hierarchy implemented here is of the form Whole Database --> <A HREF="subject.html">Subject Areas (43 areas)</A> --> Publication Issues --> Citations. You can browse a subject area for a particular issue or do full text searching for the database as a whole. At present, 48 issues (March 1985 - December 1996) have been put on-line (30880 citations). Note that the subject area name and number are part of each citation so that either may be included in search specifications.<P>

Loans or xerox copies of publications indexed in Social Sciences in Forestry are available from the Interlibrary Loan Division, St. Paul Campus Libraries, 1984 Buford Avenue, St. Paul, MN 55108. FAX (612) 624-9245. USDA employees, please use Standard Form AD-245 in submitting your requests. Others, please submit requests on the standard interlibrary loan forms through your nearest library.<P>

This bibliography will be updated quarterly on line.<P>
