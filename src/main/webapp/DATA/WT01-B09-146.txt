<h3>Southern Coalition for Advanced Transportation (SCAT)</h3><p>

<blockquote>
<B>Project Title:  Station Cars for Dade County Metro<p>
Performing Organization:  Florida Power and Light, Metro­Date Transit Agency</B><p>

<P>POC: Bob Suggs, Telephone (305) 522­4133</P>

<P><B>Program Summary: </B></P> 


<P>Ten EV station cars will be leased and used at selected station along Dade County s Metro­Rail
System for use as the flexible portion of the commuter s trip and throughout the day. Supporting
infrastructure needs will be researched and implemented Training and O & M manuals will be provided.</P>

<P><B>Benefit: </B></P><P>Rapidly growing fleets of station cars, adjacent to a large number of mass transit stations will
allow transit to become a true­door­to­door transportation service for any type of trip.
</P>
<P><B>SCHEDULE:</B></P>

<P>Ten vehicles have been received.</P>

<P>Target day for start­up is April 15, 1996. </P>

</P></blockquote>
