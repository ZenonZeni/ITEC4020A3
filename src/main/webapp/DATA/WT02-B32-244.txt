<H3>POLITICS (PO)</H3>

<B>Asian Political News--PO03</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/po03.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Kyodo News International Inc.<BR>
$2.00/article<BR>
Updated weekly, from 5/29/89<BR>
(Also in IT, Kyodo)<BR>
Offers news on the major players in the Far East, including South Korea, China,
Taiwan, Thailand, the Philippines, Hong Kong, Singapore, and Malaysia. Political
unrest as well as political evolvement of ideologies is covered.<P>

<B>Evans-Novak Political Report--PO06</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/po06.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Phillips Business Information Inc.<BR>
$3.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Available from 11/19/91--6/28/94<BR>
(Also in Phillips)<BR>
Now in its 25th year, this newsletter provides an unparalleled look into the world
of politics. Acclaimed for its hard-hitting analysis and its uncanny predictions
on the outcome of political campaigns.<P>

<B>The Hotline--PO01</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/po01.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
American Political Network<BR>
$3.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Updated daily, retained one year plus current year<BR>
(Also in Day, GT, WashDC)<BR>
Provides timely information and insights into the American political scene,
compiled from contacts in government, business, and academia. Twenty pages each
day provide insider commentary, digests of major news reports, and other
comprehensive information on political activity in the U.S.<P>

<B>Japan Policy and Politics--PO04</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/po04.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Kyodo News International Inc.<BR>
$2.00/article<BR>
Updated weekly, from 5/29/89<BR>
(Also in IT, Japan, Kyodo, PacRim)<BR>
Reports on policy-making decisions, new political appointees, and various
political parties within Japan. Includes a section on law and legal decisions made
by the Ministry of Justice, the Tokyo Supreme Court, and city courts.<P>

<B>The Political Finance & Lobby Reporter--PO02</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/po02.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Amward Publications Inc.<BR>
$1.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 6/2/82<BR>
(Also in GT)<BR>
Includes the latest available lobby and foreign agent registrations from Congress
and the Department of Justice as well as the newest political action committees
(PACs) at the Federal Election Commission.<P>
