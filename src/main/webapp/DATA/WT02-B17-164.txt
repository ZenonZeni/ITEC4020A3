<CENTER><H2>FY 1995-FY 1996 Biomass Power Program Accomplishments</H2></CENTER>
<P>
<STRONG>Promoted Commercialization of Biomass Power Through Joint Ventures</STRONG>
<UL>

<P>
<LI>Issued a solicitation for a 5-year, $80 million program to demonstrate Biomass Power for Rural Development.
</UL><P>
<CENTER><H2>FY 1993-FY 1994 Biomass Power Program Accomplishments</H2></CENTER>
<P>
<STRONG>Promoted Commercialization of Biomass Power Through Joint Ventures</STRONG>
<P>
<UL>
<LI>Dedicated an industrial-scale biomass gasification facility.
<P>
<LI>Tested a hot-gas cleanup system with gasified biomass.
<P>
<LI>Supported 10 feasibility studies for integrating feedstock supply with biomass power facility  development.
</UL>
<P>
<STRONG>Improved Today's Technology Through Partnerships with Industry and Power Producers</STRONG>
<P>
<UL>
<LI>Conducted cofiring studies and tests with the Tennessee Valley
Authority.
<P>
<LI>Worked with biomass power plant operators, industry, and
researchers to characterize the chemical and physical mechanisms
of alkali deposits in boilers.
<P>
<LI>Supported development of direct-fired gas turbine operating
on wood and coal.
<P>
<LI>Developed the Advanced Transportable Molecular Beam Mass
Spectrometer to bring analytical laboratory capabilities to biomass
power plants developed by the Biomass Power Program.
</UL>
<P>
<STRONG>Developed New Technologies</STRONG>
<P>
<UL>
<LI>Operated the world's first system using biogas from a gasifier
to fuel a natural gas turbine-generator.
<P>
<LI>Produced and tested "biocrude" oil for combustion
properties.
</UL>
<P>
<STRONG>Reduced Technical and Nontechnical Barriers to Commercialization</STRONG>
<P>
<UL>
<LI>Supported efforts of the National Biofuels Roundtable to
develop principles and guidelines for biomass energy systems.
<P>
<LI>Sponsored the First Biomass Conference of the Americas and
made plans for the second.
<P>
<LI>Supported work of the Utility Biomass Energy Commercialization
Association.
</UL>
