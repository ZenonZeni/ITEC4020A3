<BLOCKQUOTE>


<IMG SRC="gifs/bestofkemper_head.gif" ALT="Best of Kemper.com"><BR>
At the risk of seeming immodest, we hope you'll make a special effort to visit what we consider the best of Kemper.com.
<P>

<A HREF="../../holiday/holiday.html"><IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/holiday.gif"></A><BR>
<A HREF="../../holiday/holiday.html">Holiday Greetings!</A> We review the many gifts of 1996.

<P>

<A HREF="../../curious/photoalbum/photoalbum.html"><IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/photoalbum.gif"></A><BR>
What does a happy retiree look like? You'll see when you <A HREF="../../curious/photoalbum/photoalbum.html">scroll through our photo album.</A> Are you retired? We have a page just waiting for you!

<P>

<A HREF="../../curious/compareindices.html"><IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/mktindices.gif"></A><BR>
How does the stock market perform when the bond market is up -- and vice versa? To aid your understanding of how markets have performed, Kemper.com enables you to <A HREF="../../curious/compareindices.html">create and print your own graph</A> comparing the long-term performance of 12 stock and bond market indices.

<P>

<A HREF="../../funds/fundsvsindices.html"><IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/fundsvsindices.gif"></A><BR>
And how do the Kemper Funds' perform against the leading market indices? You can <A HREF="../../funds/fundsvsindices.html">create and print your own graphical comparison</A>.

<P>

<A HREF="../../curious/howmarkets.html"><IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/howmarketsreact.gif"></A><BR>
It's in the daily news but what is the significance of the monthly announcement of the country's employment situation? This page provides <A HREF="../../curious/howmarkets.html">a graphical look of how the markets react</A> to the monthly unemployment report. For more information about one of several economic measures, click on the "gear."

<P>

<A HREF="../../curious/readyretire/readyretire.html"><IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/readyretire.gif"></A><BR>
Are you ready to retire? Our <A HREF="../../curious/readyretire/readyretire.html">interactive test</A> will analyze your retirement readiness.

<P>

<A HREF="../friends.html"><IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/friends.gif"></A><BR>
While you're here, be sure to meet our <A HREF="../friends.html">Friends And Neighbors</A>. You'll <A HREF="../teamkemper/teamkemper.html">meet the professional golfers</A> who wear the Kemper Funds name (an animated arrow will show you where), learn about <I><A HREF="../intheprime/intheprime.html">In the Prime,</A></I> the public television series sponsored by Kemper Funds Group -- you can even view our <A HREF="../logowear/logowear.html">Kemper Funds Logo Wear Catalog</A>.
