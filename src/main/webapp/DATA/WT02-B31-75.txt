<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>Developing Our Talent</STRONG></FONT><P>

How far you go at New York Life depends on your own talent, initiative, and drive. We'll provide the
opportunity.<P>
New York Life offers its employees the resources to meet the challenges that lie ahead. As we broaden
the scope of our financial services, it is vital for our employees to keep ahead of the industry, hone their
skills, and grow personally as well as professionally.<P>
The primary means of career development is on-the-job training. We expect our employees not only to
master their individual responsibilities, but also to gain a sense of how their operations fit into the
larger picture. Teamwork at New York Life does not stop within any department.<P>
Job experience is supplemented by in-house professional development programs. We offer a variety of
courses such as effective presentation skills, time management, supervisory skills and product
awareness. Using a combination of techniques -- ranging from instruction-led training programs to
self-study programs -- we give employees the opportunity to learn at their own pace with minimal
interference to their work and personal lives.<P>
New York Life also encourages employees to advance their education ouside of Company programs.
Tuitition assistance is available for those pursuing degrees in business-related majors.
