<p align="left"><b>SUZY JOHNSON Medical Coordinator</b> <br>
ext. 109 Coordinates inseminations &amp; IVF procedures; serves

resource person for medical information. </p>

<p align="left"><b>JEANNE VLIETSTRA Administrator</b> <br>
ext. 106 Oversees surrogate mother documentation; serves as
liaison between surrogate mothers, CSP staff and counselors. </p>

<p align="left"><b>SANDI GREENBERG Trust Administrator</b><br>
ext. 104 Administers client funds, processes surrogate mother
expense reimbursements, oversees payment of medical expenses,
insurance premiums, etc. </p>

<p align="left"><b>NORMA CITRON Program Coordinator</b><br>
ext. 102 Facilitates initial surrogate mother screening;
responsible for data base management, gathering and processing
statistical information; assists in marketing &amp; advertising
coordination. Prepares and oversees client documentation &amp;
requirements. </p>

<p align="left"><b>CAROL DUCKWORTH Database Manager</b><br>
ext. 107 Responsible for managing the egg owner database on the
internet and assists recipients in accessing the online database.
Designs and builds applications for CSP databases and Internet
homepages. Maintains all CSP computer systems.</p>

<p align="left"><b>LYNE MACKLIN Egg Donor Administrator</b><br>
ext. 118 Assists egg donor and recipients in their progress
through the program. Coordinates documentation sent to donors and
recipients and serves as liaison between egg donor, CSP,
recipient couple, counselor, and doctor's office.</p>
