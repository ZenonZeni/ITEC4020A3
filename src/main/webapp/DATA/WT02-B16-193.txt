<p><strong>Systems Engineering and Applications</strong>
<P>
To help industry improve the performance, reliability, and operational characteristics of its
products, we test and evaluate the material, mechanical, electrical, and safety characteristics of
cells, modules, BOS components, and complete systems.  Also, to help products become
accepted in potential markets, we work to help industry and standards groups develop national
and international safety and reliability standards for PV products. 
<P>
<a href="syseng.html">Tell me more about work in Systems Engineering!</a>
<P>
To validate prototype PV technologies, we deploy systems in stand-alone and utility-tied
applications and measure and analyze their performance. These projects help demonstrate the
most cost-effective PV applications for key markets. For domestic applications, we work closely
with groups of users&#151;such as the National Parks Service, Forest Service, military agencies, and
utilities&#151;who represent large markets for PV systems.
