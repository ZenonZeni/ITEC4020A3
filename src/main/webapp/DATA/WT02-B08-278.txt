This Virtual Picture Book contains illustrations that were created in the
JPL Ocean Science Element in relation with the TOPEX/POSEIDON project. The images
were created under the scientific guidance of Dr. Lee-Lueng Fu by Denis P. 
Leconte, with TOPEX/POSEIDON data prepared mostly by Greg Pihos, but also by
other people where specified.<P>

Please note that the TOPEX/POSEIDON data available from the JPL PO-DAAC is not
image data. <P>
