<H2>What Are The Benifits Of EANx ?</H2>
<UL><P>EANx diving has many advantages over air diving.  Some of the benefits are:
<OL><LI>EANx when used with standard dive tables or computers, provides a tremendous safety factor.  In fact, when utilized in this fashion the actual nitrogen accumulation is that for a dive 10 to 20 ft shallower.  This application is actually ideal for divers who simply wish to be more  conservative,  those individuals who are not as young as they once were or those who may not be in the peak of physical fitness.
<LI>A significant increase in bottom time no decompression limits.
<LI>A reduction of the possibility of decompression sickness.
<LI>A slight reduction in nitrogen narcosis.
<LI>A reduction of decompression time if the no decompression limits are exceeded.
<LI>Reduced fatigue after the dive (declared by many EANx divers)
</OL></UL>
