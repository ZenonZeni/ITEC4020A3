<CENTER><H2>System</H2></CENTER>
<P>The DIGIAC 3000 system is a computer based training system with classroom management and computer aided instruction that meets the needs of vocational training in the field of electronics
<BR>

<CENTER><H2>Computer Managed Instruction (CMI)</H2></CENTER>
<P>DIGIAC 3000 Training Manager provides a wealth of facilities for the student, instructor and administrator.  Individual student and overall class reports provide valuable data to the administrator.


<CENTER><H2>Training Manager</H2></CENTER>
<P>Training Manager is a suite of specially written software packages that provide an impressive range of computer based classroom management facilities.
<BR>


<CENTER><H2>Computer Aided Instruction (CAI)</H2></CENTER>
<P>The computer aided instruction operating mode of the DIGIAC 3000 system offers a fully interactive learning environment.  All the course material, including the assessment questions, is presented on the computer screen.
<BR>


<CENTER><H2>The Base Unit</H2></CENTER>
<P>The rugged, durable base unit is designed to accept circuit boards from the Technical Library.  Students take readings and record results using standard laboratory test equipment.
<BR>


<CENTER><H2>The Technical Library</H2></CENTER>
<P>A study module consists of a circuit board and comprehensive laboratory manual, supplied in a durable case.  The Technical Library has been divided into topic groups, covering the field of core electronics.
<CENTER><H2>Modules Available</H2></CENTER>
Topic Group 1: Fundamentals of Electricity/Electronics:<UL>
