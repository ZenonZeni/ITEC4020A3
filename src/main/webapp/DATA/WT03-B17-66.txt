<H2>CAVE DIVING</H2>
<P><B>Training:</B>
<UL><P>New for this year, along with the introduction to <A HREF="../PLACES/cave.html">cave diving expeditions</A> outlined in our brochure we are offering apprentice and full cave diver courses.  These courses will cover more advanced cave penetration techniques, use of double tanks and technical gear, decompression diving and cave surveying.  The price structure for the two courses taken together remains the same as for the introduction to cave diving expeditions.<P>
