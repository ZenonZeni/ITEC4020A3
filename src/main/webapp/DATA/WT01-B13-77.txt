The economy provides an environment, but the markets are where
investments perform. Securities are bought and sold in many types and
sizes of markets. There are money markets for short-term investors, U.S.
and foreign markets for equities investors and a wide range of U.S. and
foreign government and corporate markets for income investors. Any
number of variables - corporate earnings, government stability,
interest rate movements, just to name a few - influence these markets
and, by extension, your success as an investor.
