<font size=+2>
Cycle Oregon IX
</font>
<p>
Cycle Oregon is a seven-day, non-competitive bicycle tour that explores a different region of rural Oregon each year.  Two thousand cyclists visit several overnight communities with a full complement of services and amenities along the way.
<Pre>

</pre>
<font size=+2>
Registration
</font>
<p>
The registration fee is $435 per person ($870 for tandem rriders) and the field is limited to the first 2,000 riders whose registration forms are received in the Cycle Oregon office with a postmark of April 1 or later.  To request a registration brochure and application, or to express your interest in volunteering for Cycle Oregon, call 503-643-8064 or 800-292-5367.
