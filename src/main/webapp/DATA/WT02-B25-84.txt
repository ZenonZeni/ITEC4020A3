<p align=center><font color="#FF0000">SCHIZOPHRENIA</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Schizophrenia is a severe mental illness that is complex, puzzling, chronic and disabling.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">The most striking symptoms of schizophrenia are referred to as &quot;psychotic,&quot; meaning out of 
touch with reality, or inability to separate real from unreal experiences.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Some people have only one psychotic episode; others have many episodes during a lifetime 
and lead relatively normal lives during the interim periods.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">An individual with chronic schizophrenia often doesn&#146;t fully recover normal functioning and 
typically requires long-term treatment, generally including medication to control the 
symptoms. Some people may never be able to function without assistance.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Approximately 2 million Americans develop schizophrenia during any given year. Men and 
women are affected with equal frequency.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">The first episodes are often seen in the teens or twenties in men and in the twenties or early 
thirties in women. </font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Schizophrenia is believed to have a genetic basis. The children of a person with 
schizophrenia have about a 10 percent chance of developing schizophrenia. By comparison, 
the risk of schizophrenia in the general population is about 1 percent.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">SCHIZOPHRENIA IS NOT &quot;SPLIT-PERSONALITY.&quot; There is a common misconception 
that schizophrenia is multiple personalities or a Dr. Jekyll-Mr. Hyde syndrome. This is NOT 
accurate. In fact, multiple personality disorder is an entirely different disorder.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">People with schizophrenia may experience:</font></p>
<blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; unusual realities</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; hallucinations</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; delusions</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; disordered thinking</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; unusual emotional expression</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; social withdrawal</font></p>
</blockquote>
