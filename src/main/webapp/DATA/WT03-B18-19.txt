<p><font size="3">Research in this field focuses on the following
areas: </font></p>

<ul type="circle">
    <li><font size="3">automatic shape recognition </font></li>
    <li><font size="3">synthesis of linear and non-linear models </font></li>
    <li><font size="3">processing of data affected by noise of
        various kinds. </font></li>
</ul>

<p><font size="3">Acvitivies carried out include: </font></p>

<ul>
    <li><font size="3">Classification/evaluation of non-Gaussian
        signals immersed in Gaussian coloured noise </font></li>
    <li><font size="3">Harmonic decomposition methodologies:
        Prony; Pisarenko; Music; Esprit; with extensions to
        multi-dimensional systems and array processing </font></li>
    <li><font size="3">Image classification methods based on
        logarithmic-polar transformations </font></li>
    <li><font size="3">Methodologies for the attribution of
        separate levels of stationarity to each component of the
        spectral density. </font></li>
</ul>
