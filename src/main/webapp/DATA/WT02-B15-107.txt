This is a form for registering for the EREN Users Group mailing list.  Users
Group members are sent e-mail messages about new resources on EREN on a weekly
basis.<p>

To register here, your Web browser must support forms.  If it does not, please
send an e-mail message to webmaster.eren@nrel.gov to register.  Please include
your complete e-mail address so that we may e-mail messages to you.<p>

The information you provide will help us develop EREN to better serve your
needs, and is only for use by developers of EREN.
