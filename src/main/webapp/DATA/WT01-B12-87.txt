<BLOCKQUOTE>
 
<IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/aboutkemper_head.gif" ALT="About Kemper Funds">
<BR>
Kemper Funds Group is a unit of 
Zurich Kemper Investments, Inc. (ZKI). ZKI is
the investment advisor to the Kemper Funds. Kemper Funds consists of 36
open-end funds with more than 70 portfolios - including international and global funds, capital
growth funds, capital growth and income funds, target equity funds,
tax-free income funds, and income funds - and seven closed-end funds. 
<P>

Kemper Funds has nearly 3 million shareholder accounts and approximately 
$45 billion in assets. ZKI is part of Zurich Insurance Group, a global organization
headquartered in Zurich, Switzerland that provides services in life and
non-life insurance, reinsurance and asset management in more than 45
countries.
<P><BR>
