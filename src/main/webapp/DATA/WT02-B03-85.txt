<H2> Why do I have a page here?</H2>
<DT> I received my Ph.D. at GSO, and my dissertation investigated the
pathways of transport and exchange of waters in the North Atlantic Current.
As part of this work, I constructed a
high-resolution neutral-surface climatology of
the North Atlantic Current region.  It is publicly available at
<A HREF="nac.html"> the NAC climatology page </A>.

<p>
I'm currently working at the National Data Buoy Center in Mississippi,
feel free 
<A HREF="mailto:ekearns@tsc.ndbc.noaa.gov"> to contact me there </A>.
</UL>

<p>
<A HREF="/danny.gif"> Want to see my son Danny? </A> There will be more
pictures soon at <A HREF="http://datasync.com/~kearns/index.html">
my personal home page </A>.
