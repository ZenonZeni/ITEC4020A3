<FONT SIZE="+1"><STRONG>Technologies</STRONG></FONT><P>

In MainStay's full service retirement programs, state-of-the-art technological
advances are used to ensure that your plan
meets your needs. Our goal is for you to use as few of your own resources as
possible in the ongoing administration of
the plan. Depending on the program you select, and the requirements of the Internal
Revenue Service, one or more of the
following may be included to facilitate a cost-effective, high quality plan:
<P>
<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>Voice Response System that can handle the majority of participant
inquiries
	<LI>Plan Installation Service to simplify the transition into a new plan, and
facilitate compliance with appropriate laws and regulations.
	<LI>Plan Manager software, a PC-based program that organizes, verifies and
transmits valuable payroll data. This
exciting 401(k) advancement also produces personalized plan forms to facilitate the
timely and accurate processing of
participant requests
	<LI>Daily valuation which updates and reconciles plan and individual
participant accounts daily
	<LI>Quarterly statements mailed within 10 business days of the quarter ending
date
	<LI>Slide shows and videos to assist in the clear communication of 401(k)
plan provisions
	<LI>Customized enrollment materials with your company's name imprinted on
each kit
	<LI>A personalized savings plan analysis for each eligible employee.
</UL>
<P>
MainStay believes technology plays an integral part in your plan's success, and the
satisfaction you have with the
administration of the plan. Our goal is to continually enhance the services we
offer, thereby enhancing the plan you have
established for your employees.
