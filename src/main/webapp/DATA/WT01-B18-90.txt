<center><h1>Welcome to the home pages of the<br>
Association of the British Pharmaceutical Industry (ABPI)</center></h1>

<p>

The ABPI is the trade association for more than a hundred companies in Britain producing prescription medicines.  Its member companies research, develop, manufacture and supply more than 90 per cent of the medicines prescribed through the UK's National Health Service (NHS).

<p>

The pharmaceutical industry carries out almost a third of all industrial research and development in the United Kingdom, spending more than £4 million a day, and is a major earner for Britain, with an annual trade surplus of just over £1,700 million.
