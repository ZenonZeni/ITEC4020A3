<P><FONT COLOR="#000000"><FONT SIZE=+1>Special knowledge is supplied to
companies by PrizzTech in: </FONT></FONT></P>

<UL>
<LI><B><FONT COLOR="#000000">EU-technology programs as helping on international
relations, as an adviser, filling the applications for EU-funds, as an

<LI><B><FONT COLOR="#000000">development of regional business projects
with other organisations</FONT></B></LI>

<LI><B><FONT COLOR="#000000">getting new companies started; new businesses
will be helped on problems with founding of the company</FONT></B></LI>

<LI><B><FONT COLOR="#000000">Research and Development (R&amp;D), product
development projects and technology transfer</FONT></B></LI>
</UL>

<P><FONT COLOR="#000000"><FONT SIZE=+1>Examples of development projects
which PrizzTech is involved in: </FONT></FONT></P>

<P><B><FONT COLOR="#000000">KanSat 2 - Get your company internationalized
in Satakunta</FONT></B></P>

<P><FONT COLOR="#000000">The aim of this project is to meet the needs of
the ever growing field of international competition for small and medium
sized companies in Satakunta. In the long run it will be guaranteed that
regional companies are competitive, for only competitive ones will succeed
internationally. Meeting of aims is followed by the growth of exportation,
technology transfers and by the number of business contacts. The practical
organisation is done by PrizzTech and the project is ordered by MTI Regional
Business Service Office.</FONT></P>

<P><B><FONT COLOR="#000000">SILK-the Development Center of Information
Technology and Business in Satakunta </FONT></B></P>

<P><FONT COLOR="#000000">SILK-project is co-ordinated by Pori School of
Technology and Economics and it aims on promoting co-operation between
computing companies, education and experts in Pori and Ulvila regions.
The goal of the SILK-project is to develop top level know how of programming
and automation technology in the region. The practical organizing is done
by PrizzTech. </FONT></P>

<P><B><FONT COLOR="#000000">Technology Incubation Service</FONT></B></P>

<P><FONT COLOR="#000000">PrizzTech provides new companies with a possibility
to start their operation in Pripoli. Incubation will supply businesses
consulting, education, contacts and R&amp;D services. Incubation also includes
help on marketing and profiling of the company. It's possible to let the
incubator to do the management and all the paper work of the company and
just concentrate on getting the business running. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT COLOR="#000000">As a part of incubation there is also SATA-STARTTI-project
which is a joint project and the participants in it are; MTI Regional Business
Service Office, Technology Development Centre TEKES in Pori, Labour Department
in Satakunta and regional business service companies. The project aims
on reducing unemployment by encouraging the unemployed to entrepreneurship.
Industrial companies are helped to get started and also young companies
are supported by this project.</FONT></P>

<P><B><FONT COLOR="#000000">PrizzNet - an international link to business
partners, markets and latest knowledge</FONT></B></P>

<P>Via PrizzNet you are able to get in touch with more than 20 Western-European
co-workers. These include both technology, science and innovation centers
and also companies specialized in technology transfer. With the help of
PrizzNet you'll meet the European business culture. The ever growing PrizzNet
is offered to you by PrizzTech.</P>

<P><B>Research and Development (R&amp;D) </B></P>

<P>The product development of companies is planned and carried out by PrizzTech.
It is specialized in technology search, which companies can use when looking
for know how of experts and new information. Innovations are made profitable
and the need and demand of information will meet with the help of PrizzTech.</P>
