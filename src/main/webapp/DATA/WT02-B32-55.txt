<b>Research Interests</b><br>
Problems of public health hazards from food borne-diseases; the
physico-chemical environment of food in respect of effects on growth and
survival of food poisoning organisms; the effect of lactic acid bacteria on the
growth and survival of pathogens and spoilage organisms in fermented foods;
food hygiene and microbiological quality assurance. <p>
<p>
<b>Teaching Acitivities</b><br>
Food microbiology including methods, specifications, food hygiene
legislation, microbiological quality assurance, food-borne diseases; effects of
processing and storage on micro-organisms in foods
