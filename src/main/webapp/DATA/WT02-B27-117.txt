<H2 ALIGN=CENTER>You care about a good life - don't you also care about
a good death? &nbsp;&nbsp;...<I><FONT SIZE=+2>We need your help</FONT></I></H2>

<P>You can assist our humanitarian work and also receive the best information
on choices for a dignified death by becoming a member/subscriber to the
Scottish Voluntary Euthanasia Society. You will receive an information
pack, our quarterly <A HREF="nl.html">journal</A>, a free living wills
<A HREF="books.html#lw">pack</A>, and (if you are over 21) the option to
apply for <A HREF="dd.html">Departing Drugs</A> (Departing Drugs is not
included in the price of membership). <B>All information is held in strictest
confidence</B> and your details will not be disclosed to any third party.
</P>

<P><I>Rates are as follows: </I>&nbsp; United Kingdom: &pound;15 standard.
Reduced: &pound;10. Gold: &pound;25. <BR>
Overseas: &pound;30 standard. Reduced: &pound;20. Gold: &pound;50. <I>Please
note: British currency only please.</I> <I><FONT SIZE=+1>T</FONT>here is
no means-testing for reduced membership. We offer this if you are on such
a very low income that to pay the full rate of &pound;15 would cause genuine
hardship. We ask you to follow your own conscience. Gold membership is
simply for those people who are able and willing to pay a little more to
help us in our work.</I> </P>

<P>Life membership is available at ten times the annual rates in all categories.
</P>

<P>You can also become: <BR>
A Supporter &pound;50 p.a.min. (Overseas &pound;100). A Benefactor &pound;100
p.a.min. (Overseas &pound;150); A Donor &pound;250 p.a.min. (Overseas &pound;300).
&nbsp; A Sponsor &pound;500 p.a.min. (Overseas &pound;550); Supporters,
Benefactors, Donors &amp; Sponsors, as well as free membership and our
quarterly Journal, receive a six-monthly bulletin &quot;Contributors Circular&quot;
and a listing of thanks in the Journal. </P>
