<h2>KEYGUIDE TO INFORMATION SOURCES ON THE POLAR 
AND COLD REGIONS</h2>

A selective guide to printed, online, CD-ROM and Internet resources relating 
to the Arctic and Antarctic regions, and to ice and snow throughout the world.  
Written by two staff members of the <a href="http://www.spri.cam.ac.uk">
Scott Polar Research Institute</a> - William Mills and Peter Speak, this book 
is due for publication by Mansell in 1997.<p>
