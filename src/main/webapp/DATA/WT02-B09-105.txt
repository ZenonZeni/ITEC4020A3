<h1><font COLOR="#0000FF">Ion-B15</font></h1>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<h2><font SIZE=+1>The Aero<b>&#183;</b>Pure Ion-B15 is one of America's most powerful
off-the-shelf commercial ionizers. It produces either positive ions or negative
ions or both--at the operator's option--and is used to remove all types
of airborne particulates or control extreme static electricity situations.</font></h2>

<p><b><font SIZE=+1>MODEL SPECIFICATIONS</font></b></p>

<p><b>FEATURES:</b> 
<br>an array of 16 needlepoint ion-producing electrodes 
<br>3000 CFM fan and sealed motor
<br>size: 28"H x 28"W x 18"D; 65 lbs. shipping wt.</p>

<p><b>ELECTRICAL:</b> 
<br>120v, 60hz operation fan and ion control rheostats</p>

<p><b>APPLICATIONS:</b></p>

<ul>
<li>service clubs, casinos and bingo parlors, convention centers, bowling
alleys, dinner theaters, night-clubs, bilges and tanks*</li>

<li>feed lots, dairy farms, rodeos, stock shows, zoos</li>

<li>any industrial process that involves airborne matter, fumes, smoke
or odors over a large floor space</li>
</ul>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<hr width=100%>

<h1><font COLOR="#0000FF">Ion-C15</font></h1>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<h2><font SIZE=+1>The Ion-C15 has the same ion capacity as the Ion-B15, but a
less powerful fan. This unit is used when ions are to be concentrated directly
on a source of particulate pollution or static electricity through the
use of mylar ducting.</font></h2>

<p><b>FEATURES:</b> 
<br>an array of 16 needlepoint io-producing electrodes 
<br>660 CFM fan and sealed motor
<br>size: 28"H x 28"W x 18"D; 65 lbs. shipping wt.</p>

<p><b>ELECTRICAL:</b> 
<br>120v, 60hz operation fan and ion control rheostats</p>

<p><b>APPLICATIONS (WHEN DUCTED):</b></p>

<ul>
<li>computer rooms</li>

<li>industrial processes which...</li>

<p>involve powder or dust 
<br>involve volatile chemicals 
<br>involve smoke 
<br>involve bad odors 
<br>create static electricity 
<br>plastic and other packaging 
<br>high-speed weaving </p>
</ul>

<p>
<br>*When combined with a Decon ozone producing machine.</p>
