<h3>Electricore</h3><p>

<blockquote>
<B>Project Title:  Electric Vehicle Mosaic Information System Development<p>

Performing Organization:  Indiana University­Purdue University at Indianapolis</B><p>

<P>Technical POC: Dr. Russell Eberhart Ph.D.</P>

<P><B>Project Summary:</B></P>

<P>Indiana University­Purdue University at Indianapolis (IUPUI) will be responsible for making
information about electric and hybrid vehicle research available to anyone connected to the Internet. The primary
area of focus would be product information but also includes electric and hybrid vehicle safety issues and Formula
Lightning electric car racing.</P>

<P>The method for distributing this information would be the World Wide Web. these pages would incorporate
descriptions, specs, and pictures, as well as contact information where appropriate. Electricore would also have
pages which would enable them to make further information available via the Internet. </P>

<P>Secondary goals include providing training for Electricore consortium members in the use of the Internet, designing a system on the World Wide Web for data collection and analysis, and identifying and implementing cost
modeling tools. </P>

<P><B>Project Deliverables:</B></P> 

<OL>
<P><LI>World Wide Web pages including:</P> 
<ul>
<LI>     Electric and hybrid vehicle product information
<LI>     Electric and hybrid vehicle safety information 
<LI>     Formula Lightning electric racing information 
</ul>

<P>

<P><LI> Internet training for Electricore consortium members</P>

<P><LI> Data collection and analysis tools 
</P>
<P><LI> Cost modeling tools</P></OL>

<P><B>Project Schedule:</B></P>
<UL>
<P><li> Being revised</P>
</UL>


</blockquote>
