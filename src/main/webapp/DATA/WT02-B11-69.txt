<CENTER><H2>Annual Reports on the Seven Major Economies of Latin America</H2></CENTER>
<P></P>
<CENTER><IMG SRC="line.gif"></CENTER> 
<P>By special arrangement with the UK publisher BMI, we offer, at a substantial
discount, 1996 Country Reports on <B>Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Colombia,
Mexico, Peru and Venezuela.</B>  These annual reports provide the very latest
market intelligence and political risk analysis, with forecasts to end-1998, on
these seven economies.</P>
<P>Running over 100 pages, each Country Report is bound in a single volume and
contains:</P>
<DL><DT><B>Forecast Summary 5 pages:</B></DT>
<DD>A sharply focused snapshot of each country's political, economic, financial
and industrial prospects through end-1998, set against global economic
assumptions.<BR></DD>
<DT><B>Political Overview 15 pages:</B></DT>
<DD>Geography and demography; the political system and legislative process; the
government and its economic and industrial policies; policy makers and key
figures; most likely alternative government and the impact on the business
environment.<BR></DD>
<DT><B>The Economy 20 pages:</B></DT>
<DD>The economic situation and background; market size, structure, trends in
major indicators; policy environment; fiscal, monetary, structural reforms;
foreign debt policy; wages and employment; exchange rate; GDP growth and
outlook.<BR></DD>
<DT><B>External Accounts 10 pages:</B></DT>
<DD>Trends in merchandise trade; trade by commodity and trading partner;
invisibles/net transfers account; forecast for current account and major
components for 1996-98.<BR></DD>
<DT><B>External Financing 10 pages:</B></DT>
<DD>Trends in foreign reserves, import cover and foreign investment; sources of
foreign financing in 1996-1998; external debt profile; projections for
1996-1998.<BR></DD>
<DT><B>The Business Environment 20 pages:</B></DT>
<DD>Trends in corporate legislation; policy towards industry, foreign
investment, foreign trade, exchange controls, price controls, labor and
taxation, financing, debt rescheduling, privatization timetable, stock market.<BR></DD>
<DT><B>Industry 20 pages:</B></DT>
<DD>Recent trends and forecasts for main sectors - agriculture; mining; energy;
utitlites and manufacturing; including food, beverages, textiles, autos,
chemicals and petrochemicals; iron and steel; construction; banking and finance;
tourism.<BR></DD></DL>
