<center><h2>Membership Information</h2></center>

RBIS plans to put together a mailing list of baseball fans in the Sacramento
area who would like to be notified of key dates and opportunities to be
involved. If you want to help with any of RBIS projects, have other ideas on
how to make a down town ballpark a reality, or just want to support the effort,
become a member of RBIS.
