<H2>Toll-free Risperdal line</H2>
<HR>

	Jannsen Pharmaceutica now has a toll-free phone line for information about Risperdal.  Consumers, families, and professionals can reach Risperdal on Call from a touch-tone telephone 24 hours a day.  After dialing 1-800-676-6225, the caller can choose from four areas of information:  1) Risperdal product information; 2) physician exchange; 3) clinical reviews; or 4) conversion and dosage.  In addition to providing the recorded information, Risperdal on Call will mail a copy of the materials you request.
