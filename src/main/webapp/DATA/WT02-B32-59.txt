<b>Research Interests</b><br>
Aspects of fermenter design; modelling and control of multiproduct processes;
solid substrate processes.  Enzymatic reactors - bioreactors for enzymes in
organic solvents; enzyme processes in extraction and solid hydrolyses.
Downstream processing; liquid membrane and reversed micelle systems.  Modelling
bioprocessing operations. Novel counter-current solid/liquid processes.
CContinuous steam sterilisation; stability and process control.<br>
<p>
<b>Teaching Activities</b><br>
 Bioprocess engineering fundamentals.  Bioreactors (fermenters and enzymatic
reactors): principles, design and engineering aspects. Downstream and
separation processes.  Membrane processes in the food and biotechnology
industries. Process design.<p>
