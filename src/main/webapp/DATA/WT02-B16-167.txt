<H3>Facilities</H3>
<p>
<p>SunLab comprises a number of state-of-the-art solar energy research 
laboratories at <a href="http://www.sandia.gov">Sandia National 
Laboratories</a> in Albuquerque, New Mexico, and at the <a 
href="http://www.nrel.gov">National Renewable Energy Laboratory</a> in 
Golden, Colorado. 
<p>Many of SunLab's facilites have been designated National User 
Facilites, meaning that these laboratories are open to outside qualified 
scientists and engineers for conducting research. SunLab's laboratories 
include:
<ul>
<li><a 
href="http://www.sandia.gov/Renewable_Energy/solarthermal/nsttf.html">The 
National Solar Thermal Test Facility</a>
<li><a href="furnace.htm">The High-Flux Solar Furnace</a>

</li></li></ul>
