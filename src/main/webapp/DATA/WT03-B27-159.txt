<H3>UNITED PRESS INTERNATIONAL (UP)</H3>

United Press International<BR>
$1.50/article<BR>
Continuous wire feed with immediate updating; 10-week retention of articles<BR>
NewsNet monitors five national and international newswires from United Press
International on a 24-hour basis. Information from these five wires is accessible
immediately through the DISPLAY and SCAN commands, and is processed continuously
for NewsFlash delivery to users who have established NewsFlash profiles. Full-text
searching of all articles is available within one hour of release.<P>

<B>UPI Business & Financial Wire--UP01W</B><BR>
(Also in FI, GB, Wires, BWires)<BR>
Continuous reporting on business and financial news.<P>

<B>UPI Domestic News Wire--UP02W</B><BR>
(Also in Wires)<BR>
Continuous reporting on all domestic news other than financial, political, and
sports.<P>

<B>UPI Political Wire--UP03W</B><BR>
(Also in GT, PO, WashDC, Wires)<BR>
Continuous reporting on political news.<P>

<B>UPI International Wire--UP04W</B><BR>
(Also in IT, IWires, Wires)<BR>
Continuous reporting on international news.<P>

<B>UPI Sports Wire--UP05W</B><BR>
(Also in Wires)<BR>
Continuous reporting on sports news.<P>
