<B>

                   RESEARCH ASSESSMENT & TRAINING
<B>
DUTIES:</B>            TWO FULL-TIME POSITIONS & ONE PART-TIME POSITION AVAILABLE
                   Conduct follow-up interviews on large scale abuse 
                   psychiatric study using a semi-structured diagnostic 
                   interview. Responsibilities include: contacting and 
                   locating subjects at six month intervals, conducting
                   interviews in the community, writing detailed narratives, 
                   editing and correcting interview books and narratives,
                   attending weekly staff meetings. Other duties as assigned.
                   
<B>
EDUCATION:</B>         B.A. degree with related experience acceptable. M.A. 
                   degree in Psychology or related field preferred.

<B>TRAINING & </B>
<B>EXPERIENCE:</B>        Familiarity with DSM-IV, previous work with substance 
                   using population, demonstrated ability to located 
                   hard-to-follow subjects.

<B>SPECIAL</B>
<B>REQUIREMENTS:</B>       Current driver's license.  Willingness to interview 
                   subjects in a variety of settings and locations.  
                   Willingness to work evening and weekends.
