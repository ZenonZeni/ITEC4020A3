<B>Bush Tucker - </B>Local indigenous food.
<P>
<B>Diamonds - </B>34% of all the diamonds in the world come from the mine at Lake Argyle.
<P>
<B>Dreamtime - </B>The Aboriginal story of creation.  Aboriginals have stories about how everything was created (ie mountains, rivers, people, dance, etc.).  Dreamtime is often depicted in Aboriginal art and songs.
<P>
<B>Frilled Lizard -</B> What a character!  This Australian lizard has a broad membrane extending from the neck and throat that can be extended like a ruff when the mouth is opened.
<P>
<B>Galah -</B> An Australian cockatoo having pale blue-gray plumage and a pink breast.
