<H1>HACCP - the video</H1>
<HR SIZE=1 NOSHADE>

<P>A new training video explaining the principles and practice of HACCP has been released by RSSL.  &#34;HACCP - How The System Works&#34; is an ideal tool for introducing HACCP to the wider workforce, senior management and the staff charged with implementing HACCP systems.</P>

<P>The video explains the theory behind HACCP, demonstrates its importance to food safety, and gives a clear guide on setting up HACCP teams, establishing HACCP procedures and monitoring their effectiveness. </P>
