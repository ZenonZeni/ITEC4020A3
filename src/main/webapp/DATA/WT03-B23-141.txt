<B><I><FONT=+2>Answers:</FONT></I></B>
<P>
<OL>
	<LI>	Who was the first American to make $100 million a year?</LI>
			<B>Al Capone</B>
	<BR><BR>
	<LI>	What innovation was made in 1929 by our sister company Sutro &amp Co.?</LI>
			<B>It devised and used the first electric stock quotation board.</B>
	<BR><BR>
	<LI>	Who tried and failed to corner the silver market in the late 1970s?</LI>
			<B>The Hunt brothers and their associates.</B>
	<BR><BR>
	<LI>	What was the slowest trading day in New York Stock Exchange history?</LI>
			<B>March 31, 1830 -- only 31 shares traded for the day.</B>
	<BR><BR>
	<LI>	Who was the first U.S. president in office to visit the New York Stock Exchange?</LI>
			<B>Ronald Reagan on March 28, 1985.</B>
	<BR><BR>
	<LI>	In what city was America's first stock exchange located?</LI>
			<B>Philadelphia</B>
	<BR><BR>
	<LI>	What company was the first to earn $1 billion in one year?</LI>
			<B>General Motors</B>
	<BR><BR>
	<LI>	In what year did Ford Motor Company go public?</LI>
			<B>1956</B>
	<BR><BR>
	<LI>	What company today was originally named the Computer-Tabulator-Recorder Company (C-T-R)?</LI>
			<B>IBM</B>
	<BR><BR>
	<LI>	What state is the corporate headquarters capital of the United States?</LI>
			<B>Delaware</B>
</OL>
