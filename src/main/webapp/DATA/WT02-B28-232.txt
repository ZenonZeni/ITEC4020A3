As a pro-active participant in the evolution of managed health care in the United
States,
NYLCare is constantly striving to introduce new health management programs.  Health
management programs are designed to enhance the quality of care received by our
members. <P>

As they become available, abstracts of NYLCare research reports on health
management
programs
will be presented here.  Abstracts of reports on other pertinent research
commissioned by
NYLCare
will also be accessible from this page.  The number of abstracts presented on this
page will expand as the NYLCare site evolves.<P>

Full drafts of all the report titles listed on this page are available from
NYLCare.  Click on the
"Contact NYLCare" button to get in touch with us.  Specify the full title of the
report you would
like, and the address to which you would like the report sent.  All reports
requested will be
sent by U.S. Mail.<P>
