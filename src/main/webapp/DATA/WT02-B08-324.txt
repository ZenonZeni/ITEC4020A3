<h3>
The Global Positioning System (GPS) receiver was installed on TOPEX/Poseidon as part of an experiment that tracks multiple GPS satellites and ground locations at the same time.  
<p>
It has proven to be a great success!  The GPS receiver simultaneously tracks signals from as many as 8 of the 21 GPS satellites that orbit the Earth.  It shows that low-cost GPS receivers can be used to determine satellite position with great accuracy.  This technique is planned to be used by many other Earth-orbiting satellites in the future.
