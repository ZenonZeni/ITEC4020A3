 <p>Redwings Horse Sanctuary is a 175 acre facility located on beautiful Monterey Bay in Carmel California.  Open to the public every Saturday, 1 to 4pm, March through December, there are a variety of activities for you to enjoy that include:</p>
<li>Meeting the rescued horses ponies and donkeys and reading about each one's history and how they came to be at the Sanctuary.

<li>Joining us for one of our special events such as the Members Only Brunch, Easter Egghunt and Haunted Barn.
<li>Helping us groom a horse or pony during your visit.
<li>Experiencing first hand the training of horses during a humane horse handling demonstration.
