<h1>Moore Inflation Predictor© <font size="5">(MIP)</font></h1>

<p><font size="4">This is a highly accurate graphical
representation of the future direction of the inflation rate </font><font
size="3">(97%+ on direction &amp; turning points and about 90%
within the likely range).</font><font size="4"> By watching the
turning points, we can profit from inflation hedges when the rate
is trending up and from Bonds when the rate is trending down (our
subscribers easily make 20% in a three month upmove and make
another 20% the following quarter in a downmove. Subscribers can
view the current predictor chart...For a more detailed
