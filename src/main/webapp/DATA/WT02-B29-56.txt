<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>The Solution</STRONG></FONT><P>


New York Life's Rollover IRA Program enables you to direct your distribution into
investments that
address the issues discussed in this section. Specifically, the need for growth, and the need
for
guaranteed fixed income.
<P>
<STRONG>All in one easy package!</STRONG><BR>
You have access to two distinct segments which may be used alone or together to fit your
needs. They
are: 
<P>
<UL>
<LI>Rollover IRA, which offers the MainStay Institutional Funds, and more than 3,500
additional funds
including over 370 No-Load, No-Transaction Fee funds, as well as stocks, bonds and other
investments. <SUP><FONT SIZE="-1"><A HREF="#1">1</A></FONT></SUP>
<LI> An Annuity, from New York Life Insurance Company.<SUP><FONT SIZE="-1"><A
HREF="#2">2</A></FONT></SUP>
</UL>
<P>
Using these two investment vehicles, you can rollover all or part of your
qualified lump-sum distribution from your plan directly to our Rollover IRA account; transfer
assets from
an existing IRA to our Rollover IRA account; or purchase an immediate guaranteed annuity
benefit.
<P>

Fund's
prospectus, which contains information about the Fund's advisory fees,
other expenses and
share

the
Institutional Service Class only, subject to the exceptions as noted in the prospectus. See
the
prospectus for more detailed information.
