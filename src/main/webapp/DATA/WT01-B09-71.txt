<h3>Electricore</h3><p>

<blockquote>
<B>Project Title:  NYCTA Bus Retrofit & Demonstration <p>

Performing Organization:   Allison Transmission Division of GMC, Delco Propulsion Systems</B><p>

<P>Technical DOC: Fred Cartwright, ATD, (317) 242­0665 </P>

<P><B>Project Summary:</B></P>

<P>This project involves a partnership between Delco Propulsion Systems (DPS), New York City
Transit Authority (NYCTA) and DARPA to demonstrate a series hybrid propulsion system in an existing 40­foot RT5
bus. The hybrid solution is seen as an alternative to the mid­life overhaul of the conventional powertrain, i.e., at the
7­8 year milestone in vehicle life, existing buses could be retrofitted with a clean and efficient hybrid powertrain. DPS
will convert an existing bus to hybrid electric and then deliver it to NYCTA for 6 months of operation. Significant DPS
technology will be applied to this project from the EVI program.</P>

<P><B>Project Deliverables:</B></P>

<P>One (I) DPS single motor series hybrid system installed in a 40­foot RT5 bus and demonstrated in NYC.</P>

<P><B>Project Schedule:</B></P>

<P><UL><LI>This project is currently under negotiations. Vehicle will be retrofitted and delivered to NYCTA within 24 months from project go­ahead. It will then be tested in NYC for 6 months.</UL></P>


</blockquote>
