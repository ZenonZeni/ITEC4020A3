<H2>Welcome to EREN -- the Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy Network!</H2>  
</center>


Please explore this electronic "information highway" and use it as a toolbox for building America's sustainable energy future.  EREN contains consumer-friendly information on the latest energy efficiency and renewable energy technologies -- energy practices and systems that will save you and your customers money, while creating new jobs, improving the nation's energy security, and helping prevent pollution and other environmental problems.<P>
 
Think of EREN as a tool for helping you research, develop, market and use energy efficient and renewable energy technologies.<P> 

You are important contributors, customers and partners in EREN. Its continued growth and usefulness will depend on how well the system serves your needs. Please send us your feedback and your ideas for how to make EREN better by emailing<P>
<UL><a href="mailto:webmaster.eren@nrel.gov">webmaster.eren@nrel.gov</a> <P></UL>
or utilizing the User Feedback Survey on the home page.<P>
