<h2 align="center"><font color="#0000FF">Waste Management
Services</font></h2>
<h3 align="center"><font color="#0000FF">Industrial Waste
Disposal</font></h3>
<p><img src="images/TRUCKS.jpg" alt="Shipping waste by rail"
align="left" hspace="10" width="220" height="134"><font
color="#0000FF"> </font></p>
<p><font color="#0000FF">The issue of industrial waste disposal
through the country varies greatly from state to state, and
environmental regulations are in a state of on going change. <strong>SKB
Environmental</strong> has developed a cross country network of
available information relating to approved disposal facilities
and regulating criteria. We can manage industrial waste disposal
needs in an efficient and cost effective manner and accomplish
this with an assurance that environmental risk is minimized for
our customers.</font></p>
<p><br clear="all">
</p>
<hr>
<h3><font color="#0000FF">Conditionally Exempt Small Quantity
Generators</font></h3>
<p><font color="#0000FF">Rules finalized by EPA in 1996
significantly limit the range of disposal facilities which can
manage construction wastes or waste from the manufacture of
construction materials if they contain small quantities of
hazardous material. <strong>SKB Environmental</strong>'s Rich
Valley Landfill meets new EPA standards for non-municipal
non-hazardous waste disposal. For conditionally exempt small
quantity generators (CESQG) from the construction industry, the
facility's </font><a href="candd.htm#cand"><font color="#0000FF">state-of-the-art</font></a><font
color="#0000FF"> design, operating practices, and performance
record mean peace of mind.</font></p>
<h3><font color="#0000FF">Asbestos</font></h3>
<p><font color="#0000FF">In Minnesota, <strong>SKB Environmental</strong>
has capacity for disposal of asbestos wastes. For projects and
generators located elsewhere in the nation, we can assist with
regulatory compliance and record keeping and arrange for disposal
and management of asbestos wastes. </font></p>
