<h1>Charts and Graphs About the Drug War </h1>

<p><a href="graph03.htm">Drug Related Deaths </a>- Number of

<p><a href="04.htm">Substance Abuse Related Deaths </a>- Number

<p><a href="06.htm">Substance Abuse Related Deaths </a>-

<p><a href="22.htm">Property Seizures </a>- Percent of people
