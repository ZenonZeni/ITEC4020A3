<H1>Factors that Contribute to Sea Surface Height</H1>
<p>
<font size = +1>
<ul>
<li>Ocean eddies - up to about 25 centimeters (10 inches)</li><br> 
<li>Temperature of the upper ocean water - up to about 35 centimeters (13 inches), similar to the contribution from ocean eddies</li><br> 
<li>Tides in the deep ocean - up to 1 meter (3 feet)</li><br> 
<li>Ocean currents or ocean circulation -  about 2 meters (6 feet)</li><br> 
<li>Gravity - up to 150 meters! (almost 500 feet!)</li><br>
