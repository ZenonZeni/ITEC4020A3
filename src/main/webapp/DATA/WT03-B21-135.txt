After starting up as an open pit mine in 1984, underground mining began in 1991 ans was completed in June 1995 with the exhaustion of reserves.  Total production for 1995 amounted to 56,800 ounces of gold at an average cash cost of $183 per ounce compared to 79,900 ounces at a cash cost of $238 per ounce in 1994.</B>
