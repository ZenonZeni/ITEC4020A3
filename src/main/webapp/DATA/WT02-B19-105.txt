<h5> Area of Research </h5>
Research is being conducted on environmental fluid dynamics,
air-sea interactions, surface wave dynamics, oceanographic
instrumentation and remote sensing.  Specific research topics
include measurements of air entrainment by breaking waves,
infared remote sensing of waves, the stability of rotating
flows, measurements of the bubble size distributions beneath
breaking waves and observations and modelling of flow over
zebra mussel beds.<p>
