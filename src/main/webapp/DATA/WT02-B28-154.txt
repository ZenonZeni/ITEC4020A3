<A HREF="pfrprisp.html">Your Spouse and Your Pension</A><BR>
If you expect to receive a pension at retirement, you must also decide how you want
it paid
out.  You want to receive enough to maintain an adequate lifestyle, but you also
don't want
to expose your spouse to unneccesary financial burden if you die.  Find out how you
can
maximize your pension and your spouse's financial security.
