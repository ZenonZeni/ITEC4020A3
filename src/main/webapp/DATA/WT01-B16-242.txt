<hr size="2" width="100%" align="left" noshade><p>

<i>You Don't Have to Die</i> is a cutting-edge publication that redefines AIDS by shattering the myths surrounding this controversial disease. Alternative therapies from around the world coupled with the latest research make this an invaluable guide to surviving and controlling autoimmune diseases.<p>

Find out why AIDS is not caused by HIV, why conventional treatments rarely work, and how diet, nutritional supplements, herbs, acupuncture, Chinese medicine, oxygen therapy, massage, and other alternative therapies can save AIDS patients and add productive <i>years</i> to their lives.<p>

Read the riveting success stories of long-term AIDS survivors and see what their bold doctors did to take them from being seriously ill to nearly symptom free. Here is the evidence at last that shows AIDS is reversible.<p>
