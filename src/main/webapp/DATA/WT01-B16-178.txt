<H1>
Water Resources of the Western United States
</H1>


The <A HREF="http://wwwrvares.er.usgs.gov/nrp/nrp.html"> National
Research Program</A> conducts basic and problem oriented hydrologic
research and the <A HREF="index_orh.html"> Office of the Regional
Hydrologist</A> provides technical and administrative management for U.
S. Geological Survey district activities in water resources data
collection, research, and analysis in the Western Region.  Districts in
the Western Region are located in Hawaii, Alaska, Washington, Oregon,
California, Idaho, Nevada, Arizona, and Utah.<P>
