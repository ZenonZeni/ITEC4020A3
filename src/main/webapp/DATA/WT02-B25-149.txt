<p align=center><font color="#FF0000">MANIC-DEPRESSIVE ILLNESS</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Manic-depressive illness, also known as bipolar disorder, is a brain disorder involving 
episodes of serious mania and depression. The person&#146;s mood usually swings from overly 
&quot;high&quot; and irritable to sad and hopeless, and then back again, with periods of normal mood 
in-between.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Manic-depressive illness usually begins in adolescence or early adulthood and continues 
throughout life. It is often not recognized as an mental illness, and people who have it may 
suffer needlessly for years or even decades.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">At least 2 million Americans suffer from manic-depressive illness. For those afflicted with 
the illness, it is extremely distressing and disruptive.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Signs and symptoms of mania:</font></p>
<blockquote>
<ul>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">Extreme irritability and distractibility</font></p>
</li>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">Excessive &quot;high&quot; or euphoric feelings</font></p>
</li>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">A sustained period of behavior that is different from usual</font></p>
</li>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">Increased energy, activity, restlessness, racing thoughts and rapid talking</font></p>
</li>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">Decreased need for sleep</font></p>
</li>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">Unrealistic beliefs in one&#146;s abilities and powers</font></p>
</li>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">Uncharacteristically poor judgment</font></p>
</li>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">Increased sexual drive</font></p>
</li>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">Abuse of drugs, particularly cocaine, alcohol and sleeping medications</font></p>
</li>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">Obnoxious, provocative or intrusive behavior</font></p>
</li>
<li><p align=left><font color="#000000">Denial that anything is wrong.</font></p>
</li>
</ul>
</blockquote>
