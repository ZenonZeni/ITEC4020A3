<h2><font color = "#2f4f2f">HEALTH CARE LAW</font></h2>

<font size = 4>

<p><img align = bottom src = "rdsquare.gif"> The Firm has long been involved in the health care industry, in both litigation and
transactional settings.  We have substantial experience in drafting the legal documentation to acquire
and form business entities that operate in the health care industry as sellers, managers and as

laboratories and medical professional corporations. </p>

<p><img align = bottom src = "rdsquare.gif"> The Firm renders business, antitrust and regulatory advice and handles major litigation
