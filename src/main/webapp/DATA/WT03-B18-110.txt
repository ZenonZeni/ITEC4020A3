<H1><A NAME="Engineering">Engineering</A></H1>
<H2>Applications Engineer</H2>
<P>
 As a part of the RWT team, you will primarily be reponsible for teaching new
users how to use  robot simulation software, as well as demonstrating the
product to potential customers. You will also provide technical support for
current users. This is a hands-on job requiring considerable interaction with
robots. Manufacturing process knowledge is a plus. Travel required.</P>
<P><B>Education:</B> Engineering degree from an accredited university program.
Can be in Mechanical Engineering (ME), Mechanical Engineering Technology (MET),
Manufacturing Engineering (MfgE), Electrical Engineering (EE), or Electrical
Engineering Technology (EET). 
</P>
<P><B>Skills:</B></P>
<UL><LI>Some robotics knowledge.</LI>
<LI>Familiar with at least one computer or robot programming language.</LI>
<LI>Good teaching skills.</LI>
<LI>Intimately familiar with computers, including DOS and Windows.</LI></UL>
<P><B>Job Responsibilities:</B></P>
<UL><LI>Train new users how to use product.</LI>
<LI>Offer Technical Support for current users.</LI>
<LI>Demonstrate product before a wide variety of audiences.</LI>
<LI>Test new products for reliability and ease-of-use.</LI></UL>
<P><B>Applicant should:</B></P>
<UL><LI>Be a non-smoker.</LI>
<LI>Able to travel.</LI>
<LI>Outgoing, personable.</LI>
<LI>Self-starter.</LI>
<LI>Able to learn new technology quickly.</LI>
<LI>Have good communication skills.</LI></UL>
