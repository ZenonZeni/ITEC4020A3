<h3>Southern Coalition for Advanced Transportation (SCAT)</h3><p>

<blockquote>
<B>Project Title: Electric Shuttle Bus for Georgia Tech<p>
Performing Organization: Westinghouse Electric Blue Bird; Georgia Tech: Georgia Power­ GNB</B><p>

<P>POINT OF CONTACT Randy Webber <BR>
Telephone: (410) 765­3G58  </P>

<P><B>Program Summary: </B></P> 

<P>This project involves the development and evaluation of a 32­ft electric shuttle bus. It will be
ADA­certified with electric accessories including wheel chair lift and air conditioning. It will be based on
the Blue Bird Q­Bus chassis design. The bus will be demonstrated on the Georgia Tech campus.</P>

<P><B>Benefit: </B></P><P>Large market interest in both school and transit buses
</P>

<P><B>Schedule:</B></P>

<P>The bus is complete.</P>

<P>The 130kW rapid charge station is complete. It has been installed on the Georgia Tech campus and is
being operated by Georgia Power Company.</P>

<P>The bus was returned to Westinghouse for hybrid tests and for a drivetrain upgrade The bus is
scheduled to return to Atlanta for operation throughout the Olympic games. Georgia Tech has
extended their participation to allow the operation during the Olympics.</P>



</blockquote>
