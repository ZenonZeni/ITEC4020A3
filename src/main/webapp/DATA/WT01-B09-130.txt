<h3>Hawii Electric Vehicle Demonstration Project (HEVDP)</h3><p>

<blockquote>

<B>Project Title:  Rapid Charging Unit (RCU) Evaluation<p>

Performing Organization:  Hawaiian Electric Company (HECO)</B><p>

P. O. Box 2750<br>
Honolulu, Hi 968400001<br>
Technical POC: David G. Waller<br>
Phone: 808-543-4794<br>
Fax: 808-543-4747<br>
EMail: dwaller@hei.com

<P><B>Project Summary: </B></P>

<P>Three RCU units will be installed, monitored and compared. HEVDP is providing 73
vehicles to the military, state and local governments, and commercial businesses. A
representative fleet will be modified to allow rapid charging. The rest of the vehicles will
form a control group. </P>

<P>The RCUs to be compared are: </P>

<ul><p>

<li> Electronic Power Technology, Inc. (EPTI) 100 kW unit<br>
<li> Northrop Grumman Corporation 100 kW unit<br>
<li> Norvik Technologies 150 kW unit
</ul><p>

<P><B>Project Deliverables:</B></P> 


<p><ul>
<li> Three operational rapid charging units.<br> 
<li> Fleet of vehicles equipped to use RCU.<br>
<li> Final Report on use of RCU and effect on batteries.<p>
</ul>

</ul>
