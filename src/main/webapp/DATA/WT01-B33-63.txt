<p>May 16, 1994 </p>

<p>Clifford A. Schaffer </p>

<p>P.O. Box 1430 </p>

<p>Canyon Country, CA 91386-1430 </p>

<p>Dear Clifford: </p>

<p>Of course I remember you... </p>

<p>I deeply regret that your mother is still in such excruciating
pain, and I can only hope you have a friendly doctor who will do
whatever is possible to control that pain. </p>

<p>I received much criticism (and some applause, however) for my
stand on legalizing drugs. </p>

<p>You may add my name to the list of those who are with you all
the way on H.R. 3100, the National-Drug Control Policy Act of
1993. </p>

<p>Please keep me informed, Clifford... and convey my very best
to your mother. </p>

<p>Sincerely, </p>

<p>ABBY </p>
