Welcome to SEMA Online! To make your visit meaningful, here are descriptions and an explanation of what SEMA Online has to offer:<BR>
 
<UL>
<LI> 
<LI> A searchable <A HREF="/directoryindex.html">database of aftermarket companies,</A> arranged by company type, name, city, state or ZIP-code location. Includes descriptions and information on how to contact the company. <BR>

<LI> A <A HREF="/sema_aiashow/search ">searchable database of aftermarket and other upcoming automotive events </A>by name, sponsor, date or location of event. <BR>

<LI> A <A HREF="/postit">message board</A> for people to post questions about automotive-related topics and have them answered by those &quot;in the know.&quot; For instance, some might use the message board to ask where they can find trick bumpers
or sets of wheels for their classic Fords, while others might express their
concerns about the most recent laws affecting their businesses. Here's where automotive-related classifieds can also be found. <BR>

<LI> A <A HREF="/whatsnew">&quot;What's New&quot;</A> section highlighting what's new on the site as well as throughout the aftermarket.<BR>

<LI> Separate home pages for <a href="/build/sections/aftermarket">aftermarket businesses</A> and <A HREF="/build/sections/consumer"> enthusiasts/consumers,</A> with information targeted toward each group. For instance, the consumer side includes legislative information that keeps users up to date on new legislation that will affect their hobby, while the trade side includes current aftermarket and SEMA/AI
Show news. <BR>

<LI> A special <A HREF="/build/sections/member">SEMA-members-only section,</A> a password-protected page through which many of the benefits of SEMA can be accessed upon command. <BR>

<LI> A <A HREF="/faq">FAQ (frequently asked questions) list</A>, a must-see for new visitors to SEMA Online.<BR>

<LI> An <A HREF="/hyperlinks">extensive list of aftermarket companies on the world wide web,</A> a definitive guide to aftermarket companies on the web. The list is
constantly growing in size and scope. The guide includes automotive
publishing companies, manufacturers, distributors and more. <BR>

<LI> The latest information about <A HREF="/semau">SEMA University</A>, one of SEMA's newest offerings.<BR>

<LI> The latest information about <A HREF="/nmc">the SEMA 1997 National Management Conference (NMC)</A>.<BR>

<LI> A place to find out about SEMA, including its <A HREF="/history">history</A> and the <A HREF="/advantages">benefits offered to its members.</A><BR>

<LI> A place to <A HREF="/cgi-html/list.html">sign up for a SEMA e-mail list,</A> a method of getting updates on what's new with SEMA and its site via e-mail. <BR>

<LI> An <A HREF="/searchsema.html"> all-site find function,</A> making it searchable by key word. <BR>
</UL>
<P></P>
Visit this site often, and see how it's changing <B>FOR YOU!</B>
