<CENTER><H2>Taking off with ScanTEK 2000</H2></CENTER>
ScanTEK 2000 recognizes that not all students have the same range of abilities.  We believe this should not exclude them from the learning opportunities available within the technology lab.  Our competency-based approach ensures that all students gain from the ScanTEK 2000 technology experience.  Each of our modules features a mixed media approach where learning materials are presented in a variety of forms, these can include elements of the following:
<UL>
	<LI>CD ROM
	<LI>Text based student workbooks
	<LI>Video material
	<LI>Computer aided instruction
	<LI>Experimental hardware
	<LI>Projects
	<LI>Supplementary study packages
	<LI>Integrated academic support
</UL>
<BR>The work is specifically designed to encompass the following:
<UL>
	<LI>Integrated basic skills
	<LI>Measurable learner outcomes
	<LI>Automatic assessment in competency areas
	<LI>Problem solving
	<LI>Technological literacy
	<LI>Interpersonal skills
	<LI>Learning skills
	<LI>Effective use of computers
	<LI>Occupational awareness
</UL>
