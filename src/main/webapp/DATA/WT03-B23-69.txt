As one of Australia's first Community Legal Centres the Fitzroy

Legal Service has had a significant <A HREF="flsabout.htm#history">history</A>,

in the delivery and development of Legal Aid in Australia. Serving

its local community since 1972 the service has been involved in

a number of community based activities relating to the law, these

include: offering Legal Advice to citizens,

taking part in <A HREF="flsabout.htm#law reform &comm ed">Law Reform and Community Legal Education</A> and

managing <A HREF="flsabout.htm#casework services">Casework</A>.

 

Follow the links to find out more about <A HREF="flsabout.htm#about">Who we are</A>,

<A HREF="flsabout.htm#operation">What do we do</A> or <A HREF="flsabout.htm#location">Where we are</A>.
