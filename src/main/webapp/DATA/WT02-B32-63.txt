<b>Research Interests</b><br>
Perception and measurement of food quality as affected by composition,
processing, storage and presentation; adaptation and sensory interactions in
recognition of food quality characteristics; illumination, food appearance and
role of light in deterioration; meat handling, processing and quality; food
colorimetry and standards.<p>
<p>
<b>Teaching Acitivities</b><br>
Sensory methodology and analysis; sensory perception of food quality and
acceptability by panel and consumer; relationships of instrumental measurement
to sensory assessment; measurement of food colour and appearance<p>
