<H1>Glycosidases in Baking</H1>
</font>
<P>
<P>A developing interest in the group is the application of enzymes in baking. To date there is one project in this area.
</ul>
<IMG ALIGN="left" SRC="cbglogo.gif"HEIGHT=75 WIDTH=75>
<P>
<UL>
<P>Simone Robinson, supported by BBSRC and RHM, is investigating the application of fungal xylanases to the manipulation of bread texture. It is hoped that application of these enzymes will lead to favourable improvements in loaf texture and quality.
