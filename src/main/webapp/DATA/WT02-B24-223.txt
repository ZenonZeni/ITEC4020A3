    <A HREF="http://econwpa.wustl.edu/EconFAQ/EconFAQ.html">

<LI>	Media Logic's
    <A HREF="http://www.mlinet.com/mle/econdata.htm">

<A NAME="Data"><H2>Economic Data Sources</H2>
<DL>

<DD><H3>General</H3>
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="http://www.lib.umich.edu/libhome/Documents.center/stecon.html">
	Statistical Resources on the Web</A> for Economics.

<LI><A HREF="gopher://una.hh.lib.umich.edu/11/ebb">
	Economics Bulletin Board</A> at UM.  Data from the 
    <A HREF="http://www.stat-usa.gov/BEN/Brochures/ebb.html#info">
	Department of Commerce</A>.  Mostly macroeconomic, trade, and some
	U.S. state data, but some other information as well. 

<LI><A HREF="http://www.stat.itd.umich.edu/data.html">
	Social Sciences Data Project</A>.  Allows one to "easily" access
	several datasets maintained at Michigan, including the Current
	Population Survey.

<LI><A HREF="http://www.icpsr.umich.edu/">
	Inter-university Consortium for Political and Social Research</A> (ICPSR)
    <A HREF="http://www.icpsr.umich.edu/archive1.html">
	Data Archive</A>

<LI><A HREF="http://www.umich.edu/~compap/icpsr.htm">
	Computer Assistance Program</A> through Michigan's
    <A HREF="http://www.umich.edu/~compap/index.html">
	Political Science Department</A>.

</UL>


<DD><H3>Macroeconomics</H3>
<UL>

<LI><A HREF="http://rsqe.econ.lsa.umich.edu/databases/citi-index.html">
	DRI Economics Database</A> (formerly <I>Citibase</I> and <I>FAME
	Economics Database</I>), courtesy of 
    <A HREF="http://rsqe.econ.lsa.umich.edu/">
	RSQE</A>.  
	(<EM><B>NOTE</B>: UM users only!</EM>)
    <BR>  
    <A HREF="http://rsqe.econ.lsa.umich.edu/databases/citi.search.html">
	Search</A> the DRI Basic Economics database.  

<LI><A HREF="http://www.stat-usa.gov/BEN/databases.html">
	STAT-USA Databases</A> from the Commerce Department.
	(<EM><B>NOTE</B>: UM users only!</EM>)

<LI><A HREF="http://www.mlinet.com/bci/bci.html">
	Business Cycle Indicators</A> from Media Logic.  Very nicely
	done charts of BEA historical data, produced with their proprietary
	Windows software.  Data itself is conveniently organized and
	available to download.  (An older(?) version of this site also exists
    <A HREF="http://www.cris.com/~netlink/bci/">
	here</A>; yet another BCI site is at 
    <A HREF="http://csf.colorado.edu/./econ/bci.html">
	Colorado</A>.)

<LI><A HREF="http://www.gpo.ucop.edu/catalog/erp.ct.html">
	1996 Economic Report of the President</A>, including the 
    <A HREF="http://www.gpo.ucop.edu/catalog/erp.cta.html">
	statistical tables</A>.  Other 
    <A HREF="http://www.gpo.ucop.edu/">
	government publications</A> are also available.

<LI><A HREF="http://www.nber.org/databases/macrohistory/contents/index.html">
	NBER Macro History Database</A>.  See also their list of
    <A HREF="http://www.nber.org/cycles.html">
	Business Cycle Dates</A>.

<LI><A HREF="http://info.umd.edu:86/Educational_Resources/AcademicResourcesByTopic/EconomicsResources/EconData/.www/econdata.html">
	EconData</A> at the University of Maryland.  
    <A HREF="http://info.umd.edu:86/Educational_Resources/AcademicResourcesByTopic/EconomicsResources/EconData/.www/contents.html">
	List of datafiles</A> available for downloading.  (Data is from 
	BEA, includes some State & Local and International data as well.)
	Requires also using their DOS-based program to read the data.

<LI>	<EM>The Economy at a Glance</EM>:  
    <A HREF="http://www.bls.gov/eag.table.html">
	HTML table</A> or 
    <A HREF="http://www.bls.gov/eag.table.html">
	text</A> version.  From the 
    <A HREF="http://www.bls.gov/blshome.html">
	Bureau of Labor Statistics'</A> (BLS)
    <A HREF="http://www.bls.gov/datahome.htm">
	data homepage</A>.

<LI><A HREF="http://www.webcom.com/~yardeni/economic.html">
	Economics Network</A>.  Organized by the Chief Economist at 
	Deutsche Morgan Grenfell/C. J. Lawrence.  More Wall Street than
	academic.  Includes recent data in chart format.  (<EM>Requires 
    <A HREF="http://www.adobe.com/Acrobat/AcrobatWWW.html">
	Adobe Acrobat</A>.</EM>)

<LI><A HREF=http://nmg.clever.net/wew/">
	World Economic Window</A>.  Lots and lots of graphs.

</UL>

<DD><H3>International</H3>
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="http://cansim.epas.utoronto.ca:5680/pwt/pwt.html">
	Penn World Tables</A> at the 
    <A HREF="http://www.epas.utoronto.ca:5680/eco/eco.html">

<UL>
