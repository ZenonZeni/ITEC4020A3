<h1><CENTER>JOURNEY OF HOPE FAMILY EDUCATION CLASSES</CENTER></h1>

<p>Our curriculum focuses on three major psychiatric illnesses (schizophrenia, bipolar disorder and major clinical depression)
emphasizing the clinical treatment of these illnesses and providing knowledge and skills that families need when faced with the difficulties of mental illness,  Courses are taught throughout Missouri by volunteers who are themselves family members. 

<h3><p>12 Education Modules</h3>

<OL>
<LI> Learning about feelings,learning about facts
<LI> Introduction to schizophrenia; diagnosis, critical periods
<LI> Introduction to depression and bipolar illness; causes and      diagnosis
<LI>Basics about the brain
<LI>Problem solving skill workshop
<LI>Medications review
<LI>What it's like to be mentally ill/empathy workshop
<LI>Communication skills workshop
<LI>Relative groups and self care
<LI>Rehabilitation
<LI>Advocacy: fighting stigma and discrimination
<LI>Certification and celebration
</OL>
<p>To find out about a class near you contact <a href="mailto:mocami@aol.com">MOCAMI</a>
 or your nearest AMI Chapter office.
