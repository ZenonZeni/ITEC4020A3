<h2 align=center><u>Recruiting<br>
</u></h2>

<P>Computer Motion, the leader in medical robotics, specializes in the design of robotic
 instruments that assist surgeons in minimally invasive surgery or endoscopy.  We are 
currently seeking candidates to fill the following positions. CMI is an equal opportunity 
employer and offers competitive compensation with an excellent benefits package which 
includes stock options.  To be considered as a team player in our dynamic organization
, please send a copy of your r&eacute;sum&eacute; with reference to the position for which 
you are applying via 
facsimile to the attention of Human Resources at (805) 685-9277 or via e-mail 
to <A HREF="mailto:GBrallier@ComputerMotion.com">jobs@ComputerMotion.com.</A><BR>

<hr>


<H3>Member of Technical Staff, Software Development</H3>
<P>A position is available for a candidate with an M.S./Ph.D. in 
Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or related field.</P>
<i>The ideal candidate will possess the following:</i>
<UL>
<LI>Understanding of software development, verification and validation practices
<LI>Experience in developing in speech recognition systems using Hidden Markov models
<LI>Experience in developing software for real-time systems
<LI>Experience with C and assembly languages for DSP’s
</UL>

<HR>

<H3>Mechanical Engineer</H3>
<P>A position is available for a candidate with a B.S./M.S. in 
Mechanical Engineering or related to robotic design.</P>
<i>The ideal candidate will possess the following:</i>
<UL>
<LI>Strong analytical skills in design engineering and practicable aptitude for visualization
<LI>Experience with AutoCAD & solid modeling software with ANSI standards drafting skills
<LI>Strong technical writing skills for creation and documentation of product test reports
<LI>Experience in creating mechanical drawings, assembly drawings, Bill of Materials (BOM) and Engineering Change Orders (ECO)
<LI>Exposure to the medical device industry
<LI>Exposure to manufacturing processes for machined parts, cast parts, molded parts, sheet metal parts, and wiring harnesses
</UL>

<HR>

<H3>Electrical Engineer</H3>
<P>A position is available for a candidate with a B.S./M.S. in Electrical Engineering.</P>
<i>The ideal candidate will possess the following:</i>
<UL>
<LI>Minimum of three years experience in new product development
<LI>Experience in computer design, FPGA design, EDA tools, C and assembly language programming
</UL>
