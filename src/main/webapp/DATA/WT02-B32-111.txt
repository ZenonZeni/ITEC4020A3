Tom specializes in tax planning and mutual funds, with a focus on self-directed RRSP's and global investment opportunities.<p>

A financial planner since purchasing his Newmarket franchise in 1986, Tom regularly conducts educational financial seminars.  His seminars focus on strategic investments, long term financial planning and an emphasis on current economic and market realities.<p>

Tom also writes financial columns for a number of local newspapers, contributes to a quarterly newsletter and is co-author of the popular "Money Management Workshop", a nine hour personal financial planning course he has been teaching since January, 1991.<p>
