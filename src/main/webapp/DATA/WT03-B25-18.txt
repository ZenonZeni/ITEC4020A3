<P>The European Center for Mechatronics located in Aachen, Germany is active in the areas robotics, sensors, mobile platforms, communnication technology and welding technology. Its roundabout 50 qualified members coming from the disciplines of electrical and mechanical engineering as well as informatics participate under the guiding hand of Prof. Drews in several national and international research projects.
