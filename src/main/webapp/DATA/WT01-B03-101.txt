<H2></H2>
<CENTER><I>Support and training for your Tribal Gaming Commission as it
protects the integrity of your games, the security of your assets, and the
sovereignty of your nation.</I></CENTER>
<H1></H1>
<CENTER></CENTER>
<HR>
<H3></H3>
<CENTER>
<H3>Planning &amp; Negotiations</H3></CENTER>
<P>&#183; Consultation and support during the negotiation and development of
your Tribal-State Compact, Tribal Ordinances, and Facility Management
Agreements.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Review of your Tribal-State Compact, Tribal Ordinances, and
Facility Management Agreements to determine the scope and authority of the
Tribal Gaming Commission.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Preparation and recommendation of an Administrative Code setting
forth the organization, operation, and funding of your Tribal Gaming Commission.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Preliminary orientation and training of <A HREF="TGCAstone.html">Gaming
Commission Members</A> when selected by your Tribe.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Preparation and recommendation of Gaming Regulations for approval
by your Tribal Gaming Commission. As a minimum, the regulations will focus on:
(a) internal control systems, (b) rules of the games, (c) security procedures,
(d) background investigations &amp; the licensing of vendors, employees &amp;
financiers, (e) patron complaint processing, and (f) disciplinary procedures for
code violations. 
</P>
<H4><A HREF="TGCASeminole.html"><IMG
SRC="image1.gif" ALIGN="BOTTOM" BORDER="0" WIDTH="19" HEIGHT="19">Click here

<H3></H3>
<CENTER>
<H3>Systems &amp; Facility Development</H3></CENTER>
<P>&#183; Pre-construction systems &amp; facility reviews to evaluate: (a)
compliance with the Tribal Ordinances, Administrative Code, &amp; Gaming
Regulations (b) the integrity of the games, (c) the protection of tribal assets,
and (d) the comfort, safety, &amp; security of patrons and employees. The
following will be reviewed, as a minimum: <BR>
<BR> &#183; all plans for construction, renovation, and/or expansion of gaming
facilities,<BR> &#183; all intended security, surveillance, and internal
control systems.<BR>
<BR> A written summary of each review will be provided to the Tribal Gaming
Commission along with any recommended changes.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Preparation and recommendation of Administrative Procedures for
approval by your Tribal Gaming Commission. These will: (a) define the structure
of the Commission Staff, (b) define the authority &amp; responsibilities of each
position, (c) establish procedures for the investigation and licensing of
vendors, employees, &amp; financiers, and (d) set forth a commission 'Code of
Professional Conduct'.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Selection, training, and supervision of sufficient Commission
Inspectors to be present during all gaming operations. 
</P>
<H3></H3>
<CENTER>
<H3>Start-up Support</H3></CENTER>
<P><BR> &#183; On-site support to the Tribal Gaming Commission during initial
operations, as necessary.<BR>
<BR> &#183; On-site supervision and/or administrative support to the Gaming
Commission Staff, as necessary.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Experienced gaming commission professionals on a temporary basis,
as necessary, to assist with initial commission operations. 
</P>
<H3></H3>
<CENTER>
<H3>Support of Continuing Operations</H3></CENTER>
<P><BR> &#183; Assist the Tribal Council, Tribal Gaming Commission, and the
tribe's legal council, as requested.<BR>
<BR> &#183; On-site reviews of gaming operations, physical security and/or
gaming commission operations, as requested.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Facility and systems development support during programs to expand
or restructure operations.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Continuing education programs for Commissioners, Inspectors, and
other commission professionals.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Budgeting and Financial Planning.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Our <A HREF="TGCAAcc.html">Tribal Gaming Academy</A>, and other
professional training programs. <BR>
</P>
<H3></H3>
<CENTER>
<H3>Investigation Of Vendors, Employees, Financiers &amp; Management Personnel</H3></CENTER>
<P><BR> &#183; Investigation of all persons and organizations seeking to
provide goods or services to your gaming operations to uncover criminal records,
inappropriate associations, conflicts of interest, financial instability, and to
insure full compliance with the licensing standards established by your Tribal
Gaming Commission.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Safeguarding and control of information gathered to prevent its
unauthorized release.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Coordination of investigative activities with your tribal police ,
as well as, state and federal agencies in order to (1) promote the efficient
exchange of information, and (2) discourage costly duplication of procedures.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Similar coordination activities involving other Gaming Commissions
that you may designate.<BR>
<BR> &#183; Follow-up investigations, as requested, on a routine basis, or in
support of: personnel actions, major purchasing decisions, Gaming Commission
hearings, and other activities designated by your Tribal Gaming Commission.<BR>
</P>
