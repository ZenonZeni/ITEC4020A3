<LI> An excellent, nontoxic plant food blended for virtually all types of vegetables.
<LI>A non-burning transplanting fertilizer containing fast-acting and slow release forms of nitrogen, phosphorous, potassium, and calcium.
<LI> For most crops, use only once at planting time.  For longer season crops, such as corn or perennial herbs, feed again halfway through the growing season.
