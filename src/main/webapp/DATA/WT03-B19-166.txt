<H1>Professional services</H1>
<P>
Melbourne is an ideal location for companies which require specialist
support services. As a sophisticated manufacturing and corporate
centre, the city is well endowed with highly talented professionals,
costed very competitively against their Asia-Pacific
equivalents.
<P>

than half those of Hong Kong and Osaka, 22% lower than Singapore,
and 45% lower than Los Angeles.</B></I>
