Data in these fact sheets were provided by Institutions of Higher Education (IHEs) responding to
Council for Exceptional Children (CEC) surveys, National Clearinghouse for Professions in
Special Education (NCPSE) surveys and members of professional associations representing the
related services.  NCPSE maintains databases of these data which are updated as new information
becomes available.  NCPSE makes no claim that these are a complete or comprehensive
list.<BR><BR>
