<p><strong>Zond Systems, Z-40</strong>
<p>The Z-40 is a three-bladed, upwind, rigid hub machine with 
active yaw drive. Zond is developing two versions of its prototype 
550-kW Z-40 wind turbine. One turbine prototype employs a full-span 
pitch control system and sits atop a tube tower.  The other turbine 
uses aileron controls for aerodynamic braking and peak power 
modulation and sits atop a free-standing truss tower.
