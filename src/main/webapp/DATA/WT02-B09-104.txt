<h2>
<br>Decon 51</h2>

<p><b>(Hotel, Motel, Automobile)</b> </p>

<p>The Decon 51 was designed for removing odors and residue from walls
draperies, carpets and furniture and auto interiors. Decon units are ozone
machines which do not produce ions but they can be used in tandem with
Ion-15 ionizers. </p>

<p><b>FEATURES: 
<br></b>up to 415 mg/hr of ozone 
<br>coverage area of up to 
<br>size: 8&quot;H x 11-3/4&quot;W x 9-3/4&quot;D; 16 lbs. shipping wt.
</p>

<p><b>ELECTRICAL: 
<br></b>120v, 60hz operation </p>

<p><b>MECHANICAL: 
<br></b>75 cfm safety fan 
<br>air and ozone controls </p>

<p><b>WARRANTY:</b> 
<br>three year parts and labor </p>

<p><b>APPLICATIONS:</b> </p>

<ul>
<li>chemical storage </li>

<li>commercial laundry-cleaners </li>

<li>garbage accumulation areas </li>

<li>used car smoke smells </li>

<li>building maintenance </li>

<li>hotel/motel/apartment unit clean-up </li>

<li>grocery stores </li>

<li>restaurant kitchens </li>

<li>food/produce storage </li>

<li>surgical area </li>

<li>fire restoration </li>

<li>drapery cleaning </li>

<li>chemical spill clean-up</li>
</ul>

<h2>Decon 52</h2>

<p>The higher-capacity Decon 52 is for cleaning areas where the ozone will
be disbursed by auxiliary fans. The big unit has approximately four times
the capacity of the smaller Decon 51. Both are available in metal or wood
grain exteriors. </p>

<p><b>FEATURES: 
<br></b>up to 1115 mg/hr of ozone 
<br>size: 8&quot;H x 19-3/4&quot;W x 9-3/4&quot;D; 26 lbs. shipping wt.
</p>

<p><b>ELECTRICAL: 
<br></b>120v: 60hz operation </p>

<p><b>MECHANICAL:

<br>air and ozone controls </p>

<p><b>WARRANTY:
