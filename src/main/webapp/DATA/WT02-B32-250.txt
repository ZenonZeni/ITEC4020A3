<H3>STANDARD & POOR'S (SP)</H3>

<B>Standard & Poor's Corporate Descriptions--SP03</B><BR>
Standard & Poor's Corporation<BR>
$8.00/article<BR>
Reports revised annually; new information loaded twice monthly<BR>
(Also in FI, GB, CoRpts)<BR>
Provides detailed reports on more than 9,000 publicly held companies with
securities on the New York, American, Over-the-Counter, NASDAQ, and a variety of
regional and international exchanges, including about 1,000 Canadian companies.<P>

<B>Standard & Poor's Corporate FirstFacts--SP02</B><BR>
Standard & Poor's Corporation<BR>
$4.00/article<BR>
Reports revised annually; new information loaded twice monthly<BR>
(Also in All industries, CoRpts)<BR>
Provides a concise overview of about 10,000 publicly held companies with
securities on the New York, American, Over-the-Counter, NASDAQ, and a variety of
regional and international exchanges, including about 1,000 Canadian companies.<P>

<B>Standard & Poor's Daily News--SP01</B><BR>
Standard & Poor's Corporation<BR>
$1.50/article<BR>
Updated daily; retained for 15 months plus the current month<BR>
(Also in All industries, CoRpts, Day)<BR>
Reports financial statements and news developments in narrative for approximately
12,000 publicly held companies trading on the New York, American, and other
regional and international exchanges.<P>
