<B>Electronic Materials Technology News--ML04</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ml04.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>

Updated monthly, from 9/1/89<BR>
(Also in EC, MG)<BR>

industry, from specialty chemicals and fiber optics through commercialization of
existing technology.<P>

Merton Allen Associates<BR>
$3.00/article; $25.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 6/1/93<BR>
(Also in RD)<BR>
Covers all areas and subjects related to material development and process
equipment design, operation and integrity.<P>

<B>High Tech Ceramics News--ML05</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ml05.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 9/1/89<BR>
(No cross-referencing)<BR>
Explores the use of high-tech ceramic materials in various industries. Covers new
products, patents, and technologies.<P>

<B>Medical Materials Update--ML16</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ml16.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 8/1/94<BR>
(Also in ME, RD)<BR>
Provides coverage on polymers, metals, carbon, ceramics, composites, surface
modifications, biologicals, biomaterials, gels, and membranes, along with their
applications as implants, adhesives, filters, tubing, organs, skin, prostheses,
topicals and coatings.<P>

<B>Microporous Materials Technology News--ML17</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ml17.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Available from 8/1/94--8/1/96<BR>
(Also in RD)<BR>
Provides Coverage on developments concerning polymers, microsponges, inorganic
sorbents, chelates, molecular sieves, clathrates, activated media and surfaces,
zeolites, ion exchange resins and membranes, and other sorbent materials.<P>

<B>Optical Materials & Engineering News--ML07</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ml07.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 9/1/90<BR>
(Also in IT)<BR>
Reports on developing commercialization, business and economic trends, contracts
and patents in the fields of advanced optical materials, detectors and detector
materials, including coatings and solid state laser <BR>
media.<P>

<B>Performance Materials--ML06</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ml06.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
August Pacific Press<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 1/8/90<BR>
(Also in AE)<BR>
Provides market intelligence for advanced materials users and suppliers in the
aviation and aerospace industries, including who's buying and selling, and how
end-users are applying advanced materials.<P>

<B>Plaspec Plastics News--ML09</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ml09.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
D&S Data Resources Inc.<BR>
$3.00/article<BR>
Updated daily, from 8/2/93<BR>
(Also in RD)<BR>
Covers pricing news on feedstocks, additives and resins, new products and
technology, recycling news, regulatory and environmental news, mergers,
acquisitions, expansions and general business news.<P>

<B>Platt's Metals Week--ML01</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ml01.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
The McGraw-Hill Companies Publications Online<BR>
$4.00/article<BR>
Updated weekly, from 9/9/91<BR>
(Also in M-H)<BR>
Provides price and market information about the international nonferrous metals
industry. Sample subjects include quotations on primary metals and ores, supply
and demand variations, and news affecting pricing. (Daily Prices tables are not
available online.)<P>

<B>Surface Modification Technology News--ML08</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ml08.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Available from 6/1/91--12/1/94<BR>
(Also in MG)<BR>
Reports on the advanced engineered surface materials industry. Provides
information to help readers keep up on the competition, examine investment
opportunities, and follow production and process developments.<P>

<B>Thin Film/Diamond Technology News--ML13</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/ml13.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc. <BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 11/1/92<BR>
(No cross-referencing)<BR>
Covers structural carbon, industrial diamonds, diamond and diamond-like films and
coating, and similar materials, such as cubic boron nitride.<P>
