<h1>Australian Centre of Advanced Risk and Reliability Engineering</h1>
The Australian Centre of Advanced Risk and Reliability Engineering Ltd
(ACARRE) is a joint venture of the University of Sydney and the
Australian Nuclear Science and Technology Organisation (ANSTO). It is a
company limited by guarantee, and has the objective of promoting
appropriate application of risk and reliability engineering and
management principles in Australia and the near region. lt operates in
three fields:
<ul>
<li>education, through undergraduate, postgraduate and external courses;
<li>research; and
<li>consulting for industry and government throughout Australia in a
range of industries including the chemical industry, oil refining,
transport, storage and distribution.
</ul>
In undertaking these activities, ACARRE draws on specialist skills from
the University of Sydney, ANSTO and elsewhere.<P>
