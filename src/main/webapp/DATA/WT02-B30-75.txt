<FONT SIZE="+1"><STRONG>Introduction to Investing</STRONG></FONT><BR>
<P>



<STRONG>College.  Retirement.  Comfort.  
Independence.  Peace of Mind.  </STRONG><BR>
Whatever your reasons for investing, they probably involve much more than money. 
They
reflect the
way
you live, the people you love, and the dreams you hope to achieve. 

<P>
Those dreams are as individual as you are.  So you can't rely on facts and formulas
alone. 
You need
an investment approach that combines professional insight with broad flexibility
and
investments that
feel comfortable for you.

<P>
The material here can help.  It explains the basic principles of investing, without
trying to fit
you into anyone
else's mold.  It outlines your options, makes constructive suggestions, and leaves
the
decisions to you
and your investment professional. 

<P>
Many investors want more than just money.  At MainStay Funds<SUP><FONT SIZE="-1"><A
HREF="#1">1</A></FONT></SUP>, we're
helping you
pursue those goals.<P>
