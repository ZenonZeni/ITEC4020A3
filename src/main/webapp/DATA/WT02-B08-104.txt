<H1>Corrections to Homework Solutions</H1>
</CENTER>

The solution to problem 4.2 contains an error.
The angle shown as theta1 in the figure is the angle between
the X0 and Z1 axes. In fact, theta1 should be the angle between
the X0 and X1 axes. Thus, the real theta1 is 90 degrees greater
than the theta1 shown in the figure.
