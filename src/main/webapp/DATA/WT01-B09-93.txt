<h3>Electricore</h3><p>

<blockquote>
<B>Project Title:  Non­Invasive Battery Measurement & Commercial Application<p>

Performing Organization: Premium Power Systems, Delphi Energy & Engine Management Systems
</B><p>

<P>Technical POC: Bill Laletin, Premium, (504) 646­1121 </P>

<P><B>Project Summary:</B></P>

<P>This project is developing a new non­invasive technique to measure battery state­of­charge. "Time Domain Spectroscopy" uses a multidimensional correlation to establish the quantitative form of the battery's transfer functions. This technology has potential applications in all areas of
electrochemistry including military electrochemical storage units, quality control in battery manufacturing, accurate
on­board EV "fuel" gauge, and many others. 
<P>
<B>Project Deliverables: </B></P>

<P>Laboratory proven circuit board state­of­charge indicator </P>

<P><B>Project Schedule:</B></P>

<P>Began September 1995 for completion in 1997. Commercial applications to continue until May
1998.
</P>

</blockquote>
