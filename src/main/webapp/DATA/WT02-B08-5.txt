<h3>Producer Membership</h3>
<p>
<ul>
<li>Voting member of RFA Board of Directors.<BR>
<li>Eligible to serve as a member of Association's Executive Committee.
<li>Provide direction on RFA policy, activities and priorities,
regular attendance at RFA meetings, participation in member conference
calls.
<li>Receive notification of all RFA member meetings and activities.
<li>Receive regular mailings of industry information, news clippings,
bi-monthly  <i>Ethanol Report</i>, and other association documentation
(briefs, hearings, comments, related studies, etc.).
<li>Access to association public awareness information, brochures,
fact sheets and other materials, and industry reports (research,
marketing, etc.) where available.
<li>Responsible for keeping the RFA office informed of significant
industry developments and member activities which would be relevant
to the renewable fuels industry in general (i.e. not proprietary
information).
<li>Responsible for providing copies of industry-related reports
or other documentation of general interest to RFA office, to assist
our efforts in keeping other members informed and facilitating
use of up-to-date information on our materials and activities.
</ul>
<BR>

<CENTER><h3>RENEWABLE FUELS ASSOCIATION MEMBERSHIP APPLICATION</h3></CENTER>
<P>
<hr width="80%">
<p>
RFA's membership year is October 1 to September 30, and renewal
notices will be sent in early September.  New members will be
invoiced by RFA.  Partial year membership fees are pro-rated according
to the number of months remaining in the current membership year.
<BR>

<P>
The applicant agrees that:<BR>
<P>
<ul>
<li>Acceptance of this application confers upon the undersigned
all the privileges and duties of a member of the Association,
and he/she agrees to abide by the Articles of Incorporation and
Bylaws, to support the objectives of the Association and to pay
the dues as set by the Board of Directors.
<p>
<li>Each member may designate one official representative to act
on its behalf in Association matters.
</ul>
