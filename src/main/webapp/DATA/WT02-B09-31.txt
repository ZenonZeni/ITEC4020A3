<STRONG><FONT COLOR="#134193" SIZE=+1><B>Notify the Regulators and Government
Agencies:</B></FONT></STRONG>
<H3>
  <CENTER>
    <I>USA Nationwide
    </I><IMG SRC="images/flagus.gif" WIDTH="67" HEIGHT="49" ALIGN=middle HSPACE="30"
	NATURALSIZEFLAG="3">
  </CENTER>
</H3>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="gopher://marvel.loc.gov:70/00/federal/fedinfo/byagency/executive/consumer/usoca.helpline">Consumer
    Complaints with Business</A> - U.S. Office of Consumer Affairs has a HELPLINE
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.ftc.gov/bcp/conline/fraud.htm">Fraud Incidents</A> -
    The Consumer Assistance Service is maintained by The National Fraud Information
    Center in cooperation with the Federal Trade Commission and the National
    Association of Attorneys General.
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.naic.org/consumer/state/usamap.htm">Insurance</A> - The
    National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC) points you to the
    Insurance Regulators in Your State.
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.usps.gov/csmrguid/usingsvc.htm#fraud">Mail Fraud or Mail
    Order Problems</A> - U.S. Postal Service: Consumer Advocate and Postal Inspector
  <LI>
    <A HREF="gopher://cpsc.gov:70/00/Haz_Report.txt">Product-Related Injuries
    and Deaths</A> - The Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) is interested
    in any incident in which a consumer product was involved with a risk of death
    or injury.
</UL>
<H3>
    <HR NOSHADE SIZE=1>
  <CENTER>
    <I>USA: State By State

<H4>
  Florida
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://legal.firn.edu/units/consumer/consume2.html">Office of the
    Florida Attorney General</A> will assist you in filing a complaint or checking
    on a Business
</UL>
<H4>
  Hawaii
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.hawaii.gov/icsd/lrb/ph_ag.htm">Department of the Attorney
    General</A> provides a listing of contacts
</UL>
<H4>
  Indiana
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.state.in.us/atty_gen/index.html">Attorney General's
    Office</A> will assist in filing a claim
</UL>
<H4>
  Maine
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.state.me.us/pfr/pfrhome.htm">Department of Professional
    &amp; Financial Regulation</A> responds to consumer complaints
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.state.me.us/ag/pubadv.htm">Public Advocate Office</A>
    welcomes utility consumers to voice their concerns about utility service,
    prices or policy
</UL>
<H4>
  Massachusetts
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.magnet.state.ma.us/dpu/dpu.htm">Consumer Division of
    the Department of Public Utilities</A> accepts complaints about utility companies
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.consumer.com/consumer/">Executive Office of Consumer
    Affairs and Business Regulation</A> handles consumer complaints online
</UL>
<H4>
  Missouri
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.state.mo.us/ago/conprot.htm">Consumer Protection
    Division/Office of the Attorney General</A> offers a Consumer Protection
    Hotline
</UL>
<H4>
  New Jersey
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.naic.org/nj/ENFCON.HTM">Enforcement &amp; Consumer Protection
    at the Department of Insurance</A> accepts consumer complaints involving
    all lines of insurance.
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.njin.net/~pgalka/bpu.html#customer">Office of Customer
    Assistance of the Board of Public Utilities</A> investigates complaints and
    assists consumers with utility billing problems.
</UL>
<H4>
  Ohio
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.state.oh.us/consum/">Consumers' Counsel</A> resolves
    complaints individuals have with their utility companies
</UL>
<H4>
  South Dakota
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.state.sd.us/state/executive/attorney/attorney.html">Office
    of the Attorney General</A> will help you become a smarter consumer
</UL>
<H4>
  Texas
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.oag.state.tx.us/WEBSITE/consumer.htm">Office of the Attorney
    General</A> says <STRONG>Get Satisfaction!</STRONG>
</UL>
<H4>
  Washington
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://www.wa.gov/ins/complaints.html">Insurance Commissioner</A>
    accepts complaints involving insurers, agents and brokers
</UL>
<H4>
  Wisconsin
</H4>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
  <LI>
    <A HREF="http://badger.state.wi.us/agencies/oci/ic_sect.htm">Information
    &amp; Complaints Section at the Office of the Commissioner of Insurance</A>
    will assist individuals who are experiencing problems with their insurance
    company or their insurance agent
</UL>
