<STRONG>How does NYLBS initially inform participants of the new
relationship?</STRONG><BR>

NYLBS provides effective communications and education by using
our comprehensive in-house
communications resources,
as well as working with other NYLBS areas
that service
plans.  First, we assess the plan sponsor's and participants' needs.  Then, we
develop a program based on our findings, using on-site meetings, educational
literature, video and slide
presentations, posters, and payroll stuffers to increase participants'
understanding of basic
investment concepts and the benefits of retirement planning.
<P>
<STRONG>What does NYLBS offer as part of its standard communications and education
program?</STRONG><BR>

We have found communicating messages in a multimedia format gets results.  As part
of our standard
services, we develop a comprehensive, multimedia employee communications and
education
campaign for our clients, including:
<P>
<STRONG>Standard Communications And Education Package</STRONG>

<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>Enrollment Kit
<UL TYPE=DISC>
		<LI>Envelope
		<LI>Investment Guide
		<LI>Graphic SPD (NYLBS Format)
		<LI>Enrollment Form/Re-enrollment Form
		<LI>Beneficiary Designation Form
		<LI>Investment Information
</UL>
	<LI>Posters
	<LI>Payroll Stuffers
	<LI>Tent Cards
	<LI>404(c) Supplement
	<LI>Prospectuses
	<LI>Investment Information
	<LI>Quarterly Participant Newsletter
	<LI>Communication Consulting for the Campaign
</UL>
<P>
Our standard offering includes a choice of four diverse themes designed to satisfy
a broad
spectrum of employee populations.
<P>
<STRONG>What additional communications products and services does NYLBS
provide?</STRONG><BR>

NYLBS will work with you to develop a customized communications solution for your
participants' needs.  We offer:
<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>Custom design and production of all print material, videos and related
communications media
	<LI>Employee meetings
	<LI>Video
	<LI>Train-the-sponsor sessions
	<LI>Account projections
	<LI>Human resource letters and announcements
	<LI>Consulting and strategy
</UL>
<P>

	<STRONG>Do participants have access via telephone voice response system
(VRS) to individual
account information?</STRONG><BR>
NYLBS' 401(k) Complete&reg; standard offering includes access to our telephone
voice response
system,
Benefits
Complete&reg;.  Participants can use the VRS to
perform a
variety of functions including:
<P>
<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>Obtain current account balances
	<LI>Model or apply for new loans
	<LI>Inquire about current investment elections
	<LI>Change current investment elections
	<LI>Make existing balance account transfers
	<LI>Request fund information
	<LI>Request a participant statement
	<LI>Change their Personal Identification Number
	<LI>Generate a Statement on Request&reg; for any time period
</UL>
<P>
The system is activated through touch-tone telephone from an 800 number and is
available 7 days a
week, 24 hours a day.
<P>
<STRONG>Does NYLBS offer live representatives to answer participant
questions?</STRONG><BR>

NYLBS' Participant Service Center is available from 9:00 a.m.-8:00 p.m. ET for
participants who need
assistance beyond what is provided through our VRS.  Our service representatives
are available to
provide general industry information, answer inquiries relating to your plan
provisions or investments, provide specific account information and initiate
participant-requested
transactions.
