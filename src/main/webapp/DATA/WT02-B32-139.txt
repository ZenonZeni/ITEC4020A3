<H2 ALIGN="CENTER">Capabilities and Services</H2>
<P>The types of legal and business capabilities and services provided by
Pierson &amp; Burnett fall into four broad categories spanning the life of a
business venture.  Additional information on the specific services offered
within each of thesecategoriess can be accessed below.</P>
<P><IMG SRC="/images/bulletsm.gif" ALIGN="LEFT" BORDER="0" HSPACE="10"><B><A
HREF="regadmin.html">Regulatory and Administrative Matters</A></B></P>
<P>The pursuit of an optimal regulatory environment, receipt of required
administrative approvals, and continuing compliance with governmental
requirements.</P>
<P><IMG SRC="/images/bulletsm.gif" ALIGN="LEFT" BORDER="0" HSPACE="10"><B>Commercial
Transactions and Business Implementation</B></P>
<P>The identification, negotiation, documentation and defense of commercial
transactions, and the guidance of business construction and operation
activities.</P>
<P><IMG SRC="/images/bulletsm.gif" ALIGN="LEFT" BORDER="0" HSPACE="10"><B>Venture
Structuring and Corporate Affairs</B></P>
<P>The formation, governance and mainenance of business entities and the
preparation of corporate agreements.</P>
<P><IMG SRC="/images/bulletsm.gif" ALIGN="LEFT" BORDER="0" HSPACE="10"><B>Legislative
and Policy Guidance</B></P>
<P>The assessment and shaping of the legal and political environments for a
successful buisness venture.</P>
