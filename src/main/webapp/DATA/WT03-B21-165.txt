<P>TVX GOLD ANNOUNCES CLOSING OF US PRIVATE PLACEMENT OF US $150 MILLION OF UNSECURED SENIOR NOTES

<P>Toronto, Ontario, January 16, 1996 ... TVX Gold Inc. announced today that it has completed a private placement in the United States of US$150 million Unsecured Senior Notes bearing interest at 7.51% and due January 15, 2001. The Notes were sold to several U.S. pension funds and life insurance companies. Nesbitt Burns Securities Inc. acted as the placement agent.

<P>Approximately US$20,000,000 of the proceeds will be used to repay drawings on operating bank lines. The balance is available for anticipated capital expenditures, for the acquisition, exploration and development of mining prospects and for general corporate purposes.
