<STRONG>The following electronic mail discussions are currently available:
<P>
     <H3>Mtn-Forum</H3>   This is the common area of the Mountain
Forum.  All who wish to subscribe are welcome and encouraged to
participate in the open discussion.  This list is moderated to
promote discussion.
<P>
     <H3>Mf-Intro</H3> A list dedicated to introducing
subscribers to one another.  New subscribers are asked to post a
few sentences describing themselves and their interest in the
Mountain Forum discussion list.
<P>
   <H3>Mf-Summary</H3>   A weekly read-only summary of the
topics discussed in the open "mtn-forum" list.  This summary is
simply a list made up of the subject line of each posting in the
last week, and a repeat posting of important announcements and
information newly available on-line.  
<P>
<H3>Mf-Lac</H3>  This moderated discussion list focusses on biodiversity and agriculture in the Andes.  It will be conducted in Spanish and all Spanish-speakers with an interest in this area are encouraged to participate.
