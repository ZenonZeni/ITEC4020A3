<H1>WRECKS OF THE WORLD<BR>
Andrea Doria:</H1>
<IMG ALT="Photo" SRC="../PHOTO-1-96/wreck1.gif">
<P>In response to many requests, Mad Dog Expeditions is now running annual Andrea Doria trips. Lying in 240 feet of cold, dark, offshore water, the Doria has been likened to the Everest of diving. The trip will be open to only experienced technical divers with mixed gas training. Trip prices are for two or three day expeditions and are for boat space only. As an additional option on some trips, first time participants may choose to have their dives guided by an expert Doria veteran. All other arrangements such as tank rental, gas fills and food may be made separately.<BR></ul>
