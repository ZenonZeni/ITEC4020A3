If you've been invested in Kemper Funds for a while, you're probably
familiar with our 24-hour automated information system.  By calling
<B>1-800-972-3060</B> and entering your account number and special
personal identification number (PIN)*, you can access specific
account information including :
<P>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
<LI>account balances
<LI>your last dividend or non-dividend transaction 
<LI>current yields and returns
<LI>income or capital gains distributed year-to-date
</UL>

<P>
and initiate certain account transactions including:
<P>
<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
<LI>exchanges between Kemper funds
<LI>EXPRESS-Transfer purchases and redemptions
<LI>telephone redemptions by check
<LI>wire redemptions
<LI>order checks for your Kemper Money Funds account
</UL>

<P>
(*Remember that your PIN number - unless you've changed it - is
the last four digits of your Social Security Number.)

<P>
 In September, <B>three new services</B> were added to the Kemper
ACCESS system.  Now, you can
<P>

<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
<LI>order a duplicate account statement,
</UL>



<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
<LI>order Invest by Mail slips and postage-paid envelopes (or
just postage-paid envelopes) for your Kemper Funds accounts,
</UL>


<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
<LI>create a &quot;fund watch&quot; list, to monitor the performance
of the Kemper Funds you're invested in and funds you're considering
for future investments. 
</UL>


<P>
<STRONG>To Create a &quot;Fund Watch&quot; List:</STRONG>


<P>
Dial 1-800-972-3060 to reach KemperACCESS.  The system will prompt
you through the following steps:


<P>
PRESS 1* for fund prices and performance

<P>
PRESS 2* to create your fund watch list
<P>
<I>Please note that to initiate an EXPRESS-Transfer purchase or
redemption and/or a wire redemption or, for certain institutional
accounts, a telephone redemption by check, you must first elect
these services in writing.  To request a form to establish any
of these services, call Kemper Shareholder Services at 1-800-621-1048.</I>
<P>
<I>The fund or its agents may be liable for any losses, expenses
or costs arising out of any fraudulent or unauthorized telephone
requests pursuant to these services through KemperACCESS or otherwise,
unless the fund or its agents reasonably believe, based upon reasonable
verification procedures, that the telephonic instructions are
genuine.  <B>The shareholder will bear the risk of loss, including
loss resulting from fraudulent or unauthorized transactions, so
long as reasonable verification procedures are followed.</B> 
The verification procedures include recording instructions, requiring
certain identifying information before acting upon instructions
and sending written confirmations.  </I>
