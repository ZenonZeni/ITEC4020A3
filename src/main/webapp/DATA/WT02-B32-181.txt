<BLOCKQUOTE><P ALIGN=Center><img src="FEB.gif"></P><p><H3><CENTER><FONT SIZE=6>February 1 -28, 1997</FONT></CENTER></H3><p>



<hr><p><img src="marley.gif"><B>Caribview</B> is pleased to bring you this wonderful event in a special, two hour Pay Per View broadcast during <B>Black History Month</B>.   The program will premiere on Viewer's Choice <B><FONT COLOR="22612F">February 6, 1997</FONT></B>,<B> Bob Marley's 52nd birthday</B>!<br>  <P ALIGN=Center><BLINK><FONT COLOR="22612F">WE HOPE YOU WILL JOIN US!</FONT>
</BLINK></P><br>
On July 7, 1996, Bob Marley's family performed in front of a record-breaking audience for <B>SummerStage</B> in New York's Central Park.
<B>Marley Magic</B>:  A Marley Family Reunion was a tribute to the late Bob Marley, featuring <B>Rita Marley</B>, <B>Ziggy Marley</B> and the Melody Makers, <B>Julian Marley</B>, <B>Damian "Jr. Gong" Marley</B>, and their Tuff Gang label mate, <B>Yvad</B>, with a special guest appearance by Bob's mother, Cedella Booker.
