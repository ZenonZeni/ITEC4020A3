<b> Facing the Challenges of ADD<br>
The Chesapeake Institute and The Widmeyer Group</b><br>
Video Package<p>
This package of materials is designed to help parents, educators, and health professionals who are struggling to help children with ADD to succeed. The material is based on a synthesis of research about ADD and identified practices that help children succeed both academically and socially. The centerpiece of this effort is two videos--<EM>Facing the Challenges of ADD</EM> (a 30-minute video directed primarily to parents), and <EM>One Child in Every Classroom</EM> (an hour-long video that provides effective teaching strategies). The package also includes a <EM>Users Guide</EM>, an extensive directory of materials, and several reproducible fact sheets. The product was developed with the support of the U.S. Department of Education's Office of Special Education Programs (OSEP).<p>
