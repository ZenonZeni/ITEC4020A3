<B>November 19, 1996 Chicago, Illinois -- </B>Kemper High Income Trust
declared a monthly dividend on October 31, 1996, of $0.075 per
share, payable November 29, 1996, to shareholders of record on
November 15, 1996. This is unchanged from the previous monthly
dividend.
<P>
<B>Activity Summary:</B>  High yield bonds posted positive returns in
October but were inhibited somewhat by economic data that showed
a slow down in economic growth.  Kemper High Income Trust
gained 3.40% in market value and 0.72% in net asset value.  By
comparison, the Salomon Brothers Extended High Yield Market Index
gained 1.16%.
<P>
<B>Fund Objective:</B> To provide the highest current income obtainable,
consistent with reasonable risk with capital gains secondary.
Investment by the fund in lower rated bonds presents special risk
considerations.
