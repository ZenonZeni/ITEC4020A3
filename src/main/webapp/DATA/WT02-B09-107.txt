<h2><font COLOR="#0000FF">Class I Electrostatic High-efficiency Furnace Filter</font></h2>

<p><font SIZE=+1>Aero<b>&#183;</b>Pure Company has available specially constructed
replacement furnace filters which, when used in conjunction with our air
purification models ZN-51, 990 and the Wizard 2 will provide the maximum
cleanup process in the space being treated.</font></p>
<p><font size=+1>In addition, the use of these filters will reduce the amount of
Ozone required to maintain clean outdoor-air-like conditions in the heated space.
Some of the features of these filters are
shown in the accompanying diagram.</font></p>
<p><font size=+1>Because of their design and special
construction, these filters have features not normally found in the ordinary
furnace filter.</font></p>
<p><font size=+1>For example:</font></p>

<ul>
<li>They are twice as effective as typical furnace filters in removing
hair, lint and pollen.</li>

<li>For dust mites and bacteria they are up to ten times more effective
as the ordinary furnace filter.</li>

<li>They are washable and re-useable many times (instructions furnished
with each order).</li>

<li>Properly handled they can be expected to last for for many seasons.</li>

<li>Their high particle removal range reduces the re-cycling of captured
pollutants on the filter surfaces.</li>

<li>They come with a standard five-year warranty against defects in workmanship.</li>
</ul>
