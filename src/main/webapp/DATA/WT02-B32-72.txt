<h2>United Kingdom</h2>
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="http://www.maff.gov.uk/">Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food (MAFF), UK.</A><br>
<i>Home page for MAFF.  Rather limited data.</i><br>
Gives access to:
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.coi.gov.uk/coi/depts/GMF/GMF.html">MAFF Press Releases</a><br>
<i>Reverse chronological listing of MAFF Press Releases. Includes many items on food - regulations, food committee reports etc - but there are a lot on farming and fishing issues.</i><br>
<li><A HREF="http://www.open.gov.uk/maff/pages/bse/bseindx.htm">MAFF information on BSE(Bovine Spongiform Encephalopathy)</A><br>
<i>Provides a lot of official information on BSE</i></ul>
<p>

<LI><A HREF="http://www.open.gov.uk/doh/dhhome.htm">Department of Health (DH), UK</A><br>
<i>Home page for DH.  Rather limited data.</i><br>
Gives access to:
<ul>
<li><A HREF="http://www.coi.gov.uk/coi/depts/GDH/GDH.html">DH Press Releases</A><br>
<i>A reverse chronological listing of DoH Press Releases.  Mostly about hospitals but some about food issues.</i></ul><p>
<p>
<LI><img src="/./images/new.gif"><A HREF="http://www.open.gov.uk/scotoff/scofhom.htm">Scottish Office, UK</A><br>
<i>Home page for The Scottish Office.</i><br>
Gives access to:
<ul>
<li><img src="/./images/new.gif"><A HREF="http://www.open.gov.uk/scotoff/penrec.htm">Pennington Group Interim Report</A><br>
<i>The interim report published by the "Pennington Group" following the E. coli 0157 outbreak in Scotland in December 1996.</i>
<li><img src="/./images/new.gif"><A HREF="http://www.open.gov.uk/scotoff/penstat.htm">Minister's Statement on Pennington Group Interim Report</A><br>
<i>Statement in House of Commons (15 January 1997)in which the Government announces that changes will be made to food hygiene legislation.</i></ul>
<p>
<LI><img src="/./images/new.gif"><A HREF="http://www.hmso.gov.uk">Her Majesty's Stationery Office</A><br>
<i>Home page for HMSO Internet Publications.  Beginning to be useful.</i><br>
Gives access to:
<ul>
<li><A HREF="http://www.hmso.gov.uk/acts.htm">Acts of Parliament</A><br>
<i>Acts are available from 1996</i>
<li><A HREF="http://www.hmso.gov.uk/stat.htm">Statutory Instruments</A><br>
<i>It is planned to have all Statutory Instruments available from 1.1.97.  As of 10.1.97 none have been posted.</i>
</ul>
<p>
<LI><img src="/./images/new.gif"><A HREF="http://www.open.gov.uk/doh/dhhome.htm">Parliament</A><br>
<i>Home page for UK Parliament.</i><br>
Gives access to:
<ul>
<li><A HREF="http://www.Parliament.uk/commons/HSECOM.HTM">House of Commons</A><br>
<i>From here you can access copies of Hansard giving the official account of debates etc in the House of Commons, and some information on Select Committees (particularly the one which looks at Euorpean legislation.</i><br>
<li><A HREF="http://www.parliament.the-stationery-office.co.uk/pa/ld/ldhome.htm">House of Lords</A><br>
<i>From here you can access copies of Hansard giving the official account of debates etc in the House of Lords, some information on Select committees and aspects of the judicial business of the Lords (including judgements delivered since 16.11.96).</i>
</ul>
<p>
<LI><a href="http://www.open.gov.uk/psd/psdhome.htm">Pesticides Safety Directorate</a><br>
<i>Contains some information about how pesticides are controlled in the UK</i><p>

<LI><a href="http://www.open.gov.uk/courts/court/cs_home.htm">Court Information</a><br>
<i>Contains brief description of structure of Courts in the UK</i><p>

<LI><b>Enforcement Work</b><br>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.cieh.org.uk/cieh/">Chartered Institute of Environmental Health (CIEH), UK</a><br>
<i>A new site with limited information at present.</i>
<p>
<li><a href="http://www.xodesign.co.uk/tsnet/">Trading Standards Net</a><br>
<i>This site is aiming to provide access to Trading Standards Departments in England, Wales and Scotland.  Under development at present so rather limited.</i><p>

<li>The following are some examples of pages giving information on the work of local authorities in enformcement:<br>
<a href="http://www.u-net.com/cheshire/consumer/trade.htm">Cheshire - Trading Standards Dept.</a><br>
<a href="http://www.camcnty.gov.uk/trads.htm#top">Cambridgeshire - Trading Standards Dept.</a
</ul><p>

<LI><A HREF="http://dspace.dial.pipex.com/town/square/ac140/index.html">Environmental Health</A><br>
<i>An interesting site with useful links for people interested in environemtnal health.  Maintained by David Denton.</i>
</UL>

</UL>
<H2>Europe</H2>
    
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="http://www.cec.org.uk/">European Commission - UK Office Homepage</A><br>
<i>Starting point for useful UK focussed information from the Commission.  Contains useful pages.</i><br>
Gives access to:<br>
<ul>
<li><A HREF="http://www.cec.org.uk/pubs/we/">The Week in Europe</A><br>
<i>A summary of the weekly activities of the Commission - index of copies avaialable</i><br>
</ul>
<p>
<LI><A HREF="http://europa.eu.int/">Europa Homepage</A><br>
<i>Starting point for the main Home Page of the European Union.  Contains many useful pages and well worth a visit.</i><br>
Gives access to:<br>

<ul>
<li><A HREF="http://europa.eu.int/en/comm.html">The European Commission</A><br>
<i>Information on the Commission</i><br>

<li><A HREF="http://europa.eu.int/en/eu/guide_en/ep.html">The European Parliament</A><br>
<i>Brief information on the European Parliament.  However, see newer site, EUROPARL below</i><br>

<li><A HREF="http://europa.eu.int/en/eu/guide_en/cj.html">The Court of Justice</A><br>
<i>Information on the Court of Justice</i><br>

<li><A HREF="http://europa.eu.int/en/eu/guide_en/council.html">The Council of the European Union</A><br>
<i>Information on the Council</i><br>
<li><A HREF="http://europa.eu.int/en/record/mt/top.html">Maastricht Treaty - Final Act</A><br>
<i>The complete Maastricht Treaty!  Not very easy to use since most of it is actually modifying the Treaty of Rome.</i><p>
</UL>
<p> 
<li><A HREF="http://www.europarl.eu.int/">The European Parliament (EUROPARL)</A><br>
<i>Homepages for the European Parliament.  Contains much useful information, reports etc.</i>
<p>
<li><a href="http://www.poptel.org.uk/john.tomlinson/brf.html">European Parliament Activities</a><br>
<i>This is part of the Home Page of an MEP.  It includes very useful summary reports of debates in the Euorpean Parliament and they are listed by subject.  Worth checking the subjects "Agriculture" and "Consumer/Health"</i><p>
</UL>
<H2>International</H2>
    
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="http://www.fao.org/">Food and Agriculture Organisation (FAO)</A><br>
<i>Home page of the FAO.  Based in Italy</i><br>
Gives access to:<br>
<ul>
<li><A HREF="http://www.fao.org/WAICENT/faoinfo/nutritio/codex/codex.htm">Codex Alimentarius Commission</A><br>
<i>Documents on the Codex including schedule of meetings, listing of reports of Codex Committees, a contents listing of the various Codex Standards and related texts which have been adopted, and a listing of Codex Contact Points (including telephone and fax numbers).</i>
<li><img src="/./images/new.gif"><A HREF="http://www.fao.org/waicent/faoinfo/economic/esn/biotech/tabconts.htm">Biotechnology and Food Safety</A><br>
<i>The complete report of a Joint FAO/WHO Expert Consultation (30/9 - 4/10/96) can be read on this page</i>
<p>
</ul>

<LI><A HREF="http://www.who.ch/">World Health Organization (WHO)</A><br>
<i>Home page of the WHO.  Based in Switzerland</i>
<p>
<LI><A HREF="http://www.wto.org/Welcome.html">World Trade Organisation</A><br>
<i>Home page of the WTO.  Based in Switzerland</i><br>
Gives access to:<br>
<ul>
<li><A HREF="http://www.wto.org/wto/ursum_wpf.html">Uruguay Round - Final Act (24-May-1994)</A><br>
<i>Summary of the contents of the Final Act including the various agreements (e.g. SPS)<br>
<li><img src="/./images/new.gif"><A HREF="http://www.wto.org/wto/agric/spsagr.htm">Agreement on the Application of Sanitary and Phytosanitary Measures (The SPS Agreement)</A><br>
<i>A complete copy of the SPS Agreement is avaialable on this page</i><br>
<li><img src="/./images/new.gif"><A HREF="http://www.wto.org/wto/dispute/bulletin.htm">Dispute Settlement</A><br>
<i>A bulletin giving information on the progress of disputes being considered by the WTO</i></ul>
<p>

<LI><A HREF="http://www.fda.gov/fdahomepage.html">USA Food and Drug Administration (FDA)</A><br>
<i>Home page of the FDA.  CAUTION - Sometimes slow to access.</i><p>

<LI><A HREF="http://www.fsci.umn.edu/foodlaw/foodlaw.html">WWW Food Law Sites</A><br>
<i>A very valuable collection of food law sites assembled by the Department of Food Science and Nutrition at the Unversity of Minnesota, USA.  Very detialed information on the USA system but some additional sites from around the world.  CAUTION - Sometimes slow to access.</i><p>

<LI><A HREF="http://www.iso.ch/">International Standards Organisation (ISO)</A><br>
<i>Home page of ISO.  Some useful information but most documents have to be purchased and are therefore not accessible via the WWW.</i><p>
</UL>
