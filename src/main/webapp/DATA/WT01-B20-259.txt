<h2><a href="1938_mhc.htm">Marihuana Conference </a></h2>

<p>held December 5, 1938 in the UNITED STATES Bureau of Internal
Revenue Building (Room 3003), Washington D. C., Called by the
Bureau of Narcotics of the United States Treasury Department.
(Complete document. Parts are listed below.) </p>

<ul>
    <li><a href="mhc1.htm">Conferees Present</a>. </li>
    <li><a href="mhc2.htm">Opening Statement by H. J. Anslinger</a>,
        Commissoner of Narcotics </li>
    <li><a href="mhc3.htm">Statement of Dr. A. H. Wright</a>,
        Professor of Agronomy, University of Wisconsin, relating
        to growth of Hemp, where seeds are produced, and Hemp is
        grown in United States. </li>
    <li>Statement of Mr. Franklin. Chief of Drug Control, State
        of New York as to growth of Marihuana in that state. </li>
    <li><a href="mhc4.htm">Statement of Dr. B. B. Robinson,</a>
        Bureau of Plant Industry, Department of Agriculture,
        relative to experiments as to World Production of Hemp,
        and quantity of Production in United States and Growth
        and Production of Cannabis, and Comparative Results
        obtained from Seed obtained from various Countries. </li>
    <li>Statement of Dr. John R. Matchett, Chief Chemist, Bureau
        of Narcotics, as to Tests made from Seeds of Hemp in
        various Countries. </li>
    <li>Statement of Mr. Henry Fuller, Consulting Chemist, as to
        his Experience in Growth of Cannabis. </li>
    <li><a href="mhc5.htm">Statement of Dr. James C. Munch</a>,
        Professor of Pharmacology, Temple University, relating to
        Effects of Marihuana on Organs of Body: </li>
    <li><a href="mhc6.htm">Statement of Dr. S. Loewe</a>,
        Pharmacologist, Cornell University Medical College, as to
        Bio-Assay of Marihuana </li>
    <li><a href="mhc7.htm">Statement of Dr. Walter Bromberg</a>,
        Senior Psychiatrist, Department of Hospitals, New York
        City, relating to varying Effects of Marihuana in various
        Classes of Individuals. </li>
    <li><a href="mhc8.htm">Discussion on Pharmacological Phases
        of Marihuana Problem.</a> </li>
    <li>Statement of H. J. Wollner, Consulting Chemist, Treasury
        Department </li>
    <li><a href="mhc9.htm">Statement of Dr. A. H. Blatt</a>,
        Howard University, Relative to Survey of Chemical
        Constituents of Cannabis Sativa. </li>
    <li><a href="mhc10.htm">Statement of Dr. John R. Matchett</a>,
        Treasury Department, containing Report of Department of
        Attacks on Marihuana Problems. </li>
    <li><a href="mhc11.htm">Statement of Dr. Joseph Levine</a>,

    <li>Statement of Mr. Louis Benjamin, Chemist, Treasury

    <li>Statement of Dr. James C. Munch, as to Tests. </li>
