<H2>DSM in the United States</H2>

<P>The United States' electric
DSM programs are well integrated into the system, and gas DSM programmes
are beginning implementation. The primary program delivery agents and
funding source are the utilities. The most common DSM technologies and
measures are, in rank order, 1) heating, ventilating and A/C equipment,
2) audit and building envelope, 3) appliance efficiency, 4)
appliance/load control, 5) lighting, 6) special rates, 7) motors, and 8)
thermal storage. The most common types of DSM programmes are 1) audit,
2) rebate, 3) loans, and 4) others, targeted toward 1) residential, 2)
commercial, 3) industrial and 4) agricultural customers.</P>
