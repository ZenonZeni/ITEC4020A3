Computerized Investing - CI home page</H2> <hr> If you own a computer
but don't use it for investment analysis, start by reading the <A HREF="cibasic1.html">Computerized Investing
Basics.</A><P>

AAII produces a bi-monthly <a href="aboutci.html">magazine </a>devoted
solely to investment related software and information services.  We
cover a variety of topics including: portfolio management,
technical analysis, fundamental screening, and
 on-line services.<P>

We review software objectively to help you decide which pieces are
best for your needs.  Some of our recent reviews include: <A HREF="casimply1.html">Fidelity Online Xpress vs. Schwab's
Streetsmart</A> and the <a href="qfplanner.html">Quicken CD ROM Financial
Planner.</a><P>

The articles listed below will give you a taste of what is offered in our Computerized Investing publication. Find out how to use your computer to analyze and manage your investments by reading the first group of articles below. Read articles from the second group for reviews of computer software programs for investors. Check back here for new articles.<p>
