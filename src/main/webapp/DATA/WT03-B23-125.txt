<TD><P><A NAME="whoweare"></A><B><I><FONT SIZE=+2>Who We Are</FONT> </I></B></P>

<P><A HREF="/time.htm">Founded
in Boston in 1892</A>, Tucker Anthony is a full-service investment banking
and brokerage firm that continues to be the dominant regional firm in the
Northeast, with a strong presence in the Chicago and Washington, DC areas
as well as Europe.</P>

<P>Today, Tucker Anthony offers clients a comprehensive choice of investment
opportunities through standard investment vehicles such as stocks, options,
tax-exempt securities, bonds and mutual funds. The firm is also engaged
in a variety of <A HREF="/special.htm">Other
Services</A> including: Freedom Capital Management Corporation, investment banking, and public finance.</P>
<P>
Tucker Anthony is a member of the New York and American Stock Exchanges
as well as regional exchanges, the NASD and other markets. Client accounts
are protected by the Securities Investor Protection Act, and the regulations
under it, for up to $10 million per customer. Up to $500,000 is covered
by the Securities Investor Protection Corporation (SIPC) and up to an additional
$9.5 million is covered under an insurance policy issued by an affiliate
of Wexford Clearing Services Corporation. Coverage of cash is limited to
$100,000.
