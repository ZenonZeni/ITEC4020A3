<h3><font color="#000080">Bipolar Disorder in Children and Adolescents</font></h3>
<P>
	Although bipolar disorder is often unrecognized or 
misdiagnosed in children and adolescents, a recent study in 
Adolescence found that, when care was taken to conduct a thorough 
diagnostic interview, bipolar disorder was the most common problem 
diagnosed in adolescents referred to psychiatric inpatient units.
