<h2>Company Profile</h2>
<p>Florinsa Farms is a multi-national rose grower, importer,
distributor and chain of retail stores and outlets located
throughout the United States and Europe. Our <a href="farms.htm">farms</a>, <a
href="ecuador.htm">located in the best climate and altitudes of
Ecuador</a>,. have consistently grown award-winning varieties
using the most modern technologically advanced systems available
in the industry while still being environmentally sensitive to
the land and practicing green&quot; conservation methods of
production.</p>
