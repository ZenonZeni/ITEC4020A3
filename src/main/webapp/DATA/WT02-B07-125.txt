<h1> Triton Technology Company Information </h1>
<hr>
<B>Corporate Overview</B>
<P>
Strength in field operations and experience with hardware integration and software development has placed Triton Technology in a position of leadership in specific technological advancements in ocean engineering.  Triton designs, develops, manufactures and markets integrated computer systems and add-on software programs for seafloor imaging. 
<P>
<B>Markets and Customers</B>
<P>
Triton serves the seafloor imaging needs of a wide variety of commercial, scientific and military customers <A HREF="reps.html"> all over the world</A>.  These customers can be grouped according to the following submarket segments:
<UL>
<LI>Geological Survey and Seafloor Mapping
<LI>Hydrographic Survey and Chart Plotting
<LI>Pipeline and Cable Route Survey
<LI>Minehunting and Q-Route Surveillance
<LI>Range and Harbor Security
<LI>Small Object Search
<LI>Analog Tape Processing and Archiving
<LI>Research and At-Sea Services
</UL>
<P>
<B> Products and Technology</B>
<P> Triton's sophisticated imaging processing systems digitize, process, display, print and store sonar and ground penetrating radar images.  These state-of-the-art products have firmly established Triton as the leader in real-time processing and post-processing equipment and technology for seafloor imaging.  Our current product line includes:
<UL>
<LI>Isis&#174; - Collect, display and save any kind of sonar data.  We guarantee compatibility with your equipment!
<LI>Target - Measure, classify and analyze small object on the seafloor.
<LI>Vista - Get the "Big Picture" with this automatic Mosaic engine.  Automatically merges all of your sonar images!
<LI>ROVFlight - See where your ROV, ship or other vehicle is on your mosiac.  Instantly recall any image from the seafloor.
<LI>SeaFlight - "fly" under water in 3-D virtual reality at 60 knots!
</UL>
<P>
<B>Sales and Marketing</B>
<P>Products and services are sold through a worldwide network of <A HREF="reps.html"> representatives</A>, distributors and VARS in over 40 countries.  The representatives work with Triton sales and engineering to develop bids and address customer support issues.
