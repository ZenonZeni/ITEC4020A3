<h3>Hawii Electric Vehicle Demonstration Project (HEVDP)</h3><p>

<blockquote>

<P>
<B>Project Summary:</B>
<P>
<B> </B>Provide online information services to DARPA's EHV program
to facilitate and support cooperative EHV design and development
and rapid dissemination of this technology to the program participants.
<P>
&#149; Collect, manage, and protect shared EHV design and development
data using an advanced clientserver database across the Internet.
<P>
&#149; Provide advanced tools and training for users to access,
understand, and utilize data, particularly through simulation
and modeling capabilities on the network. Produce useful technical
reports and summaries accessible online via Web Browsers or client
software.
<P>
&#149; Provide advanced computing capabilities and services central
to EHV development and marketing .
<P>
<B>Project Deliverables:</B>
<P><UL>
<LI> Data acquisition capabilities access the Internet.
<P>
<LI> OnLine report and summary generation.
<P>
<LI> Timeseries and statistical data analyses
<P>
<LI> Advanced simulation capabilities.
<P>
<LI> Composite materials and structures modeling capabilities.
<P>
<LI> Crash simulation.
</UL>
<P>
<B>Project Schedule:</B>
<UL>

<P>
The center was established in 1993, and the current funding will
allow it to operate for the next two years, to provide services to the DARPA program participants.

</UL>
