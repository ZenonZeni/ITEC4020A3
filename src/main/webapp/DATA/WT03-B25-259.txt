<H2>3. OUTCOMES<BR>
<HR></H2>
The means to the successful delivery of EOC outcomes are: 
<UL>
<LI>Client focus 
<LI>Outcome oriented projects 
<LI>Education and training 
<LI>National leadership and 
<LI>International identity 
</UL>
The ends to these will include: 
<UL>
<LI>Software tools 
<LI>Demonstration projects 
<LI>Advanced methods 
<LI>Documentation 
<LI>Technology transfer 
<LI>Support for the emerging EO industry and 
<LI>Data security for CSIRO. 
</UL>
As Australia's leading research organisation, the products will have high
quality and be based on excellence and depth of science.<BR>
