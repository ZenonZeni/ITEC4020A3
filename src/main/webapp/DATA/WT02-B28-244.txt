<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>Overview</STRONG></FONT><P>

You are not alone if you find certain investment and tax decisions intimidating.
You understand the need
to re-invest the retirement plan money that is yours to take when you leave your
company. However, like
many people, you may be unsure where to start. What you decide will affect your
current and future
income, your tax obligations and your short- and long-term financial security.
<P>
For a century and a half, New York Life and its subsidiaries have provided
investment assistance and
products to people like you. 
<P>
To help you make informed decisions the New York Life Rollover IRA Program offers:
<P>
<UL>
<LI>A team of highly experienced retirement specialists to help
you negotiate the investment, IRA and tax maze step-by-step, and
make informed decisions.
<LI>A wide range of stocks, bonds,
and mutual funds, including the MainStay Family of Funds and
New York Life annuities, all in one convenient account.<SUP><FONT SIZE="-1"><A
HREF="#1">1</A></FONT></SUP>
</UL>
<P>

<STRONG>Specialists who can help you every step of the way</STRONG><BR>
Our Retirement Specialists have years of experience answering investor questions
and providing
advice. When you call to ask a question, you'll speak to an experienced investment
professional who
knows your account and the financial products. They do not work on commission and
are not looking to
make a quick sale. They're looking to provide investors with the help they need. 
<P>

<STRONG>What you need to know to make the right decision</STRONG><BR>
We have provided four informational brochures to help you 
address the issues regarding your retirement savings. Each of
these brochures is written in clear, easy to understand language.
