<H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Some Metacognition References</H2>
<FONT SIZE=-1>Developed by <A HREF="../Students#JO">Jason Osborne</A></FONT><BR>
<IMG SRC="../../Lines/rule18.gif"  WIDTH=620 HEIGHT=22 ALIGN=TOP>
<BR> 
For those of you interested in reading more on metacognition, I have listed some articles you can start with.<BR>
<BR>
Berardi-Coletta, B., Buyer, L. S., & Dominowski, R. L. (1995).  Metacognition and problem-solving:  A process oriented approach.  <I>Journal of Experimental Psychology--Learning, Memory, and Cognition</I>, <I>21</I>,  205-223.<BR>
<BR>
Borkowski, J. G. (1992).  Metacognitive theory:  A framework for teaching literacy, writing, and math skills.  <I>Journal of Learning Disabilities</I>, <I>25</I>, 253-257.<BR>
<BR>
Betsinger, A. M., Cross, J. F., & DeFiore, R. M. (1994).  Problem solving and Metacognition.  <I>Perceptual and Motor Skills</I>, <I>78</I>, 1072-1074.<BR>
<BR>
Cardelle-Elawar, M. (1995).  Effects of metacognitive instruction on low achievers in mathematics problems.  <I>Teaching and Teaching Education</I>, <I>11</I>, 81-95.<BR>
<BR>
Carr, M., Alexander, J., & Folds-Bennett, T. (1994).  Metacognition and mathematics strategy use.  <I>Applied Cognitive Psychology</I>, <I>8</I>,  583-595.<BR>
<BR>
Chamot, A.U., Dale, M., O'Malley, J.M., Spanos, G.A. (1992). Learning and problem solving strategies of ESL students.  <I>Bilingual Research Journal</I>, <I>16</I>, 3-4, 1-34.<BR>
<BR> 
Fogarty, R. (1994). The mindful school: How to teach for metacognitive reflection.  ERIC document # ED377965.<BR>
<BR>
Galin, D. (1994).  The structure of awareness: Contemporary applications of William James' forgotten concept of "the fringe."  <I>Journal of Mind and Behavior</I>, <I>15</I>, 375-401.<BR>
<BR>
Ganz, M. N., & Ganz, B. C. (1990).  Linking metacognition to classroom success.  <I>High School Journal</I>, <I>73</I>, 180-185.<BR>
<BR>
Garner, R., & Alexander, P.A. (1989).  Metacognition: Answered and unanswered questions.  <I>Educational Psychologist</I>, <I>24</I>, 143-158.<BR>
<BR>
Jausovec, N. (1994).  Can giftedness be taught?  <I>Roeper Review</I>, <I>16</I>, 210-214.<BR>
<BR>
Kinnunen, R. & Vauras, M. (1995).  Comprehension monitoring and the level of comprehension in high- and low-achieving primary school children's reading.  <I>Learning and Instruction</I>, <I>5</I>, 143-165.<BR>
<BR>
Lester, F. K. (1994).  Musings about mathematical problem-solving research: 1970-1994.  <I>Journal for Research in Mathematics Education</I>, <I>25</I>, 660-675.<BR>
<BR>
Lucangeli, D., Galderisi, D., & Cornoldi, C. (1995).  Specific and general transfer effects following metamemory training.  <I>Learning Disabilities Research and Practice</I>, <I>10</I>, 11-21.<BR>
<BR>
Marine, C. & Escribe, C. (1994).  Metacognition and competence on statistical problems.  <I>Psychological Reports</I>, <I>75</I>, 1403-1408.<BR>
<BR>
McCrindle, A. R. & Christensen, C. A. (1995).  The impact of learning journals on metacognitive and cognitive processes and learning performance.  Learning and Instruction<I>, <I>5</I>, 167-185.<BR></I>
<BR>
Mevarech, Z.R. (1995).  Metacognition, general ability, and mathematical understanding.  <I>Early education and development</I>, <I>6</I>, 155-168.<BR>
<BR>
Montague, M., Applegate, B., & Marquard, K. (1993).  Cognitive strategy instruction and mathematical problem-solving performance of students with learning disabilities.  <I>Learning Disabilities Research and Practice</I>, <I>8</I>, 223-232.<BR>
<BR>
Osman, M.E., & Hannafin, M.J. (1992).  Metacognition research and theory: Analysis and implications for instructional design.  <I>Educational Technology, Research, and Discussion</I>, <I>40</I> (2), 83-99.<BR>
<BR>
Perkins, D. N., Simmons, R., Tishman, S. (1990).  Teaching cognitive and metacognitive strategies.  J<I>ournal of Structural Learning</I>, <I>10</I>, 285-303.<BR>
<BR>
Perrenet, J. C.,  & Wolters, M. A.  (1994).  The art of checking:  A case study of students' erroneous checking behavior in introductory algebra.  <I>Journal of mathematical behavior</I>, <I>13</I>, 335-358.<BR>
<BR>
Schraw, G., & Dennison, R. S. (1994).  Assessing metacognitive awareness.  <I>Contemporary Educational Psychology</I>, <I>19</I>, 460-475.<BR>
<BR>
Sinkavich, F.J. (1995).  Performance and metamemory: Do students know what they don't know?  <I>Journal of Instructional Psychology</I>, <I>22</I>, 77-87.<BR>
<BR>
Volet, S. E. (1991).  Modelling and coaching or relevant metacognitive strategies for enhancing university students' learning.  <I>Learning and Instruction</I>, <I>1</I>, 319-336.<BR>
<BR>
Weaver, C. A. , & Bryant, D. S. (1995).  Monitoring of comprehension:  The role of text difficulty in metamemory for narrative and expository text.  <I>Memory and Cognition</I>, <I>23</I>, 12-22.
