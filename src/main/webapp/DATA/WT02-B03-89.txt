<HR> <H1> North Atlantic Climatology Interface</H1> <HR>
</CENTER>

<p>
This climatology is presented as a service to researchers who are working in
the Newfoundland Basin and the North Atlantic Ocean. Please <A
HREF="mailto:edk@rafos.gso.uri.edu">notify Ed Kearns</a> if you intend to use
the products from this page; in this way any important information concerning
this climatology may be passed on to you in a timely fashion.

<p>
Upon selecting the appropriate variables below, you will be presented with an
in-line GIF89a image of your selection as well as links to the ASCII and
netCDF format data.
