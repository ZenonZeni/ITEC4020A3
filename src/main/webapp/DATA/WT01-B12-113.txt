Kemper Funds can be organized in seven general categories. For
information on an individual fund, you can access the fund's profile
according to its fund type. Alternately, you can access a fund's profile
by clicking on its name on the <A HREF="spectrum.html">
risk/return spectrum.</A> An interactive
feature available at the bottom of every fund profile enables you to
compare individual Kemper Fund's performance to one of seven broad
market indices.
