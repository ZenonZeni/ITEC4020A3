<p><a name="licensing"><font color="#000080"><strong>LICENSING
ASSISTANCE</strong></font></a></p>
<p>CSL Associates provides expert and timely assistance to
companies and foreign governments with day-to-day export
licensing requirements.</p>
<p>Our Licensing services include:</p>
<ul>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Advising on licensing requirements,
        procedures and policies;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Providing all necessary forms and
        instructions;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Reviewing licenses and agreements
        prior to submission for clarity and accuracy;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Generating license and agreement
        requests for clients in emergency situations;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Hand-delivering licenses and other
        requests to the </font><a href="http://www.state.gov/"><font
        color="#0000FF">Departments of State</font></a><font
        color="#0000FF">, </font><a
        href="http://www.bxa.doc.gov/"><font color="#0000FF">Commerce</font></a><font
        color="#0000FF"> and </font><a
        href="http://www.dtic.dla.mil/"><font color="#0000FF">Defense</font></a><font
        color="#0000FF">, and the </font><a
        href="http://www.ustreas.gov/treasury/bureaus/atf/atf.html"><font
        color="#0000FF">Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco and Firearms</font></a><font
        color="#0000FF">;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Working with government licensing
        officers to ensure the prompt processing of your
        requests;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Closely monitoring cases through
        the entire review process;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Assisting in the preparation and
        submission of commodity jurisdiction requests;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Reporting to you on the status of
        your cases.</font></li>
</ul>
<p>Our licensing system is designed to process your request in
the least amount of time possible. We closely monitor your cases
and work directly with the appropriate government agencies to
stop problems before they occur. At each step in the licensing
process, CSL can save you time and energy: by hand delivering and
picking up licenses we save days in the mail.</p>
<p>CSL will be your licensing advocate. Because of our tracking
capabilities, we note problems that arise during the review
cycle, making calls to or meeting with reviewing agencies to
address their concerns directly. We also prebrief government
officials about complex licensing programs and represent clients
at <a href="http://www.dtic.dla.mil/">Department of Defense</a>
days-in-court and <a href="http://www.state.gov/">Department of
State</a> denial appeal proceedings.</p>
<p>Through the weekly status report, we show exactly where your
licenses are in the system. We can tell you not only that your
license has gone to the <a href="http://www.dtic.dla.mil/">Department
of Defense</a> for review, but the particular branches (<a
href="http://www.army.mil/">Army</a>, <a
href="http://www.navy.mil/">Navy</a>, <a
href="http://www.nsa.gov/">NSA</a>, etc.) that have your case.
Our status report shows you more than where your licenses are in
the system. We keep track of your license expiration dates and
your registration expiration date, and remind you of reporting
requirements for Manufacturing License Agreements and
Distribution Agreements.</p>
<p>In today&#146;s highly competitive international marketplace,
companies cannot afford to be without Washington representation.
Whether CSL becomes your Washington office, or complements the
expertise of your own personnel, we will save you time, energy
and effort.</p>
<p><a name="marketing"><font color="#000080"><strong>MARKETING
ASSISTANCE</strong></font></a></p>
<p>CSL can assist you in your marketing efforts both in the
United States and abroad. Our staff continually monitors the
pulse of the international marketplace, on a regional and
country-by-country basis, and will furnish our clients with
expert advice on the prospects of successfully doing business
with specific customers. Our location in Washington, D.C. affords
us easy access to governmental and unofficial sources of critical
advice, counsel and publications in support of marketing efforts.
Our proximity to foreign embassies enables us to supply clients
with timely, cost-effective customer interface services. In
summary, CSL can provide you with substantial marketing
assistance, to include:</p>
<ul>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Support in preparation of corporate
        marketing plans;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Interfacing with embassies in
        Washington, D.C. on potential requirements;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Determining the advisability of
        pursuing potential leads;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Identification of potential foreign
        agents;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Assistance with potential </font><a
        href="http://www.dtic.dla.mil/"><font color="#0000FF">Department
        of Defense</font></a><font color="#0000FF"> procurements;</font></li>
    <li><font color="#0000FF">Representation at trade shows and
        conventions.</font></li>
</ul>
<p><a name="other"><font color="#000080"><strong>OTHER SUPPORT
SERVICES</strong></font></a></p>
<p>CSL can assist in obtaining passports and U.S. Government
publications, including Federal Register Notices; interfacing
with Congress; and arranging tours. We look forward to assisting
you with your licensing, marketing and Washington, D.C.-area
requirements.</p>
