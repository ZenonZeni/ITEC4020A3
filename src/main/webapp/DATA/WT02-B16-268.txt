<p><strong>Borneo Research Camp</strong>

<p><img border=0 align=left hspace=15 vspace=3 src="images/pvfence.gif">
Halfway around the world, in the jungles of Borneo, Indonesia, PV is helping fight the
extinction of orangutans.  Researchers at Camp Leakey were unable to count on a utility grid to
supply their power, so they looked to  PV as a solution.  That solution was partially funded and
coordinated by DOE.  Scientists designed a 960-W lighting and battery-charging system and
installed it at the camp--securely enough for a harsh environment that included not only
torrential rains, but curious apes!
<P>
See a <a target="_parent" href="http://www.nrel.gov/hot-stuff/press/borneo.html">press release</a> issued in December 1995.
