<h4 align="center">Helpline Fact Sheet </h4>

<h4 align="center"><font color="#000080"><b>Zoloft--Treatment for </b></font><a
href="depress.htm"><font color="#000080"><b>Depression</b></font></a><font
color="#000080"><b>, Anxiety</b></font></h4>

<hr>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><b>Risks or Side Effects:</b></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>- nausea</p>
    <p>- diarrhea</p>
    <p>- tremors</p>
    <p>- insomnia</p>
    <p>- drowsiness</p>
    <p>- dry mouth</p>
    <p>- sweating</p>
    <p>- sexual problems (delayed ejaculation/orgasm)</p>
    <p>- very slight weight loss</p>
    <p>- may be problematic for patients with epilepsy &amp;
    liver or kidney dysfunction</p>
    <p>- may be problematic for pregnant or nursing women</p>
    <p>- may induce mania in those with bipolar disorder </p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><b>Benefits</b></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - fewer side effects than the older tricyclic
    antidepressants</p>
    <p>2 - relieves anxiety, insomnia, &amp; psychomotor
    agitation that accompany depression</p>
    <p>3 - may be taken with or without food</p>
    <p>4 - side effects are usually mild &amp; easily managed</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><b>Dosage</b></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - standard dose is 50 to 200 mg per day taken once per
    day</p>
    <p>2 - may be taken with or without food</p>
    <p>3 - allow 6 to 8 weeks for full efficacy</p>
    <p>4 - do not consume alcohol while taking Zoloft</p>
    <p>5 - avoid caffeine or tobacco which might interfere with
    the way Zoloft is metabolized, thus making a higher dose
    necessary</p>
    <p>6 - avoid simultaneous taking of monoamine oxidase
    inhibitors (MAOIs) (ie. Nardil, Parnate, Marplan) which may
    have lethal results/allow 14 days between</p>
    <p>7 - use caution taking over-the-counter drugs
    simultaneously/consult a doctor</p>
    <p>8 - continue taking for 6 to 12 months to reduce chances
    of relapse</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><strong>What is Zoloft?</strong></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - Sertraline Hydrochloride</p>
    <p>2 - a selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI)</p>
    <p>3 - treats major depressive illness</p>
    <p>4 - acts on the neurotransmitter serotonin </p>
    <p>5 - allows electrical messages in the brain to be
    processed more smoothly &amp; efficiently</p>
</blockquote>
