<H2>Hourly NDBC Equivalent Neutral Wind (9609 and 9610)</H2>
<P>
All data between 9/96 and 10/96 accessible to us 
are converted to equivalent neutral winds (ENW) at both 10 m and 19.5 m height.  There
are different kinds of buoy mixed together, not all of which are suitable
for evaluating NSCAt winds; see station identifier.  The definition and
method of computing ENW is described in Liu and Tang [JPL Publication
96-17, 1996].  Conditions under which ENW is derived are indicated by Flag
(see README file).
