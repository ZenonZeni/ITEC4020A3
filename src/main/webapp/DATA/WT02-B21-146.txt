<TITLE>STEP Lab Hardware</TITLE>

<P>
<B>The STEP lab </B>, formerly the Interactive Image Analysis System (IIAS), currently consists of a small network of Sun MicroSystems computers and associated periperal equipment. <A HREF=uso_guide.html>Approved users</A> can either use the computers directly (in the IIAS lab) or login through one of several X-terminals scattered throughout the Geophysical Institute or via telnet from any computer with access to the Internet.
<P>
<H2>STEP/IIAS Computer Network</H2>
<UL>
<LI> IIAS - Sun 4/280 computer; SunOS 4.1.3
<LI> Sparc1 - Sun Sparc-1 computer; SunOS 4.1.1
<LI> Sparc10 - <A HREF="http://www.sun.com/smi/bang/SS10.html">Sun SparcStation 10</A>; Solaris 2.3
<LI> Sparc20 - <A HREF="http://www.sun.com/smi/bang/SStation20.html">Sun SparcStation 20</A>; Solaris 2.3

</UL>
