<h1>Alameda County Supports Medical Marijuana </h1>

<p> From a posting by NORML </p>

<p> On July 12, 1994, the Alameda County, CA Board of Supervisors
passed a resolution urging the California state legislature to
pass -- and the Governor to sign -- Senate Bill 1364 which would
reclassify marijuana from a Schedule I to a Schedule II
substance, thereby making it available for use by prescription. </p>

<p> Alameda County joins almost a dozen other locales in
California as going on record as favoring legal medical access to
marijuana. </p>
