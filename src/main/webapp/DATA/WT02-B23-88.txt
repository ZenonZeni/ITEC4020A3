<center><h2>Upcoming Events</h2></center>

If you want to support RBIS, we are encouraging RBIS members to sign up to
help with these potential upcoming events:

<ol>

<p><li>An October Golf Tournament. The tournament will be designed to raise
awareness of our efforts and to raise seed money for future events.

<p><li>Putting on a Baseball Expo at the Sacramento Convention Center on January
10-12.

<p><li>Host a Triple A Exhibition Game in March or April.

<p><li>Organizing a Tour of Major League Ballparks for elected officials,
business leaders, interested citizens and the media from the Sacramento region
to learn first-hand how these ballparks have benefited their respective
communities.

</ol>
