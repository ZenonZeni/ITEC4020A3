<center><H2><B>Don't let the sun set on your problems!</b></h2></center>

The Counseling and Student Development Center provides individual personal counseling to students at Rhodes.  The Center can also provide consultations to individuals and groups.  Referrals to community mental health providers are also available.
<P>
Appointments are preferred to ensure that students get quality service.  However, walk-ins are welcome and will be seen as soon as is feasible.  To make an appointment, call 726-3849 between 8:30 a.m. and 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday.
<P>
If you are interested in group counseling, call to talk with a staff member about your interests.  Some on campus groups may be available.  Referrals to off campus groups can be provided.
