Still, the Nasdaq, Dow, S&P 500 and the NY Composite are all performing in the upper third of my fifty International indices and sub indices. The one holdout is Biotechnology, which is lagging at the moment.<p>The Investment Funds Institute (US) announced last week that the 6007 (!!) US mutual funds now had $3.12 billion in investment funds, about half in stock funds and about one quarter each in bond/income and money market funds. Mutual fund assets are now second in size to commercial banks but ahead of private pension fund assets. Fund growth over the past six months has been about thirteen percent.
