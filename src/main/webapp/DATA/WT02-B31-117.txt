<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>New York Life Worldwide Holding, Inc.</STRONG></FONT><P>

In 1995, New York Life Worldwide Holding, Inc., continued to grow its international insurance 
operations, with sales of $58.1 million.  In Argentina, La Buenos Airs - New York Life 
Seguros de Vida S.A. now ranks number two in life insurance sales; in 
Indonesia, P.T. Asuransi Jiwa Sewu - New York Life climbed to number five.
<P>
The Taiwan, South Korea, and Mexico operations exceeded 1995 sales objectives, while aggressive 
recruiting by our subsidiary in Hong Kong resulted in the doubling of its Agency Force.  In
addition, our new international brokerage subsidiary, New York Life Worldwide (Bermuda) Ltd.,
appointed more than 20 gereral agents, which will increase its productivity.
<P>
Our involvement in the United Kingdom insurance market, through the Life Assurance 
Holding Corporation Limited, continues to focus on the acquisition and management of existing 
insurance companies and their portfolios.  And, in mainland China, we received governmental approval 
to open a second representative office.
