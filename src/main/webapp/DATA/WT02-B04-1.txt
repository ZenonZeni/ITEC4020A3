<BIG><STRONG>Internet Freedom Network</STRONG></BIG>
<P>
The first organizational meeting of the Internet Freedom Network was held in Toronto, Canada on May 13, 1996.  The meeting was sponsored by the Open Society Institute and organized by CDT.  Attendees included organizational representatives from Africa, Asia, Western and Central Europe, the United States, and Canada, as well as public policy specialists from online service and Internet software companies.
