<h3><font color="FF6666">Nano<img src="dot.gif">nmr&#153; probe</font></h3>

For applications limited by small sample quantities, the Nano<img src="dot.gif">nmr probe 
delivers extraordinary spectra. Whether you're determining the structure 
of synthetic intermediates, isolated natural products, impurities, or 
metabolites, a Nano<img src="dot.gif">nmr probe will routinely provide outstanding spectra 
from microgram, and even nanogram, quantities of sample.<P> 


<h3><font color="FF6666">Transmitter Presaturation</font></h3>

The GEMINI 2000's excellent water suppression capabilities make the study 
of biomolecules in 90% water routine. The broadband GEMINI 2000 systems are 
equipped with linear amplifiers, resulting in  phase-coherent transmitter 
presaturation and optimal stability between presaturation and hard pulses. 
Novice users can easily perform fully automatic solvent suppression 
experiments yielding consistently excellent results.<P>
