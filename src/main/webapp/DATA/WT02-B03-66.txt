<TITLE>What's New</TITLE>

<H1>New on the DOT WWW</H1>
Here are the latest happenings on the DOT Web scene.  

<HR>
<H3>January 3, 1997</H3>
We have replaced our search engine with Excite for Web Servers.  Check out
our new <A HREF="/search/">search form</A>.  Your search results will now
reflect the true location of the document on our web server rather than the
document ID of a search database.<P>

<H3>January 1, 1997</H3>
Happy New Year.<P>

<H3>December 11, 1996</H3>

The Federal Railroad Admin is now located on their own web server.  The new
URL is "<A HREF="http://www.fra.dot.gov/">http://www.fra.dot.gov/</A>".<P>

<H3>November 22, 1996</H3>
The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration announced a comprehensive 
strategy to improve air bag technology while minimizing the danger to children 
and at-risk adults.  A <A HREF="http://www.dot.gov/affairs/nht8196.htm">press 
release</A> details the steps announced Friday.  Additional information on air 
bag issues can be obtained from the NHTSA web site at 
<A HREF="http://www.nhtsa.dot.gov">http://www.nhtsa.dot.gov</A>.<P>

<H3>October 23, 1996</H3>
We've done some re-organization on our server directories.  Most of the 
subdirectories (sections) have been moved up one level (getting rid of the
"/dotinfo/" part of the URL.  If you encounter any problems accessing pages
that fall under the previous directory structure, please notify our 
<A HREF="mailto:webmaster@www.dot.gov">webmaster</A>.<P>

The "<A HREF="/help/webmap.html">DOT Web Server Quick Locator</A>" has been
updated with more sections, as well as corrected links to reflect the new
directory structure.<P>

<H3>October 21, 1996</H3>
The DOT along with the University of Tennessee is sponsoring a conference
entitled "<A HREF="/general/announce/btb-invite.html">Building Transportation's
Bridges to the 21st Century</A>".  More information is available under our 
<I><A HREF="/general/announce/announce.html">Events and Announcements</A></I> 
Section.<P>

<H3>October 8, 1996</H3>
The <A HREF="/ost/docr/">Departmental Office of Civil Rights</A> is 
now online.<P>

<H3>October 1, 1996</H3>
The <A HREF="/oig">Office of Inspector General</A> has gone on-line.
Selected reports are soon to follow.<P>
