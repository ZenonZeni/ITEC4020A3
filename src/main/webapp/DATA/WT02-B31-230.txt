Frequently Asked Questions
<BR><BR>
Q:  Who can purchase the software detailed in this catalogue?
<BR><BR>
High School Students qualify to purchase for all the products in this catalogue except Microsoft products & SimuLink, MatLab and Labview products.  You must be a full-time student to qualify for all Autodesk and Addison-Wesley’s products.
<BR>
College and University Students qualify to purchase for all the products in this catalogue.  You must be a full-time student to qualify for all Autodesk and Addison-Wesley’s products.
<BR>
Faculty and Professional Staff qualify for all products.
Institutions qualify to purchase all products except for Autodesk, Addison-Wesley software and specially marked Student/Faculty Edition Only software.  
<BR>
All Autodesk pricing in this catalogue is for student, faculty and staff.  Call for lab pricing for Alberta and British Columbia.
Autodesk products are for personal use only.  Not for institutional or commercial use.
<BR><BR>
Q:  How can I order these products?
<BR><BR>
Complete the order form.  Fax or mail your order with proof of student/faculty/staff status and payment to Merlan Scientific Ltd.  
<BR><BR>
Q:  I want to purchase Autodesk/Addison-Wesley products, but my student card does not indicate full-time status.  How do I prove I have full-time status?
<BR><BR>
Have a faculty member sign the Student/Faculty Verification Form (Section A), or submit a document from your institution indicating your full-time schedule or status.
<BR><BR>
Q:  How quickly will I get my new software?
<BR><BR>
If you pay by VISA, Mastercard, certified cheque or money order your software will be shipped the same business day your payment is processed (unless item is out of stock).  Orders with personal cheques will be held for up to 10 days to ensure clearance.  If you have not received your software within 14 days of submitting your order call for a trace.  All orders are shipped via courier, therefore, if you are in a major center one to two delivery is common.
<BR><BR>
Q:  How can you offer me such great prices?
<BR><BR>
As an Authorized Educational company we qualify to sell these full version software products to qualified students, faculty and staff at educational institutions at discount prices.
<BR><BR>
Q:  Can I return software?
<BR><BR>
Software can be returned for a full refund, only if the sealed software package has not been opened and the manual has not been damaged.  A restocking fee of 15% or $35 (whichever is less) will be charged.  Shipping charges will not be paid by Merlan Scientific Ltd.  Shipping charges to return goods will not be paid by Merlan Scientific Ltd.
<BR><BR>
Q:  Where is Merlan Scientific Ltd., located?
<BR><BR>
We are located in Georgetown, Ontario (30 Km northwest of Toronto).  <A HREF="../reachus.html">Contact</A> us for more information.
