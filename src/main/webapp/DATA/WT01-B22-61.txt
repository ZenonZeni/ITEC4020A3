<h2><font size="4">TABLE 1 Chemical Constituents of Cannabis*<br>
</font></h2>

<hr>

<p><font size="5"></font>&nbsp;</p>

<p>1. Cannabinoids: 61 known </p>

<p>a. Cannabigerol (CBG) type: 6 known<br>
b. Cannabichromene (CBC) type: 4 known<br>
c. Cannabidiol (CBD) type: 7 known<br>
d. D-9-Tetrahydrocannabinol (D-9-THC) type: 9 known<br>
e. D-8-Tetrahydrocannabinol <i>(A-8-THC) </i>type: 2 known<br>
f. Cannabicyclol (CBL) type: 3 known<br>
g. Cannabielsoin (CBE) type: 3 known<br>
h. Cannabinol (CBN) type: 6 known<br>
i. Cannabinodiol <i>(CBND) </i>type: 2 known<br>
j. Cannabitriol (CBT) type: 6 known<br>
k. Miscellaneous types: 9 known<br>
1. Other cannabinoids: 4 known </p>

<p>2. Nitrogenous compounds: 20 known </p>

<p>3. Amino acids: 18 known </p>

<p>4. Proteins, glycoproteins, and enzymes: 9 known </p>

<p>5. Sugars and related compounds: 34 known </p>

<p>6. Hydrocarbons: 50 known </p>

<p>7. Simple alcohols: 7 known </p>

<p>8. Simple aldehydes: 12 known </p>

<p>9. Simple ketones: 13 known </p>

<p>10. Simple acids: 20 known </p>

<p>11. Fatty acids: 12 known </p>

<p>12. Simple esters and lactones: 13 known </p>

<p>13. Steroids: 11 known </p>

<p>14. Terpenes: 103 known </p>

<p>15. Noncannabinoid phenols: 16 known </p>

<p>16. Flavanoid glycosides: 19 known </p>

<p>17. Vitamins: 1 known </p>

<p>18. Pigments: 2 known<br>
</p>

<p>Source: Adapted from Turner, 1980. </p>
