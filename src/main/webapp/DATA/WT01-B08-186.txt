<IMG SRC="rainbwln.gif" height=14 width=600></CENTER><BR> <BR>

<FONT SIZE=3>The Bureau of Export Administration stocks the various forms required to comply with the Export Administration Regulations.  These forms are <B>only</B> available through the mail, they cannot be faxed or sent by e-mail.  All Bureau of Export Administration forms are available free of charge.   Limited quantities are available from BXA's <A HREF="http://www.primenet.com/~bxawest"><B><I>Western Regional Office</I></B></A> in California, and most Department of Commerce's, <A HREF="http://www.ita.doc.gov"><B><I>U.S. and Foreign Commercial Service</I></B></A> District Offices. <P> 

To order forms, you may submit a request by fax at (202) 219-9179 or call our Exporter Counseling Division at (202) 482-4811.<P>
