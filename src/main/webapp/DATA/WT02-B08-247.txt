<h2>Welcome to the home page of the 
<a href="http://www.scar.org">SCAR</a> 
Working Group on Glaciology,
agreed on 7 August 1996 during the XXIV SCAR in Cambridge.
</h2>
<hr>
This home page is hosted by the ICSU 
<a href="../wdcc.htm">World Data Centre C (Glaciology)</a> 
based at the <a href="../home.htm">Scott Polar Research
Institute</A> in the <a href="http://www.cam.ac.uk">University of 
Cambridge</a> U.K. The main WWW home page for
<a href="http://www.scar.org">SCAR</a> is at 
ICAIR, New Zealand.

<b>International Programmes</b> related to this Working Group incude

<ul>

<li><a href="http://www.esf.org/eismint">EISMINT (European Ice Sheet 
Modelling Initiative)</a> hosted in <i>France</i>
<ul>
<li><a href="eismint.htm">EISMINT Model Intercomparison</a><img 
src="../images/new.gif">
</ul>


<li><a href="http://www.antcrc.utas.edu.au/scar/">GLOCHANT (the SCAR Global
Change Programme)</a> - <i>(Australia)</i>

<li><a href="http://www.antcrc.utas.edu.au/scar/ismass.html">ISMASS (Task
Force on Ice Sheet Mass Balance and Sea Level Contributions)</a> 
<i>(Australia)</i>

<li><a href="http://www.natice.noaa.gov">NIC (The National Ice Center)</a>

<i>USA</i>

<li><a href="http://www.antcrc.utas.edu.au/scar/pice.html">PICE 
(Planning Group on Palaeoenvironments from ice cores)</a> <i>(Australia)</i>

<li><a href="http://www.igbp.kva.se/index.html">IGBP (International Global 
Change Programme)</a> <i>(Sweden)</i>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.igbp.kva.se/pages.html">IGBP PAGES (Past Global 
Changes)</a>  The PAGES <a href="http://www.ngdc.noaa.gov/paleo/pages.html">

</ul>
<li><A HREF="http://igloo.gsfc.nasa.gov/wais">WAIS (West Antarctic Ice Sheet)
</A> from Bob Bindschadler, NASA Goddard Space Flight Center <i>(USA)</i>

</ul>
