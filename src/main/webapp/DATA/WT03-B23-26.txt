 <P align=center>This show continues our in depth discussions of business services available to companies dealing in emerging media and technologies.


<p align=center>Let the experts tell you what works!

<p align=center><B>John Hummer</B>, "We look for the following in a company:"
<OL align=center>
<LI>Market place the company is going after
<LI>Management - they don't have to have done this before to succeed
<LI>The technology itself.
</OL>


<BR>
<p align=center><B>Alan Koenigsberg</B>, "Although we looked at many venture capitalists for funding, we decided to self-fund."  
<br>He admits it's a difficult route but he is proud of his accomplishments, which include comprehensive on-line service to the food service industry.

<BR>
<p align=center><B>Peter Ling</B> helps companies set a successful path, regardless of funding needs.  He provides the management and structure to help assure a prosperous future.
<BR>
<p align=center><B>Dean Gloster</B>, "We deal with many clients, anyone investing in the early stage of the company invests in the Idea, but they are actually investing in the entrepreneur himself.  Patterns don't apply to everyone and you have to be flexible."

<BR>
<p align=center> Companies need to be able to communicate quickly what the product is and how it will help the consumer.
