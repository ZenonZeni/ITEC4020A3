<h3>What's in it for you?</H3>
<P>
These easy-to-use, energy-saving tips could <b>save you 10% to 50%</b> on 
your energy bills. For example, if you own your own building,<b> your energy savings will go straight to your bottom 
line</b>--dollars that you can apply toward business expansion or other capital improvements.  Even if you rent space, you probably pay energy bills; these tips can still boost your profits. They will show you how you can use energy more efficiently in six major areas: lighting, buildings, HVAC equipment, business machines, motors, and  vehicles.
