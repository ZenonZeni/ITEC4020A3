<P>Welcome to the home page for the <B>&quot;RAFOS Float Group&quot;</B>
in the Physical Oceanography Department at the University of Rhode Island's
<A HREF="http://www.gso.uri.edu/">Graduate School of Oceanography.</A>
Members of the group, headed by <A HREF="tom.html">Dr. Tom Rossby</A>,
are listed below. Our research activities focus on the dynamics and kinematics
of ocean currents, mainly in the Gulf Stream and North Atlantic Current
(NAC) regions, and on istrumentation development. Learn more about our
research activities by clicking below. This page is currently under construction,
so is continually being revised. For comments or questions, please contact
Sandra Anderson-Fontana (<A HREF="mailto:sandy@yvette.gso.uri.edu">sandy@yvette.gso.uri.edu</A>).</P>
