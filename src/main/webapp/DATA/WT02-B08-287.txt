<TABLE><TR><TD COLSTART="1"><H1>Galveston Bay "Float-Off"</H1>
<p>
<h3>This past Spring, middle-school and high-school students from Texas and Colorado participated in the <a href=http://www.tsgc.utexas.edu/tsgc/buoy.html>TOPEX Buoy Calibration Experiment.</a>
<p>
They floated one-of-a-kind shallow water buoy-type platforms carrying satellite ranging equipment and other science instruments. Their purpose was to better understand the oceans and bays near Galveston, Texas and help calibrate the TOPEX/Poseidon satellite. </h3><ALIGN="BOTTOM"></TD><TD COLSTART="2"><img src="images/float_off.gif" alt="Galveston Bay Float-Off"></TD></TR></TABLE>
