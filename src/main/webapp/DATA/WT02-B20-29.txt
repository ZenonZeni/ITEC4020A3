<strong>INTERNET SITES</strong><P>

<ul>
<li><a href=http://www.ed.gov/money.html> URL: http://www.ed.gov/money.html</a><br>
includes links to Student Guide to Financial Aid, What Should I Know About ED Grants, Education Department General Administrative Regulations (EDGAR), Guide to U.S.Department of Education Programs, Federal Register, Commerce Business Daily, and ED Grant Announcements.<P>

<li><a href=http://204.168.17.2:80/http://www.finaid.org/>  URL: http://204.168.17.2:80/http://www.finaid.org/</a><br>
maintained by Mark Kantrowitz, author of The Prentice Hall Guide to Scholarships and Fellowships for Math and Science Students, this site includes links to information about student aid.<P>

<li><a href=http://www.yahoo.com/Education/Financial_Aid> URL: http://www.yahoo.com/Education/Financial_Aid</a><br>
includes links to various scholarships and grant search services.<P>

<li><strong>National Science Foundation Science and Technology Information System (STIS)</strong><br> To access the NSF system, telnet to STIS.NSF.GOV. At the login prompt, type "public". For a user id, type in your first initial and last name with no spaces. This does not change the login id; you must continue to use "public" at the login prompt.</strong><P>

<li><strong>Federal Education Info. Service (FEDIX)</strong><br>
FEDIX links higher education communities with the federal government to 
facilitate research, education, and services. To access information on 
scholarships, fellowships, grants, and other resources, telnet to FEDIX.FIE.COM. At the login prompt, type "new". Select 1. FEDIX at the main menu. The first time that you log on, you will be asked for specific information, including name and institution. After the first time, you will be able to type in your chosen user ID without answering all the questions.<P>

<li><strong>Minority On-Line Info. Service (MOLIS)</strong><br>
A service of FEDIX, MOLIS provides information on resources for minorities. To access MOLIS, telnet to FEDIX.FIE.COM. At the login prompt, type "new". Select 2. MOLIS. (See directions for FEDIX.) <P>

<li><strong>Higher Education Opportunities for Minorities and Women (HERO)</strong><br>
A service of the FEDIX, HERO provides information on resources for women and 
minorities in higher education. To access HERO, telnet to FEDIX.FIE.COM. At the login prompt, type "new". Select 3. HERO. (See directions for FEDIX.)<p>

<li><strong>Office of Educational Research and Improvement (OERI)</strong><br>
The OERI Gopher server provides public access to education research, statistics, and information about the US Department of Education and its programs. To access, gopher to: gopher.ed.gov.<p>

<li><strong>Dept. of Education site</strong><br>
Grant Programs and Funding Availability<br>
<a href=gopher://gopher.os.dhhs.gov/11/Topics/grantsnet/
funding> URL: gopher://gopher.os.dhhs.gov/11/Topics/grantsnet/
funding</a><P>

<li><strong>GRANTS-L on LISTSERV@GSUVM1.GSU.EDU</strong><br>
Grants and Funding for International Education and Research
GRANTS-L, an initiative of the Regents' Global Center of the University System of Georgia, promotes external funding for international education and research.  Specific topics include: proposal writing and editing; federal/state laws and regulations; campus policies and procedures; animal care and use; misconduct in science; procurement integrity; consulting; cost sharing; publication rights; budget development; direct and indirect costs; grant/contract administration; client relations; Internet resources; electronic editing and software; and so on. To subscribe, send an e-mail message to:<P>

<dd>LISTSERV@GSUVM1.GSU.EDU<p>

In the body of the message, send the following:<br>
sub grants-l YourFirstName YourLastName</ul><P>
