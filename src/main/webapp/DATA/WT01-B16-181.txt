<H2>
Location of the U.S. Geological Survey, Water Resources Division, Western 
Region Branch of Regional Research and the Regional Hydrologist's Office
</H2>

The office is located in Menlo Park, California, about half-way
between San Francisco and San Jose on the large penninsula west of San
Francisco Bay.<P>

Three location maps are available:
<UL>
  <LI>A small scale (large area) map of the 
      <A HREF="http://online.wr.usgs.gov/info/mparea1.html">
      San Francisco Bay Area</A>
  <LI>A medium scale (medium area) map of the 
      <A HREF="http://online.wr.usgs.gov/info/mparea2.html">
      Menlo Park Area</A>, including the Deer Creek Facility in
      Palo Alto and the Marine Facility in Redwood City
  <LI>A large scale (small area) map of the 
      <A HREF="http://online.wr.usgs.gov/info/mparea3.html">
      USGS Menlo Park Campus</A>
</UL>

The office is in Building 15 of the Western Regional Headquarters of
the U. S. Geological Survey at 345 Middlefield Road.  It is most
easily reached from U.S. 101, the Bayshore Freeway, but it can also be
reached from U.S. 280.<P>

<H2>
San Francisco Airport to the U.S.G.S. Western Region Headquarters
</H2>

Get instructions at the airport for getting to US 101 (Bayshore
Freeway) south bound.<P>

Go south on US 101 17.5 miles to the first Willow Road exit.<P>

Go west on Willow Road 1 mile to Middlefield Road (stop light, Chevron
station on the right).<P>

Turn right on Middlefield Road and go ~ 0.3 mile to Survey Lane on the
left.  Watch for the stone U.S. Geological Survey gatepost.<P>

Turn left on Survey Lane, proceed to the visitor parking around the
flag pole.  <P>

Bldg. 15 - the <A HREF="http://online.wr.usgs.gov/info/mparea3.html">
McKelvey Building</A> - is the large building you passed on the right after 
turning onto Survey Lane

<H2>
San Jose Airport to the U.S.G.S. Western Region Headquarters
</H2>

Get instructions at the airport for getting to US 101 (Bayshore
Freeway) north bound.<P>

Go north on US 101 about 14.5 miles to the west bound Willow Road exit.<P>

Go west on Willow Road 1 mile to Middlefield Road (stop light, Chevron
station on the right).<P>

Turn right on Middlefield Road and go ~ 0.3 mile to Survey Lane on the
left.  Watch for the stone U.S. Geological Survey gatepost.<P>

Turn left on Survey Lane, proceed to the visitor parking around the
flag pole.  <P>

Bldg. 15 - the <A HREF="http://online.wr.usgs.gov/info/mparea3.html">
McKelvey Building</A> - is the large building you passed on the right after 
turning onto Survey Lane
