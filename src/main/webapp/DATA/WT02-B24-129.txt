<h1 align=center>Table 3. Biological Correlates</h1> 
<hr>
<dt><b>Depression</b> 
<dd>Abnormal neuroamine metabolism
<dd>Sleep rhythm disturbance 
<dd>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Early-onset REM sleep 
<dd>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fragmented stage IV sleep   
<dd>Aberrant neuroendocrine secretion 
<dd>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nonsuppressible cortisol secretion 
<dd>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Blunted thyrotropin release 
following TRH  
<dd>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Blunted growth hormone release    
<dd>Abnormal left limb electrodermal response  
<dd>Left limb motor hemisyndromes  
<dd>Right cerebral (parietal) cognitive dysfunction  
<dd>Hypovigilance  
<dd>Right cerebral hypometabolism  
<dd>Response to neuroamine re-uptake blocking drugs<br><br> 
 
<dt><b>Mania</b> 
<dd>Abnormal neuroamine metabolism<br> 
<dd>Right limb motor hemisyndromes<br> 
<dd>Left cerebral cognitive dysfunction<br> 
<dd>Response to dopamine receptor blocking drugs<br> 
<dd>Response to lithium<br><br> 
 
<dt><b>Primary Disorder of Vigilance</b> 
<dd>Left limb motor hemisyndromes 
<dd>Hypovigilance 
<dd>Hypersomnolence<br><br> 
 
<dt><b>Narcolepsy</b> 
<dd>Inappropriate REM-onset sleep activity 
<dd>Hypovigilance 
<dd>HLA-DR2 phenotype<br><br> 
 
<dt><b>Learning Disability (Right Hemisphere)</b> 
<dd>Subtle left limb hemiatrophy 
<dd>Left limb motor hemisyndromes 
<dd>Right cerebral hypometabolism<br><br> 
 
<dt><b>Sociopathy</b> 
<dd>Absence of cognitive dysfunction 
<dd>Absence of parietal-temporal dysfunction 
<dd>Absence of motor abnormalities<br><br> 
 
<dt><b>Learning Disability (Left Hemisphere)</b> 
<dd>Subtle right limb hemiatrophy 
<dd>Right limb motor hemiatrophy 
<dd>Left cerebral hypometabolism<br><br>
