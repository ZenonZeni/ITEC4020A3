<CENTER><H2>GUIDE TO THE CHILD'S LEARNING STYLE (GCLS)</H2></CENTER><P>
<CENTER><EM>Stott, Denis H. et al.</EM></CENTER><P>
<BR>The GCLS is a brief questionnaire completed by the classroom teacher. By  examining the child's learning patterns, an early assessment of how a child  will perform academically can be determined. It yields scores along three  dimensions of learning style: avoidant rejecting, inattentive distractible, and apprehensive  unresponsive. These noncognitive dimensions supplement cognitive ability data in predicting school success.<BR>
*NOTE* Also see Study of Children's Learning Styles listed separately in  this catalog.<BR>
*NOTE* The manual for this test is the same as for the Study of Children's Learning Styles.<BR>
RANGE: kindergarten<BR>
ADMINISTRATION: 2 minutes<BR>
