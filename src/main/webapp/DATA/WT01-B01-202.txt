<H2><CENTER><U>AESOP Key Benefits<BR>
</U></CENTER></H2>

<P>
<A NAME="OLE_LINK1">AESOP is a robotic arm that holds and moves
the laparoscope for the surgeon during minimally invasive procedures.
AESOP key benefits include:</A><BR>

<H3>To the Hospital </H3>

<UL>
<LI>Eliminates excessive personnel costs associated with salaries,
overtime, sick leave and employee benefits (FTE).
<LI>Enhances the hospital's image as offering the latest in healthcare technology.
<LI>Encourages surgeons to bring their patients to a hospital
where robotic enhancement technology is made available.
<LI>Provides an assistant 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.<BR>

</UL>

<H3>To the Surgeon </H3>

<UL>
<LI>Frees both hands to perform more efficient surgery and acts
as a third arm. 
<LI>Returns complete procedure control to the surgeon.
<LI>Shortens procedure time allowing the surgeon to utilize his
or her time more efficiently.
<LI>Heightens procedure accuracy by keeping video image completely
stable.
<LI>Eliminates miscommunications and frustration between surgeon
and assistant. 
</UL>

<H3>To the Nurse </H3>

<UL>
<LI>Allows the scope holder to perform more responsible productive
tasks.
<LI>Eliminates fatigue associated with scope holding.
<LI>Less time under anesthesia.
<LI>Allows nurses to provide increased patient care and attention.

</UL>
