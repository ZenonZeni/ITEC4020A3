<P ALIGN=CENTER><B><FONT SIZE=5>Protect Yourself: WHILE YOU
TRAVEL</FONT></B></P>

<P><STRONG>THINK BEFORE YOU EAT!</STRONG><BR>

<BR>

Travelers need to be aware that since no typhoid vaccine is 100%
effective, vaccination is not a substitute for careful attention to
food and water.<B> Always think before you put
</B><STRONG>anything</STRONG><B> in your mouth!</B></P>

<H1><IMG SRC="Do.gif" WIDTH=54 HEIGHT=54 BORDER=0 ALIGN=middle
<A HREF="image7.map">DO'S</A>
</H1>

<UL>
   <LI>Drink <B>water</B> only if you know it's been <B>boiled</B> or
   treated. Coffee and tea are safe if made with water that has
   boiled
   
   <LI>Drink <B>canned or bottled</B> carbonated water, soft drinks,
   beer and wine.
</UL>

<P><B>Remember:</B></P>

<UL>
   <LI>The <B>outside of wet containers</B> should be dried BEFORE
   opening and pouring.
   
   <LI>Drink directly from the bottle after <B>wiping</B> the
   container's lip - UNLESS the bottles have been stored in a wet
   cooler, since contaminated water can seep under the cap.
   
   <LI>Eat ONLY fruit you can <B>peel yourself</B>. Remember,
   TOMATOES are classified as fruits and should be peeled.
   
   <LI>Eat only food that has been <B>cooked</B> AND IS STILL HOT.
   
   <LI>Eat pasteurized dairy products.
</UL>

<H1><IMG SRC="Don't.gif" WIDTH=54 HEIGHT=54 BORDER=0 ALIGN=middle

<A HREF="image7.map">DON'Ts</A></H1>

<UL>
   <LI>Don't eat raw or partially cooked fish or shellfish (including
   ceviche) .
   
   <LI><B>Don't use ice cubes</B> - where water is contaminated, ice
   will be too.
   
   <LI>Don't eat leftovers.
   
   <LI>Don't eat watermelon - water is often injected to increase
   weight
   
   <LI>Don't eat <B>salads</B> or leafy greens - they may have been
   washed with contaminated water.
   
   <LI>Don't eat food from <B>street vendors</B>
   
   <LI>Don't use tap water to <B>brush your teeth</B> -use bottled
   carbonated water.
   
   <LI>Don't lay your toothbrush down on the sink or countertop - it
   could be contaminated. Cover the bristles with a plastic cap that
   snaps on .
</UL>
