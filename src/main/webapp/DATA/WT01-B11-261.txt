<H4>
<B>Performance Highlights</B>
</H4>
<UL>
<LI>Following a three-month rally, interest rates rose steadily during December.  Inflation fears were rekindled when a lower than expected trade deficit, coupled with consumer spending that exceeded expectations, led to an upward revision in fourth quarter GDP.
<LI>During the month, we purchased a Guaranteed Investment Contract (GIC) and three GIC Alternatives at attractive valuation levels into the Fund.
<LI>The Fund retains its high credit quality (AA+) and is currently targeting an average maturity of 2.6 years.
<LI>The Fund's liquidity position remains strong with 14.6% of its assets invested in money market instruments.  During the next 3 months, we will reduce this position by placing intermediate maturity GIC alternatives with high quality issuers
</UL>
