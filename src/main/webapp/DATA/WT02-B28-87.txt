<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>5 Year Term Insurance</STRONG></FONT>
<P>
5 Year Term (5YT) offers affordable, temporary life insurance protection.  The
premiums are guaranteed and level for the first five years, then increase and are
expected to remain level within each successive five year period, but are not
guaranteed to do so.
<P>
The coverage 5YT provides could help families and business owners safeguard their
financial future.  The proceeds can be used to help pay mortgages and other debts,
fund a child's college education, and help a family continue its standard of
living.
<P>
<STRONG>The Benefits of 5YT</STRONG><BR>
<UL>
<LI>The peace of mind of pure life insurance protection
<LI>A death benefit that is, in most instances, free from federal income tax
<LI>Guaranteed renewable coverage through the expiry age (see"Product Specifics")
<LI> The privilege to convert to a permanent policy that builds cash value (See
"Conversion Privilege")
<LI>The financial strength of New York Life
</UL>
<P>
<STRONG>The Uses of 5YT</STRONG><BR>
There are many situations where 5YT may be appropriate.  Here are just a few
examples:
<UL>
<LI>Temporary Need for Protection
<DL>
	<DT>When coverage is needed for a relatively short period of time, 5YT can be
ideal.  A common use is when a mortgage or student loan is taken.
<P>
<LI>Limited Funds
<DT>In situations where life insurance is essential but dollars are scarce, 5YT
could serve as a stop-gap.  Young people starting their careers can choose 5YT,
then convert to a permanent plan as their finances improve.
<P>
<LI>Supplemental Insurance
<DT>5YT can be added as a rider to some permanent plans to help boost the death
benefit.  It's an affordable way to increase total insurance coverage.
<P>
<LI>Provide Family Coverage
<DT>Riders can be added to the policy to insure your spouse and/or children,
guarenteeing their future insurability (see "Riders" section for more information)
<P>
<LI>Protection For Businessowners
<DT>5YT can be used as a hedge to cover outstanding loans, shielding partners or
shareholders from financial hardship.
</DL>
</UL>
<P>
<STRONG><FONT SIZE=+1>Product Specifics:</FONT></STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>Issue Ages:</STRONG><BR>
Base Plan - 15-75<BR>
Rider - 0-75
<P>
<STRONG>Expiry Age:</STRONG><BR> 90 (New York: 80)
<P>
<STRONG>Minimum Face Amount:</STRONG><BR>
Base Plan - $50,000<BR>
Rider - $10,000
<P>
<STRONG>Premium Modes:</STRONG><BR>  
New York Life offers several convenient ways to pay premiums:  <EM>annually,
semi-annually, quarterly, monthly,</EM> through our <EM>NYL-A-Plan,</EM> or via our
automatic bank drafting method, <EM>Check-O-Matic (COM).</EM>
<P>
<STRONG>Premium Guarantee:</STRONG><BR>
Premiums are guaranteed and level for the first five years.  The <EM>Modification
of Premium Guarantee Rider</EM> can be added to assure level premiums for the
second 5 year interval.  See the "Available Riders" section below for more details.
<P>
<STRONG>Risk Classes:</STRONG><BR>
Based on defined underwriting criteria, New York Life offers the following risk
classes:  <EM>Preferred</EM> (for face amounts of $250,000 and more),
<EM>Non-smoker, Select Standard, Standard,</EM> and <EM>Substandard A-G.</EM>

<P>
<STRONG>Conversion Privilege</STRONG><BR>
5YT policies allow the owner to convert all or part of the policy into permanent ,
cash value life insurance without furnishing evidence of insurability.  In all
states, except New York, the conversion period is, generally, the later of 10 years
or age 30.  For more detailed information on the conversion privilege and the New
York variation, please contact your agent.
