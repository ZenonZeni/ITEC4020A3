<BR>
 
The L-Enews mailing list aims to inform people in developing countries of ongoing
projects, to share brief analyses of important energy issues, and to disseminate
information on potential sources of funding and interesting publications. It also
promotes the exchange of ideas on the implementation of energy-efficient and
renewable energy technologies in developing countries.

<PRE> 
To subscribe, send email to the following:

  listserv@fem.unicamp.br


with this command in the body of the message:

  subscribe l-enews [your name]
