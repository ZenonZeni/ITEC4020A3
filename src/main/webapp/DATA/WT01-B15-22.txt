<center><font size=+3 color=#003399>Setting Your Default Homepage</font></center>
<hr noshade color=#bf0000>

<font size=+2>S</font>etting your default hompage is easy in Netscape. Simply go through the following steps and then Netscape will startup with your new custom homepage, giving you quick access to great links, great software, great search tools, and much, much more.<br>

<ul>
<li>Click the <i>Options</i> menu choose <i>General Preferences...</i>
<li>Click the <i>Appearance</i> tab
<li>Change the <i>Startup</i> textbox so it reads <b>http://www.compulinks.com/</b>
<li>Click the <i>OK</i> button
</ul>

<font size=+2>T</font>hat's it! Next time you start Netscape, you'll go directly to your custom homepage. (You can also get "home" quickly by pressing the Netscape <i>Home</i> button on the toolbar - try it now!). <p>
