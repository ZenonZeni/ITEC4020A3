<H3>PR NEWSWIRE (PR)</H3>

<B>PR Newswire--PR01W</B><BR>
PR Newswire Association Inc.<BR>
$1.00/article<BR>
Continuous wire feed with immediate updating; 10-week retention of articles<BR>
(Also in FI, GB, BWires, CoRpts, Wires)<BR>
Full text of press releases prepared by corporations, public-relations agencies,
labor unions, civic and cultural organizations, political parties, and government
agencies. Releases cover corporate movements, including mergers and acquisitions,
new product offerings, major personnel shifts, and more.<P>

NewsNet monitors PR Newswire on a 24-hour basis. Articles are available
immediately for full-text retrieval in SCAN mode in hourly segments. Full-text
searching of all articles is available with one hour of release.<P>

Broadcast or redistribution of any portion of the PR Newswire file--whether or not
for resale -- in full, in part, in full text or in abstract, is prohibited without
the expressed written consent of PR Newswire.<P>
