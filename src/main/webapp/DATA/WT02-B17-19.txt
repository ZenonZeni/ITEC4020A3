<b>Welcome </b> to the Superconductivity for Electric Systems 
World Wide Web site&#151;your resource for current information on superconducting technologies and 
the national Superconductivity for Electric Systems Program. We invite you to learn more about
superconductivity.

<p>
<dl>
<b>Did you know </b> that superconducting devices will allow us to transmit electricity through power 
lines more efficiently? Click on <a href="whatis.htm">What is Superconductivity?</a> to learn more 
about the benefits of the technology. 

<p><b>Did you know</b> that the United States, if it commercializes its superconducting products, 
could be supplying most of the world's power system equipment by 2010? Check out 
<a href="program.htm">Superconductivity Program</a> and 
<a href="partner.htm">Superconductivity Partnerships</a> to meet the teams that are moving the science 
into the marketplace.

<p><b>Want to test your knowledge of superconductivity?</b> Take a quiz in one of our curriculum 
guides. You'll find them among our <a href="pubs.htm">Educational Materials</a>. Or browse the most 
current research findings in <a href="techstat.htm">Technology Status</a>.

<p><b>Don't miss </b><a href="pubs.htm">additional publications</a> for more details on the science, 
research, and development of superconductivity.</dl>
