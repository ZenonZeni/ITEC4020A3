<BR><H2>About this Web Site</H2>

<P>
<STRONG>This Web site has been created by the Fitzroy Legal Service Inc.
as an educational and information initiative. <p>The site is made
available with the generous support of <A HREF ="acknowl.htm">Telematics Trust</A>.</STRONG><P>

<STRONG>The Fitzroy Legal Service acknowledges the generous support of <A HREF="http://www.infoxchange.net.au"><i>The Info</i> Xchange</A>, who have provided the server space for this web site.</STRONG>

The Fitzroy Legal Service is committed to providing access to the best legal information and resources from around the world, with a primary focus on materials relevant to Australia.

Many of the links provided enable students of the law to access web sites that present alternative or critical perspectives of the workings of legal systems, both nationally and internationally.

you should consult a lawyer licensed to practice in your jurisdiction.</a>
