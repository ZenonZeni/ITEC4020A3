<H3>FOOD AND BEVERAGE (FB)</H3>

<B>Agri-Industry Europe--FB20</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb20.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Asia Intelligence Wire<BR>
$3.00/article<BR>
Updated monthly, from 10/25/96<BR>
(Also in BT, AIW)<BR>
This monthly title originates in Belgium. It provides coverage on the 
initiatives of the European institutions and other major organizations in the 
field of agri-industry for food and non-food uses.<P>

<B>Agri Service International--FB21</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb21.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Asia Intelligence Wire<BR>
$3.00/article<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 10/18/96<BR>
(Also in AIW)<BR>
This biweekly title originates in Brussels, Belgium. It provides common 
agricultural policy and all the main international economic events related to
the agricultural sector.<P>

<B>Agweek--FB11</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb11.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Knight-Ridder Inc.'s Grand Forks Herald<BR>
$2.00/article; $35.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, retained for 10 weeks<BR>
(Also in GT, IT, KR)<BR>
Comprehensive coverage of the week's events and trends in agriculture, both in the
U.S. and abroad, including regular coverage of the U.S. Department of Agriculture
and the departments of agriculture or its counterparts in other important
nations.<P>

<B>Food Chemical News--FB07</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb07.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
CRC Press Inc.<BR>
$3.00/article; $50.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 12/23/91<BR>
(Also in BT, CH)<BR>
Provides information on government regulation of food and food additives, early
warnings of changes in government food regulation policy, FDA and USDA proposals,
major enforcement actions, precedent-setting court cases, and more.<P>

<B>Food Ingredients News--FB17</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb17.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 8/1/94<BR>
(Also in RD)<BR>
Covers new food ingredient materials, products, and processes that are figuring
more and more prominently in the delivery of better nutritious, balanced, and
designed foods. Issues include regular features, such as, flavors and enhancers,
fortifiers, Nutraceuticals, and functional additives, along with company news,
regulation and legislative trends, and new technology.<P>

<B>Food Labeling & Nutrition News--FB10</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb10.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
CRC Press Inc.<BR>
$3.00/article; $35.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 10/8/92<BR>
(Also in AD, GT)<BR>
Focuses on labeling, advertising and packaging news from FDA, USDA, FTC, and other
federal and state agencies involved in regulating information on labels of food
packages. Also covers so-called "green" labeling issues.<P>

<B>FoodByte News--FB14</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb14.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Parker & Associates<BR>
$3.00/article; $35.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 9/20/93<BR>
(No cross-referencing)<BR>
Provides weekly coverage of food and beverage news, including new product and
ingredient introductions, mergers and acquisitions, regulatory information, and
analysis. Summaries of the Federal Register and food-related sections of the Food
and Drug Administration Enforcement Report are included.<P>

<B>Ice Cream Reporter--FB04</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb04.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
FIND/SVP<BR>
$3.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 11/1/89<BR>
(Also in GB)<BR>
Provides news and analysis of the events and decisions that affect the ice cream
industry in all its aspects, from manufacturing and distribution to advertising
and legislation.<P>

<B>Kane's Beverage Week--FB06</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb06.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Whitaker Newsletters Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 12/9/91<BR>
(Also in AD)<BR>
Devoted exclusively to the business of marketing beer, wine, and spirits.
Continuous coverage includes federal and state legislation and regulation of the
industry, mergers and acquisitions, products, research, and updates on the
activities of the anti-alcohol lobby.<P>

<B>Leisure Beverage Insider--FB15</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb15.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Whitaker Newsletters Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $45.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 9/20/93<BR>
(No cross-referencing)<BR>
Covers the nonalcoholic beverage industry, including carbonated soft drinks,
bottled water, "New Age" beverages, fruit juices, dairy products, tea, coffee, and
related packaging.<P>


<B>U.S. Department of Agricultural Trade Leads--FB22</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb22.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Asia Intelligence Wire<BR>
$3.00/article<BR>
Updated daily, from 10/15/96<BR>
(Also in AIW, Busops)<BR>
This daily title provides requests (to buy) from trading firms and government
agencies in search of agricultural items and food.<P>

<B>Washington Beverage Insight--FB02</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/fb02.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
George Wells & Associates Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 4/27/84<BR>
(Also in GT, MG)<BR>
Reports and interprets current government actions affecting the soft-drink and
alcoholic-beverage industries, including legislation, regulation and court
decisions in franchising, ingredient safety, labeling, forced deposits, taxation
and advertising. Coverage includes Congress, federal agencies, state legislatures,
associations, and courts.<P>
