<BR>Dear Carl:
<BR>
<BR>I am in receipt of <A HREF="olsen-02.html"> your letter</A> dated August 17, 1996, in which you request support to legalize marijuana.
<BR>
<BR>The Governor's Alliance on Substance Abuse supports enforcement of current drug laws, and opposes the legalization of marijuana.&nbsp;  We will not support efforts to minimize the dangers of using marijuana for reasons you and I have already discussed.
<BR>
<BR>We are sympathetic to Iowans who have health problems, and encourage them to seek legal medical treatment.
<BR>
<BR>As always, thank you for your comments on the subject.
<BR>
<BR>Sincerely,
<BR>
<BR>Dale R. Woolery
