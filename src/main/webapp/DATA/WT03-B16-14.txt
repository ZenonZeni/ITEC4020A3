<H4><img src="triangle.gif">Welcome to <i>Towers Perrin</i> online</h4> 

<ul><ul>
<p><b><i>Towers Perrin</i></b> is an independent, international management consulting firm that advises clients in both the private and public sectors on a wide array of human resource, employee benefit and compensation issues.  Worldwide, the firm serves nearly 8,000 clients in more than 45 countries. <b><i>Towers Perrin</i></b> employs more than 5,000 people and is owned by its active employees (Principals).

<p>This online service is designed to make it easier for the firm's clients and others to access and retrieve certain information published by <b><i>Towers Perrin</i></b>.  Initially, only our bimonthly publication <a href="headlines.html"><i>Worldwide Pay and Benefits Headlines</i></a> is available online. <a href="headlines.html"><i>Headlines</i></a> focuses primarily on international pay and benefit issues, including emerging trends, new legislation and other developments in major countries around the globe.  In addition to accessing the current issue of <a href="headlines.html"><i>Headlines</i></a>, users can instantaneously search and retreive articles from our <a href="headlines.html"><i>Headlines</i></a> archives, which are cross-linked and referenced to related articles.

<p>Also included is a graphical interface that makes it easy for users to communicate with <b><i>Towers Perrin</i></b> offices throughout the world.  By clicking on the map icon, you can access a listing of our <a href="offices.html">worldwide office locations</a>, including telephone and facsimile numbers, and contact names.  You can also e-mail us directly if you want further information about <b><i>Towers Perrin</i></b> or have questions about any of the articles in <a href="headlines.html"><i>Headlines</i></a>.  Simply click on the name of the contact nearest you in the listing of <b><i>Towers Perrin</i></b> office locations for your region.

<p><i>We need your feedback!</i>  Whether we continue and expand this online service will depend in part on user input.  Thus we welcome your suggestions and views: How can we improve this site?  Is this a useful medium for accessing <b><i>Towers Perrin</i></i> information?  Are there any specific issues you would like to see covered in <a href="headlines.html"><i>Headlines</i></a>?  What other <b><i>Towers Perrin</i></b> publications or information should be included in this service?  Please send your comments or questions to <a href="mailto:ico@towers.com">ico@towers.com</a>.

<p>We encourage you to visit this location frequently, as we're already planning a number of enhancements and additions.  And, of course, there will be a new issue of <a href="headlines.html"><i>Headlines</i></a> every other month.  In Short, watch this site.
