<P><H2> Catalog Description</H2>
<BLOCKQUOTE>

Operational techniques used in describing, analyzing and designing linear continuous systems;
modeling, equilibrium points and linearization; Laplace transforms; response via transfer functions,
stability; performance specifications; controller design via transfer functions; frequency response;
simple nonlinearities. 

</BLOCKQUOTE>
