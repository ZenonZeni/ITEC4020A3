In September, Kemper introduced its exclusive sponsorship of a new public television series that focuses on topics of particular interest to those between the ages of 45 and 60. In 26 half-hour programs, <I>In the Prime</I> explores themes such as financial planning, mid-life career changes, elder care and approaches to a healthy life. The series, produced by Washington, D.C.-based public television station WETA, airs on PBS stations nationwide.
