The online catalog of tests lists over 1,000 currently published tests, plus some intervention resources and some texts. If you would like us to ammend one of the test descriptions (submit a mini-review), or have us add a publication to our database, just email the information to us. You can email us by selecting the <I><B><A HREF="mailto:mdangus@psychtest.com">mdangus@psychtest.com</A></B></I> link on any of our pages.<P>
This entire site currently runs to about 2 megabytes uncompressed. The <A HREF="catcont.htm">Testing Materials Resource Book: Online Edition</A> which is the largest part of the site, is a set of HTML files generated from the test database maintained by M.D. Angus & Associates Ltd. A printed copy of <EM>The Testing Materials Resource Book</EM> is available free of charge in Canada. International users should use the electronic verswion of the catalog. The printed  Resource Book will never be as current as the electronic edition. If you want your own copy of the catalog to look up test prices and information without connecting to the internet, just download <A HREF="CATALOG.ZIP">CATALOG.ZIP</A>.
 Unzip the file set in its own directory of your hard drive and then just open the file with your browser without even having to connect to your internet provider.<P><H4>How to Find a Test in the Online Catalog:</H4><P>
The easiest way to locate a specific test is to select Option 2)<A HREF="catcont.htm">Resource Book Online</a> on the sidebar frame. This link takes you to the Test Catalog contents file. From the contents file you can do a text search using your browser for the test name, or to use the contents file links to quickly locate the test by subject.<P>
<H4>Test Development In Progress</H4>
If you want to participate in test development in progress, select the Research Department link on this page. You will be presented with descriptions of test item pools under development, and will be offered a chance to contribute an item by emailing it to us. <P>

<H4>About this site:</H4><P>
Development of this site was initiated in March of 1996. We believe our role as providers of information to customers and colleagues will be enhanced by the internet. Your comments and assistance in this regard will be invaluable. Please let us know what you think of the site. Email us your ideas. A site like this will only work if it is a collaborative effort between us and the people who use it.<P>
Many aspects of the site are still very rough, and require polishing up. We would very much appreciate any comments about typographical or other errors like broken links which you find. To return to this start page from a child page you may select one of the <a HREF="frontpge.htm"> M.D. Angus&amp; Associates Ltd.</a> links which occur on every page in this web site.<P>
