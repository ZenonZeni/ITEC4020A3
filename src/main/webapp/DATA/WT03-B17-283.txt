<PRE>
Monitoring is when you regularly inspect the lawn for  signs  of
harmful and beneficial lawn pests.   Before  you  cut  the lawn,
you should inspect  it  for  signs  of stress.   Here a garden
notebook becomes very handy.  Write the date and
location and what signs you have observed and also write
down what  you did to correct the problem.
