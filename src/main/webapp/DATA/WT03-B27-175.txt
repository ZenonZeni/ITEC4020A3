<H2>
Offline Bulletin
</H2>


<P>The Offline Bulletin is a feature designed to keep you up-to-date with title code, pricing, frequency, and other changes in the NewsNet database.  <P>

<H4>Title Name Changes</H4>
The title of Knight-Ridder Financial News Wire (KR01w) from Knight-Ridder/Tribune Information Service has changed to Bridge News.<P>
The title of <b>Information Law Alert: Voorhees Report</b> (LA19) from IOMA has changed to <b>Information Law Alert: An Ioma Report</b>.<P>
<b>Food Labeling News </b>(FB10) has changed its title to <b>Food Labeling & Nutrition News</b>.<P>
<b>Cancer Biotechnology Weekly</b> (HH15) has changed its title to <b>Cancer Weekly Plus</b>.<P>
<h4>Title Merges</h4>
<b>Business Computing Brief</b> (EC60), from Financial Times Information, has merged into<b> Multimedia Business Analyst</b> (PB56).  The issues 2/17/94 through 3/2/95 of<i> Business Computing Brief</i> will remain online for reading, searching, and scanning.<P>
<b>Datatrends Report on DEC</b> (EC03) from DataTrends Publications Inc., has merged into The Report on Microsoft (EC100).  The issues 11/1/89 throughout 9/1/96 of <i>Datatrends Report on DEC </i>will remain online for reading, searching, and scanning.<P>
