<H3>PHASE 1: TO BEGIN THE PROCESS:
</H3><P>
<UL><LI>Review the enclosed materials thoroughly<BR>
<LI>Call Center to request the Retainer Agreements which will include:<BR>
<UL><LI>Center's Retainer Agreement
<LI>Counselor's Retainer Agreement
<LI>Egg Recipient Questionnaire to be completed
<LI>Request for pictures to be used in the matching process
<LI>Request for initial Registration fee of $500.00 payable to Counselor.
<LI>Mail above to Center. Clients participation in our program is registered.
</UL></UL><H3>PHASE 2: CHOOSING YOUR DONOR:
</H3><P>
<UL><LI>Psychological -Consultation with counselor, Counselor will call couple
<UL><LI>Presentation of potential donors
<LI>Introduction of donor and recipient couple
<LI>Donor psychologically screened and evaluated
<LI>Psychological clearance report<BR>
</UL><LI>Medical -Medical screening of donor
<UL><LI>Medical clearance report on donor
<LI>Medical clearance for recipient couple<BR>
</UL><LI>Legal -Recipient couple contacts &amp; retains attorney
<UL><LI>Review &amp; finalize egg donor contract with attorney
<LI>Contract sent to egg donor
<LI>Egg donor consults with her attorney
<LI>All sign contract &amp; return them to recipient couple's attorney
<LI>Legal clearance report<BR>
</UL><LI>Financial - Following funds become due at this point.
<UL><LI><TT> $ &#160;500.00 Second half of Psychologist's fee of
$1,000.00</TT>
<LI><TT> $ &#160;100.00 Non-Southern CA surcharge, Non-Southern CA surcharge, if Couple resides outside	of Southern California</TT>
<LI><TT> $1,500.00 Center's Administrative fee</TT>
<LI><TT> $2,500.00 Egg Donor's fee - made payable to the egg donor</TT>
<LI><TT> $ &#160;250.00 Legal Fees - Egg Donor</TT>
<LI><TT> $ <U>&#160;750.00 </U>Legal Fees - Recipient Couple - Atty. John
Ciccarelli</TT>
</UL>
<UL><LI><TT><b> $5,600.00</b></TT>
</UL></UL><H3>PHASE 3: CYCLING BEGINS
</H3><P>
<UL><LI>Synchronization of recipient and egg donors' cycles, aspiration,
fertilization and embryo transfer to recipient occurs
</UL><P>
<H3>PHASE 4: ASPIRATION PERFORMED
</H3><P>
<UL><LI>Once the doctor confirms the aspiration occurred, the egg donor receives
her fee. The egg donor schedules a follow up appointment with the doctor's
office one month after aspiration. The Center eagerly awaits news from the
recipient couple!!
</UL><P>
