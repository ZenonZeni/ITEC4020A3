<H2>Programs and Grants</H2>
The Energy Foundation awards grants and takes direct
initiatives in five areas: <P>
<BLOCKQUOTE><img src="../grf/blt.gif" alt="*"><A HREF="utilities/index.html"><B>Utilities</B></A>, <br>
<img src="../grf/blt.gif" alt="*"><A HREF="buildings/index.html"><B>Buildings</B></A>, <br>
<img src="../grf/blt.gif" alt="*"><A HREF="transport/index.html"><B>Transportation</B></A>, <br>
<img src="../grf/blt.gif" alt="*"><A HREF="renew/index.html"><B>Renewable Energy</B></A>, and <br>
<img src="../grf/blt.gif" alt="*"><A HREF="integrated/index.html"><B>Integrated Issues</B></A>. <br>
</BLOCKQUOTE><P>
Its geographic focus is the United States, with special
emphasis on regional initiatives.
<P>
In this section we describe our programs and illustrate
them with the grants given in 1992, 1993, and 1994. Thus
far, a total of 281 grants worth $30,915,038 (over four
years) have been made to 132 organizations.
