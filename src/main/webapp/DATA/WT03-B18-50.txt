<h1 align="center"><i>ODIN</i></h1>

<h2><i>Omni Directional Intelligent Navigator</i></h2>

<h3 align="center">Autonomous Systems Laboratory - University of
Hawaii</h3>

<hr>

<p align="center"><img src="f-odin.gif" align="center"
width="184" height="244"> <img src="odin.gif" align="center"
width="340" height="240"> </p>

<hr>

<p>This is a mixed hierarchical and parallel architecture, with a
Supervisor that handles mission parameters on the basis of lower-level
information and three separate block functions, each with four
levels of intelligence: Sensory Data Base deals with the
environment, its representation and sensing; Knowledge Base
handles system knowledge according to particular Rules; Planner
deals with problems related to vehicle heading and control. A
particular adaptive approach has been adopted for vehicle
control: a Parameter Adaptation Algorithm is used to estimate
system and environmental parameters, so that the control
structure can quickly adapt to changing conditions: this is
performed by means of a neural network, whose advantage is the
ability to adapt to structural changes. </p>
