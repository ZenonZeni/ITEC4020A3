<h2>EVALUATION CRITERIA FOR PHASE I APPLICATIONS</h2>
<p>
<H4>Volume 1 - Technical and Business Plan</H4>
<p>
The following elements in Volume 1 of the Phase I application (as 
described in the solicitation section titled "Application Preparation 
Instructions") will be evaluated and scored for Phase I projects:
<p>
<b>Phase I Application Evaluation Factors</b><p>
Volume 1 - Technical and Business Plan<p>
<p>
<dl>I. Description of the Project (10 Points) <p>
   <dl>A.   Unique aspects of the proposed integrated system and its 
proposed uses<br>
   B.   Completeness of the project description<br>
   C.   Completeness of the proposed Phase I study</dl>
<p>
II.     Discussion of the Technical Approach (30 Points) <p>
   <dl>A.   Credibility and thoroughness of the Applicant's explanation 
of the technical
        approach/feasibility for the proposed integrated system<br>
   B.   Applicant's understanding of the proposed system technical 
barriers and associated issues, especially systems integration issues<br>

   C.   Thoroughness of the Applicant's technical approach to overcome 
technical barriers and issues in order to develop the integrated system 
and associated components, if required</dl>
<p>
III.    Proposed Business Plan Development for the Integrated System (25 
points) <p>
  <dl> A.   Credibility of the Applicant's approach to determining 
potential markets<br>
   B.   Reasonableness of Applicant's methodology for determining 
system/commodity prices<br>
   C.   Credibility of the Applicant's strategy for determining potential 
market penetration of the proposed integrated system<br>
   D.   Thoroughness of approach for development of marketing 
strategy<br>
   E.   Thoroughness of approach for determining technical and financial 
requirements for
        establishing a manufacturing capability for the proposed 
system</dl>
<p>   
IV. Phase I Planning (20 Points) <p>
   <dl>A.   Soundness and Completeness of the Applicant's Phase I work 
scope/plan<br>
   B.   Thoroughness/reasonableness of the Phase I schedule and key 
activities<br>
   C.   Reasonableness of schedule</dl>
<p>
V. Teaming Arrangements for Phase I (15 Points) <p>
   <dl>A.   Capabilities/experience of the Applicant and other team 
members<br>
   B.   Availability of the required facilities and equipment<br>
   C.   Technical/business capabilities of Applicant and team member key 
personnel<br>
   D.   Commitment from Applicant and team members</dl></dl>
<p>
Scores will be based on the clarity and completeness of the information 
provided and the evidence presented to support the consortium's ability 
to complete the proposed Phase I project.  The total possible score for 
Volume 1 of the Phase I application will be 100 points, with the point 
distribution as shown in the above list of evaluation factors.
