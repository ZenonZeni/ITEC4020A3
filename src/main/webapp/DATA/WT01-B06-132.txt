<A HREF="mailto:lynn@conception.com">Lynn Marie Westphal</A>, M.D., F.A.C.O.G. graduated summa cum laude from Lawrence University in Appleton, Wisconsin.  She earned her M.D. degree at Stanford University and did her residency training in obstetrics and gynecology at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) and Stanford University.  After her residency, she was involved in a number of research projects and was full-time faculty at Stanford University Medical Center for two years.  Her research efforts were recognized by a grant from the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists.  She is board-certified in obstetrics and gynecology and completed a fellowship in Reproductive Endocrinology and Infertility at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF).  Currently, she is on the clinical faculty at Stanford University and UCSF.
