<p><font size="6"><img src="images/cotopsm.jpg" align="middle"
width="200" height="276">Cotopaxi</font></p>
<p>Grown exclusively by Florinsa in Ecuador, the Cotopaxi is a
bi-color variety with a creamy white outside and livey fuscia
inside. Cotopaxi is very resistant to traveling and known for a
long vase life. This rose is truly a delicacy for the
discriminating client who wants the luxury of displaying it and
is willing to pay for one of nature's best.</p>
