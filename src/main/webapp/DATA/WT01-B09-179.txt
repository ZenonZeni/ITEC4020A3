<B>"An Electronic Conference on Balancing Livestock, the Environment and Human Needs"</B><BR>
 Sponsored by the International Livestock Research Institute

global electronic conference from March 10 to May 24, 1997. <P>

<P>The International Workshop on Dynamics of

consequences of land-use/land-cover changes in HKH.  <P>
