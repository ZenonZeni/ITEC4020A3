Golden is the primary field organization for the <a 
href="http://www.eren.doe.gov/">Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy (EE).</a>  In conjunction with EE's six Regional Support Offices, Golden works directly with industry, academia and the states in cost-shared partnerships to further the development and commercialization of energy efficiency and renewable energy technologies. <p>

This information aims to assist companies and the public, including small and 
small disadvantaged businesses, educational institutions, governmental 
entities and individuals, in conducting business with the Golden Field 
Office.<p>

The information identifies contracting and grant opportunities available through Golden, the Support Offices, and <A 
href="http://www.nrel.gov">the National Renewable Energy Laboratory.</a>  For information on other Department of Energy (DOE) 
offices, please go to the DOE Headquarters <a href="http://www.pr.doe.gov/">Office 
of Procurement and Assistance Management's "Doing Business With DOE" 
page</a>.
