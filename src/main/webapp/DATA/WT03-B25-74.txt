<H1>Total Color Control</H1></CENTER>

<BLOCKQUOTE>
<P>The Color Select System for total color control provides an easy and accurate means of changing coating material in any 
application. It is flexible enough to handle as many as 15 different coating materials. 
<P>  The system saves time and reduces operating costs. Its multi-color handling capability, automated ease with which color 
changes are made and the elimination of multiple paint cans, means less downtime for color changes. 
<P>  All wetted parts in the valve and manifold are 300 series stainless steel or Teflonr, for compatibility with various coatings, 
including high-solids, water-bornes and catalyzed materials. Dual porting allows circulation through the valve. A stainless steel plug is 
also provided for single inlet applications. 
<P>  The system requires 70 to 100 psi air operating pressure. Required fluid pressure is 300 psi maximum. Fluid flow is 3,800 
cc/min, with paint viscosity at 700 cp. 
<P>  Source: <A HREF="/itwrans/index.html">ITW Ransburg Electrostatic Systems</A>, Toledo, Ohio.


</BLOCKQUOTE>
