<p><br>
<img src="gifs2/jeff-pic.gif" align=right>
The Victorian Government is committed to creating a pro-business
environment in Victoria.  We are working within a timeframe up
to the year 2001 to establish Victoria as an internationally competitive
business centre, a leader in scientific and technological research
and development and an aggressive competitor in world markets.
Private investment is the key to Victoria's economic prosperity,
and I urge investors to consider Victoria as the location for
development.
