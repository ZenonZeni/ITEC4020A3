<H2><CENTER>Ace Hardware</CENTER>
</H2>
On behalf of a major consumer packaged goods client, Coddington Environmental
created an environmental marketing guide for ACE Hardware store managers.
The very first of its kind in the industry, <I>How to be a Green Retailer</I>
is a step-by-step guide to working with community schools, utilities, government
agencies and the media to promote environmental stewardship, education and
green products.<BR>
<BR>
Manufacturer and trade environmental management and marketing partnerships
are the most powerful business development opportunity to emerge from the
environmental responsibility movement. <BR>
<BR>
Coddington Environmental helps manufacturers develop trade partnerships
and merchandising programs that focus on consumer environmental education,
green product promotion and solid waste management.
