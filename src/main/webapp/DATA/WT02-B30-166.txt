As an employer, you recognize the importance of providing a

comprehensive benefits program that allows you to attract and

retain the best employees.  In today's economy, you are also

cognizant of the need to control rapidly escalating health care

costs.<P>



If you currently offer a NYLCare health benefits program for

your employees, you already know the advantages of 

NYLCare.  This section of the NYLCare Web

site will provide you with information to help you respond to the

ever-changing health benefit needs of your workforce. <P>



If your organization is considering the implementation of a

health benefits plan, this site will help you prioritize your

needs.  It will also answer many questions you may

have about the services that NYLCare has to offer.  NYLCare is

one of the nation's leading health benefits service providers. 

As a result, NYLCare is well-positioned to help you design and

implement a health benefits package that will meet the needs of

your workforce, while allowing you to control your costs.  As an

integrated national health benefits service provider, we have the

scope and resources necessary to meet your needs.  Although

national in scope, NYLCare can meet your local requirements

through our network of eleven regional offices.  Use this section

of the site to see what NYLCare can do for you and your

business. <P>



This section of the NYLCare site will continue to expand and

evolve in the future.  Click on "Contact NYLCare" to let us know

what information you need! <P>
