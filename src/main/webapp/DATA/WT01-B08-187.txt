<IMG SRC="blueball.gif"> <FONT SIZE=3><B>Telephone Access/Voice Information Services:</B><P>

BXA's voice mail system can be accessed using a touch-tone phone, and is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.  To access recorded information on export licensing and related topics, simply call our Exporter Counseling Division in Washington D.C. at (202) 482-4811, or either one of our <A HREF="http://www.primenet.com/~bxawest"><b><i>Western Regional Offices</i></b></a>  in California at (714) 660-0144 or  (408) 748-7450 and follow the instructions.<p>

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif"> <b>STELA, the System for Tracking Export License Applications:</b><p> 

STELA is an automated voice response system that can be accessed using a touch-tone phone.  It provides applicants with the status of their license and classification applications, and is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.   STELA can give exporters authority to ship their goods for those licenses approved without conditions.  To obtain information about your license application from STELA, dial (202) 482-2752 and enter the Application Control Number.  Press "0" for instructions.<p>

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif"> <b>ELAIN, the Export License Application and Information Network:</b><p>

ELAIN allows you to submit license applications electronically once you have received authorization from BXA.  To find out more about ELAIN, or to request information on the certification requirements, contact our Exporter Counseling Division.  
<P>

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif"> <b>ERIC, the Electronic Request for Item Classification:</B><P> 

ERIC, a supplementary service to ELAIN, allows you to submit commodity classification requests electronically to BXA.   To find out more about ERIC, or to request information on the certification requirements, contact our Exporter Counseling Division at (202) 482-4811. <P>

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif"> <b>Fax on Demand:</B><P>

This automated system enables you to access information regarding the services of BXA's <A HREF="http://www.primenet.com/~bxawest"><b><i>Western Regional Offices</i></b></a> and other export-related topics 24 hours a day, seven days a week at no charge.  To use the system, simply dial (714) 660-0144.  For information regarding defense diversification initiatives administered by the Western Regional Office, call (714) 660-1960.  The fax on demand system will guide you through the system which you operate by pushing the numbered keys on your phone pad.  To use the system effectively, we suggest you order the menu of documents that includes all the instructions you will need.  You may order up to five documents per call.  If there is no backup of requests, your fax machine will receive the documents within minutes!<P>

<IMG SRC="blueball.gif"> <b>NETFACTS and FASTFACTS</B><P>

BXA's Western Regional Office disseminates regulatory changes and other current topics of interest on a periodic basis through Internet e-mail and broadcast fax.  Our Internet e-mail service is called "NetFacts" and our broadcast fax service is called "Fast Facts."   To be placed on either system, please contact our Exporter Counseling Division at (202) 482-4811.<P>
