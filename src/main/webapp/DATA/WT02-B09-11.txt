        governs the Arms Export Control Act (AECA) and regulates
        the export of military technology from the United States.

        comprehensive list of items controlled by the ITAR. The
        ITAR includes definitions, licensing procedures,
        licensing exemptions, agreement procedures, penalties and
        much more. Application of the ITAR is the responsibility
        of the Office of Defense Trade Controls and the </font><a
