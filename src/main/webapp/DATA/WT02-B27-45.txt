<P>
Barry Shealy is an Assistant Professor at UB focusing on mathematics
teacher education. He is particularly interested in cognitive processes of change as teachers' adopt new practices. Contexts for his investigations have included teachers learning to use technology, re-thinking assessment practices, investigating mathematical models and applications, beginning to teach, and making sense of reform initiatives.
