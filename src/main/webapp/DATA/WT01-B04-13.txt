            <p align="center"><font size="4"><b>SuperGen Obtains
            Right To License Novel Anticancer Compounds</b></font></p>
            <p>EMERYVILLE, CA (December 12, 1996) &#150;
            SuperGen, Inc. (Nasdaq: SUPG &amp; SUPGW), an
            emerging pharmaceutical company, announced it has
            signed an agreement giving it the right to license
            from Garnett McKeen Laboratory, Inc. (GML) the
            worldwide therapeutic rights to develop and
            commercialize a novel class of metalo-organic
            compounds. SuperGen initially will explore the
            compounds&#146; potential for the treatment of a
            broad range of cancers. </p>
            <p>SuperGen will be responsible for clinical
            development and commercialization efforts for the
            drug candidates that SuperGen chooses to pursue. GML
            will receive royalty payments on eventual product
            sales by SuperGen, as well as SuperGen stock based on
            the achievement of certain human clinical and
            regulatory milestones.</p>
            <p>&quot;We continue to secure proprietary platforms
            for the anticancer drugs of tomorrow even as we
