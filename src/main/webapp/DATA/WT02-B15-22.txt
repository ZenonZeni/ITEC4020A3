	<h2>881 Data</h2>
	</center>
		This is wage data from more than seventy different commodities that was entered from paper to
Microsoft Excel 5.  The range of years was from 1987 to 1995.  The data tracked in the
years were Crop, Activity, Approximate Acreage, Peak Labor Requirement, Estimated Number Working
(Manweeks),Wage Range Low, Wage Range High, Wage Range Midpoint, Piece Rate per Bale Low, Piece Rate per Bale
High, and Piece Rate per Bale Midpoint.  Maybe you can find this information useful.
