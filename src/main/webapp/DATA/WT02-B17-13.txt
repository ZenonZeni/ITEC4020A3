<H2>Proof-of-Concept</H2>

<br>

<p>Proof-of-concept projects in the DOE Hydrogen Program foster 
partnerships 
among the project members (for example, between energy market specialists 
and 
energy system manufacturers) that stimulate investments in the 
technologies of the 
future.  The long-term goal of the program is to provide support to 
industry-led 
projects that will help to make substantial inroads into energy markets 
and provide 
long-term reductions in "greenhouse gas" emissions.  For further 
information, select
