<CENTER><H2>CLOCK TEST, THE</H2></CENTER><P>
<CENTER><EM>Tuokko, Hadjistavropoulos, Miller, Horton, & Beattie</EM></CENTER><P>
<BR>The purpose of this test is to efficiently evaluate visuo-spatial construction, visual perception, and abstract conceptualization.  The three tests are drawing, setting, and reading.  Each component can be completed in about 5-10 minutes.  It is useful in differentiating between normal elderly and those suffering from dementia or cognitive impairment.  Errors are broken down into omissions, perseverations, rotations, misplacements, distortions, substitutions, and additions.  <BR>
Complete kit includes the manual, administration tent, 25 forms, and 25 profiles.<BR>
