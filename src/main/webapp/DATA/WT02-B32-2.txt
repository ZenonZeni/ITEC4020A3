<h2>General Enquiries</h2>

<h3>Telephone</h3>
<ul>
<B>Please note that from 8 April 1996 the local area telephone code for Reading will change from 01734 to 0118, with a 9 prepended to existing Reading telephone numbers. The old style numbers will be available in parallel with the new until 1 January 1998.</B>
<P>
<li>If you know the name of the person you need to contact, please use the telephone information given in either the <a href="#top">Department Directory and Home Pages</a> or in the <a href="http://www.rdg.ac.uk/CommsDir/home.html">University Communications Directory</a>
<P>
<li>Alternatively, please telephone the University switchboard on <cite>+44 (0)118 9875123</cite> and ask the operator to connect you.
<P>
<li>If you do not know the name of the person you need to contact, please telephone the Departmental Office on <cite>+44 (0)118 9318700</cite> for assistance.
<P>
<li>The departmental Industrial Training Office can be contacted on <cite>+44 (0)118 9318710</cite>.
</ul>
