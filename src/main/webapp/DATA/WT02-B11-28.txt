<a HREF="5.html"><h3>Indonesian partners file suit -- Placer fires shot in battle for Busang</h3></a>
<p>
<strong>by James Whyte</strong><p>
Claiming that his company is "the best company to develop Busang," PlacerDome (PDG-T) President John Willson fired the opening round in what he hopeswill become a bidding war between his company and Barrick Gold (ABX-T) forthe Busang gold deposit in Indonesia.
<p>
Deprived of a chance to make a deal with Bre-X Minerals (BXM-T) to develop
the gold deposit in East Kalimantan, Placer Dome is offering Bre-X a merger
on essentially equal terms.
<p>

<a HREF="6.html"><h3>Bema and Arizona Star expand mineralization at Aldebaran</h3></a>
<p>
<strong>by Rob Robertson</strong><p>
<strong>VANCOUVER</strong> - Deep drilling at the Aldebaran property in northern Chile indicates thatthe Cerro Casale gold-copper zone is much wider and deeper than initiallyprojected.
<p>
Owership of the property is shared by Arizona Star Resource (AZS-V) and
Bema Gold (BGO-T), with 51% and 49% interests, respectively. Their current
drill program is attempting to confirm the grade of the main porphyry zone.
<p>
