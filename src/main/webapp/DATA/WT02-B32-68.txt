<FONT COLOR="#00FF00">
<h2>Higlights of Career</h2>
</FONT>
<ul>
<li>Graduated in 1972 with BSc (1st Class) Chemical Engineering.
<li>Worked in Food Process Development with Spillers Foods, Cambridge (1972 - 1974).
<li>Graduated in 1975 with MSc Food Science from Leeds University, UK.
<li>Worked for Marks and Spencer Ltd as Retail Food Technologist with responsibility for developing quality products meeting legal requirements in co-operation with supplying food factories.
<li>Joined The University of Reading as Lecturer in 1983; Became specialist in food law, its enforcement and in aspects of quality assurance in the food industry.
<li>Whilst working at the University, Gained PhD degree with a study of food law enforcement in the United Kingdom.
<li>Has researched and lectured in Africa and Eastern Europe - in particular, visits have included Tanzania, Zimbabwe, Hungary and Romania.
<li>Under the EC Phare Programme, acted as the food law  consultant to an agri-food study of Romania in 1992 which  involved several weeks investigating the structure and activities relating food safety control in Romania.
<li>Joint Editor of the "<a href="http://www.mcb.co.uk/liblink/bfj/jourhome.htm">British Food Journal</a>".
<li>Editorial Board Member of <a href="http://www.elsevier.nl/locate/foodcont">Food Control</a>.
<li>Member of the Technical and Legislative Committee of the Institute of Food Science and Technology (UK).
</ul>
