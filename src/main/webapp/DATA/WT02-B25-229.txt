<P>The Alliance for the Mentally Ill of Maryland (AMI of MD) is an advocacy organization for family and friends of people with serious mental illness, and people who have a mental illness.  We are affiliated with the <A HREF="http://www.nami.org"> National Alliance for the Mentally Ill</A> which has over 1,000 affiliates and 140,000 members throughout the United States and Canada.</P>

<P>AMI of MD has 1,700 members in eighteen local chapters serving Maryland's twenty-three counties and Baltimore City.  With our new statewide toll-free Helpline, we provide information and referrals to appropriate services such as family self-help support groups and mental health services for persons with serious mental illness and their family members.
</P>
