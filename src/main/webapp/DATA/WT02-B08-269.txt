Extract from a classified LANDSAT MSS image of the Monchegorsk area showing the distribution of
 vegetation in June 1992. The image shows Lake Imandra (centre), Monchetundra and Chunatundra 
(grey/light brown areas left of centre), and Khibin (grey area at bottom right). It covers an area of approximately 
100 km north-south by 50 km east-west.
