<p><strong>New World Power Technology Company North Wind 250</strong>
<br>The North Wind 250 is an advanced technology, 250 kW wind 
turbine developed by the New World Power Technology Company under 
the DOE Wind Turbine Development Program.  The two-bladed, upwind 
turbine uses aircraft-style aileron controls that deploy in high 
winds to smooth out energy spikes. The turbine shown is located in 
the San Gorgonio Pass near Palm Springs, California.
