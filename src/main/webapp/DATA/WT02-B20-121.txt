<H3>The 1997 Susan Gorin Phillips Award</H3>
</TD></TR>
<TD WIDTH=475 ALIGN=LEFT>



<STRONG>Background and Candidates:</STRONG><P>

The Susan Phillips Gorin Award was initiated in 1981 by Student CEC to honor a regular CEC member who has demonstrated exemplary personal and professional qualities while making outstanding contributions to Student CEC and to exceptional children.<P>

Nomination for this award includes two letters of recommendation, one which must be from a current Student CEC member.  The following is information needed to complete the nomination:<P>

1.	Each letter of recommendation should be no more than two pages.<P>

2.	No less than two and no more than four letters of recommendation should be received per nominee.  One must be from a current Student CEC member.<P>

3.	Letters of recommendation should include:<P>
<DL><DT><DD>
	a.	Nominee's contribution in providing leadership and involvement opportunities for Student CEC members.
	<DD>b.	Nominee's involvement with exceptional children and within the field of special education.
</DT></DL>

<STRONG>Deadline:</STRONG> Nominations must be postmarked by December 2, 1996.<P>

<STRONG>Send to:</STRONG><BR>
	Student CEC Executive Committee<BR>
			c/o Coordinator of Student Activities<BR>
			1920 Association Drive<BR>
			Reston, VA  20191-1589<P>
