<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>Age May Play a Role in Your Tax Planning</STRONG></FONT><P>

<STRONG>Are you over age 59 1/2? (Or were you born before January 1, 1936?)</STRONG><BR>

  You may be eligible for favorable tax treatment if you take the money as a lump sum.<P>

<STRONG>Are you younger than 59 1/2?</STRONG><BR> Your options are the most restricted in terms
of
the tax treatment for your distribution. Unless you do a rollover or leave the money in your
employer's plan, it may be subject to both the early withdrawal penalty and immediate
taxation as current income.
