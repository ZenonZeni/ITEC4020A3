<CENTER><I><FONT FACE="ARIAL"><FONT COLOR="#008000"><B>Health Information</B></FONT></FONT></I></CENTER>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><B>No. 2</B></FONT><P></P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>Inhalation:</B></I> N/A.</FONT><P></P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>Eye Contact:</B></I> N/A.</FONT><P></P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>Skin Contact:</B></I> May cause skin irritation. </FONT><P></P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>Ingestion:</B></I> May cause nausea, vomiting, gastrointestinal pain, cramps and diarrhea. Large doses may cause death.</FONT><P></P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>Chronic Exposure:</B></I> Prolonged or repeated skin contact may cause
dermatitis.</FONT><P></P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>Aggravation of Pre-existing Conditions:</B></I> <FONT FACE="ARIAL">Persons with pre-existing skin may be more susceptible to the effects of this substance.</FONT></FONT><BR>

<B><FONT COLOR="#9B004E" FACE="ARIAL"><U>Section III-B:</U></FONT></B>
<CENTER><I><FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><FONT COLOR="#008000"><B>First Aid</B></FONT></I></FONT></I></CENTER>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><B>No. 2</B></FONT><P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>Inhalation:</B></I> N/A</FONT><P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>Skin Exposure:</B></I> Wash with mild soap and apply moisturizer</FONT><P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>Ingestion:</B></I> If swallowed, induce vomiting immediately by giving
two glasses of water and sticking finger down throat. Never give anything by mouth to an unconscious person. Call a physician immediately.</FONT><BR><P></P>
<B><FONT COLOR="#9B004E" FACE="ARIAL"><U>Section IV</U></FONT></B>: 
<CENTER><I><FONT FACE="ARIAL"><FONT COLOR="#008000"><B>Precautionary and First Aid Procedures</B></FONT></FONT></I></CENTER>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><B>No. 1</B></FONT>
<P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>Precautions:</B></I> Wash thoroughly after handling.</FONT>
<P>
<FONT FACE="ARIAL"><I><B>First Aid:</B></I> If swallowed, induce vomiting immediately by giving
two glasses of water and sticking finger down throat. Never give anything by mouth to an unconscious person. If inhaled, remove to fresh air. If not breathing, give artificial respiration. If breathing is difficult give oxygen. In case of contact, immediately flush skin or eyes with plenty of water for at least 15 minutes. In all cases call a physician. (see section III)</FONT><BR><P></P>
