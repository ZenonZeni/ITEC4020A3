<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>What the Ratings Agencies Say</STRONG></FONT><P>

New York Life Insurance Company and New York Life Insurance and Annuity Corporation have 
among the highest rankings from the leading independent ratings agencies, including Standard &amp; Poor's
(AAA) and Duff & Phelps (AAA) for claims-paying ability, and A.M. Best (A++) and Moody's (Aa1) for 
financial strength.  As one of America's top insurance companies, New York Life has helped seven generations 
of families  achieve their financial goals.  Our long history and our heritage of financial strength
and integrity ensure that we'll be there when you need us.
<P>
Here's what the experts say:
<P>
<STRONG>Standard &amp; Poor's</STRONG><BR>
New York Life's Rating: AAA<BR>
New York Life Insurance and Annuity Corporation's Rating: AAA
<P>
"The 'AAA' claims-paying ability rating of New York Life Insurance Co. (NYLIC) reflects its solid
market position, favorable capitalization, above-average earnings performance, and strong asset quality.
New York Life Insurance &amp; Annuity Corp. (NYLIAC), also rated 'AAA,' shares common management with NYLIC
and is strategically important to the parent company."
<P>
<P>
<STRONG>Duff &amp; Phelps Credit Rating Co.</STRONG><BR>
New York Life's Rating: AAA<BR>
New York Life Insurance and Annuity Corporation's Rating: AAA
<P>
"...The rating reflects the company's excellent asset quality, stable and profitable business mix, 
conservative operating leverage, and large size. Duff &amp; Phelps Credit Rating Co. also rates New York
Life's wholly owned subsidiary, NYLIAC, 'AAA.'  This
rating is based on NYLIAC's excellent asset quality, high profitability, and the strength and 
support of its parent." 
<P>
<P>
<STRONG>Moody's Investors Service</STRONG><BR>
New York Life's Rating: Aa1<BR>
New York Life Insurance and Annuity Corporation's Rating: Aa1
<P>
In a recent press release, Moody's noted that New York Life and New York Life Insurance and Annuity 
Corporation's strengths include: "...high quality assets, with relatively low exposure to commercial 
real estate; good market penetration in a few regional group health markets; New York Life's 
strong capital base of more than $5.6 billion; and the group's excellent liquidity position." Moody's 
went on to state that New York Life's career agency force is "an advantage that competitors cannot readily duplicate."
<P>
<P>
<STRONG>A.M. Best</STRONG><BR>
New York Life's Rating: A++<BR>
New York Life Insurance and Annuity Corporation's Rating: A++
<P>
"The rating of New York Life Insurance Company reflects the company's strong market position and excellent distribution 
capacity in its core life, annuity, and pension 
lines of business.  This rating also recognizes the company's superior operating fundamentals, its 
favorable profitability, excellent asset quality, and strong capitalization." 
<P>
