<h3><a name="Join"></a>How to become a member of LDA-CA</h3>
<p>Our annual dues of $30.00<font SIZE=-1>*</font> provide:
<ul>
<li>membership in the national, state and local organizations
<li>subscriptions to the national newsletter <b><i>Newsbriefs</i></b>,<br>
our state newsletter <b><i>The GRAM</i></b>,<br> and local newsletters where
available
<li>mailings from the national, state and local LDA organizations
<li>discounts on conference registration fees
</ul>

Please send your name, address, phone number, and your check for $30 to:
<blockquote>
LDA-CA Membership<br>
655 Lewelling Blvd, #355<br>
San Leandro, CA 94579
</blockquote>

<p><font SIZE=-1>*Fees vary slightly among chapters. You will be billed
for the difference.</font></p>
