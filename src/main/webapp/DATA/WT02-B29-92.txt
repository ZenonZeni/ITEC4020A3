<STRONG><FONT SIZE="+1">Accelerated Benefits</FONT></STRONG><P>

This feature is designed to offer financial relief and peace of mind to terminally ill
individuals.
Accelerated Benefits allow insured employees diagnosed with a terminal illness to request
that a
portion of their Basic and Supplemental Life Insurance benefits be paid to them while they
are living. 
The funds received may be used for any purpose.
