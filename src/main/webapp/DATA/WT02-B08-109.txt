<P>This course is intended to give a rigorous introduction 
to nonlinear control systems from a geometric point of 
view with applications to engineering systems.
<P>
Text:  Isidori, A.,  Nonlinear Control Systems, 
2nd Edition, Springer--Verlag, New York, 1989.


<H2>Tentative Course Outline</H2>
<UL>
 <li>  Differentiable Manifolds, Distributions, The Frobenius Theorem
 <li>  Examples of Nonlinear Control Systems
 <li>  Decomposition of Nonlinear Control Systems:  Reachability and Observability
 <li>  Local Coordinate Transformations, Exact Linearization via Feedback
 <li>  The Zero Dynamics, Asymptotic Stabilization and Output Tracking, Disturbance Decoupling
 <li>  Observers with Linear Error Dynamics
 <li>  Introduction to Singular Perturbations and Integral Manifolds
 <li>  Applications and Special Topics
 </UL>
