<P>
Kemper Municipal Bond Fund, America's first retail municipal bond fund, celebrated its 20th birthday on April 20. 
<P>
<IMG SRC="gifs/20yr_munibond_logo.gif" ALIGN=LEFT ALT=" ">
Since Kemper introduced its fund, 1,847 tax-free mutual funds have been launched, representing approximately $252 billion in municipal bond fund assets as of September 30, 1996.
<P>
Kemper Municipal Bond Fund Class A shares have outperformed the Lipper Analytical Services, Inc. general municipal debt mutual fund category averages for the one, five and 10-year periods ended September 30, 1996.
<P>
Kemper tax-free funds, including Kemper Municipal Bond Fund, Kemper Intermediate Municipal Bond Fund and eight state tax-free income funds, totaled $4.9 billion in assets as of September 30, 1996.
