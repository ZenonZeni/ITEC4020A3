<h1 align="center"><i>UMIHICO</i></h1>

<h3 align="center">Mitsubishi Heavy Industries </h3>

<p>Naval Postgraduate School </p>

<hr>

<p align="center"><img src="f-umihi.gif" align="center"
width="229" height="118"> <img src="umihi.gif" align="center"
width="170" height="180"> </p>

<hr>

<p>This is a three-layered architecture in which commands are
sent by each layer to the one below. The layers manage waypoint
execution, obstacle avoidance and motion control. From bottom to
top: Servo Controller: receives heading and pitch angle commands
and sends thrusters commands; Obstacle Avoidance: uses the sonar
data to calculate headings and avoid obstacles; Route Tracking:
produces the sequence of waypoints to follow and checks if the
given goal has been achieved. </p>
