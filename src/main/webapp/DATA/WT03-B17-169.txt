   <H2>TASC: Preliminary mesocosm experiments, March 1996</H2>
    <HR>
    <BR>
  
Please find below a timetable for the first Mesocosm experiments conducted at the Large Scale Facility at Espengrend (Bergen) from March to May, 1996. The activity have now been going for several weeks, and after some initial problems with catching enough Calanus females in the fjords around, three bags are now running. There has been a lot of practical challenges and the two 
Ph.D.'s (Bernt and Catherine) has been learning alot, which will be of great help for designing the real experiments in 1997 and 1998.
The timetable which has been developed by Benni and Bernt is given below:<p>
