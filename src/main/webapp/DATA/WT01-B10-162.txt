<h2>Primecare Savings and Discounts</h2>



<h3 align=left>Tax Relief Version for Over 60s.</h3>

If you're over 60 and resident in the UK, you can save a further 24% with standard rate tax relief even if you don't pay tax. For joint policyholders, only one person needs to be over 60 to get tax relief on Primecare. This is not available to common law spouses, partners or dependent children, or residents of the Channel Islands and Isle of Man.



<H3>Up to 50% No-Claims Discount</H3>

With most private medical insurance you pay the same premiums whether you claim or not. With Primecare you get an immediate no-claims discount of 25%. This rises every year until, after 5 years without making a claim, you earn a maximum 50% discount. Even if you do make a claim you don't lose all your discount, but simply move back two discount levels. And we guarantee you will never pay more than the basic rate.
