<H1 ALIGN=CENTER>General Travel Tips</H1>

<H3>DON'T LEAVE HOME WITHOUT:</H3>

<UL>
   <LI>Passport/ International Certificate of Vaccination/Yellow Card
   
   <LI>Additional Photo ID ( in case your passport is lost)
   
   <LI>Copies of all prescriptions
   
   <LI>Extra pair of glasses or contact lens
   
   <LI>Sufficient syringes AND a written doctor's authorization for
   their use if you require injectable medication
   
   <LI>First aid kit for minor emergencies
   
   <LI>Personal hygiene products
   
   <LI>Making 2 copies of all your important documents - take 1 with
   you and leave another copy at home
</UL>

<H3>&nbsp;</H3>

<H3>REMEMBER:</H3>

<P>Keep all important documents, medications and glasses <B>WITH
YOU</B> in your carry-on luggage.</P>
