<h5> Area of Research </h5>
A new theory of kinetics has been developed from quantum and 
statistical mechanics.  It is being used to study: Light 
emission from molecules adsorbing on metal surfaces; The
configurational stability of a two phase, fluid system 
subjected negligible gravity; Crystal growth under
microgravity conditions; A mobile, light-weight DC power
supply consisting of a fuel cell and hydrogen storage system.<p>
