<H1>Benefits of FundStation</H1>

<P>
The benefits of FundStation cross many organizational boundaries.
Since it can be difficult to factor them all into the cost/benefit
decision before you have had a chance to actually use FundStation,
we have created the following list of questions. This list is
intended to help you consider all the ways that FundStation can
benefit your operation. 
<P>
Think about each of the following areas in which FundStation can
play a cost-reduction or revenue-enhancing role. In all cases,
FundStation provides solutions to these questions, eliminates
manual effort, eliminates errors and provides more and better
options for disseminating mutual fund information to all of your
audiences. We feel confident that as you explore this list, the
true value and benefits of FundStation will become apparent.
<H2>Fund Accounting </H2>

<UL>
<LI>How much time does your fund accounting team spend every day
preparing reports of daily pricing information for distribution
to clients? How much time do they spend checking and rechecking
the values to ensure correctness before sending the report? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>How much time does your team spend every day creating summary
reports for management that indicate how well the funds are performing?

</UL>

<UL>
<LI>How much time do people spend standing in front of a fax machine
in order to distribute these reports to the desired recipients
before they go home at night? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Would you like to send each recipient a customized version
of the daily report? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>How much time do people spend manually entering fund pricing
data into other systems like a voice response unit, a trust system
or a retirement plan participant recordkeeping system? How much
time is spent checking those numbers to make sure they are correct?

</UL>

<H2>Third Party Reporting </H2>

<UL>
<LI>How much time does your staff spend every day, week and month
preparing surveys for third party agencies? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Are there some surveys and reports that you would file if
only you had the time and manpower to work on them? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>How much time, paper and postage would you save if you could
send a data file to these agencies via modem rather than via fax
or reports sent by overnight delivery? 
</UL>

<H2>Performance Reporting </H2>

<UL>
<LI>How much time is spent every day calculating yields and rates
of return in a spreadsheet? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>How much time is spent at month-end calculating rates of return
for all your funds? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Do your calculations follow the SEC and AIMR guidelines, or
do you use approximations because these guidelines are too complex?

</UL>

<UL>
<LI>How much time is spent preparing the month-end reports after
the rates of return are calculated? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Would clients realize a value if they could get month-end
performance reports on the first business day of the new month?

</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Can you calculate a rate of return from any day to any day
in the life of a fund? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Can you guarantee that yields and rates of returns are kept
accurate, even if someone changes historical NAVs or distributions?

</UL>

<H2>Clients </H2>

<UL>
<LI>How much happier would your clients be if they received their
faxed reports a half hour or an hour earlier than they do now?

</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Would clients rather receive a data file than a faxed report?
This might allow them to have data entered to their own systems
more quickly and with fewer errors. 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>What if you could supply them with the software that loaded
the fund pricing data into their systems for them? Would this
help cement the relationship with your client? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Do clients ask for customized reports that are too much trouble
to produce now? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Would clients find it useful to receive month-to-date and
year-to-date income amounts, yields and rates of return on their
daily reports? What about other calculations that you may not
have time to calculate now? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Do clients ever ask for copies of reports distributed weeks
or months ago? How much time and effort does it take to reproduce
those reports? 
</UL>

<H2>Broker/Dealer Services </H2>

<UL>
<LI>Do your broker/dealers and your broker/dealer support team
have all the information they need to sell the funds? How much
time do you spend delivering that information? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Would more information, in customized formats, delivered in
a timely fashion, encourage broker/dealers to sell more of your
funds? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Would on-line access to a database of historical fund pricing
data be of value to your broker/dealers? 
</UL>

<H2>Marketing/Sales </H2>

<UL>
<LI>How much easier would sales be if you could automatically
fax daily reports on certain funds to your prospects? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Would prospective clients be impressed if you could show them
a rate of return from any date to any other date? What if you
showed them monthly or weekly rates of return since the fund's
inception? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Can you send a data file to on-line Internet services that
specialize in mutual fund information? If you did, with no extra
time or effort every day, would you reach more prospective customers?

</UL>

<H2>Internal Fund Management </H2>

<UL>
<LI>Does management receive the information they need every day
to stay abreast of fund performance? How much time does your staff
spend getting them that information? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Would customized reports for different managers make their
jobs easier, by allowing them to concentrate on the data they
consider important? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>How much paper would you save if these reports could be sent
via your internal e-mail system? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>How much time could you save by using the e-mail system to
distribute reports rather than having someone walk copies of the
reports to individual desks? 
</UL>

<H2>Auditing </H2>

<UL>
<LI>How much time does your team spend generating reports for
the auditors? How much time could you save if, with the click
of the mouse, you could generate reports of average net assets,
or show the detail behind a rate of return calculation? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>How much time does your team spend generating reports of NAVs
and rates of return since inception of a fund for the auditors
review? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>If all this information were available quickly and easily,
could you reduce the time and cost of each audit, allowing your
people to concentrate on the products? 
</UL>

<H2>Shareholder Recordkeeping </H2>

<UL>
<LI>How much time do people spend manually entering end-of-day
pricing data into the transfer agency system? How much time is
spent to verify that the information is correct? 
</UL>

<H2>Other Service Providers </H2>

<UL>
<LI>Could you strengthen the relationship with a transfer agent
or a participant recordkeeping firm by providing fund pricing
data in a data file formatted so that the service provider can
load this data directly into its systems? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Could you save time currently spent reconciling and verifying
data with other vendors if this transfer of information was automated?

</UL>

<H2>Internal MIS </H2>

<UL>
<LI>What savings would occur if you did not need to involve corporate
MIS for every new report or inquiry that you need? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>What savings could MIS achieve by utilizing the centralized
repository for fund information found in FundStation? Could this
open database serve as the core of a corporate-wide information
system? 
</UL>

<UL>
<LI>Are you looking for ways to take better advantage of already
implemented corporate technology? Would you like to better leverage
your investments in technology such as fax servers and e-mail?

</UL>
