<H1>
Melbourne and regional Victoria
</H1>

<H4><I>
Victoria, its capital city, Melbourne, and regional areas offer outstanding 
advantages for companies which serve international and domestic markets.
</H4></I>
<BR>
The State of Victoria lies in the fertile south-eastern coastal region 
of Australia and covers an area of 227,600 square kilometres, about 3% of 
the Australian continent. With 4.5 million people (25% of the national 
population), Victoria is the most populous state after New South Wales.
