<h2>How Americans Die </h2>

<p> <b>This page contains some miscellaneous stats on how
Americans die, and other subjects. </b> </p>

<p> <img src="../graphics/blueline.gif" align="BOTTOM"> </p>

<p> Tobacco smoking is the single most preventable cause of death
in the </p>

<p> U.S. In 1988, an estimated 434,175 premature deaths were
attributed </p>

<p> to smoking tobacco. </p>
