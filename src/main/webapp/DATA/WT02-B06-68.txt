Dear Deirdre:<BR>
<BR>
Thank you for your questions regarding PRODIGY's handling of e-mail. PRODIGY
will hold a member's unopened email for a period of 30 days, after which
it is deleted from the system. Once a member opens an email message, it
remains in the system for three days, and is then deleted. If they wish,
members can instruct PRODIGY to store a message for an additional 15 days,
or they can delete the message and it will be purged from the system within
24 hours.<BR>
