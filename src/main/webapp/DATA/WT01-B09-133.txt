 <B>Project Summary:</B>
<P>
                   The RapidTech Team is comprised of TaylorDunn
of Anaheim, California, Detroit Center Tool (DCT) of Detroit,
Michigan, and RapidTech based in Silicon Valley in San Jose, California.
<P>
                   DARPA EV 94 funded the team to design and revolutionize
the manufacturing process of an electric utility vehicle. The
electric vehicle Industry has suffered in its inability to produce
in volume and this project addresses many of the obstacles that
have prevented manufacturing productivity and innovation.
<P>
<B>Project Deliverables:</B>
<P>
                   RapidTech will deliver a &quot;right from the
box&quot; &#153; kit as well as a manufacturing plan to the State
of Hawaii.
<P>
<B>Project Schedule:</B>
<P>
                  This project was launched on December 9, 1994
and will conclude on July 31, 1996.
