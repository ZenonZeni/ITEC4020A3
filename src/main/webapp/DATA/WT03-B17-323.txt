<CENTER><H1><STRONG><IMG SRC = blgrade2.gif>Natural Tree Care Rules</STRONG></H1></CENTER>
<CENTER><IMG SRC = qs_rbbr.gif></CENTER><BR>
<PRE><HR>
<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#1... Never feed trees a chemical fertilizer.

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#2... Trees require regular watering. Use a drip system whenever possible.<BR>
Avoid watering trunk of tree. Trees are just like women; they love slow deep watering.

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#3... Always use filtered or solarized or well water. Never use straight city <BR>
water as it may contain chlorine or some other chemical that can poison <BR>
the trees.  See Garden Filter page (coming soon).

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#4... Allow time for trees to Heal. The bigger the tree, the longer it will <BR>
take to Heal.

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#5... Raise the energy level of the tree  thru the addition of compost rich in <BR>
trace minerals and bacteria. Trees love rock Dust!

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#6... Know the law of cause and effect. Control the cause and you control the <BR>
effects. Trees with high energy levels are not attacked by pests.

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#7... Don't cut down trees.

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#8... Learn your trees. Plant only trees that will grow in your area. <BR>
Give your favorite tree a name.

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#9... Protect trees from dogs. Dog urine is very acidic and will injure the <BR>
tree's root system.

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#10... Cultivate the soil regularly. The soil will get packed so that water <BR>
and air may not reach the roots.

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#11... Mulch. Provide plenty of mulch.

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#12... Protect trees from people!

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#13... Do not poison the soil. Avoid spilling gasoline, paint, salt etc...

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#14... Remove any tight wire supports as this will strangle the tree as it <BR>
grows.

<IMG SRC = goldstar.gif ></A>#15... Plant ground covers. They make a great living mulch which<BR>
 conserves water.
