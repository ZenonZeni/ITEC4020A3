<H1>OCRWM National Information Center Services</H1>
<HR>

The OCRWM National Information Center was established to provide a means for easy access to information about plans and activities within the waste management program.  The National Information Center is located at 600 Maryland Avenue, SW, Suite 695, Washington, D.C.  To visit the Center, which offers publications and videotapes free of charge, or to find out more about the OCRWM Program, please call (202) 488-6720. 
  

<H3>Services currently offered by the OCRWM National Information Center include:</H3>
<UL>

<LI>Employment of the <A HREF="http:/www.rw.doe.gov/">OCRWM Home Page</A>
<LI>Retrieval of OCRWM photographs through the Digital Media Database (an electronic graphics library)
<LI>Publication of the <A HREF="/pages/current/bullet/bullet.htm">The OCRWM Enterprise</A>, OCRWM's semiannual newsletter
<LI>Distribution of <A HREF="Pubs.HTM">OCRWM publications</A>, including OCRWM's educational curriculum
<LI>Distribution of <A HREF="Videos.HTM">OCRWM videotapes</A>
<LI>Responding to public mail <A HREF="/comments/compage1.htm">inquiries about the OCRWM Program</A> received via the OCRWM Home Page and other sources.
</UL>


<H3>The following services were discontinued due to budget reductions in fiscal year 1996:</H3>

<UL>
<LI>The OCRWM-wide exhibits program
<LI>Utility outreach program
<LI>Teacher-training workshops.
</UL>
