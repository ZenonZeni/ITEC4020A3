<H2><A HREF="http://www.iea.org/impagr/imporg/effene/effene.htm" >Energy End-Use Technology  
</A>Implementing Agreements</H2>
<HR>

<P>
To achieve a high efficiency and to eliminate duplication of effort,
strong links are maintained with <A HREF="http://www.iea.org/" >IEA  
</A>Energy End-Use Technology Implementing Agreements in related
areas. These include:
<P>
Energy end-use in buildings:
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="http://www.ecbcs.org/" >IEA Energy Conservation in Buildings and Community Systems Programme  
</A>(ECBCS)
<LI><A HREF="http://www.aivc.org/" >IEA ECBCS Annex 5 - Air Infiltration and Ventilation Centre  
</A>(AIVC)
<LI><A HREF="http://www.iea.org/impagr/imporg/effene/a24dhc.htm" >IEA District Heating and Cooling  
</A>
<LI><A HREF="http://www.HeatPumpCentre.org/" >IEA Heat Pump Centre  
</A>(HPC)
</UL>

<P>
Information Centres:
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="http://www.caddet-ee.org/" >IEA Centre for the Analysis and Dissemination of Demonstrated Energy Technologies  
</A>(CADDET)
<LI><A HREF="http://www.ornl.gov/CADDET/caddet.html" >IEA CADDET USA  
</A>
<LI><A HREF="http://www.etde.org/" >IEA Energy Technology Data Exchange  
</A>(ETDE)
</UL>

<P>
Enabling Technologies:
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="http://www.luth.se/depts/sb/fo_vatten/projects/iea" >IEA Energy Storage  
</A>
</UL>
