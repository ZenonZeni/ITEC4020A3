<h3>LDA Periodicals</h3>

<p>LDA publishes two periodicals; <b><i><a href="Journal.html">Learning
Disabilities: A Multidisciplinary Journal</a> </i></b>and 
<b><a href="newsbriefs/">LDA <i>Newsbriefs</i></a></b>. The
<b><i>Journal</i></b> is a semi-annual technical publication oriented
toward professionals in the field of learning disabilities (subscription: 
$25/year); <b><i>Newsbriefs</i></b> is a non-technical publication
distributed six times a year to all LDA members (non-member subscription:
$13.50/year).

<p>A <a href="forms/journal.html"><b><i>Journal</i> subscription
form</b></a> and a <a href="forms/newsbriefs.html"><b><i>Newsbriefs</i> 
subscription form</b></a> can be downloaded. Contact our <a
href="Email.html">National Office</a> for further information concerning
subscriptions, advertising, or submission of articles for
possible publication.

<h3>Occasional LDA Publications</h3>

<p>LDA publishes books, booklets, brochures, papers, pamphlets, etc., on
many topics related to learning disabilities. Some recent publications
are reviewed on the <a href="Resource.html">Resources</a> page. An extensive
listing of LDA (and other) publications available from LDA
is given in the <a href="store/">Bookstore Index</a>.
Several of our <a href="pamphlets/">pamphlets and information packets</a>,
along with several <a href="Aindex.html">articles from LDA publications</a>,
have been posted on this web site. <br>
