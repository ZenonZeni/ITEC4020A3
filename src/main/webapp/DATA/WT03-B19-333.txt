<H3>Research Interests</H3>

Analytical Chemistry;  Aquaculture nutrition Chemistry;  Fatty acid chemistry.
My research is concerned with the application of modern analytical techniques 
such as gas chromatography, GC-mass spectrometry and high performance liquid 
chromatography to the analysis of feed species important in tropical 
aquaculture. Several projects have been concerned with lipid, carbohydrate 
and protein content and fatty acid composition of recently isolated species 
of tropical microalgae and macroalgae (seaweeds). Fatty acid studies have 
centred on long-chain polyunsaturated fatty acids such as eicosapentaenoic 
acid and docosahexaenoic acid, which are essential for the survival and 
growth of juvenile stages of many invertebrate species of importance to 
the aquaculture industry.<P>
