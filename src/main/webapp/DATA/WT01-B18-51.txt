<B><U><FONT SIZE="+1">Explanation</FONT></U></B>:  <P>

<B><I><FONT SIZE="+1">Temperature (F) -</FONT></I></B>  <P>

	<B>MnHi - </B> Mean High  <BR>
	<B>MnLo - </B> Mean Low  <BR>
	<B>Medn - </B> Median (not mean because the NOAA equipment registers only high and low each day)  <BR>
	<B>HiHi - </B> Highest High  <BR>
	<B>LoHi - </B> Lowest High  <BR>
	<B>HiLo - </B> Highest Low  <BR>
	<B>LoLo - </B> Lowest Low  <P>

<B><I><FONT SIZE="+1">Rainfall (inches) -</FONT></I></B> <P>

	<B>Rtotl - </B> Total  <BR>
	<B>Rmean - </B> Mean (monthly or daily)  <BR>
	<B>Nrd - </B> Number of raindays  <BR>
	<B>Daymx - </B> Maximum in one day<BR>
	<B>Mnt - </B> Month  <BR>
	<B>Jdy - </B> Julian Day  <BR>
	<B>Yr - </B> Year    <P>
