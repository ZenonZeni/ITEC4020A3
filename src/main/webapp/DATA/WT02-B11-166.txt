With all the people that have complained to me personally about the<br>
Penn Intouch system (Not like I have anything to do with it), I was<br>
kind of wondering why there hasn't been a larger public student<br>
movement to get its current incarnation mothballed.  It scares me to<br>
think that people will be accessing information that should be highly<br>
confidential from computer labs and the like, using their Birthdates<br>
and SS#'s. I mean, who in their right mind would decide to implement<br>
such a system in the first place? What worries me as well as that<br>
those same people a wrapping themselves in a false security blanket by<br>
using the netscape commerce server (I don't trust anything that<br>
doesn't have publically available sources).<br>
<p>
Anybody care to comment?<br>
