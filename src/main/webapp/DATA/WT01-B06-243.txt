<H1><CENTER>Inversion of atmospheric measurements </CENTER></H1>
<P>
Inversion of ground based and of limb sounding from satellite
of passive observations at sub-mm wavelengths is the topic of
an ongoing project related to observations of ozone and ozone
chemistry. Ground based observations of ozone are ongoing at the
<A HREF="http://www.oso.chalmers.se">Onsala Space Observatory </A>and
<A HREF="http://www.misu.su.se/~martin/odin.html">ODIN </A>will
observe by means of limb sounding technique. Estimation of different
inversion techniques and system simulation analysis are parts
of the program.<BR>
