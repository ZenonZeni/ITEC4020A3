<H2>Photobiological Production</H2>
<br>

<p>In photobiological systems, hydrogen is produced as a result of the 
way certain biological organisms use sunlight.  Some photosynthetic algae 
and 
bacteria absorb energy from sunlight and then use that energy to split 
water into 
hydrogen and oxygen.  Researchers are working to increase the efficiency 
of these 
biological systems for producing hydrogen.  Click on any of these 
projects to learn 
more about research efforts:
