<H1>Analysis</H1>
<HR SIZE=1 NOSHADE>



<P><IMG ALIGN=RIGHT SRC="nmr.gif" alt=nmr align=bottom>Much of our analytical work is directed at helping manufacturers meet their <A HREF="fis.html">statutory obligations</A> and at achieving the high standards expected by their customers. Checking the safety, composition and purity <A HREF="authenticity.html">(authenticity)</A> of products is a regular requirement, as is troubleshooting. </P>

<P>RSSL&#39;s <A HREF="labs.html">laboratories</A> have the range and depth of expertise to undertake analysis from simple compositional studies to the determination of analytes at parts per billion or lower.</P>

<P>Our analysts work to recognised methods (eg AOAC, BP, USP etc) and to procedures developed in-house. Externally accredited quality systems such as NAMAS and GLP are applied where appropriate.</P>

<P>For more information on how we can address your specific analytical requirements call <STRONG>0800 243482</STRONG> or complete the <A HREF="enquiry.html">enquiry form.</A></P>
