<TITLE>How to Report an Attack on the Press</TITLE>

<BODY bgcolor="#FFFFFF">
<img src="../pictures/cpjlogo.gif" HEIGHT=90 WIDTH=90 ALIGN=LEFT><Center></cite><br><BR></cite><BR></cite><h1>How to Report an Attack on the Press<br></h1></center></cite>
<br><hr>
<blockquote>

<BR>
CPJ needs accurate, detailed information in order to document abuses of press freedom 
and effectively help journalists in trouble. CPJ corroborates the information and 
takes appropriate action on behalf of the journalists and news organizations involved.<BR>
<BR>
<HR>
<BR>
<STRONG>What to report: </STRONG>
<BR>
<BR>
<STRONG>Journalists who are: </STRONG>
<BR>
<ul>
<li>Missing<BR>
<li>Killed<BR>
<li>Arrested or kidnapped<BR>
<li>Wounded<BR>
<li>Assaulted<BR>
<li>Threatened<BR>
<li>Harassed<BR>
<li>Wrongfully expelled<BR>
<li>Wrongfully sued for libel or defamation<BR>
<li>Denied credentials<BR>
<li>Censored<BR>
</ul>
<BR>
<STRONG>News organizations that have been: <BR>
</STRONG>
<ul>
<li>Attacked, raided or illegally searched<BR>
<li>Closed by force<BR>
<li>Wrongfully sued for libel or defamation<BR>
<li>Censored<BR>
<li>Materials confiscated or damaged<BR>
<li>Editions confiscated or transmissions jammed<BR>
</ul>

<HR>
<BR>
<STRONG>Information Needed: </STRONG><br>
<BR>
<STRONG>CPJ needs accurate, detailed information about: <BR>
</STRONG>
<ul>
<li>Journalists and news organizations involved<BR>
<li>Date and circumstances of incident<BR>
<li>Background information<BR>
</ul>

<BR>
<STRONG>What happens next: <BR>
</STRONG>
<STRONG><BR>
</STRONG>
<STRONG>Depending on the case, CPJ will: <BR>
<BR></STRONG>
<ul>
<li>Confirm the report.<BR>
<li>Pressure authorities to respond.<BR>
<li>Notify human rights groups and press organizations around the world, including IFEX, Article 19, Amnesty International, Reporters Sans Fronti&egrave;res, PEN, International Federation of Journalists and Human Rights Watch.</li>
<li>Increase public awareness through the press.<BR>
<li>Publish advisories to warn other journalists about potential dangers.<BR>
<li>Send a fact-finding mission to investigate.<BR>
<BR>
</ul>
