<B>4. WORKING GROUPS<BR>
<BR>
</B>In the medium and longer term, the EOC and Divisional scientists and
groups will jointly seek funds from the Sectors and external bodies for
Tasks. These Tasks will be developed in 1996/97 through the action of a
range of Working Groups. Potential WGs for this year include: 
<BLOCKQUOTE>Airborne Remote Sensing Issues;<BR>
Hyperspectral Specification;<BR>
Spectral Libraries;<BR>
Generic Issues in Atmospheric Correction;<BR>
Future sensors and satellites and processing implications;<BR>
Cal &amp; Val issues in international missions;<BR>
Energy &amp; Water Balances &amp; Fluxes; <BR>
Optical Water Quality and Ocean Colour;<BR>
Spectral Unmixing;<BR>
Radar applications (including land and marine);<BR>
Software implementation and /or Commercial interactions in processing and
VAP release;<BR>
Cloud issues - cloud clearing and cloud climatology;<BR>
Measurable Standards for Earth Observation Products;<BR>
Data Integration Issues.</BLOCKQUOTE>
