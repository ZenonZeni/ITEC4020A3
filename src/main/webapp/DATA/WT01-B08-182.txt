<img src="r_star.gif"> <FONT SIZE=3><B> Things to look for in export transactions!</B><P>

<UL><IL><img src="lightblu.gif"> If one of the parties to your transaction has a name or address that is similar to an entity on the U.S. Department of Commerce's <A HREF="ftp://ftp.fedworld.gov/pub/bxa/1_denial.txt"><B><I>List of Denied Persons</I></B></A> or if the transaction has circumstances similar to one of those listed in the Commerce Department's (BXA's) <A HREF="redflags.htm"><B><I> Red Flag Indicators </I></B></A><P>

<IL><img src="lightblu.gif"> You should contact the Bureau of Export Administration's (BXA) <A HREF="#index1"><B><I>Office of Export Enforcement</I></B></A><P>

<IL><img src="lightblu.gif"> If you are being asked to participate in an export transaction that you believe may be illegal, or if you have information that such an illegal transaction may be about to occur, you are encouraged to contact BXA's <A HREF="#index1"><B><I>Office of Export Enforcement</I></B></A> immediately .<P>

<IL><img src="lightblu.gif"> If you are being asked to participate in the Arab boycott of Israel, or if you are being asked questions like<BR>
<UL><LI> "What are the nationalities of the members of your Board of Directors?"<BR><LI>
 "Is your firm related to the ABCXYZ company?"<BR><LI>
 "Will you send us a list of your proposed suppliers?"</UL>

Before you respond you are encouraged to call BXA's <A HREF="#index2"><B><I>Antiboycott Compliance Office Advice Line</I></B></A> immediately.<P>

<UL><IL>Any response you make to these types of questions, regardless of whether the answer is a <B>"yes"</B> or a<B> "no," </B>may be a violation of the U.S. Department of Commerce's antiboycott regulations. You may be required to report the boycott requests you receive.  The advice line duty officer will assist you in determining your reporting requirements.  Callers to the BXA  <A HREF="#index2"><B><I>Advice Line</I></B></A>  may remain anonymous if they wish.<P><A NAME="index1"></A></UL>  

Click <A HREF="oacprogr.htm"><B><I>here</I></B></A> for more information on BXA's Antiboycott program.<P></UL>
