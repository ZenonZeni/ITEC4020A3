<center><h2>RBIS Committees</h2></center>

We are encouraging our members to get involved by working on one of four
committees.

<ul>

<p><li>Events - coordinate logistics of RBIS events;

<p><li>Finance - set fundraising goals and oversee the fundraising efforts for RBIS;

<p><li>Marketing - Create overall logistical strategy for image-building and marketing
of RBIS' activities and merchandise;

<p><li>Public Relations - work with local media, produce a quarterly newsletter, a
video presentation, and create a speakers bureau.

</ul>
