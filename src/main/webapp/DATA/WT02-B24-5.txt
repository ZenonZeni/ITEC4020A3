<h1 align=center>How to become a member of LDA</h1>

<p>
LDA has state and/or local affiliates in every state. When you
join LDA, you will also be enrolled in your state and local affiliate.
Annual dues vary among affiliates, but are usually about $25.00, which
includes subscription fees for our national newsletter, <b><i>Newsbriefs</i></b>,
and state and local newsletters. </p>

<p>To join LDA, send your name and address, along with $25.00* ($30 foreign),
to:
<blockquote>
<p><b>
  LDA<br>
  4156 Library Road<br>
  Pittsburgh, PA 15234-1349 </b>
</blockquote>

<p>A <b><a href="appl_nm.html">Membership Application Form</a></b> may be
printed for convenience.


<p>___________________
<br>*Dues vary by states. Yours may be slightly higher; if so, you will
be billed for the difference. </p>
