<B>JOB POSTING</B>

<B>
POSITION:</B>          Research Scientist I - Grade 18
                   Salary: $35,237 ($17,618)  50% 
<B>
LOCATION:</B>          New York State Psychiatric Institute
                   Therapeutics/Anxiety Disorders Clinic
<B>
DUTIES:</B>            Candidate will be responsible for screening all potential 
                   patients.  She/he will also be responsible for 
                   administering structured diagnostic interviews for all 
                   patients accepted into one of the anxiety clinic 
                   protocols.  The incumbent will also be conducting 
                   independent evaluations of patients enrolled in treatment 
                   studies.  Other duties, such as coordination and 
                   monitoring of ongoing clinical trials may be assigned as
                   needed.
<B>
EDUCATION:</B>         Bachelor's degree and 2 years of professional research 
                   experience in psychology, or a master's degree and 1 year 
                   of professional research experience.  Two years minimum of 
                   clinical experience preferred.

<B>TRAINING & </B>
<B>EXPERIENCE:</B>        Training in clinical assessment and structured clinical 
                   interviews.

<B>SPECIAL</B>
<B>REQUIREMENTS:</B>       Must be bilingual in English/Spanish.
