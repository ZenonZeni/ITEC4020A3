       <H1>Kovda</H1>  <IMG SRC="../../grafikk/russia.gif" align="right"><br>   <BR><p>
</center><strong>
The old fishery village of Kovda just south of Kandalaksia. The housings and church of this city is not altered since the 16th century. Very picturesque scene well worth visiting.
It is located close to the major highway running from St. Petersburg to Murmansk.
