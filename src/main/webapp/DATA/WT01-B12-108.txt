<BR>
Supplied with a steady stream of government reports, corporate earnings
releases, analysts' forecasts and miscellaneous other data,
market-watchers are never at a loss for information. Their challenge: To
understand what's in each snapshot - as well as in the surrounding
environment that an individual market report may not have captured.
<P>

Here's where you'll find current market news, including our outlook and economic 
interpretations by John Silvia, chief economist for Kemper Funds' management. 
Because of the changing nature of this information, you'll
want to check back here often.
