        <h2>Information </h2>
        <h3>Administration, Faculty and Staff </h3>
        <p>In early 1993, the Institute's Board of Directors
        completed an integrated Long Range Plan to establish St.
        Joseph's as the nation's leading auditory/oral school for
        hearing impaired children. </p>
        <p>Guided by this plan and managing from our mission,
        Institute President Michael Keller and St. Joseph's
        management team are advancing that vision for the
        betterment of our children. </p>
        <p>The school program is under the direction of Sister
        Joyce Buckler, Principal for the past 39 years has made
        her one of the nation's most respected educators of the
        deaf. Sister Joyce has a Masters degree in Audiology from
        Northwestern University and a doctorate in Special
        Education from Columbia University. She has served
        several terms on the AG Bell Association Board of
        Directors and is a founder of the network of Option
        Schools. </p>
        <p>The Institute's faculty is comprised of 30
        professionals -- holding doctoral, Masters and
        undergraduate degrees in deaf education -- with more than
        400 years of combined experience. </p>
        <p>They are complemented by a degreed librarian, licensed
        child psychologist, certified audiologists, speech
        pathologists, early childhood specialists, registered
        nurses and trained counselors. And the loving Sisters of
        St. Joseph. </p>
        <p>No less dedicated to our work is the support staff --
        house parents, child care aides, administrative, dietary,
        maintenance and housekeeping personnel. All reflect St.
        Joseph Institute's special combination of family warmth
        and assured professionalism. All have an uncommon
        dedication to our common mission. </p>
        <p>You are invited to visit St. Joseph Institute for the
        Deaf to see our campus and meet our people. Appointments
        are appreciated and easily scheduled. Contact Sr. Joyce
        Buckler, Principal, or Michael Keller, President, at
        (314)993-1507 (Voice and TDD) or write to them at: 1483
        82nd Boulevard, Saint Louis, Missouri 63132. </p>
