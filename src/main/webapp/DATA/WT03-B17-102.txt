<H1>WRECKS OF THE WORLD<BR>
Empress of Ireland (Canada):</H1>
<IMG ALIGN=right SRC="ship.GIF" ALT="Photo of Empress"><P>The ship lies eerily on her side in 160 feet of water just off the port of Rimouski.  Her structure remains almost intact, preserved by the near freezing water.  Despite the number of divers that have visited her, she still contains numerous artifacts as well as the human remains and ghosts of many unfortunate prisoners.  The strong currents, frigid water, depth and limited visibility make for challenging diving, to be undertaken by only advanced, trained wreck divers.  For those who possess the skills, this wreck provides the only accessible opportunity to explore a historic tragedy of such a scale.
<P>The expedition can be combined with any level of technical certification but is inappropriate for divers with no previous deep wreck and cold water experience.  NITROX and decompression oxygen are available on site.  TRIMIX and argon fills are available by special request.
