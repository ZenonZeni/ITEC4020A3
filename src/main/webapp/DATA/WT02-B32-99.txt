<H1>Enzyme and Molecular Biotechnology Group</H1>
</font>
<P>
<P>The Enzyme and Molecular Biotechnology Group is part of the Biotechnology and Biochemical Engineering group of the Department of Food Science and Technology, The University of Reading. Led by <a href="index.htm">Dr. Bob Rastall</a>, it is primarily interested in carbohydrate bioengineering and bioremediation. </P>
</ul>
<IMG ALIGN="left" SRC="cbglogo.gif"HEIGHT=85 WIDTH=85>
<ul>
<P>Specific areas of interest include:</P>
<UL>
<LI><a href="enzsnth.htm">Enzymatic synthesis of oligosaccharides</a></LI>
<LI><a href="strucfun.htm">Structure-function relationships in glycosidases</a></LI>
<LI><a href="Prebiot.htm">Synthesis of prebiotic oligosaccharides</a></LI>
<LI><a href="baking.htm">Glycosidases in baking</a></LI>
<LI><a href="biorem.htm">Bioremediation</a></LI>
</UL>
