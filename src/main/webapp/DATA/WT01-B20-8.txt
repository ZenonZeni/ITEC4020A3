<CENTER><B>Correction</B></CENTER>

<P><SPACER TYPE="HORIZONTAL" SIZE="50"><B>NORML</B> erroneously reported in it's Weekly Press Release 
(March 21, 1996) that <B><I>Court TV</I></B> will be televising the trial of 
New York City Medical Marijuana Club coordinator Johann Moore.&nbps;  
At present, the trial court has not yet approved <B><I>Court TV</I></B> (or any of it's affiliated programming) to televise this notable and important legal challenge to a significant aspect of cannabis' prohibition.
<BR><SPACER TYPE="HORIZONTAL" SIZE="50"><B>NORML</B> apologizes to Mr. Moore, <B><I>Court TV</I></B>, and it's affiliates for any confusion caused by <B>NORML</B>'s reporting error.
<BR><SPACER TYPE="HORIZONTAL" SIZE="50"><B>NORML</B> and other organizations in the cannabis reform community strongly implore <B><I>Court TV</I></B> and other media outlets to cover this interesting and complex trial.
<BR><SPACER TYPE="HORIZONTAL" SIZE="50"><I>For more details on the case of Johann Moore, please contact 
Cures-not-Wars @ (212) 677-4899.&nbps;  For more information on 
Cannabis Buyers Clubs, please contact Allen St. Pierre of <B>N0RML</B> @ 
(202) 483-5500.</I>
