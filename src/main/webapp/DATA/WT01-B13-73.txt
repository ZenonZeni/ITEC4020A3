<BLOCKQUOTE>

<IMG SRC="gifs/salescharges_head.gif" ALT="Sales Charges">
<BR>
The maximum initial sales charge (Class A) is 5.75% for Kemper Blue Chip
Fund, Kemper-Dreman Contrarian Fund, Kemper-Dreman High Return Fund,
Kemper-Dreman Small Cap Value Fund, Kemper Horizon Fund, Kemper Europe Fund, 
Kemper Quantitative Equity Fund,
Kemper International Fund, Kemper
Growth Fund, Kemper Technology Fund, Kemper Small Capitalization Equity
Fund, Kemper Total Return Fund and Kemper Value+Growth Fund. 
<P>

The maximum
initial sales charge (Class A) is 4.50% for Kemper Income Funds (3.5%
for Kemper Adjustable Rate U.S. Government Fund and Kemper
Short-Intermediate Government Fund) and 5.0% for Kemper Target Equity
Funds. 
<P>

Sales charges are reduced or eliminated under certain conditions
as described in the funds' <A HREF="../prospectus.html">prospectuses.</A>
