<h4 align="center">Helpline Fact Sheet </h4>

<h4 align="center"><font color="#000080"><b>Depakote--Treatment
for </b></font><a href="bipolar.htm"><font color="#000080"><b>Manic
Depression</b></font>&nbsp;</a></h4>

<hr>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><b>Side Effects</b></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - harmful to fetuses</p>
    <p>2 - liver problems may develop, especially in the 1st 6
    months of treatment</p>
    <p>3 - problems with white blood cell counts &amp; blood
    platelets</p>
    <p>4 - regular blood tests reduce the risk of side effects</p>
    <p>5 - nausea, drowsiness, dizziness may occur but often
    subside over time</p>
    <p>6 - possibility of rashes, hair loss, or itching</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><strong>What is Depakote?</strong></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - divalproex sodium</p>
    <p>2 - marketed by Abbott Laboratories</p>
    <p>3 - treatment of manic episodes associated with Bipolar
    Disorder/Manic Depression</p>
    <p>4 - introduced to the public in 1983 as a treatment for
    epilepsy</p>
    <p>5 - introduced as a treatment for Bipolar Disorder in June
    of 1995</p>
    <p>6 - the 1st medication approved for the treatment of manic
    symptoms in 25 years</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><strong>Dosage</strong></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - administered orally</p>
    <p>2 - available in 3 tablet strengths</p>
    <p>3 - recommended initial dose is 750 mg daily taken in
    divided dosages (less in elderly patients)</p>
    <p>4 - gradual increase until one is taking the lowest dose
    that will produce the desired clinical effect</p>
    <p>5 - most respond to treatment within 10 days </p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><strong>Who should not take depakote?</strong></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - those with liver disease or significant liver
    dysfunction</p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote>
    <p>2 - pregnant women</p>
    <p>3 - nursing mothers</p>
    <p>4 - those taking certain other medications (consult with a
    physician).</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><strong>Benefit of depakote</strong></font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - one of two treatments (the other is lithium) available
    for treating the manic symptoms of Bipolar Disorder - offers
    an option. </p>
</blockquote>
