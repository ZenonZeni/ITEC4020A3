<h1>Douglas Wilkinson</h1>

<hr>
<B>
I left the department on Friday 31 May 1996 to work for a Reading based company - Checkpoint Security Services Ltd. My email address there is <a href="mailto:dwilkinson@checkpoint.co.uk">dwilkinson@checkpoint.co.uk</a>.
</b>
<hr>
<P> 
I graduated from the University of Reading with a BSc in Biotechnology in July 1990. Following this I was involved in a biotechnology research project before taking over responsibility for computer services within the department of Food Science and Technology in March 1993.
<p>
My first task was to commission a small departmental PC lab of six 386 machines served by a Novell 3.11 file server. Immediately following this I was responsible for extending the network throughout the rest of the department and subsequently for:
<p>
<ul>
<li>Management and development of the departmental Novell Netware 3.11 network comprising 2 servers and approximately 150 client PCs.
<li>User training and trouble-shooting for all systems in use in the department.
<li>Overall responsibility for the creation and management of database systems used for administrative purposes.
<li>Administration of deparmental <a href="http://www.let.rug.nl/pegasus/">Pegasus Mail</a> system .
<li>Management of departmental World Wide Web server.
<li>Specification and sourcing of all computer systems and software used in the department.
<li>Development of the use of computers in teaching.
</ul>
I have considerable experience in the following areas of computer networking:
<ul>
<li>Novell NetWare 3.11 network operating system administration.
<li>Novell LAN WorkPlace for DOS and Windows (plus some experience of SunSoft PC-NFS).
<li>Thin Ethernet cable installation.
<li>Concurrent support of IP and IPX network traffic using generic packet drivers and ODI/VLMs.
<li>Microsoft Windows for Workgroups 3.11 (with Novell NetWare 3.11).
<li>HTML authoring and WWW administaration.
</ul>
I also regularly provide installation and user support for the following software applications:
<ul>
<li>Microsoft Windows 3.xx
<li>Microsoft DOS 3.3 - 6.22
<li>Microsoft Word 2.0 and 6.0 for Windows
<li>Microsoft Excel 4.0 and 5.0 for Windows
<li>Microsoft Powerpoint 4.0 for Windows
<li>CA SuperCalc 5.5 for DOS
<li>WordPerfect 5.1 for DOS
</ul>
