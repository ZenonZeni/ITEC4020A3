 <H1>Karelia</H1>  <IMG SRC="../../grafikk/russia.gif" align="right"><br>   <BR><p>
</center><strong>
The Republic of Karelia is an enchanting experience in the misty light of sunset. This region, where poverty is quite common, was taken by USSR after the famous Winter War. This region is experiencing a time lag of 70 years comparing to the Western World. Small villages where time has seized to affect the inhabitants are quite common. General impression of Karelia: Poor area with small villages, but very friendly people.<p>
