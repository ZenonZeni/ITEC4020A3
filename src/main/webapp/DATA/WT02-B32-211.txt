<H3>BIOTECHNOLOGY (BT)</H3>

<B>Applied Genetics News--BT03</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bt03.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 10/1/89<BR>
(Also in CH, HH)<BR>
Monitors the business and technology of biotechnology, keeping readers up to date
on the individuals and companies making headlines, as well as coverage of patents,
contracts, university research, and more.<P>



<B>Biotech Business--BT06</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bt06.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Worldwide Videotex<BR>
$4.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 7/1/88<BR>
(Also in HH, RD)<BR>
Provides news and information on biotechnology products, developments, and
companies. Highlights marketing and investment opportunities and reports on
finances, management, company mergers, clinical drug trials, and scientific
breakthroughs. Written for venture capitalists, stock market investors, drug
company executives, and researchers seeking investors.<P>


<B>Biotech Equipment Update--BT12</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bt12.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Worldwide Videotex<BR>
$5.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 3/1/94<BR>
(No cross-referencing)<BR>
Covers the latest biotechnology lab and<BR>
manufacturing equipment and products. Includes reports on new products in addition
to reports on vendor research, development, and manufacturing.<P>


<B>Biotech Financial Reports--BT14</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bt14.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Worldwide Videotex<BR>
$5.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 5/1/94<BR>
(Also in FI)<BR>
Provides the latest financial reports of biotechnology companies. Lists the
financial figures of the winners and the losers as well as the reasons behind the
bottom lines and the firms' present and future strategies.<P>


<B>Biotechnology Business News--BT13</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bt13.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Financial Times Ltd.<BR>
$3.00/article; $45.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 3/25/94<BR>
(Also in FT, RD)<BR>
Provides insightful analysis and commentary on the international biotechnology
industry. Focuses on research and scientific news, product launches, regulatory
updates, mergers, acquisitions and joint ventures, licensing deals, and financial
and commercial results of biotech companies.<P>


<B>Biotechnology Newswatch--BT08</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bt08.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
The McGraw-Hill Companies Publications Online<BR>
$4.00/article<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 1/15/90<BR>
(Also in FB, HH, RD, M-H, Pharma)<BR>
Examines the business, legal, and industrial ramifications of biotechnology,
including the developments in genetic engineering processes.<P>


<B>Comline Japan Daily:  Biotechnology--BT07</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bt07.html">Sample Issue</a><BR> 
Comline International Corp.<BR> 
$4.00/article<BR> 
Updated daily, from 1/5/90<BR> 
(Also in FB T, ME, Comline, Japan, PacRim, Pharma)<BR> 
Concentrates on new pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and research conducted
at major laboratories.  Includes articles on equipment used in basic research,
industrial applications of bioreactors and new biotechniques, developments in
agricultural science, and the results of research on current topics such as
anticancer, anti-AIDS, and anti-thrombic agents.<P>

<B>High Tech Separations News--BT04</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bt04.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 9/1/89<BR>
(Also in RD)<BR>
Covers the fast-growing fields of organic and biomolecular separations,
including the technologies, economies, markets, and business.<P>


<B>Life Sciences Biotechnology Update--BT15</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bt15.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Merton Allen Associates<BR>
$3.00/article; $25.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 4/1/95<BR>
(Also in ME, HH)<BR>
Presents reviews of published information relating to the life sciences and
biotechnology; including, agriculture; food; medicine; health; disease; and much
more. Issues cover reports, papers, assessments; bibliographies; database
searches.<P>


<B>Membrane & Separation Technology News--BT05</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bt05.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Business Communications Co. Inc.<BR>
$4.00/article; $50.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 9/1/89<BR>
(Also in FB, RD)<BR>
Explores the science, technology, and business angles of microfiltration, reverse
osmosis, ultrafiltration, cross filtration, gas separation, electrodialysis,
specialty chromatography, and an array of other separation opportunities.<P>
