<H1>Healthcare</H1>
<HR SIZE=1 NOSHADE>


<P><IMG ALIGN=RIGHT SRC="pharma100.gif" alt=pharmaceutical>
RSSL can carry out a variety of routine quality control analyses and contamination investigations to free in-house R&D staff for more productive work.</P>

<P>A number of pharmaceutical manufacturers use RSSL for these routine services, and increasingly many more companies are also using our <A HREF="sensory.html">sensory evaluation</A> services to help formulate medicines that taste more acceptable to the public.</P>

<P>RSSL has also been successful in transferring other disciplines from the food industry to the healthcare industry including HACCP systems, <A HREF="training.html">training</A>, product stability studies and the <A HREF="ers.html">Emergency Response Service</A>.</P>

<P>RSSL also has a <A HREF="biotechnology.html">biotechnology</A> unit which has the expertise and failities to supply biomass for screening programmes.</P>
