This section of the NYLCare site has been designed for New York

Life agents and independent brokers.<P>



Agents and brokers are critical to the success of NYLCare.  As a

wholly-owned subsidiary of the New York Life Insurance Company,

New York Life agents and independent brokers are our 

primary link to our clients.  <P>



<STRONG>New York Life Agents:</STRONG>  As agents, you are our

customers' single point of contact for purchasing and managing a

complete range of health and related benefit options.  NYLCare is

committed to providing you with the information you need, when

you need it.<P>



<STRONG>Independent Brokers:</STRONG>  As independent brokers,

you are a trusted, impartial source of information for your

clients.  <P>



If you are a New York Life agent, or an independent broker, this

section of the Web site is designed to help you service the needs

of your respective clients.<P>

Evolution of this section of the NYLCare Web site will be

directly dependent on your input.  Click on "Contact NYLCare" to

let us know what resources you

would like to see included.<P>

newsletters.<P>
