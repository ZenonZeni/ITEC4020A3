<H1>Directions</H1>
<HR SIZE=1 NOSHADE>

<P>The University Of Reading can be reached by car from the M4 leaving at either junction 10 (westbound) or junction 11 (eastbound) and following directions on the map below. Reading railway station is on a direct line from London Paddington (30 minutes) and connects to Heathrow and Gatwick airports (60 minutes and 90 minutes respectively). Visitors arriving at Reading railway station are advised to take a taxi to the university campus.</P>
