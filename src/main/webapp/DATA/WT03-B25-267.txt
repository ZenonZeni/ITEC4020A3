<H2><CENTER>Proposal Guidelines<BR>
<HR></CENTER>
</H2>
<H2></H2>
<B>Purpose: A Project Proposal is the basic unit of management and communication
of EOC supported activities. The details listed below are necessary prerequisites
for that support.</B>
<H2></H2>
<B>1. Identification</B>
<UL>
<LI>Task title 
<LI>Task leader and participants (giving details of affiliation and FTE)
<LI>EOC focus 
<LI>Sector &amp; Segments 
<LI>SEO Classifications 
</UL>
<H2></H2>
<B>2. Task Affiliation &amp; Interactions</B>
<UL>
<LI>Thread activities to which it contributes 
<LI>Thread impacts in Sectors 
<LI>Task impacts in Sectors 
<LI>Attractiveness &amp; Feasibility 
</UL>
<H2></H2>
<B>3. Research plan</B>
<UL>
<LI>Background 
<LI>Objectives 
<LI>Key questions 
<LI>Proposed methodology 
<LI>Budget &amp; Funding (Both items must match, total costs are real costs
to CSIRO, including overheads and write-backs) 
<LI>Communications (peer group &amp; public) 
<LI>Expected outcomes by Sector 
<LI>Data management 
<LI>Time management (Scheduling components &amp; milestones, illustrated
using Microsoft Project, or equivalent) 
</UL>
<H2></H2>
<B>4. Peer assessment</B>
<UL>
<LI>Task nominee 
<LI>EOC nominee 
</UL>
<H2></H2>
<B>5. Corporate responsibilities</B>
<UL>
<LI>Commercial plan &amp; Intellectual property (To be endorsed by the COSSA
Business Manager) 
<LI>EEO issues 
<LI>OH &amp; S issues 
<LI>Security issues 
</UL>
