<H1> PISCES Upgrade Facility at UC-San Diego </H1>	
</center>

The PISCES-Upgrade device is a plasma facing component
test facility.  The scope of the technical research encompasses erosion and
redeposition, impurity transport, heat removal, and the structural integrity of
components cyclically exposed to a plasma environment.  The facility provides a
plasma source in an open geometry that permits and encourages the investigation
of innovative divertor concepts.  PISCES-Upgrade, using the same reflex arc
technology as PISCES-B, is designed to operate steady-state at the following
plasma parameters: ne = 1 x 10<sup>20</sup> m<sup>-3</sup>, Te = 5-30 eV, Ti =
1-20 eV.  Controllable heat loads of up to 35 MW/m<sup>2</sup> will be
delivered over a variable target area of 60-150 cm<sup>2</sup>.<p>
