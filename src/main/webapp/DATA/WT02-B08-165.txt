<P><FONT COLOR="#000000">PrizzTech Ltd. is located in Pripoli Center of

<P><FONT COLOR="#000000">There are many projects seeking the newest technology

<P><FONT COLOR="#000000">PrizzTech Ltd. was established in 1989. Capital

the city of Pori, (71,4 %) and Regional Development Fund of Finland (KERA)
(28,4 %). PrizzTech is situated in Pripoli Center of Technology in Pori
and there are, depending on the amount of running projects, about 8 persons
working at the time. </FONT></P>
