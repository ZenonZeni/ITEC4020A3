In your lifetime, you will have several, varying financial goals. To
help in your pursuit of these goals, Kemper Funds includes a mix of funds
designed for an array of investment objectives. Our risk/return spectrum
presents the range, from higher risk/return potential to lower
risk/return potential. For Kemper Funds' comparative total returns for
each of the last 10 years, as well as the current year to date, you'll
want to view our comparative total return tables, organized by each
class of shares. And, for the latest in Kemper Funds, be sure to check
out our new fund introductions.
