<H2>Purpose  </H2>
<P>
To investigate the strength of advanced business services in Melbourne
and their contribution to the international competitiveness of
Victorian industry. A demonstration project will focus on the
interaction of Melboume's industrial design industry and its automotive
industry.
<H2>Background</H2>
<P>
The L | E | K Partnership's 1994 report Intelligent Exports and
the Silent Revolution in Services (funded in part by Business Victoria)
emphasised the importance of advanced
knowledge and innovative capacity in attaining a competitive edge.
<P>
The demonstration project will focus on Victoria's strengths in
industrial design.
<P>
In competing with other States and countries for investment, Victoria's
automotive industry has a competitive strength in the sophistication
of Melbourne's design services.  The Ford Falcon is designed in
Victoria.
<H2>Description</H2>
<P>
Ford Australia will participate in a demonstration program to
audit and illustrate the:
<P>
<UL>
<LI>Strength of Melbourne's design industry.
<LI>Nature of its interaction with the automotive industry.
<LI>Breadth of professional services supporting enterprises.
<LI>Contribution of the design industry to Victoria's international
competitiveness.
</UL>
<P>
<H2>Users</H2>
<P>
Firms in the design and related industries and the automotive
industry.
<H2>Outcomes</H2>
<P>
Data on the:
<P>
<UL>
<LI>Extent of the contribution of the design industry to the automotive
sector.
<LI>Capacity of the design industry to nurture the competitive
strengths of Victoria's wider industrial community.
</UL>
