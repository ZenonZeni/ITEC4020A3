<H3>Establishing The Links </H3>
<HR>

<P>
The acceptance of the findings reported here will largely be determined
by the credibility of the argument that links biodiversity with
contemporary satellite measurements of landcover. The following
sections establish those links. We begin the argument with this
image. <HR>

<P>
<A HREF="photocd/0648_006.gif"><IMG SRC="photocds/0648_006.gif" ALIGN="BOTTOM"></A>
<P>
<I>We present an uncommon view of biodiversity. It is a landscape
scaled view, an overview like this image, that seeks understanding
of general trends rather than detail. Concern for biodiversity
is sometimes disparagingly called biological stamp collecting.
Using this metaphor, our concern is to understand the workings
of the postal system.</I><HR>
