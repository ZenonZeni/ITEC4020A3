<P><B><FONT COLOR="#FFFFE8"><FONT SIZE=+1>The Alliance for the Mentally
Ill of Illinois (AMI-IL), located in Springfield, Illinois, is a not-for-profit,
grass roots organization dedicated to improving the lives of persons who
suffer from severe and persistent mental illness (neuro-biological brain
disorders), their families and friends. We offer support, education and
advocacy toward this end. As an affiliate of the National Alliance for
the Mentally Ill, we consist of a membership of over 3,000 individuals,
with 40 local affiliates providing services within the state of Illinois.
As a state organization, we offer:</FONT></FONT></B></P>

<UL>
<LI><B><FONT COLOR="#FFFFE8"><FONT SIZE=+1>Information and referral support
on a state-wide toll-free line: 1-800-346-4572.</FONT></FONT></B></LI>

<LI><B><FONT COLOR="#00FF00"><FONT SIZE=+1><A HREF="PAGE3.HTM">40 local
affiliates</A></FONT></FONT></B></LI>

<LI><B><FONT COLOR="#FFFFE8"><FONT SIZE=+1>Sponsorship of the <A HREF="PAGE4.HTM">Journey
of Hope</A><A HREF="file:///C|/NETSCAPE/WEBPAGE/JOH.HTM"> </A>program for
family members and friends of those suffering from severe and persistent
mental illness.</FONT></FONT></B></LI>

<LI><B><FONT COLOR="#FFFFE8"><FONT SIZE=+1>A statewide <A HREF="PAGE5.HTM">Consumer
Council</A></FONT></FONT></B></LI>

<LI><B><FONT COLOR="#FFFFE8"><FONT SIZE=+1>NAMI's Homeless and Missing
