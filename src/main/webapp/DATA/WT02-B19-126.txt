<h5> Area of Research </h5>
Shell Theory - the response of slit tubing to loading, supporting
design and analysis of antennas and structural booms for
spacecraft; Tension Field Theory - applied to membranes with body
forces, supporting design and analysis of solar power generation
panels for spacecraft; Gyrodynamics fo dissipative bodies, collinearity
theorems for simple body solid gyroscopes, attitude drift studies of
spinning spacecraft; Chaotic behaviour of mechanical systems. Complementary
formulations in variational mechanics.<p>
