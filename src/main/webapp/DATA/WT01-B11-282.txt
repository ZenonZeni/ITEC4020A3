<H4>
<B>Performance Highlights</B>
</H4>
<UL>
<LI>
<H4>

</H4>
<LI>
<H4>
During October and November the fixed income market rallied, fueled by election results and data indicating a slowing economy and low inflation.
</H4>
<LI>
<H4>
The money market yield curve (yields on securities with maturities of up to 1 year) flattened during December -- year-end borrowing demand forced yields higher in very short maturities (overnight to 1 month), while yields fell 10 to 20 basis points for longer maturities.
</H4>
<LI>
<H4>
We were cautious about holding many securities maturing in January, as rates often decline during that month.
</H4>
<LI>
<H4>
The average maturity of the portfolio shortened slightly during the quarter.  The Fund's assets increased by $140 million.
</H4>
</UL>
