<H3>INSURANCE (IN)</H3>

<B>Commercial Policy Guide--IN16</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in16.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Financial Times<BR>
$3.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Available from 4/1/94--4/1/96<BR>
(Also in FT)<BR>
Designed to help readers provide reliable advice to their clients, each issue
delivers concise and accurate analysis and a wide range of topics. Coverage
includes property Contingency, business travel, legal expenses, consequential
loss, credit and creditors, hotels and shops fleet and commercial vehicles,
contractor's liability, product tampering, professional indemnity and much
more.<P>

<B>Disability Compliance Bulletin--IN11</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in11.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
LRP Publications<BR>
$3.00/article; $45.00/issue<BR>
Available from 11/25/94--5/9/96<BR>
(Also in GT, LA)<BR>

Delivers a sharp and current perspective on the quickly expanding rights of
Americans with disabilities and the legal obligations of employers, care
providers, business owners, and others. Issues report the current developments in
Congress, the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission, the U.S. Departments of
Education, Justice, Transportation, and other relevant state and federal
agencies.<P>

<B>East European Insurance Report--IN12</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in12.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Financial Times Ltd.<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 3/1/94<BR>
(Also in FT, EEurope)<BR>
Provides reporting and analysis of the insurance industry in Eastern Europe.
Focuses on property, casualty, and personal lines of insurance; individual market
reports; liability issues; and new products and policies.<P>

<B>Federal & State Insurance Week--IN04</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in04.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
JR Publishing Inc.<BR>
$5.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 2/27/87<BR>
(Also in GT)<BR>
Covers political and legislative developments affecting general insurance
issues.<P>

<B>IMMS Weekly Marketeer--IN02</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in02.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Insurance Marketing Services Inc.<BR>
$1.00/article; $35.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 10/31/83<BR>
(Also in FI, IV)<BR>
The week's news in the rapidly changing world of insurance and financial
services.<P>

<B>The Insurance Accountant--IN01</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in01.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
American Banker-Bond Buyer<BR>
$3.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 2/21/94<BR>
(Also in FI, AmBanker)<BR>
Covers changes in accounting principles among insurers. Focuses primarily on
regulatory, legislative, and accounting policy, as well as developments at the
Federal Accounting Standards Board and the federal government.<P>

<B>Insurance Industry International--IN18</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in18.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Lafferty Publications Ltd.<BR>
$3.00/article; $50.00/issue<BR>
Available from 1/1/95--6/1/96<BR>
(Also in IT, Lafferty)<BR>
Reports corporate performance and strategy, with particular attention to personal
lines worldwide.<P>

<B>Insurance Industry Litigation Reporter--IN08</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in08.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Andrews Publications<BR>
$4.00/article; $45.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 9/10/92<BR>
(Also in LA, Andrews)<BR>
Key areas of coverage include exclusionary clauses, trigger and scope of coverage,
duty to defend and damnify, interpretation of policy language, statutory
regulation of insurer conduct, breach of contract, duty of good faith and
discrimination in denial of coverage.<P>

<B>The Insurance Regulator--IN03</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in03.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
American Banker-Bond Buyer<BR>
$3.00/article; $20.00/issue<BR>
Updated weekly, from 2/21/94<BR>
(Also in GT, AmBanker)<BR>
Monitors the constant threat by Congress to regulate the insurance industry.
Contains ledgers of state-by-state news concerning each state commissioner's
activities.<P>

<B>Life Insurance International--IN19</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in19.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Lafferty Publications Ltd.<BR>
$3.00/article; $50.00/issue<BR>
Updated monthly, from 1/1/95<BR>
(Also in IT, Lafferty)<BR>
Covers trends and developments in all sectors of the industry, including life
institutions, banks, building societies, pension funds, and intermediaries.<P>

<B>Personal Policy Guide--IN17</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in17.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Financial Times<BR>
$3.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Available from 4/1/94--4/1/96<BR>
(Also in FT)<BR>
Designed to help its reader provide reliable advice to their clients, because
personal lines brokers face formidable competition from insurers selling direct
enabling comparisons to be made. Coverage includes travel, residential buildings,
personal accident and sickness, contents, valuables and executive homes, and much
more.<P>

<B>Workers' Comp Executive--IN07</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in07.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Commanon Corp.<BR>
$4.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 9/16/91<BR>
(Also in GT)<BR>
Covers worker's compensation develop ment in the United States, with emphasis on
California. Issues include legislative, regulatory, production, and marketing
information.<P>

<B>World Corporate Insurance Report--IN13</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in13.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Financial Times Ltd.<BR>
$3.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 3/25/94<BR>
(Also in FI, FT)<BR>
Extensive coverage of international business insurance news and developments from
the perspective of both individual product groups and the companies issuing
them.<P>

<B>World Insurance Report--IN14</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/in14.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Financial Times Ltd.<BR>
$3.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>
Updated biweekly, from 3/25/94<BR>
(Also in FI, FT)<BR>
Extensive coverage of international nonlife and re-insurance news, from the
perspective of both individual product groups as well as the companies issuing
them.<P>
