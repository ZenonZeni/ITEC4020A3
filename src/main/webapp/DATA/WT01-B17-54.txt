       <H3 ALIGN="CENTER"><CENTER>BREWER'S YEAST FOR BLOOD
<BR>SUGAR METABOLISM</CENTER></H3>

        The importance of stabilizing blood sugar is related to endless biological
processes, from 'avoiding diabetes and hypoglycemia to problem-free menstruation and
menopause.  Brewer's yeast, the richest known source of naturally occurring chromium,
has been shown to help blood sugar metabolism.  The trouble is, large quantities are
required.  Chromium polynicotinate supplements (200 micrograms daily) are recommended
for those who don't wish to swallow the 36 to 40 brewer's yeast tablets necessary for
effective blood sugar control.


<BLOCKQUOTE><B>Source:</B>  <I>Wellness Advocate</I> (newsletter), Aug 1994.  PO Box 531, Franktown, CO
80116. $24 annually, 12 Issues.</BLOCKQUOTE>

<HR WIDTH=85%>

<BLOCKQUOTE><I>Diabetes </I>  is a disease in which blood glucose levels exceed the normal range.  It
is characterized by the inability to fully utilize glucose as the energy source. 
Diabetes is due to impaired insulin metabolism, causing sugar to enter blood and
urine.
<P>     <I>Hypoglycemia </I>  refers to abnormally low levels of sugar in the blood.  Hypoglycemia
may occur with or without gross clinical symptoms.
</BLOCKQUOTE>
<HR><A NAME="CONJUNC"> </A>
<H3 ALIGN="CENTER"><CENTER>CURE FOR CONJUNCTIVITIS</CENTER></H3>

One tablespoon goldenseal root powder, one teaspoon salt, and 250 mg vitamin C
dissolved in one quart of water makes a good home remedy for conjunctivitis, commonly
known as &igrave;pink eye.&icirc;  Let the solution settle and use the clear liquid for an eye
wash several times a day.


<BLOCKQUOTE><B>Source:</B>  <I>Dr. Lendon Smith 's Health and Humor Newsletter</I>, vol 94: 1 0. PO Box
5824, Portland, OR 97228. $24 annually, 12 Issues.</BLOCKQUOTE>

<HR>
        <H3 ALIGN="CENTER"><CENTER>VITAMIN C HELPS DETOXIFICATION</CENTER></H3>


<P>     In recent years it's been shown that ascorbic acid acts as a scavenger in our
bodies, chasing after and destroying harmful substances called free radicals.  New
studies confirm that adequate vitamin C nutrition may prevent common cellular
degenerative diseases associated with lipid, or fat, oxidation.


<BLOCKQUOTE><B>Source:</B>  <I>Free Radical Biology and Medicine</I>, Apr 1994, 16(4):417-26.</BLOCKQUOTE>


<HR><A NAME="TEA"> </A>
<H3 ALIGN="CENTER"><CENTER>PROTECT YOURSELF WITH GREEN TEA
</CENTER></H3>

People drink more tea worldwide than any other beverage except water.  Good
thing, because some teas are protective, rich in substances called polyphenols, which
act as antioxidants . Under specific conditions, they can inhibit tumor growth. 
Green tea, the kind most often drunk in Asia, lowers the risk of cancer of the
esophagus, but the protective effect is reduced when people drink it scalding hot.


<P>     Whether it's tea, soup, coffee, or hot chocolate, don't make a habit of
swallowing liquids boiling hot.  Drinking scalding liquids can actually promote
cancer of the esophagus.


<BLOCKQUOTE><B>Source:</B>  <I>University of California Berkeley Wellness Letter</I>, Sep 1994.  Health
Letter Association, PO Box 412, Prince Street Station, NY, NY 100 120007. $24
annually, 12 issues.</BLOCKQUOTE>
 
<HR><A NAME="STROKE"> </A>
<H3 ALIGN="CENTER"><CENTER>FISH LOWERS STROKE RISK
</CENTER></H3>

        Fish consumption may lower the risk of stroke.  Moderate consumption leads to
mild, beneficial blood &quot;thinning,&quot; which helps prevent strokes that are caused by
blood clots in the arteries of the brain.


<BLOCKQUOTE><B>Source:</B>  <I>Harvard Heart Letter</I>, Oct 1994. 164 Longwood Ave, Boston, MA 02115. $32
annually, 12 issues.</BLOCKQUOTE>
