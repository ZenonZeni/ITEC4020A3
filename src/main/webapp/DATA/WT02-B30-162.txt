A wealth of health care information is currently available on the World Wide Web.  For your

convenience, we have included links to a few health care sites that will appeal to both our

members and non-members alike.<P>



Before you click on one of the links below, bookmark this page so that you can return to the

NYLCare site easily after exploring these outside links.<P>



This list will expand over time.  If there is a useful link that you think should be included

here, please let us know by clicking on the "Contact NYLCare" button above.<P>







<STRONG>WellnessWeb</STRONG><BR> 

<A HREF="http://www.wellweb.com">http://www.wellweb.com</A><P>

Chosen by Health Care Internet Alert as one of "10 Best Health Care Sites," Wellness Web

is a

fully-indexed, user-friendly site that is a collaboration among patients, health care

professionals, and other caregivers.  It provides up-to-date medical information on

countless

topics and has links to many informative and entertaining sites on all aspects of

wellness.<P>

<STRONG>University of Kansas Medical Center and Dykes Library</STRONG><BR>

<A HREF="http://www.kumc.edu">http://www.kumc.edu</A><P>

This University of Kansas Medical Center site provides information on hospitals, clinics,

support groups, as well as full access to the library catalogue, including an electronic

request service for journal articles and books.<P>





<STRONG>Health Vision: The diet and weight loss / fitness home page</STRONG><BR>

<A

HREF="http://www1.mhv.net/%7Edonn/diet.html">http://www1.mhv.net/~donn/diet.html</A><P>

This home page is designed in an easy-to-follow manner. It offers users facts, tips and

information regarding dieting, weight loss and fitness. The site never over-sells users on

its ideas. Equipped with a comprehensive list of comparable links, the site reveals a

commitment to aid users on how to learn more about their health.<P>
