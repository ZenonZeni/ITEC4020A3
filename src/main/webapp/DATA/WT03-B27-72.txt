align="middle" width="150" height="226">Champagne</font></p>
<p>Champagne is known for its long stems and few thorns. While
the flower bud is smaller initially, it opens full and large. The
strong neck prevents the rose from hanging. Champagne is ideal
for weddings, graduations and other such events.</p>
