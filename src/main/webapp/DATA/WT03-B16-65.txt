<h3>Jakarta, Indonesia, 8 April 1996</h3>

Jakarta shows up as the continuous greyish-green area. The new airport is towards the northwest. The nearby dark coastal plain shows the conversion of swamps to fish ponds. East of the swamps is the elliptical Pluit Reservoir, and next to it, the Pasar Ikan-Sunda Kelapa area with a rectangular extension into the sea where the sailing ships dock. The National Monument is the bright spot in the middle of the red and green square of Medan Merdeka, south of the old city.
