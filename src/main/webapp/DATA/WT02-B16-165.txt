<h2>InfoNet Quick Guide</h2>
<br>

<h3>Here's how to use the DOE Hydrogen Technologies Information 
Network:</h3>
<br>

<a href="rdrmp.htm"><IMG SRC="graphics/read.gif" border="0" Vspace="4" 
Hspace="4" ALT="Reading Room Icon" ></a> <br>
Click on the <a href="rdrmp.htm"><b><I>Reading Room</I></b></a> for 
articles, fact sheets, reports, and publication lists that explain how 
hydrogen is used today, and can be used tomorrow, as an energy source and 
fuel.
<p>

<a href="tekstat.htm"><IMG SRC="graphics/tech.gif" border="0" Vspace="2" 
Hspace="4" ALT="Technology Status Icon"></a>
<p>
 Select <a href="tekstat.htm"><b><I>Technology Status</I></b></a> for 
technical project summaries that describe how teams of scientists and 
engineers from DOE laboratories, industry, and universities are exploring 
and developing new hydrogen technologies for many energy and fuel uses, 
both at home and abroad.
<p>

<a href="partners.htm"><IMG SRC="graphics/partner.gif" border="0" 
Vspace="2" Hspace="4" ALT="Partnerships Icon" ></a> 
<p>Click on <a href="partners.htm"><b><I>Partnerships</I></b></a> to read 
about opportunities in collaborative research and development projects, 
and to find out more about the activities of the National Hydrogen 
Association and our technology-development working groups.
<p>

<a href="htap.htm"><IMG SRC="graphics/advis.gif" border="0" Vspace="2" 
Hspace="4" ALT="Partnerships Icon" ></a>
<p>Select <a href="htap.htm"><b><I>Hydrogen Technical Advisory 
Panel</I></b></a> for information about the12-member board that advises 
DOE on research priorities.  Check this part of the InfoNet for an update 
on the panel's recent activities and a current membership list.

<p>

<a href="learn.htm"><IMG SRC="graphics/learn.gif" border="0" Vspace="2" 
Hspace="4" ALT="Learning Center Icon"></a>
<p>Whether you are a student, a teacher, or just curious, the <a 
href="learn.htm"><b><I>Learning Center</I></b></a>, currently under 
development,  will contain information about current educational 
activities sponsored by DOE and others involved in hydrogen technology 
development, as well as "fun facts" about hydrogen.

<p>

<a href="news.htm"><IMG SRC="graphics/hot.gif" border="0" Vspace="2" 
Hspace="4" ALT="Hot News Icon" ></a>
<p>Finally, click on <a href="news.htm"><b><I>Hot News</I></b></a> for 
important announcements about meetings, breakthroughs, and other current 
events in hydrogen technology research and development. You'll also find 
current Business Opportunities for DOE and industry partnerships and 
related upcoming meetings.<p>
 Please email <a href="mailto:kalime@tcplink.nrel.gov">kalime@tcplink.nrel.gov</a>  your 
suggestions, questions, or comments. <p>
<b>Be sure to let us know if you need any other information! </b>
