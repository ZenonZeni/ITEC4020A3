<H4>Glossary and Abbreviations </H4>
<HR>

<P>
<B>MSS</B>: Multispectral Scanner. The principal instrument carried
by the Landsat satellite series.
<P>
<B>ELZ</B>: Extensive Landuse Zone. The central core of the continent
where the principal landcover disturbance threat is from grazing
or fire.
<P>
<B>ILZ</B>: Intensive Landuse Zone.The more heavily populated
coastal regions, where the principal landcover disturbance threat
is from clearing for crops, pastures and forests.
<P>
<B>pfc</B>: Projected Foliage Cover, measured as a percentage
from a vertical view.
<P>
<B>EGE</B>: Enhanced Greenhouse Effect
<P>
<B>LGA</B>: Local Government Area
<P>
<B>SLA</B>: Statistical Local Area.
<P>
<B>AUSLIG</B>: Australian Surveying and Land Information Group.
Part of the Australian Government Department of Administrative
Services and responsible for many map based products.
<P>
<B>ACRES</B>: Australian Centre for Remote Sensing. Part of AUSLIG
and responsible for Landsat reception in Australia.
<P>
<B>ABS</B>: Australian Bureau of Statistics. The Australian Government
body responsible for collecting statistical information on many
facets of Australian life.
<P>
<B>AEST</B>: Australian Eastern Standard Time.
<P>
<B>Albers Projection</B>: A map projection, such that equal areas
on the map correspond to equal areas on the ground.
<P>
<B>WRS</B>: World Reference System. An identification system used
to refer to the position of a satellite scene on the surface of
the earth.<HR>
