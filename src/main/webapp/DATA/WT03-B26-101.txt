<H3>Web SPOT</H3>
<HR>In January 1996, SPOT opened business on the World Wide Web: the newest
and most popular part of the Internet.<BR>
<BR>
Clicking homepage link icons calls text and graphics about SPOT subjects
of interest. For technical details or history, click SPOT System. Also try:
Products &amp; Services, Commercial Network, SPOT News, or Examples of Use.
To contribute suggestions or ask for help, click Contact Us.<BR>
<BR>
SPOT's Web page includes a demonstration of the DALI catalogue. The main
catalogue opens with annual payment of around Aus$125, or Aus$630 for permanent
access. Users can request information about the availability of scenes of
interest (specifying latitude and longitude), or view thumbnail sketches
of the scenes, which also give technical data. It will soon be possible
to specify regions of interest by clicking on index maps. <BR>
