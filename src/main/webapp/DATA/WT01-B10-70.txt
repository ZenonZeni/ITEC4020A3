An indispensable compilation of current information and more, this sourcebook provides you with everything you need to know: the most up-to-date research; new treatments, both surgical and medical; alternative treatments, including nutrition; common myths about the disease; pregnancy, labor and postpartum experiences of women with endometriosis; advice for coping physically and emotionally; and inspirational personal case histories of women sharing their struggles and successes. <p>
