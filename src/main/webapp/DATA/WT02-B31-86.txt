<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>Fixed Rate Annuities</STRONG></FONT><P>

New York Life offers two different fixed rate annuity products to meet your needs.<P>

<A HREF="psinanra.html">Single Premium Retirement Annuity (SPRA2)</A><BR>
The Single Premium Retirement Annuity helps you save for your retirement on a tax-deferred
basis.  If you're worried about saving enough for retirement, this product may help you to
realize your retirement goals.
<P>
<A HREF="psinania.html">Single Premium Immediate Annuity (SPIA)</A><BR>
The Single Premium Immediate Annuity helps ensure that you don't outlive your retirement
savings. To find out more, click on this link.
