State Associations for the gifted traditionally are advocacy organizations for gifted
students.  Membership in these organizations includes parents, teachers,
administrators, and researchers concerned with the education of gifted students.  State
associations lobby for legislation, provide information, sponsor annual conventions,
organize workshops, and offer support to parents and teachers attempting to meet the
needs of gifted children.  Listed below are the contact addresses of those state
associations for which ERIC EC has current information.  We appreciate the assistance
of the Duke University Talent Identification program in compiling this list.<P>
