<h5> Area of Research </h5>
Fluid flow and mass transfer; study of fluid and solute transport 
through tissues, flow through polymeric networks and filtration
problems; Numerical and experimental studies of blood flow in
large arteries, study of hemodynamic basis of arterial disease;
outflow of aqueous humour within the eye, as related to the problem of 
glaucoma.<p>
