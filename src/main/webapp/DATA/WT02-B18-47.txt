This bibliography pulls together, from various disciplines, literature relating to the conservation and sustainable development of tropical forests. All of the publications cited are in the collections of the University of Minnesota Libraries and are available (photocopy or loan) from Interlibrary Loan Division, St. Paul Campus Libraries, 1984 Buford Avenue, St. Paul, MN 55108. FAX (612) 624-9245.<P>

This bibliography will be updated annually on line.<P>
