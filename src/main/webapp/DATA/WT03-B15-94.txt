Dear Mr. Tay Khee Wee,<br>I have sent you an email concerning the above and<br>look forward to your response. I suggest that you<br>poll the com port for a change in the state of its<br>flags before attempting to transmit the next<br>command. This is because the controller is busy<br>and loses the first few characters of the next<br>command, thus causing a syntax error when it <br>eventually starts to take notice of its serial<br>port again.<br>Alternatively, you could configure the port for<br>an infinite number of retries from DOS but I am<br>not certain if this will affect the operation of<br>your .exe file.<p>Regards,<p>Nick Oakley for Robot Simulations Ltd.
