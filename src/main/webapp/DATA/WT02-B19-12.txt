Engineering Faculty,<BR>University of Sydney
</H1>
The Engineering Faculty of the
<A HREF=http://www.usyd.edu.au/>University of Sydney</A> is located on the
<A HREF=/tour/map>Darlington Campus</A> of the University.
The Faculty has been part of the University of Sydney for
over a hundred years and has had a long and interesting
<A HREF=history>history</A>.
<P>
The Faculty is made up of several departments/schools, research
groups and centres:
<BR>
<ul>
<LI><A HREF=http://www.ae.su.oz.au>Aeronautical Engineering Department</A></li>
<LI><A HREF=ftp://ftp.chem.eng.usyd.edu.au/www/chemeng.htm>Chemical Engineering Department</A>
 <ul>
 <li><A href="centres/acarre">Australian Centre of Advanced Risk and Reliability Engineering</A></li>
 <li><A href="centres/chefoun">Chemical Engineering Foundation</A></li>
 </ul>
<li><A HREF="http://www.civil.su.oz.au/">School of Civil and Mining Engineering</A></li>
 <ul>
 <li><A href="centres/cenase">Centre for Advanced Structural Engineering</A></li>
 <li><A href="centres/cengr">Centre for Geotechnical Research</A></li>
 <li><A href="centres/civfoun">Civil and Mining Engineering Foundation</A></li>
 <li><A href="centres/otg">Ocean Technology Group</A></li>
 </ul>
<li><A HREF=http://www.ee.su.oz.au>Electrical Engineering Department</a></li>
 <ul>
 <li><A href="centres/elefoun">Electrical Engineering Foundation</A></li>
 <li><A href="http://www.sedal.su.oz.au">SEDAL (Systems Engineering and Design Automation Laboratory</A></li>
 <li><A href="http://www.speech.su.oz.au">Speech Technology Research Group</A></li>
 </ul>
<LI><A HREF=http://www.me.su.oz.au/>Department of Mechanical and Mechatronic Engineering</A></li>
 <ul>
 <li><A href="http://www.camt.usyd.edu.au">Centre for Advanced Materials Technology</A></li>
 </ul>
<li><A href="centres/aciic">Australian Centre for Innovation and International Competitiveness (ACIIC)</A></li>
<li><A href="centres/warren">Warren Centre for Advanced Engineering</A></li>
</ul>
The faculty also participates in the following projects with other
faculties and universities:
<ul>
<li><A href= "centres/agsei">Australian Graduate School of Engineering Innovation</A></li>
<li><A href="http://www.oftc.usyd.edu.au">Optical Fibre Technology Centre (OFTC)</A></li>
<li>Australian Technology Park</li>
</ul>
<HR>
The Engineering Faculty publishes details of courses, and the
University laws and by-laws covering Engineering degrees in the
<A HREF=handbook>Faculty Handbook</A>. The handbook also contains details
of how to <A HREF=contact>contact</A> the Faculty.
<P>
For potential students in engineering, there is a program called
<A href="cse">Chancellor's Industry Scholarships in Engineering</A>
which provides financial assistance and links between the students and industry.
