<P>MainStay Institutional Funds Inc.  provides you with eleven specially designed no-load mutual funds offering a broad range of investment choices to suit the time horizons, risk tolerances and investment goals of virtually every investor. 

<P>MainStay Institutional Funds Inc. are distributed through NYLIFE Distributors Inc., a NASD member.  

<P><STRONG>Experience And Expertise</STRONG><BR>
MainStay Institutional Funds' investment advisers each have specialized expertise and extensive experience in their designated areas of responsibility.
