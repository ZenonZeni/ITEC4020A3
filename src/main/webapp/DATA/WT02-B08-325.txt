<h3>
We also independently verify TOPEX/Poseidon's data by measuring the ocean-to-satellite distance from Earth. This is done using laser tracking on land and sea-level monitoring instruments placed on Texaco's Platform Harvest, located off the coast of California.  
<p>
Data from the Platform Harvest are compared with those taken by TOPEX/Poseidon as it flies over the platform to ensure that the satellite's altimeter is working properly and is returning accurate measurements.
