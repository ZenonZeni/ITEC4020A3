<PRE><H2>
         League of Women Voters Education Fund (LVWEF)</H2><H3>
                               Contract DE-FC01-92RW00245
</H3><P>
The League of Women Voters Education Fund (LWVEF) is a nonpartisan, research and
citizen education organization that encourages the informed and active participation of citizens
in government.  Established in 1957, the LWVEF offers education programs and materials to
foster citizen understanding of major public policy issues, and to help citizens effectively take
part in the democratic process.
