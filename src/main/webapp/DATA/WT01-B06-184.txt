      <B>Welcome to the official Made in the USA Foundation Homepage.</B>
      <P ALIGN=Center>
      <EM> </EM> <B>We are a 60,000 member, non-profit organization composed of
      individuals, trade unions and corporations who are concerned about American
      competitiveness and economic leadership. We believe that strong and effective
      action must be taken to reduce American reliance on imports, to increase
      purchases of U.S. products domestically and overseas and to ensure that the
      standards of fair trade are applied evenly around the world.</B>
