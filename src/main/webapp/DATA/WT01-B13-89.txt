Kemper Funds originated in Chicago in 1948, when five entrepreneurs formed a mutual fund to capitalize on a technological breakthrough called television. Much - the company's name, ownership and scope of its funds' investments - has changed since then. While Kemper headquarters today remain in Chicago, the company's reach extends far and wide. We share our success with the many friends, neighbors and partners we invite you to meet on the following pages.
