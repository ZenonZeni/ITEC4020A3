<P>
 <B>Project Summary:</B><UL>
<P><DT>
<LI> Technology Study<DD>  Conduct research and design of high technology
and high energy electric vehicle components, including stateoftheart
energy management systems, advanced motor controllers, drive trains,
regenerative braking, and high energy batteries. Identify an electric
drive system design which optimizes torque, speed, and power of
at least 200 kW.
<P>
<LI> Drive Train Systems Fabrication and Installation<DD>  Fabricate
two high power, high torque, electric vehicle drive train systems.
Mount and install one drive train system, with real time data
acquisition, energy management, and vehicle instrumentation appropriate
to verify vehicle performance, in a vehicle provided by Sportech.
Design the modifications necessary to install a drive system into
an electric Gvan furnished by Hawaiian Electric Company. Prepare
and conduct preliminary design reviews of proposed modifications,
to include design details and system specification.
<P>
<LI> System Demonstration and Testing<DD>  Conduct demonstration
and evaluation of instrumented vehicles Verify performance specifications.
<P></DT></UL>
<B>Project Deliverables:</B>
<P><UL>
            <LI> Technical Report recommending and detailing
the optimum electric drive system.
<P>
 <LI> Test results from system verification testing.
<P>
 <LI> Converted van.
<P></UL>

<B> Project Schedule:</B>
<P>
<UL> 
<P>
<LI>Complete Technical Report of optimum electric drive system.
<P>
<LI>Complete drive system installation in contractor vehicle.

<P>
<LI>Convert van.
<P>
<LI>Conduct system verification testing. 
<P>
 <LI> Delivery of van and final report. Qtr 4
