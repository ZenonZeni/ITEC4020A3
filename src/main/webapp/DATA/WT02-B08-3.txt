<h2>RENEWABLE FUELS ASSOCIATION<br><br>
PUBLICATIONS</h2><br clear=all>
<center><img src="blue_line.gif" width="100%"></center>
<b>
<P>
 The Renewable Fuels Association continually releases publications
related to the production and use of renewable fuels.  The following
publications are currently available to order as designated:
<P>
<blockquote>
<B><i>Ethanol Report.</i></B>  Weekly briefing papers regarding
current issues of interest to the industry.  Available upon request.
<P>
<B><i>Changes in Gasoline III, The Auto Technician's Gasoline Quality
Guide.</i></B>  Advanced motor fuels technical manual designed
for auto mechanics and car care specialists - $2.00.  Also available
on video - $13.50.
<P>
<U><B><i>Ethanol Plant Development Handbook.</i></B></U>  Provides information
regarding the points to consider when construction an ethanol
plant.  Includes industry resources.  $10.00.
<P>
<B><i>Washington Legislative and Regulatory Update.</i></B>  Report
explaining the current status of Congressional and Administrative
actions affecting the domestic ethanol industry.  Available to
RFA members.
<P>
<B><i>Ethanol Program Operations Guide.</i></B>  RFA Recommended
Practices #930601.  Provides step-by-step guidance for implementing
a successful gasoline ethanol blending program.  Available to
RFA members.
<P>
<B><i>Ethanol and Gasoline Ethanol Blends, Fuel Quality Considerations
and Recommendations.</i></B>  RFA Recommended Practices #911201.
 Provides an overview of specifications and quality parameters
for ethanol and gasoline ethanol blends.  Available to RFA members.
<P>
<B><i>Downstream Ethanol Blender Benefits Resulting From Existing
&amp; Proposed Regulatory Programs.</i></B>  RFA Information Paper
#940301.  Detailed explanation of reformulated gasoline oxygen
credits for downstream blenders.  Available to RFA members.
<P>
<B><i>Fuel Volatility Regulations for Gasoline Ethanol Blends
Issues for Common Carriers.</i></B>  RFA Information Paper #940302.
 Explanation of RFA motor fuel regulations specifically relating
to common carriers.  Available to RFA members.
</blockquote>
