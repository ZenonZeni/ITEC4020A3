<h2>Look for New FEMP Training Catalog in Late October</h2>

Subscribers to the FEMP FOCUS will receive the <I>FEMP FY 1997
Training Catalog</I> in late October.  The catalog lists the 13
FEMP-provided training courses in facility energy and water management
and provides dates, locations, and registration information on
key National conferences where FEMP-sponsored symposia are conducted.
 The catalog also provides guidance on how to find energy and
management training sponsored by other organizations through FEMP's
Training Event Locator System.  Information on how to register
for GSA-provided energy management training is also available
in the catalog.  Copies of the catalog may be obtained at no cost
through the FEMP Help Desk at 800-DOE-EREC (363-3732).
