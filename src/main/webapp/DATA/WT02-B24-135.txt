<h2 align=center>Table 8. Left And Right Cerebral Hemisphere 
Functions</h2>
<hr>
<h3 align=center>Left Cerebral Hemisphere Functions </h3>

<h4>Left Parietal Temporal Cortex</h4>
<blockquote>
<p><b>Primary Sensory Functions</b>
<blockquote>
Sensation of right body<br>
Perception of right visual field<br>
Appreciation of sound from right ear
</blockquote>

<p><b>Cognitive Functions </b>
<blockquote>
Language Skills: 
<ul>
<li>comprehension and expression of oral and written language including
storage and recall of symbols and nominals
<li>storage of common nouns and action verbs (inner vocabulary)
<li>rules of grammar and structure of language
<li>verbal word recognition (inner speech) 
</ul>
<p>Praxias - command type<br> 
Emotional Functions<br> 
Denial, oppositional, non-compliance, and hostile anger (mania)<br>
Obsessions and compulsions <br>
"Learned" pessimism and negativity<br> 
Pedantic, rigid responses <br>
Rationalization 
</blockquote>
<p><b>Attentional Functions</b>
<blockquote>
<p>Attentiveness to right space><br>
Minor role in vigilance (tonic state)
</blockquote>
</blockquote>
<h4>Left Frontal Lobe Functions</h4>
<blockquote>
<p>Fundamental movement of right body<br>
Right voluntary gaze<br> 
Clarity of verbal thought (freedom from auditory-verbal hallucinations 
and delusions) 
</blockquote>
<hr>
<h3 align=center>Right Cerebral Hemisphere Functions</h3>

<h4>Right Parietal Temporal Cortex</H4>
<blockquote>

<p><b>Primary Sensory Functions</b>
<blockquote>
<p>Sensation of left body<br>
Perception of left visual field<br>
Appreciation of sound from left ear<br>
</blockquote>
<p><b>Cognitive Functions</b>
<blockquote>
<p>Spatial orientation<br>
Spatial relations (right-left discrimination)<br>
Sequencing of symbols, objects, and events<br>
Timing and time perception<br>
Music appreciation<br>
Recognition of objects and faces<br>
Geometric communication<br>
Non-verbal communication<br>
Praxias - coordinated motor behavior
</blockquote>

<p><b>Emotional Functions</b>
<blockquote>
<p>Prosody
<br>Primary emotionality
<br>Empathy and comprehension of emotionality
<br>Affective behavior (depression)
<br>Wit and humor
</blockquote>

<p><b>Attentional Functions</b>
<blockquote>
<p>Arousal
<br>Vigilance: alertness - wakefulness (phasic states)
<br>Attentiveness: Right and left space
</blockquote>

<p><b>Primary Visual Imagery</b>
<blockquote>Picture-to-picture storage and representation
</blockquote>
<p><b>Symbolization (symbolic representation)</b>

<blockquote>Picture-to-word storage and representation
(understanding the surrounding world)
</blockquote>
</blockquote>
<h4>Right Frontal lobe functions</h4>
<blockquote>
<p>Fundamental movement of left body 
<br>Left voluntary gaze 
<br>Motor persistence 
<br>Order (formal type: seeing the world as a series of 
interrelated entities) 
<br>Planning 
<br>Volition - intention ("the will") 
<br>Diligence - work ethic - drive 
<br>Executive control 
<br>Abiding by rules and regulations: (social conduct);  reputation
</blockquote>
