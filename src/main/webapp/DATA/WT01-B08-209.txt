<P><B><FONT FACE="MS Sans Serif"><FONT COLOR="#0000A0">In March, the U.S.
Department of Commerce's Bureau of Export Administration (BXA) in concert
with U.S. Commercial Service, located in five U.S. embassies in Europe, will hold a series of seminars
on the &quot;new&quot; U.S. Export Administration (export control) Regulations
which became effective on December 31, 1996. These programs will include
a full-day course on the regulations with a second day reserved for scheduled
individual consultations between BXA representatives and program participants.
For registration fees and additional information, please check with the
local U.S. embassy point of contact. </FONT></FONT></B>
