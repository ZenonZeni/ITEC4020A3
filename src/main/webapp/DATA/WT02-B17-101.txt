<H2>Electrolysis</H2>

<P>

Electrolysis is a method of producing hydrogen using an electric

current to separate hydrogen from other elements.  Researchers

are investigating systems that have potential to reduce costs

and improve efficiencies.  For more details, see the technical

project summaries in this section:
