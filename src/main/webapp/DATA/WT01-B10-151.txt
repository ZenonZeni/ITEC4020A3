<h2 align="center"><font color="#0000FF">Compost &amp; Yard Waste
Management</font></h2>
<p><font color="#0000FF">Studies indicate that yard waste is one
of the largest categories (20% to 25% on average) of solid waste
generated by a community. Many states, including Minnesota where <strong>SKB
Environmental</strong>'s corporate offices are located, have
banned it from landfills and from solid waste processing
facilities such as incinerators. Even if allowed under state
legislation and local ordinances, disposing of yard waste with
other household trash may not make economic sense. <b>SKB
Environmental</b> can turn a disposal problem into an
environmentally beneficial product for soil enhancement or
landscaping.</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#0000FF"><img
src="images/LOADNGSM.jpg" alt="front end loader and compost"
align="bottom" width="300" height="138"> </font></p>
<p><font color="#0000FF">At its own sites or on a contract basis, <b>SKB
Environmental </b>will provide services tailored for the needs of
your community. Services offered include: on-site staffing;
mechanized debagging; aerated static pile composting; product
screening, bagging and marketing; and brush and wood chipping,
with either mobile or on-site grinders. We are proud of our
record in providing cost-effective, reliable and problem-free
Yard Waste Processing services. For further information about <strong>SKB
Environmental</strong>'s services please contact us.</font></p>

<h2 align="center"><a name="compsites"><font color="#0000FF">Sites</font></a><font
color="#0000FF"> and Locations</font></h2>
<p><font color="#0000FF"><strong>SKB Environmental</strong> is
now Minnesota's largest Yard Waste processing company. Annually,
approximately <b>40,000</b> tons of leaves, grass clippings and
brush are composted and<b> 20,000</b> tons of wood waste and
brush are processed into landscaping mulch.</font></p>
<p><font color="#0000FF">At each of our composting sites, leaves
and grass are processed into compost using the aerated static
pile method. Finished compost is screened and marketed for sale
in bulk or in bags. Landscape mulch is produced from brush and/or
wood wastes.</font></p>
<hr>
<h3><img src="images/TREE.gif" alt="Tree button" align="texttop"
hspace="10" width="30" height="30"><font color="#0000FF">Empire
Township</font></h3>
<p><font color="#0000FF"><strong>SKB Environmental</strong> has
opened a composting site on 37 acres within Dakota County's
resource recovery project site in Empire Township. This facility
accommodates two types of composting, yard waste only and
blending of yard waste with other source separated compostable
materials such as food or gardening wastes which are then
composted using innovative containment technologies. Containment
systems control odors and nuisance impacts while saving space. A
uniform, high quality compost is produced for landscapers and
other users. Using static aerated pile composting and <strong>SKB
Environmental</strong>'s unique yard waste de-bagging system, the
yard waste compost produced is reliably of a quality that is
bagged as potting soil, compost, topsoil, and other soil
amendments sold for unrestricted retail distribution. Under the
facility's license from Dakota County, it has the capacity to
process approximately 78,000 tons annually. </font></p>
<h3><img src="images/TREE.gif" alt="Tree button" align="texttop"
hspace="10" width="30" height="30"><font color="#0000FF">
Lakeville Site</font></h3>
<p><font color="#0000FF"><b>SKB Environmental</b> operates the
Lakeville Yard Waste Compost Facility on 10 acres within a 21
acre site located south of Co. Rd. 42 on Pilot Knob Road in Apple
Valley, MN. The facility is open to the public, serving the
residents of Lakeville, Apple Valley, Farmington, Rosemount, and
surrounding communities in this rapidly growing part of the
Metropolitan Area. </font></p>
<h3><img src="images/TREE.gif" alt="Tree button" align="texttop"
hspace="10" width="30" height="30"><font color="#0000FF">Cottage
Grove Site</font></h3>
<p><font color="#0000FF">The City of Cottage Grove's Compost
Facility at Highway 61 &amp; Jamaica Avenue is operated on a
contract basis. The facility serves city residents and households
from nearby Hastings and from communities north of Cottage Grove. <strong>SKB
Environmental</strong> utilizes approximately 3 of the site's 20
acres. </font></p>
<h3><img src="images/TREE.gif" alt="Tree button" align="texttop"
hspace="10" width="30" height="30"><font color="#0000FF">City of
Minneapolis</font></h3>
<p><font color="#0000FF"><strong>SKB Environmental</strong>
manages all yard waste for the City of Minneapolis, MN (1990
population 368,363) at this 17 acre site located at 620 Malcolm
Avenue SE. Yard waste delivered to the site is now transferred to
another of <strong>SKB Environmental</strong>'s sites for
composting. </font></p>
