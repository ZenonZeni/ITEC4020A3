<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>What We Are Looking For</STRONG></FONT><P>

New York Life strives to be the easiest company with which to do business. The values that drive New
York Life make our customers our number one priority. We're seeking individuals who not only bring
their skills to our organization, but who are committed to our long-standing principles of:<P>
<UL>
<LI><STRONG>Integrity</STRONG><BR>
Our employees adhere to the highest ethical standards in all business dealings. We treat our
customers with the respect and honesty they deserve.<P>

<LI><STRONG>Accountability</STRONG><BR>
Whether or not your job allows you final authority, it is important to make sound decisions regarding
your work. Everyone at New York Life takes responsibility for their actions -- from senior management to
first-year employees.<P>

<LI><STRONG>Innovation</STRONG><BR>
Regardless of the area you choose, your work will require a keen analytical ability and a thorough
understanding of the job's intricacies as well as its broader implications. Curiosity and alertness are
valuable traits. We're looking for people who have the courage and willingness to change, to create
something new, in order to do a better job.<P>

<LI><STRONG>A Sense of Urgency</STRONG><BR>
In the dynamic financial-services industry, it is important that we stay one step in front of the
competition. No task is ever "routine". A sense of urgency prefaces all the skills and values our
company upholds. It strikes a balance between doing the work now and doing it well. It brings projects
to fruition, and adds to the quality of our products and services. It means getting the job right the first
time...every time.<P>
</UL>
<STRONG>What you will get</STRONG><BR>
Working at New York Life offers advantages designed to make your job more rewarding and to increase
your productivity. In addition to offering a competitive salary, the company offers the following benefits
to full-time employees:<P>
<UL>
<LI>a subsidized flexible benefits program that allows you to customize your medical and insurance
coverage.
<LI>a tax-advantaged payroll deduction investment plan that includes a company matching feature.
<LI>a retirement program
<LI>liberal holiday and vacation schedules
</UL>
