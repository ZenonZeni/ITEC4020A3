<h1 align=center>Statement by Justin Raimondo,
<br>Republican candidate for U.S. Congress, 8<sup>th</sup> District </h1>

<p><font SIZE=+2>I endorse Proposition 215. </font></p>

<p><font SIZE=+2>As a conservative Republican, I believe that the government
has no business interfering in the decisions made between a patient and
his or her doctor. Physicians and other healthcare professionals should
be free to dispense marijuana or any other drug that they feel provides
comfort and benefit to their patients. </font></p>

<p><font SIZE=+2>I urge all Republicans to vote Yes on Proposition 215.</font></p>
