<H2>Radar Systems and Communication </H2>
<P>
This course is part of the master program for Digital Communications,
Systems and Engineering of the School of Electrical and Computer
Engineering, and will be given period 4.
<P>
<A HREF="mailto:kge@oso.chalmers.se" >Dr. Gunnar Elgered </A>
has the main responsibility for the course..
<P>
The course contains lectures and exercises complemented with some
laboratory work. An industrial visit is planned.
<P>
The course will be based on:
<P>
S. Kingsley and S. Quegan: Understanding Radar Systems, published
by in 1992 by McGraw-Hill Book Company
<P>
The book contains a large number of chapters and the course will
not cover all of them, such as e.g. chapter 7, 10, 12, and 13.
<P>
The book will be complemented by handouts concerning e.g. communication
systems.
<P>
Compulsory homework will also be part of the course.
<P>
The written examination will be on May 27, 1997, 8.45 - 12.45,
in room MN.
