<P>
NAVFeed is the only stand-alone product that can automate the
delivery of your NAVs to the NASDAQ. Realize NAVFeed's many benefits:
<UL>
<LI>Eliminate manual entry.
<LI>Eliminate errors.
<LI>Speed delivery of data to the wire services and the NASDAQ.
<LI>Relieve deadline pressure. 
</UL>

<P>
NAVFeed has flexible levels of automation to meet the needs of
any shop. It can be run in-house or through our service bureau.
NAVFeed's features include:
<UL>
<LI>A Windows based front-end to control the process.
<LI>Real-time release of NAVs as they become available.
<LI>Single workstation or LAN-based operation.
<LI>Custom interfaces available to your accounting system.
<LI>Access to the service bureau over the Internet.
<LI>Support for the new Rolling Dissemination Policy.
<LI>Support for year-end summary transmissions and Money Market
special weekly summaries. 
</UL>
