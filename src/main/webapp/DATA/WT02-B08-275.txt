<H2>Other Resources</H2>
<p>
<font size=+1>

<p>
<font size=+1>
Many people don't know that <a href=http://www-ccar.colorado.edu/~setzke/tides.html>earth tides</a> result from the gravitational attraction of the sun and moon <i> on the solid earth.</i> You might think that earth tides are small -- however, they typically have vertical displacements of many tens of centimeters!
