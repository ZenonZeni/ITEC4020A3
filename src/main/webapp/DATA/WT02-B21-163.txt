<H1>Shusun's Home In Cyberspace</H1>
Dr. Li has background in geography and remote sensing. He joined the Geophysical Institute in 1990. His present research activities include SAR interferometry, general SAR and optical sensor remote sensing applications in quantifying and modeling interactions among the atmosphere, sea ice and ocean.
