<h4> Areas of Research </h4>

<h5>Energy Studies</h5>
Experimental and numerical studies of combustion,  focusing on air 
pollution emissions,  waste combustion and industrial furnaces.  
Techniques include experimental flow reactor studies,  chemical kinetic 
modeling and computational fluid dynamic modeling.

<h5>Environmental Engineering</h5> 
Experimental and numerical studies of the control of air pollution from 
industrial sources.  Examining new air pollution control techniques such as 
using of additives to reduce organic air emissions and improving 
turbulent mixing to reduce carbon monoxide levels.  Investigating the 
pollutant generating processes using a laboratory combustor. Developing 
predictive models of the fluid flow and chemical reactions that control 
pollutant formation.
