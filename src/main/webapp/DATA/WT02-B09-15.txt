<p><font color="#000080">CSL Associates was founded in 1986 by
Cecile S. Shure to assist exporters of military and dual use
equipment and technology in their efforts to understand and
comply with the requirements of the U.S. Government. CSL consults
for a wide range of clients, from small computer companies
producing encrypted software to large defense firms manufacturing
jet engines and sophisticated electronics equipment. Several
foreign governments and companies also have sought the advice and
counsel of CSL on their own transfer issues.</font></p>
<p><font color="#000080">CSL specializes in addressing export
licensing and technology transfer concerns, from application
processing to compliance and training (see enclosures for more
detailed information about our areas of specialization). CSL
works closely with the </font><a href="http://www.state.gov/">Departments
of State</a><font color="#000080">, </font><a
href="http://www.bxa.doc.gov/"><font color="#000080">Commerce</font></a><font
color="#000080">, </font><a href="http://www.dtic.dla.mil/"><font
color="#000080">Defense</font></a><font color="#000080">, </font><a
href="http://apollo.osti.gov/"><font color="#000080">Energy</font></a><font
color="#000080">, and </font><a
href="http://www.ustreas.gov/treasury/bureaus/atf/atf.html"><font
color="#000080">Treasury (BATF)</font></a><font color="#000080">
to ensure timely processing of all license requests.</font></p>
<p><font color="#000080">CSL&#146;s mission is quite simple: to
ensure that our clients receive the best possible advice and
service available, on a cost-effective basis, in pursuit of
international business. </font></p>
