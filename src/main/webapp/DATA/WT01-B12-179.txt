<BLOCKQUOTE>


<IMG SRC="gifs/intheprime_logo.gif" ALT="In The Prime">
<P>
<IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/intheprime_shows_head.gif" ALT="Show Topics">

<P>

<B>What Now?</B><BR>
Episode #1<BR>
What have today's mid-lifers accomplished -- personally, professionally?

<P>

<B>Am I Grown Up?</B><BR>
Episode #2<BR>
Explore what it means to be a true adult.

<P>

<B>Career Change</B><BR>
Episode #3<BR>
What are the options and the possibilities?

<P>

<B>Couples And Money</B><BR>
Episode #4<BR>
Evaluate how to resolve financial issues.

<P>

<B>Creativity</B><BR>
Episode #5<BR>
Discover how to start, continue and enjoy the creative process during mid-life and beyond.

<P>

<B>Minds At Work</B><BR>
Episode #6<BR>
Does the human brain lose its capacity to learn as we age?

<P>

<B>Take A Chance</B><BR>
Episode #7<BR>
Meet several mid-life "thrill/risk-takers" and examine the impact and nature of risks -- personally and professionally.

<P>

<B>Looking For Love</B><BR>
Episode #8<BR>
Explore romance at mid-life.

<P>

<B>Future Of Work</B><BR>
Episode #9<BR>
Examine the impact of technology on the job market, and the effects of today's mid-lifers.

<P>

<B>Community</B><BR>
Episode #10<BR>
People present varying definitions of community and debate whether or not today's mid-life generation has succeeded in creating a "sense of place."

<P>

<B>Hearts And Minds</B><BR>
Episode #11<BR>
Learn about alternative treatments to traditional Western medicine, especially regarding cancer and heart disease.

<P>

<B>Search For Soul</B><BR>
Episode #12<BR>
Examine the spiritual awakening that seems to be affecting today's mid-lifers.

<P>

<B>What's To Eat?</B><BR>
Episode #13<BR>
Assess the challenges of healthy eating, while not sacrificing enjoyment in today's fast-paced lifestyle.

<P>

<B>Me No More</B><BR>
Episode #14<BR>
Examine the common mid-life change in focus from "me" to "they."

<P>

<B>You've Got A Friend</B><BR>
Episode #15<BR>
Discuss how the meaning of friendship changes at mid-life.

<P>

<B>Life Choices</B><BR>
Episode #16<BR>
Understand the experience of regret that develops from "inaction" or "actions that did not produce the expected outcome."

<P>

<B>Let's Rock</B><BR>
Episode #17<BR>
Look at how music culturally changed today's mid-lifers, and what elements of that culture have managed to endure.

<P>

<B>Take Charge</B><BR>
Episode #18<BR>
From the opposing perspectives of two leading financial authorities, learn how to get out of debt, save money and invest wisely.

<P>

<B>Spiritually Speaking</B><BR>
Episode #19<BR>
Discover how to implement spirituality into everyday life, as well as maintain good health through the mind.

<P>

<B>Generations</B><BR>
Episode #20<BR>
Evaluate the generations of this century -- what distinguishes one from another, and what insight can be gained from determining our place in history.

<P>

<B>Time For Sex?</B><BR>
Episode #21<BR>
Review the current status of sex at mid-life.

<P>

<B>Life And Death</B><BR>
Episode #22<BR>
Examine the stages of grief and why they are critical.

<P>

<B>The Better Half</B><BR>
Episode #23<BR>
Address the issues of mid-life transition in family life, career and the ability to remain financially secure.

<P>

<B>Mom And Dad</B><BR>
Episode #24<BR>
What are the unusual and difficult problems facing today's mid-life generation as they take on the role of caretaker to their parents? Three leaders in public policy and services present the many options now available.

<P>

<B>Youth For Sale</B><BR>
Episode #25<BR>
The search for eternal youth and denial of the aging process has become an even more elaborate process; evaluate the current trends, the methods and the long-term effects.

<P>

<B>Leading Citizens</B><BR>
Episode #26<BR>
Assess the professional and societal achievements of today's mid-life generation.
