<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>Benefits of the Program</STRONG></FONT><P>



<STRONG>A wide array of investment choices<SUP><FONT SIZE="-1"><A
HREF="#1">1</A></FONT></SUP></STRONG><BR>

Our Rollover IRA Program offers you a virtually unlimited choice
of investments which can help you reach your financial goals.
You can invest in the MainStay Funds<SUP><FONT SIZE="-1"><A
HREF="#2">2</A></FONT></SUP>
and over 3,500 other mutual funds (including over 370 No-Load, No-Transaction Fee
funds<SUP><FONT SIZE="-1"><A HREF="#3">3</A></FONT></SUP>), and all the equity and
fixed
income markets. You also have the opportunity to purchase a guaranteed immediate
annuity from 
New York Life.
<P>

<STRONG>The convenience of a single account</STRONG><BR>
New York Life's Rollover IRA Program provides you with the convenience of managing
all your
retirement assets in a single account. Easy-to-read, consolidated monthly statements
reflect all your investments and IRA activity during the period. Our program
also enables you to keep
your publicly-traded company stock and other securities you may own along with your
mutual funds and
annuity.
<P>

<STRONG>High quality, low cost</STRONG><BR>
The New York Rollover IRA Program gives you the opportunity
to have all custodial fees waived by simply having $10,000 or more in assets at
year end (December
31). For balances below $10,000 the annual custodial fee is only $24.
