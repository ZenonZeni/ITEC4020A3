Scott Polar Research Institute, University of Cambridge</H2>  
  
<h3>The World Data Centre C (Glaciology) is funded by the 
<a href="http://britac3.britac.ac.uk/rs/">Royal 
Society</a> and is located in the <a href="lib/libhome.htm">Library</a> of 
the Scott Polar Research Institute within the 
<A HREF="http://www.cam.ac.uk/">University of Cambridge</A>.</h3><p> 

This WWW page was previously hosted by 
<a href="http://www.muscat.co.uk/">Muscat Ltd</a>, suppliers of database
and search software to the SPRI Library.<p>

WDC C (Glaciology) has one half-time member of staff and is responsible
for abstracting articles and books on ice and snow from European authors.
These abstracts are included in the SPRILIB database and in the <a
href="http://www.muscat.co.uk/ccdp/spri/wdccg.html"> ICE AND SNOW
database</a> on the World-Wide Web.<p>

WDC C (Glaciology) also hosts the home page for the 
<a href="scar/wwg.htm">SCAR Working Group on Glaciology</a>. See also 
<a href="http://www.scar.org">SCAR</a>.
<hr>

<h3>The Jökulhlaup in Iceland</h3>

During October and November 1996 WDC C (Glaciology) maintained up-to-date 
web pages on the <a href="jok/jok.htm">jökulhlaup</a> (surge of glacier 
meltwater) from Grímsvötn caldera lake following the volcanic eruption under 
the Vatnaj&ouml;kull icesheet.<br>
