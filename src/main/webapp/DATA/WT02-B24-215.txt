<H3><A HREF="WhosWho.html#Hilary">HILARY HANAFIN, PH.D.</A>
</H3><P>Dr. Hilary Hanafin, Ph.D. is a licensed clinical psychologist and
a member of the American Psychological Association, the California State
Psychological Association, and the American Fertility Society. Dr. Hanafin
lectures across the country regarding the psychological aspects of surrogate
parenting and adoption. Dr. Hanafin's doctoral dissertation is entitled,
'The Surrogate Mother: An Exploratory Study', and she presented
'<A HREF=Hanafin.html>Surrogate Mothers: Reassessing Human Bonding</A>' at
the 1987 American Psychological Association convention. She continues to
collect testing and interview data on women who participate in third party
reproduction.
<P>Clinical works include counseling infertile couples, surrogate mothers,
egg donors and birth mothers. Dr. Hanafin is the
<A HREF="WhosWho.html#Hilary">Chief Counselor for the Center for Surrogate
Parenting &amp; Egg Donation, Inc.,</A> and she is responsible for the
psychological screening, testing, and interviews conducted on the women who
apply to the program and leads the mandatory surrogate mother/egg donor support
group meeting and counsels the surrogates and donors throughout their
participation. Dr. Hanafin makes the decision regarding the 'matching' of
surrogates and couples, counsels the intended parents and works closely with
the physicians, attorneys and administrators. Currently the psychological
team, (Hilary and her co-counselors <A HREF="WhosWho.html#Carol">Carol Wolfe,
MFCC</A>, <A HREF="WhosWho.html#Donna">Donna Robertson, LCSW</A>, <a href="WhosWho.html#Elaine">Elaine Gordon, PhD</a> and <a href="WhosWho.html#KARENC">Karen Chernekoff, MFCC</a> ) are working with over 90 surrogate mothers and have counseled close to 200 other women who have completed the program. She also counsels traditional adoptions and egg donors.
