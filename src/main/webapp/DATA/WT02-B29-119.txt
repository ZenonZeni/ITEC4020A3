<FONT SIZE="+1"><STRONG>Site Navigation Tips</STRONG></FONT><P>

We have incorporated several features to make is easy for you to make your way around this site.<P>  

<STRONG>Relative Location Within the Site</STRONG><BR>
At the top of this page, you can see a header entitled "Welcome Aboard".  Other pages in this site have similar headers and, in some cases, subheaders.  A subheader will be located directly beneath the header (this page has no subheader).  The header and subheader indicate your relative location in the site.<P>

<STRONG>Returning to the Top of a Section or Subsection</STRONG><BR>
If the headers or subheader is bright blue it is an active "hyperlink".

If you click on a blue header title, the top level page for that section will be displayed.

If you click on a smaller blue subheader title, the top level page for that particular subsection will be displayed.<P>



<STRONG>Navigating Within a Section or Subsection</STRONG><BR>
At the bottom of most pages within a section, you will also see lateral links to other pages within that section. <P>

<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>The page you are on will be displayed in bold type, but will not be active.  For example, at the bottom of this page, you can see that you are on the "Site Navigation Tips" page.  <BR>

	<LI>Any other pages listed are active links. For example, you can click on the "Welcome Aboard" link below to connect to that page.  Try it now!
</UL>
