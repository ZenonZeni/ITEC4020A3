<H3>UNIQUE <i>CLASSIFICATION REQUEST</i> REQUIREMENTS FOR ENCRYPTION ITEMS</I></H3>
</center>
<OL>
<LI><b>Mass Market Encryption Software</b><p>
<IL> For encryption items identified in &#167 742.15(b)(1) of the encryption regulation, please type the words "Mass Market Encryption Software" in Block 9 of form BXA-748P to expedite review of the request (see below).  If you do not insert this phrase, processing of your classification request will be delayed.  Refer to Supplement No. 6 to Part 742 criteria.<p>
<IL><img src="block9a.gif"><p>
<LI><b>Key Escrow, Key Recovery and Recoverable Encryption</b><p>
<IL>For encryption items identified in &#167 742.15(b)(2) of the encryption regulation, please type the word "Encryption" in Block 24 of form BXA-748P to expedite review of the request.   If you do not insert this word, processing of your classification request will be delayed.  Refer to Supplement No. 4 and No. 5 to Part 742 criteria.<p>
<IL><img align=bottom src="block24.gif"><p>
<LI><b>Non-Recovery Encryption Supported by a Business and Marketing Plan</b><p>
<IL>For encryption items identified in &#167 742.15(b)(3) of the encryption regulation, please type the word "Encryption" in Block 24 of form BXA-748P to expedite review of the request.  If you do not insert this word, processing of your classification request will be delayed.  Refer to Supplement No. 7 to Part 742 criteria.<p>
