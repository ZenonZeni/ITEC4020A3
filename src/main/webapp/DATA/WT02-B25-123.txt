<p align="center"><font size="4"><b>and Brain Disorders:</b></font></p>

<p align="center"><font size="4"><b>Texas, 1994</b></font></p>

<p align="left">In Texas, where discrimination against brain
disorders has ended for state employees, one managed care
company&#146;s total cost of treating state employees and their
family members with brain disorders was one-fifth the cost of
treating cardiovascular disease in 1994.&nbsp;</p>
