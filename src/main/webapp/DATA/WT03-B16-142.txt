VIAM Communications Group Ltd. is a publishing company based in New York and Hanoi.  The company was formed in 1993 to publish information regarding Vietnam and South East Asia.  Print publications include <b>T<small>HE</small> V<small>IETNAM</small> B<small>USINESS</small> J<small>OURNAL</small></b> and the <b>V<small>IETNAM</small> B<small>USINESS</small> Y<small>ELLOW</small> P<small>AGES</small></b>.  The company also produces and sponsors conferences and seminars on Asian business issues as well as providing consulting services to companies seeking to do business in Vietnam.<p>
