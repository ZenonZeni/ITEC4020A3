<H2>I keep reading that NITROX is risky. Is this true?</H2>
<UL><P>Some of those who question the use of NITROX lack the certification, experience and complete knowledge to competently write and report on Nitrox recreational use.  With proper training the risks of EANx diving are similar to air diving.  Both have strict depth and time limitations.  The treatment of decompression Sickness (DCS) in recompression chambers is also similar with both EANx and air.</UL>
