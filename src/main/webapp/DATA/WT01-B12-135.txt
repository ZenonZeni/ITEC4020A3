<BLOCKQUOTE>

<IMG SRC="gifs/tour_head.gif" ALT="Tour Our Site">
<BR>
Welcome aboard! On this tour, you're free to chew gum, play your music
loud and stand in the aisles to get a better view.
<P>

To orient yourself, you should know that Kemper.com has six main hubs.
Clicking on one of them will take you into a separate hierarchy.        
<P>

<A HREF="../about/aboutkemper.html">
<IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/aboutkemper_icon.gif" WIDTH=38 HEIGHT=44 ></A>
<BR>

If you're looking for general information about the company as a whole,
you'd go to 
<A HREF="../about/aboutkemper.html"> About Kemper Funds.</A>
<P>

<A HREF="../curious/curious.html">
<IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/curious_icon.gif" ALT="For the Curious
Investor" WIDTH=38 HEIGHT=39 ></A>
<BR>

For general investing and financial planning
information, head for <A HREF="../curious/curious.html"> For The Curious
Investor.</A>
<P>

<A HREF="../funds/kemperfunds.html">
<IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/funds_icon.gif" ALT="Kemper Funds" WIDTH=38 HEIGHT=28></A>
<BR>

To obtain detail about a Kemper Fund and its performance, as well as
an explanation of the importance of a financial representative, drop in
at <A HREF="../funds/kemperfunds.html"> Kemper Funds. </A>
<P>

<A HREF="../shareholders/shareholders.html">
<IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/shareholders_icon.gif" ALT="Just For Kemper
Funds Shareholders" WIDTH=38 HEIGHT=28></A>
<BR>

The better informed Kemper Funds shareholders will want to spend time in
<A HREF="../shareholders/shareholders.html"> Just For Kemper Funds
Shareholders.</A>
<P>

<A HREF="../learn/learn.html">
<IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/learn_icon.gif" ALT="To Learn More" WIDTH=38 HEIGHT=40></A>
<BR>

<A HREF="../learn/learn.html"> To Learn More </A> is a starting point
for people who'd like to know more
about Kemper Funds, including a system for referrals to an area
financial representative.
<P>

<A HREF="../financialrep/financialrep.html">
<IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/financialrep_icon.gif" ALT="Are You A Financial Representative" WIDTH=38 HEIGHT=43></A>
<BR>

The sixth hub, <A HREF="../financialrep/financialrep.html"> Are You A
Financial Representative?, </A>  is a
password-restricted section of the site. Think of it as a 24-hour sales
and marketing meeting for the financial representatives who offer
Kemper Funds to the investing public.

<P><BR>
<B>As you make your way through the site, there are several features that
you might want to use:</B>
<P><BR>
<P><BR>


<A HREF="../menu.html"><IMG BORDER=0 SRC="../gifs/icons/kemper_blkwht.gif" ALT="Kemper Funds"></A>
<BR>
The Kemper Funds logo at the bottom right-hand of every page will take
you back to the welcome page.
<P><BR>

<A HREF="../prospectus.html"><IMG SRC="../gifs/link_wht_prospectuses.gif" BORDER=0 ALT="Prospectuses And Important Information"></A><BR>
Clicking on the Prospectuses And Important Information link at the top left-hand corner of every page enables you to request a prospectus.
<P><BR>

<A HREF="../learn/contact_learn.html"><IMG SRC="../gifs/link_wht_contactkemper.gif" BORDER=0 ALT="Contact Kemper"></A><BR>
Kemper is just a click away.
<P><BR>

<A HREF="../search.html"><IMG BORDER=0 SRC="gifs/search_icon.gif" ALT="Search"></A><BR>
Search will enable you to search the entire site for a keyword or phrase.
<P><BR>

<IMG BORDER=0 SRC="../gifs/whizdom_iconlarge.gif"><BR>
Our Financial Whiz-dom icon flags an interactive feature.
<P><BR>

<A HREF="../sitemap.html"><IMG SRC="../gifs/link_wht_sitemap.gif" BORDER=0 ALT="Site Map"></A><BR>
For detail about what's in each one of our hubs, go to the <A HREF="../sitemap.html">Kemper.com Site Map</A>.
