<h2>DOE Innovative Financing Results Workshop</h2><p>
 
<B>When: </B> January 23, 1997<br>
<B>Where: </B> Denver, Colorado at the Marriott Denver Tech Center. <p>
 The workshop will 
focus on demonstrating how energy efficiency and pollution prevention projects can be 
cost effective instruments for U.S. industry.  Industrial companies and representatives of 
the financial community will be brought together to learn how successful projects have been 
developed and assist in identifying the most appropriate financing. <p>
For additional information, call 1-800-ENV-PLUS.
