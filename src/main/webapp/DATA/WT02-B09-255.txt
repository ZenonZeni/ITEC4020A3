<H4><u>Multiculturalism</u></H4>
The Burnaby and New Westminster communities are made up of many different nationalities. People from around the world choose to live here. Some immigrants and new Canadians have a difficult time adjusting to their new surroundings. There are several organizations that can help to assist with adjustments to life in Canada.
<p>
Multicultural societies develop educational programs and services, in response to community needs, to promote the integration and full participation of all ethnocultural groups into the life of the community.
