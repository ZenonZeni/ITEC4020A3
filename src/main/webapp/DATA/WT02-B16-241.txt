<h2>Electrical Contacts</h2>
<p><strong>Metallic Grids</strong>

<p><p><img border=0 hspace=15 vspace=3 align=left src="images/contacts.gif">
<font color="#F79D00">Grid contacts on the top surface of a typical cell are designed with many thin, 
conductive fingers spreading to every part of the cell's surface.</font>
<br clear=all>

<p>Grids can be expensive to fabricate and can even affect dependability.  To make top-surface
grids, we can deposit metallic vapors on a cell through a mask or paint them on via a screen-printing 
method.  For the highest quality (but at the greatest cost), photolithography is the
preferred method.  This involves transferring an image via photography, as in modern printing.
