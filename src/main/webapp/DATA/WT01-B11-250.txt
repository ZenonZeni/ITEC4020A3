<H4>
<B>Performance Highlights</B>
</H4>
<UL>
<LI>The S&amp;P 500 Index of large company stocks gained 8.34% during the fourth quarter, translating to a solid 22.96% return for 1996.  The economic environment of steady growth and low inflation, coupled with strong corporate earnings, helped fuel the stock market rally.
<LI>Annual returns were led by the performance of the technology and financial sectors, which gained 42.34% and 31.79%, respectively.  The capital goods and energy groups, both of which returned in excess of 20% during the year, also performed very well.
<LI>Communications services and utilities, with annual returns of -1.88% and 0.73%, respectively, were the poorest performing groups in the Index. 
</UL>
