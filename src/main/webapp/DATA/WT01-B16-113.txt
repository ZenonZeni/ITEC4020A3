Dr Florian Glodeanu
is Head of the Strategy and Development Department in the Nuclear Power Group of the Romanian Electricity Authority, RENEL. He graduated in physics from the Timisdara University. He was awarded a doctorate in physics in 1980 by the Institute for Nuclear Physics and Engineering in Bucharest.
