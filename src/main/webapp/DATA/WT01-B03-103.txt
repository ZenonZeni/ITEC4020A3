<H2>ARTHUR E. IRWIN</H2></CENTER>
<H2>Education </H2>
<P>Harvard University: Graduate School of Business Administration, (MBA 1969)<BR>
Syracuse University: Maxwell School of Political Science &amp; Public
Administration (BA 1963) (Distinguished Military Student) 
</P>
<H2>Professional Experience</H2>
<H4>1993-Present, President, <A HREF="TGCA.html">Tribal Gaming Commission
Administrators, Inc. (TGCA)</A>, Toms River, NJ</H4>
<P>President of this consulting firm which provides training and support
services to Tribal Government Gaming Commissions. TGCA is a subsidiary of
<A HREF="index.html">Irwin Gaming, Inc.</A> a consulting firm which provides
services in the areas of Gaming Operations, Government Relations, Gaming
Regulations, &amp; Corporate Development. As President of Irwin Gaming, Inc.,
coordinate programs in administration, marketing, and corporate development.
Director: <A HREF="TGCAAcc.html">Tribal Gaming Academy </A>.  President,
<A HREF="tgmlaun.html">Tribal Gaming Management, Inc.</A></P>
<H4><BR> 1985-Present, &quot;Writing for Results&quot;, Fall River, MA</H4>
<P>Owner/Operator: of this award-winning service which specializes in marketing
consulting and business/technical communication. Services include news releases,
annual reports, marketing literature, speeches, procedure manuals, newsletters,
A/V scripts and presentations. Ad agency sub-contracted projects include
advertising copy, new product releases, technical manuals, and trade magazine
articles for a variety of technological, industrial, and manufacturing clients.
Designated 1987 Entrepreneur of the Year by the University of Massachusetts
(Dartmouth) and the U.S. Small Business Development Corporation. 
</P>
<H4><BR> 1974-1976 Unigraf Advertising of N.J., Inc. (Industrial Advertising
Agency)
</H4>
<P>Founder/Principal: of this suburban New York firm. Responsible for
marketing, administration, and business development. Shared responsibility for
account servicing, creative projects, and copy production. 
</P>
<H4><BR> 1965-74, 76-85 Commercial Bank Management in NY, NJ and New England</H4>
<P>Progressed from Senior Financial Analyst through Planning Officer, Credit
Officer, VP &amp; Treasurer, to Senior VP. Functioned as Internal Consultant,
Credit Manager, Senior Financial Officer, Senior Operating Officer, and
Coordinator of Marketing, Public Relations &amp; Business Development.
Experience with branch banking, holding company operations, new product
development, security &amp; control systems, and mergers &amp; acquisitions.
Developed, implemented and documented policies, procedures and operating
systems. Direct responsibility for data processing, EFT, and ATM systems. 
</P>
