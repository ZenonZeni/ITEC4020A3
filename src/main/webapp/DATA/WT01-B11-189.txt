<CENTER>
<B>Forest Fire Emergency Area Declared in Northwest Ontario


</B>

</CENTER><P>
Natural Resources Minister Chris Hodgson today announced that to ensure public safety an Emergency Area Order is in effect from noon today in fire regions in northwest Ontario .<P>

"I am concerned about public safety in the high-risk zones around the fires currently burning in the northwest," said Mr. Hodgson. <P>

The Emergency Area Order covers zones 3,4,5,6,7,9,10 and 12 of the northwest fire region. Please refer to map below. <P>

Under the Forest Fires Prevention Act, the Minister has the authority to close roads or stop the movement of people into  areas that are unsafe in fire emergencies. The Act also gives the Minister authority to request residents to evacuate if fires are dangerously close to their community.<P>
