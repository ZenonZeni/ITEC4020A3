<FONT SIZE="+1"><STRONG>Simplified Employee Pension Plan (SEP) Questions and
Answers</STRONG></FONT><P>


<STRONG>Q:	Who can establish SEP plans?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:</STRONG>	Corporations, sub-chapter S corporations, self-employed
individuals,
sole
proprietorships, partnerships, and non-profit organizations.
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	What is the limit on contributions to the plan?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:	</STRONG>Maximum annual contributions of 15% of compensation based on
maximum annual compensation limit of $150,000 ($150,000 x .15 = $22,500) or
$30,000
per
participant, whichever is less.
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	When must the plan be established?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:</STRONG>	By the tax filing due date (including extensions).
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	When must contributions be made?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:	</STRONG>Contributions must be made by the tax filing deadline plus
extensions.
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	Is the employer a fiduciary?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:</STRONG>	No.
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	Is plan administration required (e.g., annual reports to the
IRS)?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:</STRONG>	No.
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	What are the eligibility requirements for participants?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A: </STRONG>	Maximum requirements are 3 years of service out of preceding
5 years,
21
years of age and earnings of at least $400 per year.
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	What are the vesting requirements?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:</STRONG> There's immediate 100% vesting upon entry into plan.
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	When can money be withdrawn from the plan?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:	</STRONG>Money may be withdrawn without penalty after age 59 1/2. 
Individuals must
start distributions no later than April 1st  of the year following the year they
turn 70 1/2.
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	How are distributions taxed?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:</STRONG>	Taxed as ordinary income.
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	Are loans available?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:	</STRONG>No.
<P>
<STRONG>Q: 	Are annual contributions required?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:</STRONG>	No.
<P>
<STRONG>Q:	How are contributions/benefits determined?</STRONG><BR>
<STRONG>A:	</STRONG>Contributions are determined annually by the employer.
