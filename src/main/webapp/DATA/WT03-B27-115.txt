<H3>BUSINESS WIRE (BW)</H3>

<B>Business Wire--BW01W</B><BR>
Business Wire<BR>
$1.00/article<BR>
Continuous wire feed with immediate updating; <BR>
10-week retention of articles<BR>
(Also in FI, GB, CoRpts, BWires, Wires)<BR>
Business Wire delivers timely, full-text press releases and announcements prepared
and transmitted to Business Wire by over 10,000 U.S. companies. These contributors
include many of the nation's largest and most visible corporations as well as
thousands of the most active, rapidly growing OTC <BR>
organizations.<P>

Business Wire's coverage includes reports of such corporate developments as new
product announcements, earnings reports, securities offerings, mergers and
acquisitions, major personnel shifts, and contract awards, in virtually all
industries.<P>

NewsNet monitors Business Wire on a 24-hour basis. Articles are immediately
available for full-text retrieval in SCAN mode in hourly segments. Full-text
searching of all articles is available within one hour of release.<P>
