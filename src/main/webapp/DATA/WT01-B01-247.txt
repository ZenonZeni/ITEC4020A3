<H1>The James L. Madden Center for Maritime Studies</H1>

<P>
SEA's Madden Center and its beautiful Woods Hole Road campus are
located on the scenic hilltop site of an old Cape Cod estate. Dogwoods,
apple trees, and perennial gardens accent the historic stonework
remaining from the 1889 mansion. Our academic complex includes
a lecture hall, classrooms, a laboratory, student library, computer
room, study areas, and faculty and staff offices. Return to the
<A HREF="../bilboard/index.htm">SEA home page</A>.
