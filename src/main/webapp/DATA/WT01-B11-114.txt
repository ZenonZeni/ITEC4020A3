<CENTER><H2>May is Rabies Awareness Month</H2></CENTER><P>
This site provides information about rabies and how people can protect themselves, their families and their pets from it.<P>

You'll find information about fox rabies, which we have in Ontario, and raccoon rabies, which we <B>do not yet</B> have in Ontario.<P>

It is important to remember: <B>rabies is rabies</B>.  The precautions that Ontarians have always taken against rabies are effective for any type of rabies.<P>
This page links you to a wide range of information on rabies.<P><BR>
