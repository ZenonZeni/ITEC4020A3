<A NAME="Features"></A>
<IMG ALIGN=ABSBOTTOM SRC="http://www.rixan.com/unixicon.gif" WIDTH=18 HEIGHT=18> <STRONG><FONT SIZE=+1> ROBOSOL FRIEND Features</FONT></STRONG><P>
<ul>
<li><li>5 axis orthogonal soldering robot capable of setting soldering conditions for 20 types of continuous soldering or 30 types of point soldering.<br>
<li>Maximum speed of 600mm/sec (x and y axes) with position repeatability of +/-0.05 mm (X, Y, and Z axes) has been achieved through the use of precision DC motors and ball screws.<br>
<li>Teaching, programming, soldering and other operations are easily accomplished through the use of a user friendly teach box with 80 character LCD screen.<br>
<li>A unique system of crossed notches on the bottom of the soldering tip, along with a projection on the top of the heater element, provide the user with positive tip position.
</ul>

<P>

<A NAME="Applications"></A>
<IMG ALIGN=ABSBOTTOM SRC="http://www.rixan.com/unixicon.gif" WIDTH=22 HEIGHT=22> <STRONG><FONT SIZE=+1> ROBOSOL FRIEND Applications</FONT></STRONG><P>
The LS-5TPS II can perform:<br>
<ul>
<li><li>linear soldering on SM components (SOPs, QFPICs and the tape automated bonding (TAB) terminals of LCDs).<br>
<li>soldering on minute and delicate components.<br>
<li>linear soldering on large-sized connectors.
</ul>
