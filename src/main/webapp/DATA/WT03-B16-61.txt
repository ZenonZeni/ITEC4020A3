<h3>Mekong Delta, Vietnam, Summer 1996
</h3>
This image is the colour composite of three ERS-2 scenes (Red: 4 May 1996, Blue: 9 June 1996, Green: 14 July 1996) of the Mekong Delta near the town of Can Tho.  The multitude of colours displayed illustrates the various stages of rice crop in the field.  A network of irrigation canals is also visible.  The banks of the canals are lined with vegetation which changes little over time, and hence appears grey.
