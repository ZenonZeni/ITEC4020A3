<H1>SAR Fast Delivery Image</H1>

<p>
The product is a multi-look (speckle-reduced), ground range, digital image intended for near real-time applications. No correction
is applied for the SAR antenna pattern or range-spreading loss.
<p><hr><p> 
<h2>Product characteristics</h2>
<pre> 
Pixel size (range - across track)          20.0 m 
           (azimuth - along track)       ~ 15.9 m 
Scene area (range - across track)          100 km 
(azimuth - along track)                  ~ 96 km 
Scene size (range - pixels)                5000 pixels per line 
(azimuth - lines)                          6300 lines 
Pixel depth                                16 bits 
Product location accuracy                  1000 m 
Total product volume                     ~ 63 Mbytes 
Projection                                 ground range 
Number of looks                            3 
</pre>
<h2>Media</h2>

</ul>
