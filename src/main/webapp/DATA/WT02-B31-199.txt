The World Wide Web offers a wealth of movie-related resources, information
and gossip, with literally thousands of sites produced by major Hollywood
studios, independent producers and distributors, and official film
organisations, as well as by enthusiastic fans around the world. Over the
next few months, the Greater Union Movie Links page will develop an
extensive and regularly updated list of the best of them. If you know of a
link that we should list, please e-mail its name, URL, and a brief
description to <a href=mailto:spike@mpx.com.au>spike@mpx.com.au</a>. <I>Last updated 16th December, 1996</I>.
