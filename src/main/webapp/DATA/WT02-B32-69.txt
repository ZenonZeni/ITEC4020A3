These pages have been prepared to assist students taking courses in food Law at the University of Reading. In particular this includes module <b>FS304 (New number FS652) Quality Assurance</b> given to a number of undergraduate courses and module <b>FS955 Food Legislation</b> given to MSc courses in Food Science (both options) and MSc Food Technology (Quality Assurance Option).
<p>
<p>
Visitors from elsewhere are welcome to browse and use these pages.  Comments, additional material, etc are weclome.  E-mail to <a href="mailto:D.J.Jukes@afnovell.reading.ac.uk"> D.J.Jukes@afnovell.reading.ac.uk</a>
