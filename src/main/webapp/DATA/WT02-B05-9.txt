<H2>Court Decision Challenges Constitutionality of Crypto Export Controls</h2>
<FONT SIZE=-1>First Posted on December 18, 1996</FONT>
<P>
A Federal judge in San Francisco has issued an opinion in Dan Bernstein's challenge to U.S.
encryption laws, invalidating export control provisions on encryption source code as an
unconstitutional prior restraint on free speech.
