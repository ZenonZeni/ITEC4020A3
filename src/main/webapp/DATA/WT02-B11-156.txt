Please excuse the cross-posting, I'll keep it short.<br>
<p>
With my colleagues at First Virtual, I have recently completed what we<br>
intend to be the definitive statement about what FV has learned from a<br>
full year of processing payments over the Internet.  This is not<br>
intended as a puff piece, but a serious academic-style overview.  We're<br>
hoping it will prove to be a valuable resource for anyone trying to<br>
process payments on the net, even those who disagree with some aspects<br>
of FV's approach.  The paper will be presented next week at the<br>
"Frontiers in Electronic Commerce" conference in Austin.<br>
<p>
In Postscript, it's a 14 page paper with a nearly 30 page appendix, so<br>
it doesn't *have* to be a long paper to read unless you want it to be.<br>
:-)   Plain text and postscript versions are available on the net by<br>
anonymous ftp:<br>
