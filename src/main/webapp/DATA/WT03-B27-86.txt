<h3>Perfection from the Farms</h3>
<p><img src="images/farm7.jpg" align="bottom" width="232"
height="350"> </p>
<p>After our flowers have been properly packed, they go through a
second inspection of quality. That concentrates on the flower
itself and to the quality of its packing. Once approved, these
flowers go through a hydration period in our cold rooms which
will guarantee their capacity to travel long distances and
overcome the stress from handling to its final destination: the
consumer.</p>
