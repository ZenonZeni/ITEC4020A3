<BR>

Kemper has been an industry leader in developing innovative mutual 
fund concepts:
<P>

<UL TYPE=CIRCLE>
<LI>
<A HREF="../funds/mmfunds.html">Kemper Money Market Fund</A> was one of the nation's first money market
funds. (1974)

<LI>
<A HREF="../funds/muni_bond.html">Kemper Municipal Bond Fund</A> was 
the nation's first municipal bond mutual fund. (1976)

<LI>
Kemper Investment Portfolios was one of the first 12-b1 fund families,
enabling investors to invest 100 percent of their money upfront and pay
the cost of distribution over time. (1984).

<LI>
KemFlex was one of the first sophisticated retirement plan recordkeeping
systems to be introduced by a mutual fund distributor, thereby
simplifying plan administration and reducing plan cost. (1988)

<LI>
Kemper addressed the long-term goals - and security concerns - of many
investors with the introduction of the <A HREF="../funds/retire_series.html"> 
Kemper Retirement Fund</A> series,
which guarantees the return of original investments at a set date if
held until maturity and dividends are reinvested. (Investors who redeem
prior to maturity or do not reinvest dividends do not benefit from the
guarantee.) (1990)

<LI>
With PREFERRED K, Kemper created an integrated turnkey approach for
implementing cost-effective 401(k) programs. (1991)

<LI>
Kemper introduced a worldwide fund with the guaranteed return of the
original investment at maturity - <A HREF="../funds/worldwide.html">
Kemper Worldwide 2004 Fund</A>. (Investors
who redeem prior to maturity or do not reinvest dividends do not benefit
from the guarantee). (1994)
</UL>
