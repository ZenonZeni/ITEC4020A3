From this arrangement it seems that the blue chip stocks have a lions share of the market action. Incidentally, in Canada the blue chip TSE 35 is also doing better than the TSE 300.<p>The Dow has passed my original target of 6200-6400 and appears to want to keep moving higher. As stated several times in the past (and is being repeated by everybody), rising interest rates are probably a key to a market reversal. However, investors are continually hearing that the US economy is slowing to more modest growth rates. If this turns out to be true, then the Federal Reserve may not need to raise rates to slow the economy. Let's hope that this is the way that it works. I'm still concerned about a correction in sometime in the late winter.
