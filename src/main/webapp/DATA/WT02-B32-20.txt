<P>
<P>
This research group is strongly active and well funded by the Biological
and Biotechnological Science Research Group , &nbsp;the Eurpean Commision
and industry and is mainly focussed on sweet taste chemoreception and
&nbsp;flavour and colour biogeneration during food processing.
<P>
Particular areas under study are the water structuring properties of sugar
and sweeteners in relation to taste, solution studies and molecular dynamics
of sweeteners. &nbsp; Maillard chemistry (flavour and colour), participation
of phospholipids in heat induced flavour, meat aroma in relation to inosinine
monophosphate, flavour binding and release
