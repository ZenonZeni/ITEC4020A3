<p><em><strong>Title</strong></em><strong>. </strong>Phase Transformations: Industrial Applications</p>
<p><em><strong>Abstract</strong></em><strong>. </strong>
While our understanding of phase transformations is ever
increasing, the ability of industry to use this information is going in the
opposite direction. The two cultures are diverging rather than converging.
Solution paths to some current industrial materials problems are suggested
based upon what the speaker knows about materials.
