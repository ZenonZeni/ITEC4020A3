<p><font size="+1">The <b><i>Journal</i></b> is a technical publication,
published twice a year, oriented to professionals in the field of learning
disabilities. A vehicle for disseminating the most current thinking on
learning disabilities, it provides information on research, practice,
theory, issues and trends regarding learning disabilities from the
perspectives of varied disciplines involved in broadening the
understanding of learning disabilities. The disciplines represented in
the <b><i>Journal</i></b> include adults, advocacy, assessment, college
programs, cultural differences, early childhood, public and private education,
families, higher and adult education, law, mental health, public policy,
research, science, social and emotional issues, social work, technology,
and vocational and career education. </font>

<p><font size="+1">The <b><i>Journal</i></b> is edited by Jeannette
Fleischner, Ph.D.</font>

<p><font size="+1">The subscription rate is $25.00 per year. To subscribe,
print, fill out and mail the <a href="forms/journal.html">subscription
form</a> or contact the LDA <a href="Email.html">National
Office</a>.</font>
