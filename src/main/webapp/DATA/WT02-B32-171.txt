You are on your way towards becoming a famous photographer!  Enter the "Images and Experiences of the African Diaspora:  Families and Culture" contest, in conjunction with Black History Month.  This contest allows you a chance to compete with photographs, capturing the true images and experiences of the family and its culture on camera.   
<P>We also allow the winners an opporunity to exhibit their work as professional photographers.  Simply, follow the listed rules and deadlines to enter! <B>Sorry, this contest is restricted to non-professionals. Only one photo per person will be accepted.</B>  Good Luck!<P>
<hr><CENTER><dl><p><table><p><tr><p><td><CENTER><img src="camera.gif"></CENTER><FONT COLOR="0000DD"><h2>Contest Guidelines</h2></FONT></CENTER></td></tr></table><p><B>  Step One</B>:  Grab a camera with film, don't forget the flash!<P>
<B>Step Two</B>:  Take a photo that captures and depicts a positive  image and/or experience  of an African American/Caribbean Family and/or Culture.<P>
<B>Step Three</B>:  Submit a 8  1/2 by 11 photograph, which should include the negative.  Don't forget to <I>place your name, address and telephone number on the back of the picture</I>.  All entries must be received no later than February 7th!<P><CENTER><B>Send the photo and negative to</B>: <br> Inner City Broadcasting Corporation<br>WLIB Radio<br>3 Park Avenue<br>41st Floor<br>New York, N.Y.  10016.<br> Attention:  WLIB Promotions Dept.<p>
<hr><CENTER><dl><p><table><p><tr><p><td><img src="artdis.gif"></td><td><H3><B>February 12, 1997</B></H3></CENTER></td></tr></table>
<P>
A total of twelve pictures will be selected, based on originality, passion and creativity.  During the week of February 17 - 23 these pictures will be displayed at the Schomburg Center.<P><hr><center><dl><p><table><p><tr><p><td><img src="moneygrp.gif"></td><td><h3><b>
February 28, 1997</b></h3></center></td></tr></table></center><P>
After choosing the twelve selected finalist, WLIB will announce the three finalists and the grand prize winner.  Prizes consists of gift certificates from Pathmark and moneys towards a course in Professional Photography.
