HEART DISEASE: GARLIC-GINKGO COMBO REDUCES CHOLESTEROL
  <!-- --[ End title ]-- -->


</b></font>
<hr color="#ff0000" size="1" width="80%" align="left">
<p>

<blockquote>
<P>



  <!-- --[ Insert the article text here ]-- -->

<P>
A Garlic-Ginkgo Combination Lowered Cholesterol Rates In 35 Percent Of Patients 
With Levels Ranging From 230 To 390 - Even During The Christmas / New Year's Season, 
When People Generally Tend To Eat Less Wholesome Foods. 
After Discontinuing The Supplement, Cholesterol Levels Increased Again.

<P>
<I>Source: Arzneimittel-Forschung, 1993 Sep, 43 (9):978.</I>

<P>
Garlic-Ginkgo Combinations Should Contain 150 Milligrams Of Garlic And 40 
Milligrams Of Ginkgo (From A 50-To- I Extraction).
