   <font size="3" face="Arial">
    <b>How do I navigate through the site?</b><br>
    The red bar at the top of the screen contains links to each part of the 
    site. Click on a section name (contained between brackets) 
    to move to that area of the site. Once you are in that section there may 
    be other links and buttons to press which give more information.

    <p>

    <b>How do I get help with the site?</b><br>
    Use the <em>Help</em> button on the navigation bar to bring you
    to this page.  If you are still having problems with the site,
    please send email to <a href="mailto:help@alternativemedicine.com">help@alternativemedicine.com</a>.<p>

    <b>What is the best browser to use with this site?</b><br>
    This site works best with Microsoft Internet Explorer 3.0, but Netscape Navigator will suffice.<p>

    <b>Why do some of the tables in this site sometimes appear blank?</b><br>
    Due to a bug in Netscape Navigator 3.0, sometimes the contents of
    tables will appear blank.  To remedy this situation, click on the
    <em>Location/URL</em> field (where you would type in a new address to a 
    website) in your browser and hit <em>Enter</em>. Unfortunately,
    reloading using the <em>Reload</em> button/menu item does not solve the problem!
