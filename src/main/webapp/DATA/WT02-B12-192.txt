	<font size=6>About Migration News</font><p>

	</center>


		<a href=mntxt.htm>Migration News</a> summarizes the most important immigration and integration

developments of the preceding month.<p>

	Topics are grouped by region: North America, Europe, Asia, and Other.<p>

	There are two versions of Migration News.  The paper edition is about 8,00

words in length, and the email version about 15,000. <p>

	The purpose of Migration News is to provide a monthly summary of recent

immigration developments that can be read in 60 minutes or less.  Many issues

also contain summaries and reviews of recent research publications. <p>

	Distribution is by email.  If you wish to subscribe, send your email address

to: Migration News <migrant@primal.ucdavis.edu><p>

	Current and back issues may be accessed via Internent on the Migration News

Home Page-- http://migration.ucdavis.edu<p>

	There is no charge for an email subscription to Migration News.  A paper

edition is available by mail for $30 domestic and $50 foreign.  Make checks

payable to UC Regents and send to: Philip Martin, Department of Agricultural

Economics, University of California, Davis California 95616 USA. <p>

	Migration News is produced with the support of the University of

California-Berkeley Center for German and European Studies, the German Marshall

Fund of the  United States, and the Pew Charitable Trusts.<p>
