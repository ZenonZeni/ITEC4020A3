<P>Dear Colleague,<BR>
<BR>
Recent evidence suggests a close connection between panic disorder and depressive
disorders. Many investigators have noted significant comorbidity of the
two disorders. 1,2<BR>
<BR>
Our Symposium explores the role of serotonin in depression and anxiety.
We review the clinical and epidemiologic findings in relation to panic,
anxiety and depression.<BR>
<BR>
We will look at an Australian perspective on the treatment of panic disorder
and review the data on paroxetine - the only Selective Serotonin Re-uptake
Inhibitor in the world with an indication for treating panic disorder.<BR>
<BR>
Perhaps not all SSRI's are the same?<BR>
<BR>
We look forward to your participation.<BR>
Professor Graham Burrows Professor Jim Ballenger<BR>
