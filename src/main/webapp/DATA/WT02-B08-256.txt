<h3>False-colour infrared image of the Kola Peninsula, Russia</h3>
<img src="kola.gif" align=RIGHT>
The data used to generate this image were acquired from the
AVHRR instrument on board a NOAA satellite. The darkest parts of the image
show areas of water, and the white or very light blue parts show cloud. The
red colour is produced by vegetation.<p>
