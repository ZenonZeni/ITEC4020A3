<h5> Area of Research </h5>
Experiments are being conducted to measure the heat transfer 
between heated solid surfaces and impinging liquid droplets
and sprays.  Measurements are made of droplet evaporation
rates and surface temperature variation. The dynamics of impact
of a single droplet are photographed.  Droplets of water, 
hydrocarbons, and liquid nitrogen have been studied.<p>
