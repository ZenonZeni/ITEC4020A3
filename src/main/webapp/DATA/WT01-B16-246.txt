<hr size="2" width="100%" align="left" noshade><p>

Headaches--whether it's a migraine, tension, sinus, cluster, trauma, eyestrain, allergy-sensitivity, or any of 12 different types covered in this book--can be eliminated for good.<p>

Robert Milne, M.D., and Blake More present the most effective treatment strategies from nutrition, chiropractic, acupuncture, acupressure, homeopathy, bodywork, biofeedback, aromatherapy, and many more modalities.<p>

All you need to know is your symptoms. From here, the authors skillfully help you identify the type and probable cause of your headache, then guide you through treatment options suited for your condition, including self-care remedies.<p>

Milne and More present the latest clinical research and back it up with dozens of real-life success stories of men, women, and children who are now headache-free.<p>
