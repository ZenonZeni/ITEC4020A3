<H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Some On-line Resources Related<BR>to Teaching in Inclusion Settings</H2>
<FONT SIZE=-1>Developed by <A HREF="../Students#JT">Jan Tona</A></FONT><BR>
<HR> 
<A HREF="http://www.uni.edu/coe/ren-grp/">Renaissance Group</A>: Consortium of universities noted for teacher education programs hosts this web site, which includes information on: The renaissance group; Philosophy of inclusive education; Teacher competencies needed; Cooperative teaching; teaching strategies; preparing students/teachers for inclusion; description of an inclusive classroom; and resources (books, organizations, web sites)... and more!<BR>
<BR>
<A HREF="http://www.ed.gov/IDEA/amend95/http://www.uvm.edu/~mgiangre/index.html">Michael Giangreco's Home Page</A>:  Faculty member of University of Vermont, Michael Giangreco has authored numerous articles on teaching in an inclusion setting, particularly with children with deaf-blindness and multiple disabilities. This web site includes references and abstracts of many articles, as well as other resources.<BR>
<BR>
<A HREF="http://www.asri.edu/cfsp/brochure/abtcons.htm">Consortium On Inclusive School Practices</A>:  Collaborative effort to build the capacity of state and local education agencies to serve children and youth with and without disabilities in school and community settings. Includes:
<UL>
<LI> catalog of products from funded projects<BR>
<LI> <A HREF="http://www.asri.edu/cfsp/brochure/inclinks.htm">multiple inclusion links</A><BR> 
<LI> on line search for articles<BR>
<LI> <A HREF="http://asri.edu/CFSP/brochure/pubs/pub207.htm">information on articles and brochures</A> available for free or at minimal cost
</OL>

<A HREF="http://www.familyvillage.wisc.edu/edu_incl.htm">The Family Village Inclusions Resources</A>: Includes information on where to go to chat with others; on-line newsletters; recommended readings; books, videos,  newsletters & other resources; and conferences, workshops, institutes, among other items.<BR>
<BR>
<A HREF="http://www.usoe.k12.ut.us/sars/inclusion/inclusion.html">Utah's Project for Inclusion</A>:  Includes resources and information on training / workshops; the inclusion network of Utah; and resources.  Also has inclusion related links.<BR>
<BR>
<A HREF="http://www.ed.gov/IDEA/amend95/">Department of Education</A>: Information on the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act.<BR>
