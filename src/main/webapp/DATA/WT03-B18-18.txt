<h1 align="center">Robotics Department</h1>

<h4 align="center">Head: <a href="/staff/cv_gian.htm">Gianmarco
Veruggio</a></h4>

<p>The Robotics Department at <a href="/Welcome.html">CNR-IAN</a>
was set up in 1989 to advance understanding in marine robotics,
particularly concerning Unmanned Underwater Vehicles (UUVs) and
tele-operated systems. </p>
