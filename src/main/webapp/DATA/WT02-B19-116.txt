<h4> Research Activities </h4>
           Specializing in the analysis of experimentally measured turbulent
flows,
           he is developing analytical models based on organized structures
in steady and nonsteady flows. 
           This done with experimental tools that include multipoint hotwire
measurements, Laser Doppler Velocimetry 
           and Particle Image Velocimetry.  He is working with Prof.  J.
Wallace investigating fluid flow in 
           Spark Ignition engines. In collaboration with Prof. J. Keffer, he
is investigating the expanded turbulent 
           plane wake.
