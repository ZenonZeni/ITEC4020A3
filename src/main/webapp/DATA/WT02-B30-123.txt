<P><A NAME="objective"><STRONG>Investment Objective</FONT></STRONG></A>
<BR>Seeks to realize current income consistent with reasonable opportunity for future growth of
capital and income.
<P>
<BR>

<A NAME="f_profile"><STRONG>Fund Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>

<UL>
<LI>The Fund's investment objective is to realize current income consistent with reasonable
opportunity for future growth of capital and income.
<LI>The Fund invests primarily in common stocks of companies with growth in revenues and
earnings per share superior to that of the average of common stocks comprising  the S&amp;P 500 Composite Stock Price Index.
<LI>The Fund's long-term debt investments will consist primarily of securities which are
rated A or better by S&amp;P or Moody's or, if unrated, deemed to be of comparable
creditworthiness by the investment adviser. 
</UL>

<P><A NAME="i_profile"><STRONG>Investor Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>Investors seeking a core fund to which equity or income investments can be added
<LI>Investors seeking income and long-term growth potential
<LI>Investors seeking to manage risk through diversification across companies, industries, and
asset classes
<LI>Income investors seeking to enhance their total return
</UL>
Before you invest...
<BR>Like any mutual fund that invests in stocks and bonds, the Fund's returns and net asset
value will vary with market conditions and changing interest rates.
