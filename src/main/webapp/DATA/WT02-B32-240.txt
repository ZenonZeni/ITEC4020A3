$3.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>

Covers topics such as advanced computer systems and networks, quality
programs, and the shift from defense to commercial requirements.<P>

<B>Sensor Business Digest--MG16</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mg16.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>

(Also in EC, RD)<BR>

industries, including new products, technology, and emerging trends. In addition,
the variables affecting national and international markets are analyzed.<P>

<B>Sensor Business News--MG23</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/mg23.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Phillips Business Information Inc.<BR>
$3.00/article; $30.00/issue<BR>
Available from 5/24/95--5/22/96<BR>
(Also in Phillips)<BR>
Analyzes today's sensor market, the players, and the business opportunities. Also
offers financial analysis of this $6.5 billion marketplace.<P>
