			<font size=+1 face="Times New Roman,Times">
				VADUZ, LIECHTENSTEIN&#0151;What a year it’s been for <a href=http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158>InterLotto</a>. Launched on 7 October 1995, the world’s first online lottery service is preparing to celebrate its first birthday on Monday, a year to the day when operations begun. To celebrate a year of successful operations, InterLotto are staging a special promotion ‘Big Birthday Bash’ with 5,000 Swiss Francs being given away randomly to lotto players.  
			</font>
			<br><br>
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">
				&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
					When InterLotto fired up its massive Internet servers on a snowy Liechtenstein day last year, they offered players a chance to win over US $1,000,000 over the Internet in a weekly lottery which boasted one of the best chances of success of any lotto game in the world. They still do, but now there are also a wide range of other games available, making InterLotto the right bet for those who want to win big money fast.
			</font>
			<br><br>
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">
				&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
					‘Big Birthday Bash’ starts on Saturday morning. <a href=http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158>Login to InterLotto</a> and play a few of the games and you stand a chance of winning a random prize. Prizes are awarded when contestants play a game, a notice of each prize being awarded will be posted on the front page of the site. 
			</font>
			<br><br>
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">
				&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
					An InterLotto press release which was distributed electronically late last night, described the promotion, ‘Yes, it's true. On Monday, <a href=http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158>Interlotto</a> will be one year old!! To celebrate we are giving you the presents!’ 
			</font>
			<br><br>	
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">
				&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
					Prizes in ‘Big Birthday Bash’ will range between 10 and 100 Swiss Francs. And <a href=http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158>InterLotto</a> will give out cash until the entire 5,000 Swiss Francs has been distributed. A similar promotion, entitled ‘Score of Winners’ was held during the year with a jackpot pool of SFr 2,700. This time the jackpot’s almost twice the size, so there are many more chances to win and cash in on <a href=http://www.interlotto.li/cgi/hserver.exe?mainlot+serve=_mn_main+urlref=3300000852158>InterLotto’s</a> popularity.
			</font>
			<br><br>	
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">
				&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
				To make sure that Monday is marked as a special day in the world of Online entertainments, the real winning starts on InterLotto’s actual birthday, as prizes are staggered through the weekend with the majority of the surprise rewards being rewarded on Monday (7 October).  	
			</font>
			<br><br>	
			<font face="Times New Roman,Times">
				&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
					Help InterLotto celebrate their birthday and win up to SFr 100 for free now!
