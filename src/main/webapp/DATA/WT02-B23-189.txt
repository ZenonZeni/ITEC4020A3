<h3 align=center>Current Approaches in Early Childhood Research:
Identification and Intervention</h3>

The panel will discuss identification issues and intervention strategies
with respect to children less than eight years old who have, or are at
risk for, Learning Disabilities. Panelists will present state of the art
information followed by questions and discussion.
