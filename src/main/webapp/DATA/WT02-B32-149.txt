<H2 ALIGN="CENTER"><A NAME="DavidS"><FONT COLOR="#004080">David J. Sally</FONT></A></H2>
<P><A HREF="mailto:djsally@comspacelaw.com">djsally@comspacelaw.com</A></P>
<P>David Sally, an associate with the firm, represents clients in a broad range
of corporate, commercial and securities matters.  Prior to becoming an associate
with Pierson and Burnett, Mr. Sally worked as in-house legal counsel to Alcatel
Data Networks Inc., a global telecommunications company, where he represented
the company on a variety of transactional and litigation matters pertaining to
commercial, international, corporate and securities law.</P>
<P>Mr. Sally has experience working with SEC registration statements, initial
public offering documents, and various commercial agreements, including joint
venture and strategic alliances, distribution and sale agreements, product
development and technology agreements and licenses, consulting and services
agreements, as well as other complex third party commercial agreements.  He has
assisted with the coordination of  the corporate affairs of various publicly
traded and privately held companies.</P>
<P>Prior to and during law school, Mr. Sally worked as a licensed registered
representative for Charles Schwab &amp; Co., Inc., the nation's largest discount
brokerage firm, and Jack White &amp; Co., where he was responsible for account
management.  His responsibilities included executing stock and option trades,
finishing market and industry information and settling client and broker trades.
He is a member of the state bar associations of California and Maryland and a
member of the American Bar Association.</P>
