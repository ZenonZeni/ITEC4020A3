<p><font size=+1>

From hammerheads, white tips, and the occasional whale shark, to huges schools of jacks, tuna, pods of dolphins, groups of rays, and turtles, Cocos offers a plethora of opportunities to experience  some of the world's most exciting marine life in their native habitat.
