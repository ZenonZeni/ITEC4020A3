Toronto, Ontario, May 28, 1996 ... TVX Gold Inc. announced today that the legal strike at its 60% owned and operated Casa Berardi mine which commenced April 19, 1996, has been settled effective immediately. With the assistance of a mutually agreed conciliator, management and the Casa Berardi Syndicat des Metallos d'Amerique Union members agreed last night to a 3 year contract, effective retroactively from March 7, 1996. The overall cost of the contract averages 4.5% per year. The Casa Berardi mine has been under the care and maintenance of salaried staff since the inception of the strike, and is expected to return to normal operations today.
