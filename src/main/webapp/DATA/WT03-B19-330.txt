<H3>Academic Staff</H3><P>

<P>The Mathematical Modelling of Physical and Chemical Systems (MMPCS) 
group consists of four faculty members of the School of Mathematical 
and Physical Sciences in the Faculty of Science at the Northern 
Territory University and a number of Research Students and 
Post-doctoral Fellows.
