<H3>Some SPOT applications </H3>
<HR>
<UL>
<LI>Mapping, at scales 1:100,000 to 1:25,000; urban mapping. 
<LI>Landcover monitoring. 
<LI>Agricultural yield estimates. 
<LI>Supplementing census data. 
<LI>Fisheries monitoring. 
<LI>Forestry operations. 
<LI>Landuse Geographic Information Systems. 
<LI>Construction planning, highlighting the best road routes, avoiding areas
too steep, too exposed, environmentally sensitive, etc. 
<LI>Environmental impact studies. 
<LI>Insurance payout estimates, following natural disasters. 
<LI>Geo-mineral exploration. 
<LI>Well placement. 
<LI>Tectonic monitoring and prediction. 
<LI>Topographic 3D images, used for: catchment management; geological interpretation;
flight simulation; radiotransmitter siting; etc. 
</UL>
