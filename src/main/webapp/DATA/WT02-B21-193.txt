<CENTER><H2>Current Issues #1</H2></CENTER>
<P>
<CENTER><H4>Total Respondents: 385</H4></CENTER><BR><HR>
<UL>
</PRE><LI><B>Did you vote in the 1996 Presidential election?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  248]  64.4% - <I>Yes</I>
[   51]  13.2% - <I>I was ineligible (not a US Citizen)</I>
[   40]  10.4% - <I>I was ineligible (too young)</I>
[   31]   8.1% - <I>No</I>
[    7]   1.8% - <I>I was ineligible (not registered)</I>
[    5]   1.3% - <I>No Answer</I>
[    3]   0.8% - <I>I was ineligible (convicted felon)</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Generally speaking, do you feel your vote is important?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  286]  74.3% - <I>Yes</I>
[   82]  21.3% - <I>No</I>
[   17]   4.4% - <I>No Answer</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Do you feel the US has a responsibility to provide humanitarian aid to other nations in need when possible?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  195]  50.6% - <I>Yes</I>
[  111]  28.8% - <I>No</I>
[   64]  16.6% - <I>Only if there is an economic interest for us involved</I>
[   15]   3.9% - <I>No Answer</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Do you feel the US has a responsibility to provide military aid to other nations in need when possible?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  146]  37.9% - <I>No</I>
[  109]  28.3% - <I>Yes</I>
[  105]  27.3% - <I>Only if there is an economic interest for us involved</I>
[   25]   6.5% - <I>No Answer</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Which nation/area do you feel represents the biggest threat to the US?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  110]  28.6% - <I>International terrorists</I>
[   91]  23.6% - <I>Local terrorists/extremists/militia</I>
[   88]  22.9% - <I>China</I>
[   40]  10.4% - <I>Other</I>
[   29]   7.5% - <I>Iran</I>
[   13]   3.4% - <I>No Answer</I>
[   11]   2.9% - <I>Korea</I>
[    3]   0.8% - <I>Cuba</I>
</PRE><LI><B>How do you feel about same-sex marriages?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  211]  54.8% - <I>They should be allowed</I>
[  144]  37.4% - <I>They should not be allowed</I>
[   30]   7.8% - <I>No Answer</I>
</PRE><LI><B>To deal with drug problems, what would you suggest?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  108]  28.1% - <I>Legalization & government regulation of some drugs</I>
[   90]  23.4% - <I>Harsher penalties; more compliance with zero tolerance ideals</I>
[   57]  14.8% - <I>Legalization & government regulation of all drugs</I>
[   52]  13.5% - <I>More emphasis on programs addressing poverty, unemployment & social decay</I>
[   43]  11.2% - <I>More emphasis on education</I>
[   16]   4.2% - <I>More religious influence throughout society</I>
[   13]   3.4% - <I>More emphasis on treatment programs</I>
[    6]   1.6% - <I>No Answer</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Are you Pro-Life or Pro-Choice?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  255]  66.2% - <I>Pro-Choice</I>
[  107]  27.8% - <I>Pro-Life</I>
[   23]   6.0% - <I>No Answer</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Generally speaking, how do you feel about affirmative action programs?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  148]  38.4% - <I>They should be abolished</I>
[   86]  22.3% - <I>They should be promptly phased out</I>
[   63]  16.4% - <I>They should be maintained for the time being</I>
[   60]  15.6% - <I>They should be maintained until minorities are elevated to equal stature in employment</I>
[   28]   7.3% - <I>No Answer</I>
</PRE><LI><B>How do you feel about US Immigration policies?</B><BR>
<PRE>[  216]  56.1% - <I>The US should be more selective in determining who can immigrate</I>
[   59]  15.3% - <I>Our borders should be open to all</I>
[   40]  10.4% - <I>Immigration should be easier to obtain</I>
[   24]   6.2% - <I>Our borders should be completely closed to all immigration</I>
[   21]   5.5% - <I>No Answer</I>
[   17]   4.4% - <I>Immigration should be phased out</I>
[    8]   2.1% - <I>We should reject immigrants from third world countries</I>
</PRE><LI><B>Of the items listed, what would you most want to see happen (to society) in your lifetime?</B><BR>
<PRE>[   68]  17.7% - <I>A cure for cancer</I>
[   57]  14.8% - <I>Invention of cold fusion/a clean power source</I>
[   54]  14.0% - <I>A united planet Earth</I>
[   47]  12.2% - <I>A cure for AIDS</I>
[   39]  10.1% - <I>Regular space travel/expansion into the Universe</I>
[   34]   8.8% - <I>An end to world hunger</I>
[   24]   6.2% - <I>Global acceptance of my religion</I>
[   24]   6.2% - <I>Contact with extraterrestrials</I>
[   17]   4.4% - <I>Elimination of all nuclear weapons</I>
[   14]   3.6% - <I>No Answer</I>
[    7]   1.8% - <I>Movement to a less industrial economic base (such as Agrarian)</I>
</UL><P>
