<I>Exceptional Children</I> is published 4 times per year (Fall, Winter, Spring, and
Summer) by The Council for Exceptional Children (CEC), 1920 Association Drive,
Reston, Virginia 20191-1589.   All members receive <I>Exceptional Children</I> as part
of their membership package.<P>

Subscriptions to <I>Exceptional Children</I> are available without membership in CEC.
<P>

U.S., $58 per year<br>
Foreign-Surface, $66 per year<br>
Foreign-Air Printed Matter, $95 per year<br>
Single copy price is $10. <p>
