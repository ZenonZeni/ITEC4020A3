<h2>What Is Video for Windows&#153; (VFW)</h2>
<p>Video for Windows is a software product that installs onto your system as MediaPlayer. Audio Video Interleaved (<strong>AVI</strong>) 
digital video technology is the multimedia file format used by Microsoft Video for Windows.</p>
<h2>What Is Apple QuickTime&#153;</h2>
<p>QuickTime is a software product that installs onto your system as MoviePlayer. Movie (<strong>MOV</strong>) digital video technology is the 
multimedia file format used by Apple's QuickTime for Macintosh and Apple's QuickTime for Windows. </p>
