Welcome to Marine Watch, the international news journal about events occuring on, under and over the oceans of the planet. Printed and mailed quarterly from Point Reyes Station, California, USA, Marine Watch is designed to serve an informed readership in the worldwide ocean community. This internet edition contains material from current and recent editions (some of it abridged) and is designed to provide a sampling of the printed edition as well as provide a way for the world-wide ocean community to contact us. For more about Marine Watch's raison d'&ecirc;tre, please see our editorial from the premier issue, published in the Fall, 1995<BR>
