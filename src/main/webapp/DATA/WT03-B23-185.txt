THOMAS O. MIX, JR., received his law degree from Gonzaga University School
of Law. Mr. Mix is a graduate of Kemper Military College, studied for a year

<P>
DARYL A. RODRIGUES received his law degree from Gonzaga University School
of Law. Mr. Rodrigues is a graduate of Seattle University and attended Stichting
