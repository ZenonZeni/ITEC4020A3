<CENTER><B>KASSANDRA MINES</B></CENTER>

<P>Toronto, Ontario, October 31, 1995 ... TVX Gold Inc. is pleased to announce that the following statement with respect to the advancement of the completion of the purchase of the Kassandra Mines assets by TVX Gold was issued late yesterday by the Ministry of Industry, Energy and Technology of the Government of Greece.
<CENTER>
"MINISTRY OF INDUSTRY, ENERGY & TECHNOLOGY<BR>

PRESS ROOM<BR>

ATHENS, 30.10.1995<BR>

PRESS RELEASE</CENTER>

<P>The Ministry of Industry, in co-operation with all co-responsible Ministries, is progressing with an accelerated pace all matters related with the agreement for the sale of the Kassandra Mines to the Canadian company, TVX Gold Inc. Within this framework, the negotiations are focused on pending issues such as fixing the grant with which the investment will be subsidized for the establishment of the gold plant, as well as the issuing of all necessary permits for the location, establishment and operation of the gold plant.

<P>The promotion of the investment will open the horizons for the development of the Kassandra Mines, will be a source of employment for Chalkidiki and will be a benefit for the national economy."
