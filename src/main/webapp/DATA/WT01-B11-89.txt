<h3><u>ICSI at the GOAL Program:</u></h3>
In-Vitro Fertilization with ICSI (intracytoplasmic sperm injection) for severe male infertility started Aug 1995.<br>
As of December 1996 we have 43 pregnancies out of 119 embryo transfers (36%) and 124 egg retrieval procedures (35%).
<br>
MESA and TESA are being successfully performed at the GOAL program.
