<BR><hr>
<B><CENTER>GOVERNMENT NEWSPAPERS</CENTER></B><P>

<B>National Newspapers</B><BR>
1. Addis Zemen--Amharic daily. Circulation 18,000.<BR>
2. Ethiopian Herald--English daily. Circulation 8,700.<BR>
3. Berissa--Oromigna daily. Circulation 2,500.<BR>
4. Al Alem--Arabic weekly. Circulation 2,000.<P>

<B>REGIONAL NEWSPAPERS AND MAGAZINES</B><BR>
1. Addis Lisan--Amharic weekly, Region 14 Information and Culture Bureau. Circulation 5,000­10,000.<BR>
2. Addis--Amharic quarterly magazine, Region 14 Information and Culture Bureau. Circulation 5,000.<BR>
3. Bekur--Amharic biweekly, Amhara Region Information and Culture Bureau. Circulation 5,000.<BR>
4. Kalacha Oromiya--Oromigna biweekly, Oromiya Information and Culture Bureau. Circulation 5,000.<BR>
5. Ye Debub Negat--Amharic monthly, Southern Ethiopia People&#146;s Information and Culture Bureau. Circulation N/A.<P>

<B>Ethiopian People&#146;s Revolutionary Democratic Front (eprdf) 
Coalition Party Newspapers </B><BR>
1. Oromiya--Oromigna biweekly, Oromo People&#146;s Democratic Organization (OPDO). Circulation 19,000.<BR>
2. Abiotawi Democracy--Amharic weekly, EPRDF. Circulation 18,500.<BR>
3. Yedebub Hizboch Dimts--Amharic biweekly, Southern Ethiopia People&#146;s Democratic Front (SEPDF). Circulation 15,000­16,000.<BR>
4. Mahtot--Amharic Weekly, Amhara National Democratic Movement (ANDM). Circulation 13,000.<BR>
5. Weyin--Tigrigna weekly, Tigrai People&#146;s Liberation Front (TPLF). Circulation 13,000.<BR>
6. Sojat--Amharic monthly. Silte People&#146;s Democratic Party. Circulation 5,000.<BR>
7. Harari Tebe--Harari monthly. Harari National League (HLN). Circulation 2,000.<P>
