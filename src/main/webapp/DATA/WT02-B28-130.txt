<FONT SIZE=+1><STRONG>Investing for Your Future</STRONG></FONT><P>


The sooner you start investing, the more  you can set aside and the longer your
money
can work for you.  While past performance is no guarantee of future results, in the
past, those
who have
stayed invested for 20 years or more have generally earned positive total returns.<P>
	People who wait to invest may be missing opportunities.  No one can say where the
markets will
move next.  If you're looking for the "perfect moment," you may never know when it
comes. 
The wise
strategy is to invest today and adjust your holdings over time.<P>
