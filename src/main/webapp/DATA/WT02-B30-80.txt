<STRONG><A NAME="objective">Investment Objective</A></FONT></STRONG>
<BR>
Seeks a high level of current income, consistent with safety of principal.
<P>
<BR>

<STRONG><A NAME="f_profile">Fund Profile</A></FONT></STRONG>
<BR>

<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>Except during temporary defensive periods, not less than 65% of the value of the
Fund's net
assets will be invested in U.S. government securities.

	<LI>To enhance the Fund's income potential, the managers invest in a wide range of U.S.
government securities - from Treasury bills, notes, and bonds to higher yielding Ginnie Mae,
Fannie
Mae, and Freddie Mac certificates.

	<LI>With diversification by maturity, coupon, and issuer, the Fund seeks to provide a
yield and
stability advantage over individual investments in government securities.

	<LI>Experienced professionals monitor the markets and adjust the portfolio daily,
seeking current
income consistent with safety of principal.
</UL>
<P>
<BR>


<STRONG><A NAME="i_profile">Investor Profile</A></FONT></STRONG>
<BR>

<UL TYPE=DISC>
	<LI>Conservative investors seeking high current income
	<LI>Income investors who prefer the strength of underlying investments issued or guaranteed by
the U.S. government<SUP><FONT SIZE="-1"><A HREF="#1">1</A></FONT></SUP>
	<LI>Equity or growth and income investors seeking  a conservative income component for their
portfolios.
</UL>

<P>Before you invest...
<BR>Shares of the fund are not guaranteed. Like other bond funds, your investment return and
principal value will fluctuate with interest rates and market conditions.
