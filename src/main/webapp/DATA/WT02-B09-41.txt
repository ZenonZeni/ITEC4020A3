<H2 align=center>Discover and Use More of Your Potential!</H2>



Rutherford Publishing, Inc.'s goal is to produce newsletters that help individuals and organizations discover how to take positive actions in key areas of their lives and to encourage people to use more of their potential.<P>



Rutherford Publishing, Inc. produces five newsletters each month. These newsletters include: Total Wellness®, The Leadership Advantage, The Slight Edge™, Tyme Management™, and Connections™.



<ul>



<LI><A HREF="rback.html">Background on Rutherford Publishing, Inc.</A><P>



<LI><A HREF="totalwell.html">Total Wellness®</A>, the first newsletter in North America to incorporate the Total Person Concept™ motivates individuals to set personal goals and take action in all of the key areas of life: family and home, physical and health, mental and educational, financial and career, spiritual and ethical, and social and cultural.<P>



<LI><A HREF="lacurrnt.html"> The Leadership Advantage</A>, a monthly, eight-page newsletter, provides executives, managers, supervisors, and other professionals with suggestions for ongoing leadership development. <P>



<LI><A HREF="securrnt.html"> The Slight Edge™</A>, a monthly, eight-page newsletter, provides executives, middle managers, supervisors, professional business owners, and sales associates with tips and suggestions for ongoing personal development.<P>



<LI><A HREF="tmcurrnt.html"> Tyme Management™</A>, a monthly, four-page newsletter, provides executives, managers, supervisors, secretaries, assistants, owners, sales associates, and other people in the workforce with tips and suggestions on improving time management.<P>



<LI><A HREF="cocurrnt.html"> Connections™</A>, a monthly, eight-page newsletter, provides individuals with helpful tips, articles, self-help tests, and checklists that will enrich people's lives, increase their well-being, provide encouragement for living, and help improve their relationships.<P>



</ul>
