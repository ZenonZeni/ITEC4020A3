<center>
<h1>Convention Center</h1>
</center>

<a href=images/convention_center.jpg>
<img align=left src=images/convention_center.gif></a>
The Sacramento Convention Center expansion was completed in 1996 at an expense
of $85 million.  The design was by Vitiello Associates, Inc. (Sacramento) in
association with Loschky Marquardt Nesholm (Seattle).
<br clear=all>

<center>
<h3>Outstanding Architectural Features</h3>
</center>

<a href=images/convention_center2.jpg>
<img align=right src=images/convention_center2.gif></a>
<ul>
<li>Fish-eyed, 160' X 32' windows dominate east end.
<li>Side wall features row of arched windows inlaid with granite and marble.
<li>Each main entrance lit by blue saber lights that extend 50 feet in the air.
<li>Upper-level terraces dotted with trees and vines grown on custom trellises.
</ul>
<br clear=all>

<center>
<h3>Facilities</h3>
</center>

The Convention Center offers 384,359 square feet of space that can be used
separately or jointly for events of any kind:

<ul>
<li><a href=convention_center_activity.html>Activity Building</a><p>
<li><a href=convention_center_ballroom.html>Ballroom</a><p>
24,500 square feet. Seats 2,500 theater style/1,800 banquets<p>
<li><a href=convention_center_exhibit_hall.html>Exhibit Hall</a><p>
136,945 square feet. Seats 6,500 theater style on telescopic risers<p>
<li><a href=convention_center_theatre.html>Theatre</a><p>
Seats 2,452<p>
</ul>

<h3>Demographics</h3>

<ul>
<p><li>In-house Catering Service: Classique Catering
<p><li>Art: $1 million in public art
<p><li>2,000 hotel rooms downtown, 10,000 hotel rooms within minutes by shuttle
<p><li>Only 15 minutes distance from airport
</ul>
