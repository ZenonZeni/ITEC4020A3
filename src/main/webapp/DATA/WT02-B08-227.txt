<p> <h2>Academic Life</h2>
</CENTER>
<UL>
<UL>
<LI> I graduated from the <A HREF="http://www.ph.surrey.ac.uk/">University of Surrey Physics Department</A> with an upper second class honours degree in Physics & Optoelectronics in 1993. 
The course included an industrial placement year (Ocean Modelling) with the Institute of Oceanographic Sciences.
 My final project was on computer generated holograms for I which I gained a prize for the best project.
<LI>I now work as a research student for the <A HREF="http://www.meto.govt.uk/sec5/sec5pg1.html">Hadley Centre for Climate Prediction & Research</A> (<A HREF="http://www.meto.govt.uk/">UK Met. Office</A>)
<LI>  I'm in my final  year (eek!) of my PhD
<LI>  I'm based at the<A HREF="http://www.spri.cam.ac.uk/"> Scott Polar 
Research Institute</A> and <A HREF="http://www.trinhall.cam.ac.uk/">Trinity Hall</A>, <A HREF="http://www.cam.ac.uk/">University of Cambridge</A>
<LI> My research topic is "Modelling the Sea Ice Thickness Distribution in the <A HREF="http://megasat.emi.dtu.dk/images/aniser96.mpg">Greenland Sea</A>".
</UL>
</UL>
