<br><strong>Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy</strong>

<p>Since 1963, research sponsored by the Department of Energy have 
resulted in more R&D awards than any other single organization in the 
world, winning more awards than the rest of the federal government 
combined.  The Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy alone has
received 51 awards since 1980.
