<H2>About Primecare</H2>

At Prime Health, we are determined to provide private medical insurance which gives you truly comprehensive cover at a budget price. Too many similar-looking policies miss crucial elements of cover which may be vital to your wellbeing. With Primecare, however, we have included:

<ul>

<LI> full cover for eligible in-patient treatment and day-care surgery

<LI> full cover for out-patient consultations and tests

<LI> home nursing (up to 13 weeks per year)

<LI> out-patient physiotherapy (up to &#163;1000 per year)

<LI> alternative medicine (up to &#163;1000 per year)

<LI> overnight accommodation for a parent with a sick child (up to age 14)

<LI> cash benefit - for in patient treatment under the NHS

<LI> Helpline service

<LI> Tax relief for customers aged 60 or over <I>(Parent accommodation, alternative medicine and NHS cash benefits do not apply.)</I>

<LI> No overall maximum claim limit

</ul>

<p>

Primecare premiums start at an exceptionally low price of &#163;4.89 a week with &#163;100 excess, which is the approximate cost of a plan with restricted cover. Your Primecare cover starts from the moment you call us. And to give you even better value, we'll give you an immediate no-claims discount of 25%. This rises to a maximum 50% discount after 5 consecutive years without a claim.
