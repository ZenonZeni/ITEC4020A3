<CENTER><B>Now Available : <br>
Information Paper on Gasoline Quality &amp; the Classic Auto<BR>
</B></CENTER>
<P>
 Downstream Alternatives Inc. (DAI) has announced the release
of a new information paper entitled &quot;Changes in Gasoline
&amp; The Classic Auto.&quot;  The eight paper information paper
covers topics such as fuel oxygenates, octane, fuel volatility,
materials compatibility, gasoline additives, and storage.  These
topics are related specifically to areas of interest to the classic/vintage
vehicle owner.<BR>

<P>
 The paper was written by DAI President Robert E. Reynolds.  Mr.
Reynolds is the principle author of the widely circulated &quot;Changes
in Gasoline&quot; manual series which boasts a circulation exceeding
a half-million copies.  Mr. Reynolds indicated, &quot;With the
recent release of our latest manual &quot;Changes in Gasoline
III&quot; and more changes occurring to gasoline, we have received
a lot of information requests from classic auto owners and classic
car clubs.  This information paper addresses the most frequently
asked questions we receive.&quot;<BR>
