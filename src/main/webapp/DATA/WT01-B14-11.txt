<BR>
Are you getting married in the morning? Or is there any other pending
event - the coming of age of a minor on a custodial account, the
addition of a companion as a joint tenant, the death of a joint tenant -
that will affect the ownership of your mutual fund account?

<BR><BR>

Any change in the ownership of your Kemper Funds account requires transferring the
shares to a new Kemper account. And, for security reasons, not all
privileges from your original account can be transferred. However, you
can easily establish privileges on your new account by completing a
Kemper Account Privileges Form, which can be requested from Shareholder
Services.

<BR><BR>

While visiting Kemper.com, you can go to <A HREF="forms.html">
Printable Forms</A> and print one or more forms that enable you to 
establish individual privileges. Just complete, sign and return 
to Kemper at the address shown on the form.
