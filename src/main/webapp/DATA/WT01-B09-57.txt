<h3>Mid Atlantic Alternative Vehicle Consortium</h3><p>

<blockquote>

<B>Project Title: All Electric Combat Vehicle Study<p>

Performing Organization: Hybrid Electric M113 Demonstrator <p></B>

Technical POC: Alan Loss<br>

Phone: (408) 289-3972 <br>

Fax:  (408) 289-3597 <p>

<B>Project Summary:</B><p>

The goal of this project is to upgrade the existing electric drive M113 personnel
carrier to improve performance and space efficiency. Activities include: design and fabrication of an
advanced lead acid batterybased energy storage system, a 55 kW Auxiliary Power Unit (APU), a
distributed networkbased vehicle power management system, installation of these components and a
low noise band track into the existing electric drive M 113 and support to subsequent testing and
vehicle demonstrations.<p>

<B>Project Deliverables:</B> <p> A running vehicle, demonstrations, final report  <p>

<B>Project Schedule:</B> <p><ul><li> The project was initiated in June 1995 and will complete vehicle assembly in
December 1996.</ul>
