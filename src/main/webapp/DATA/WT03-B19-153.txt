<H1>Victoria's key industrial strengths</H1>
<P>

<I><B>Victoria offers internationally competitive advantages for companies in
established and newly emerging services and manufacturing sectors. </I></B>
<P>
<B><I>The State's strong export growth shows that Victoria is the place for 
companies to achieve sustained growth and profitability. </B></I>
<P>
The Victorian Government has identified a range of key industry sectors in which
 it is concentrating its efforts to generate the greatest rate of sustainable 
economic growth for Victoria and at the same time enhancing the State's international competitiveness.
<P>
The key industry sectors selected have the following characteristics:
 <UL>  
    <LI> They enjoy a growing share of world trade and form a significant part of
 Victoria's industrial, educational and research base. These sectors are:
<I>
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="visaerospace.html">Aerospace</A>
<LI><A HREF="visaviation.html">Aviation</A>
<LI><A HREF="vischemicals.html">Chemicals, plastics and rubber</A>
<LI><A HREF="viseducation.html">Education services</A>
<LI><A HREF="visinfotech.html">Information technology and communications</A>
<LI><A HREF="vispharma.html">Pharmaceuticals</A>
<LI><A HREF="visscientific.html">Scientific and medical equipment</A>
<LI><A HREF="vistourism.html">Tourism</A>
<LI><A HREF="viswaste.html">Waste management and recycling</A>
</I>
</UL>       <P>
<LI>Each sector adds value to Victoria's existing natural resource advantages
. These sectors are:
<I><BR>
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="visfood.html">Food processing</A>
<LI><A HREF="visforest.html">Forest products</A>
<LI><A HREF="vistextiles.html">Textiles, clothing and footwear</A>
</I>
</UL>
<P>
<LI>They are industries in which Victoria has a strong industrial base and 
which have strong linkages with sectors in the above two categories. 
<BR>
Examples of
these sectors are:
<I>
<UL>
<LI><A HREF="visauto.html">Automotive</A>
<LI><A HREF="visengineer.html">Engineering</A>
<LI><A HREF="vispackage.html">Packaging</A>
<LI><A HREF="vistransport.html">Transport and storage</A>

</I>
</UL>
<P><BR><P>
In aggregate, the key sectors account for about 30% of Victoria's economic output
. The 11 key sectors within manufacturing have combined sales of $35 billion and
employ 80% of Victoria's total manufacturing workforce. Education services and 
tourism exports are worth $2 billion and waste management sales are around $200 
million.
