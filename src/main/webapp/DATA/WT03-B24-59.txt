<H1>Degree Requirements.</H1>
<P>
<HR>
<P>
The Master of Science in Software Systems Engineering requires a minimum of 30
semester hours of graduate-level courses. The following six core courses (18
semester hours) are required:
<P>
<UL>
<DT><A HREF = "c-l-swse.html#swse619">SWSE 619</A> Software Construction
<DT><A HREF = "c-l-swse.html#swse620">SWSE 620</A> Software Requirements and Prototyping
<DT><A HREF = "c-l-swse.html#swse621">SWSE 621</A> Software Design
<DT><A HREF = "c-l-swse.html#swse623">SWSE 623</A> Formal Methods and Models
<DT><A HREF = "c-l-swse.html#swse625">SWSE 625</A> Software Project Management
<DT><A HREF = "c-l-swse.html#swse626">SWSE 626</A> Software Project Lab
</UL>
<P>
and either the professional track, consisting of four electives, for students
pursuing a terminal master's degree in software systems engineering, or the
research track, consisting of two  electives and a 6-semester-hour thesis, which
is primarily intended for students planning to pursue a Ph.D. degree with
emphasis on software systems engineering.
