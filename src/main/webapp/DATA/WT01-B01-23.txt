PlaNet(NZ) is a national network that is fully linked to the <br>
Internet as well as other international networks, most significantly,<br> 
the APC(Association for Progressive Communications). As an Internet<br>
Service Provider and APC Member, PlaNet(NZ) provides specific local<br>
and international forums and WWW services for non-governmental, <br>
community, educational and environmental organisations.
