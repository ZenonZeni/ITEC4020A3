<A NAME="comm">

<P><B><FONT SIZE=+1>T</FONT></B>he Board of Directors of the Northern California Independent Booksellers Association meet approximately ten times per year. Organization projects and activities are carried out by the NCIBA Executive and Associate Directors with the aid of the Board of Directors and the organization's standing committees. Current committees include:

<UL>

<li>Executive Committee
<li>Education
<li>Promotions
<li>Member Services
<li>Free Expression
<li>Internet / Website
<li>Trade Show

</UL>

<P>Booksellers or individuals in the trade interested in volunteering for one of the above committees should contact the NCIBA office.
