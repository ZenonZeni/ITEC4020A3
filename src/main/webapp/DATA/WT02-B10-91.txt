INET Corporation is a privately-held, California Corporation founded in 1978. The company was an early participant in the SBIR program and utilized this R&D in combination with a license from a major company.<P>

In 1993 INET introduced the IMS 2000, an autonomous data acquisition, process control, and portable test and measurement system  that can be used in the electric utility industry to provide equipment condition monitoring, display, alarm, and control. (1, 2)<P>

INET is a preferred supplier to TVA.<P>
