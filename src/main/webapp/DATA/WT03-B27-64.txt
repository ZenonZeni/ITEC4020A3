<p><img src="images/farms.jpg" align="bottom" width="377"

<p>This is where it all begins. The fresh, clean air of the
Andean mountains. The steady climate of Ecuador and the most
beautiful varieties of roses of Florinsa. </p>
<p><img src="images/farm1.jpg" align="bottom" border="2"
