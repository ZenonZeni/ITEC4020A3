<h3 align=center><font color="#FF0000">PANIC DISORDER</font></h3>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Panic disorder is a medical disorder that strikes suddenly and appears to be 
unprovoked.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">People with panic disorder experience unexpected and repeated episodes of intense fear 
accompanied by a set of unexplained physical symptoms.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Before getting the help they need, many people with panic disorder visit as many as 10 
physicians and undergo extensive, costly and unnecessary medical procedures.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">3 to 6 million Americans will have panic disorder in their lifetimes.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Approximately 1 million people have panic disorder in any given year.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Women are twice as likely as men to develop panic disorder.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Panic disorder typically strikes in young adulthood, with the median age of onset occurring 
at 24 years.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">According to research, 20 percent of people with panic disorder attempt suicide.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">One-third of panic disorder sufferers begin to avoid situations where they fear an attack may 
occur or where help is not available.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">About 50 percent of people with panic disorder will have an episode of clinical depression 
some time in their lives.</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">Symptoms include:</font></p>
<blockquote>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; Racing or pounding heartbeat</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; Chest pains</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; Dizziness, lightheadedness, nausea</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; Difficulty breathing</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; Tingling or numbness in the hands</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; Flushes or chills</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; Dreamlike sensations or perceptual distortions</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; Terror - a sense that something unimaginably horrible is about to occur and one 
is powerless to prevent it</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; Fear of losing control and doing something embarrassing</font></p>
<p align=left><font color="#000000">&#183; Fear of dying</font></p>
</blockquote>
