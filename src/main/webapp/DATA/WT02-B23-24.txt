Confluence Technologies designs and builds client-server based
application software for the mutual fund and trust industries.
The company's mission is to provide solutions that leverage the
tremendous power and low cost of personal computers into reduced
costs and increased productivity for its clients.
<P>
Confluence's full-service approach provides clients with installation,
data conversion, training, documentation, customer support and
custom designed extensions. The company combines its years of
financial services experience with its technical expertise in
building applications to supply the best possible solutions to
its clients.<BR>
