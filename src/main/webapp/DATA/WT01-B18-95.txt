<a name=GI><center><h2>Information</h2></center></a>
The Office of Health Economics (OHE) was founded in 1962 by the Association of the British Pharmaceutical Industry (ABPI).
The OHE has established an international reputation for the acquisition and collation of health statistics and for its booklets, briefings, monographs, and compendiums.

<P>

The OHE studies and links medical, social and economic data from government, academic and pharmaceutical industry sources, and carries out a limited programme of original research. Its work is reviewed by an editorial board and advice and assistance is given by a panel of honorary advisors and many anonymous specialists who act as ad hoc referees on particular topics.
