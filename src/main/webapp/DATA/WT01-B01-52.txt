<td valign=top><size=2>If you have information about a journalist or news organization being threatened, 
harassed or attacked, contact CPJ immediately.</tr><BR>
<tr><td width=30% valign=top><font size=2><STRONG>Support Our Campaigns</STRONG>

<td valign=top><size=2>Write a letter in support of journalists who are in trouble and stay on top of press 
freedom problems by subscribing to CPJ's quarterly newsletter.</tr><BR>
<tr><td width=30% valign=top><font size=2><STRONG>Become a Member</STRONG><br>
Individuals from $35<BR>
Companies from $1,000<BR>
<BR>
<td valign=top><size=2>
Show your support for your colleagues and stay informed about press conditions by 
becoming a member of CPJ. Gift memberships are also available

<tr><td width=30% valign=top><font size=2><STRONG>Attend the International Press Freedom Awards Dinner</STRONG><br>
November 1996 New York City<BR>
<td valign=top><size=2>
The International Press Freedom Awards Dinner honors the struggle of journalists who 
risk their lives to report the news. It is a major gathering of journalists and media 
companies, and it raises more than half of CPJ's operating funds. Show your support 
for freedom of the press by participating in this important event.</tr><BR>
</tr><BR>
<tr><td width=30% valign=top><font size=2><STRONG>Buy CPJ's Publications</STRONG><br>
Members receive a 50 percent discount<BR>
<td valign=top><size=2>
CPJ's reports on press conditions around the world shed light on emerging political 
developments. They are essential reading for reporters and anyone interested in freedom 
of expression or human rights.</tr><BR>
<tr><td width=30% valign=top><font size=2><STRONG>News Coverage and Letters to the Editor</STRONG>
<td valign=top><size=2>
Coverage of the problems faced by independent journalists in developing countries 
is the best way of furthering the cause of press freedom worldwide. CPJ urges reporters and news organizations to cover stories about attacks on the press. CPJ encourages everyone else to write 
to their local news organization, expressing concern for the safety and freedom of journalists 
around the world.</tr>
