<B>TITEL/TITLE </B><BR>

V&auml;rdering av lokala luftf&ouml;roreningseffekter. Hur v&auml;rdera bilavgasernas h&auml;lsoeffekter i t&auml;torter?
Valuation of the local effects of air pollution. How to place monetary values on health effects of exhast emissions in urban areas.<BR>
<BR>

<B>PUBLICERINGSDATUM/DATE OF PUBLISHING </B><BR>

April 1995<BR>
<BR>
<BR>
<!-- Horizontal Line Tag --><HR>
<BR>
<BR>


<B>UTGIVARE/PUBLISHER</B><BR>

KFBs DNR 93-224-23<BR>

KFB - Kommunikationsforskningsberedningen, Stockholm<BR>
<BR>


<B>REFERAT (Syfte, Metod, Resultat)</B><BR>

<B>Syfte: </B>Projektets syfte &auml;r att utveckla en metod f&ouml;r ekonomisk v&auml;rdering av lokala skadeeffekter, s&auml;rskilt h&auml;lsoeffekter, av bilavgaser i t&auml;tortsmilj&ouml;er. V&auml;rdet uttrycks i kronor per kg avgaskomponent och beror av utsl&auml;ppets l&auml;ge, tidpunkt, av v&auml;dersituationen etc.<BR>




<P><B>Metod:</B> Hela kedjan fr&aring;n emission till skadev&auml;rdering ing&aring;r: Emission-spridning-exponering-dos-ekomisk v&auml;rdering. Vid dosber&auml;kning har den lokala spridningsmodellen f&ouml;r G&ouml;teborgsregionen till&auml;mpats. Ber&auml;knade halter har kopplats till befolkningsdata f&ouml;r att skatta stadsdosen. H&auml;nsyn har &auml;ven tagits till lokala haltf&ouml;rh&ouml;jningar i gatumilj&ouml;n (ger gaturumsdosen). De ekonomiska v&auml;rderingarna av s&auml;nkta luftf&ouml;roreningshalter utg&aring;r fr&aring;n betalningsviljor som framkommit i andra sammanhang. 
</P>




<P><B>Resultat: </B>Ett ekonomiskt v&auml;rde har &aring;satts varje inandat mg av de olika bilavgaskomponenterna : kv&auml;veoxider, kolv&auml;ten (VOC) och partiklar. Denna dosv&auml;rdering skall kunna anv&auml;ndas i alla svenska t&auml;torter. I en storstad visar sig ett genomsnittligt utsl&auml;pp leda till en inandad dos som till 2/3 &auml;r &#148;stadsdos&#148; och till 1/3 &auml;r &#148;gaturumsdos&#148;. I mindre st&auml;der st&aring;r gaturumsdosen f&ouml;r en l&auml;gre andel. Ett utsl&auml;pp i centrala G&ouml;teborg leder till en tiopotens h&ouml;gre dos &auml;n genomsnittsutsl&auml;ppet i staden. F&ouml;rslag ges dessutom till en separat ekonomisk v&auml;rdering av utsl&auml;ppen med h&auml;nsyn till naturskadorna (40-45 kr/kg kv&auml;veoxider och ca 20 kr/kg VOC oberoende av exakt l&auml;ge av utsl&auml;ppet).</P>

<BR>


<B>ABSTRACT (Aim, Method, Results)</B>

<BR>
<B>The aim</B> of the project is to develop a method for monetary valuation of local health effects of exhaust emissions in urban areas. The value will be expressed in SEK per kg of emission component. It is dependent on the location and time of the emission, the weather conditions etc.<BR>




<P><B>Method:</B> The following chain has been studied: emission-dispersion-exposure-dose-economic valuation. For the dose calculations was used the local dispersion model that has been developed for Gothenburg. The calculated concentrations were coupled to population density data to get the &#148;urban dose&#148;. Local excess concentrations in the streets have also been considered and will give the &#148;street dose&#148;. The valuations of health effects have used already available WTP data (&#148;willingness to pay&#148;). 
</P>




<P><B>Results:</B> A monetary value has been given to each inhaled mg of different exhaust emission components: nitrogen oxides, hydrocarbons (VOC) and particulate matter. The derived dose valuation can be used in all Swedish towns and cities. It is shown that an average car emission in a Swedish big city will lead to a total inhaled dose that is composed of urban dose (2/3) and street dose (1/3). In a smaller town the street dose amounts to a smaller share of the total dose. An emission in central Gothenburg will lead to an approx ten times higher dose than the average car emission in Gothenburg. Moreover, the report suggests a separate monetary value of the emissions with respect to effects on nature (on forests, lakes etc): 40-45 SEK per kg of nitrogen oxides and approx. 20 SEK per kg of VOC.</P>





<P><B>Key words:</B> air pollution; automobile exhaust emissions; dispersion model; environmental economics; health effects; dose calculation; monetary valuation; population dose; urban areas
</P>

<B>TITEL/TITLE </B><BR>

Biomassebaserade energib&auml;rare f&ouml;r transportsektorn
Biomass-based energy carriers in the transportation sector<BR>
<BR>

<B>PUBLICERINGSDATUM/DATE OF PUBLISHING </B><BR>

Mars 1995<BR>
<BR>

<B>UTGIVARE/PUBLISHER</B><BR>


KFBs DNR 93-146-23<BR>

KFB - Kommunikationsforskningsberedningen, Stockholm<BR>
<BR>


<B>REFERAT (Syfte, Metod, Resultat)</B><BR>

Syftet med rapporten &auml;r att studera de tekniska och ekonomiska f&ouml;ruts&auml;ttningarna f&ouml;r att genom anv&auml;ndning av olika biomassebaserade energib&auml;rare inom transportsektorn erh&aring;lla minskade utsl&auml;pp av koldioxid (CO2) och att &ouml;versiktligt studera inverkan p&aring; andra milj&ouml;faktorer av en &ouml;kad anv&auml;ndning av biomassebaserade energib&auml;rare.<BR>




<P>Reduktionen av CO2 -emissioner per hektar jord- och skogsmark utnyttjad f&ouml;r biomasseproduktion 
(kg CO2 /ha, &aring;r) samt kostnad f&ouml;r CO2 -reduktion (SEK/kg CO2) ber&auml;knas f&ouml;r ers&auml;ttning av bensin och diesel med rapsmetylester, biogas fr&aring;n lusern, etanol fr&aring;n vete samt etanol, metanol, v&auml;tgas och elektricitet producerad fr&aring;n Salix och avverkningsrester. 
</P>




<P>Av studerade drivmedel ger de som baseras p&aring; Salix st&ouml;rst CO2-reduktion per hektar. Anv&auml;ndning av metanol fr&aring;n Salix och avverkningsrester tycks p&aring; medell&aring;ng sikt ge l&auml;gst kostnader f&ouml;r CO2-reduktion. Anv&auml;ndning av br&auml;nslecellsfordon, i vilka metanol eller v&auml;tgas utnyttjas som energib&auml;rare, kan p&aring; l&auml;ngre sikt komma att m&ouml;jligg&ouml;ra ett mer energieffektivt utnyttjande av biomassa &auml;n dagens fordon med f&ouml;rbr&auml;nningsmotorer.</P>

<BR>


<B>ABSTRACT (Aim, Method, Results)</B><BR>

The purpose of the report is to study the technical and economic prerequisites to attain reduced carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions through the use of biomass-based energy carriers in the transportation sector, and to study other environmental impacts resulting from an increased use of biomass-based energy carriers.<BR>




<P>CO2 emission reduction per unit arable and forest land used for biomass production (kg CO2/ha, year), and costs for CO2 emission reduction (SEK/kg CO2) are estimated for the substitution of petrol and diesel with rape methyl ester, biogas from lucern , ethanol from wheat and ethanol, methanol, hydrogen and electricity from Salix and logging residues.</P>





<P>Of the studied energy carriers, those based on Salix provide the largest CO2 emission reduction. In a medium–longperspective, the costs for CO2 emission reduction seem to be lowest for methanol from Salix and logging residues. The use of fuel-cell vehicles, using methanol or hydrogen as energy carriers, can in a longer perspective provide more energy efficient utilization of biomass for transportation than the use of internal combustion engine vehicles.

</P>

<B>TITEL/TITLE </B><BR>

Sustainable Mobility in Europe and the Role of the Automobile. A Critical Inquiry<BR>
<BR>


<B>PUBLICERINGSDATUM/DATE OF PUBLISHING </B><BR>

August 1995<BR>
<BR>

<B>UTGIVARE/PUBLISHER</B><BR>


<B>KFBs DNR</B> 93-84-63<BR>

KFB – Kommunikationsforsknings-beredningen, Stockholm<BR>
<BR>



<B>ABSTRACT (Aim, Method, Results)</B><BR>

<B>
The aim</B> of the inquiry is to study the current responses in Europe to the problems of road transport, particularly the problems of increasing car use. The basic point of departure is the new Common Transport Policy (CTP) of the EU as of December 1992. The catchword of this document, &#148;sustainable mobility&#148;, has also inspired the title of the report.<BR>




<P><B>Methods:</B> Relevant actors, that is, organizations of various types and at all levels wich play a role in influencing the transport policy in Europe, are identified. Written documents containing strategies for the transformation of existing transport systems are interpreted and subjected to critical analysis focusing on the future role of the automobile in the transport systems.</P>





<P><B>The findings</B> may be summarized in the folloving way: The present situation of road transport in Europe is a result of interaction failure rather than of market failure or government failure. The reason for this is lack of agreement in the perceptions of problems, goals, measures and their implementation. A way out of this dilemma is indicated; intensified controversies about the preceptions could create opportunities for new forms of interaction.


</P>
