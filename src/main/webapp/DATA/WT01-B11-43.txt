<PRE>
A search through our chapter library's set of Contact! magazines reveals the
following articles of interest to those looking into Mazda Rotary engines for
aircraft use.

Issue 1:  "Alternative Power for Experimental Aircraft" Chris Ross.  Includes 
an exploded view of a Ross planetary gear PSRU as well as info on a Mazda 
BD-4, a Mazda Lancair, a Mazda T-18, and reference (in a table of Ross 
installations) to 3 Lancairs, 1 Long EZ, 1 RV-4, 1 Cozy, and 2 Velocitys.  
It also includes 1/5th scale drawings of a 13B with a ross redrive on it.

Issue 1:  "Flying a Mazda Rotary"  Neil A. Kruiswyk.  Mazda Lancair 325.

Issue 5:  "Mazda Rotary Powered Super Cuby" Medford A. Bony.  Mazda 12A with 
a belt PSRU in a Super Cuby

Issue 23:  "Converting the Mazda RX-7 Rotary Powerplant"  Jim Mayfield.  13B
with Ross PSRU on a 2 place gyrocopter.

Issue 25:  "Mazda 13B Powerplant Installation in a Long EZ"  Ron Gowan.

Issue 25:  "More on Mazda Rotarys"  Ed.  Lists several other known Mazda
conversions.
