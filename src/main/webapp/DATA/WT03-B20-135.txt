<H1>Company profile</H1>
<HR SIZE=1 NOSHADE>

<IMG ALIGN=RIGHT SRC="building.gif" alt=RSSL's Headquarters align=bottom>


<P>RSSL is the UK&#39;s leading independent provider of <A HREF="research.html">research</A>, <A HREF="analysis.html">analysis</A> and <A HREF="consultancy.html">consultancy</A> services to the worldwide food, drink, consumer goods, <A HREF="healthcare.html">healthcare</A> and chemical industries. The company was formed in 1987 and its services are based on the expertise of over 100 <A HREF="heads.html">staff</A>, many of whom have international reputations. </P>

<P>Our <A HREF="laboratories.html">laboratories</A> are amongst the most modern in Europe and RSSL continually invests in the latest instrumentation and methodology. We are also constantly searching for talented scientists to strengthen our team. RSSL&#39;s multidisciplinary laboratories offer a range of techniques and services and the quality of our work is assured by externally accredited quality systems such as NAMAS and GLP. </P>

<P>All our work is undertaken confidentially, and our client base of over 2000 companies, includes many well known internationals. However, RSSL still manages to contribute to the wider industry knowledge through <A HREF="training.html">training courses</A>, <A HREF="contract.html">newsletters</A> and <A HREF="http://www.inpress.co.uk/Press_RSS">press releases</A>.</P>
