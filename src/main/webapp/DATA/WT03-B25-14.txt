<P><CENTER><B><FONT color=red SIZE=+1>EVERYONE IS NOT SO FAR AWAY</FONT></B></center>

<P><IMG align=left hspace=10 width=300 height=333 SRC="image/graph.gif" ALT="regional graph">

<P>Major airports serve San Francisco, Oakland, San Jose and Sacramento. From these points we've calculated that from San Francisco to Oakland its only 11 miles, to San Jose 47 miles, to Sacramento 85 miles.

<P>From Oakland to San Jose its 46 miles, to Sacramento 78 miles.

<P>From San Jose to Sacramento, its 119 miles. Though Northern California is a diverse and active book community, "everyone is not so far away."
