<P><A NAME="objective"><STRONG>Investment Objective</FONT></STRONG></A>
<BR>Seeks capital appreciation together with current income. Certain of the Fund's investments have speculative characteristics.


<P><BR>
<A NAME="f_profile"><STRONG>Fund Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>

<UL>
<LI>The Fund is managed like an equity fund, with a focus on securities that show strong prospects
for capital appreciation.
<LI>Under normal market conditions the Fund will invest at least 65% of the value of its net assets
in "convertible securities," that is, bonds, debentures, corporate notes,  preferred stocks, or
other securities which are convertible into common stock.
<LI>The Fund will invest not more than 5% of its total assets in securities rated less than B by Moody's
or Standard &amp; Poor's.
<LI>Using  security selection and portfolio management strategies, the Fund manager seeks to control risk while pursuing capital growth and current income. 
</UL>

<P><A NAME="i_profile"><STRONG>Investor Profile</FONT></STRONG></A>
<UL>
<LI>Investors seeking the potential for capital appreciation and current income
<LI>Income investors seeking the potential to enhance their total return
<LI>Investors seeking to diversify stock or bond  investments with securities that may combine the
benefits of both
<LI>Investors with a moderate to long investment time horizon
</UL>
Before you invest...
<BR>Some of the Fund's investments may be unrated or have speculative characteristics. The portfolio 
managers seek to offset these risks through broad diversification across issuers and industries.
