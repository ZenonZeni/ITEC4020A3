<h1>Sacramento Sports Commission</h1>
<h3>A Commission of the
<a href=goverment.html>City and County</a>
of Sacramento</h3>
</center>
<br clear=all>

<p><hr><p>

The
<a href=commissioners.html>Sacramento Sports Commission</a>
was formed in 1987 to act as the coordinating
and facilitating body to augment the efforts to attract professional
<a href=franchises.html>sports franchises</a>
and
<a href=accomplishments.html>major sports events</a>
of the
<a href=venues.html>variety of venues</a>
in the
<a href=profile.html>Sacramento region</a>. An example of our efforts
would be the recent
<a href=rbis.html>Regional Ballpark in Sacramento (RBIS)</a>
initiative.

<p>

Working in partnership with the
<a href=http://www.sna.com/cvb/>Sacramento Convention &amp; Visitors Bureau</a>,
The Sacramento Sports Commission provides expertise to event organizers in the
areas of event management, local corporate development, business and
governmental support in the Sacramento region.
