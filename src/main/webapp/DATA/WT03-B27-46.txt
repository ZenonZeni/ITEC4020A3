<H2><B><CENTER>PJP Online Sponsor Information</B></H2></CENTER>
<P>
<center><img border=2 src="trackban.gif"></center>
<p>
PJP Online, with events happening year around, is expected to quickly develop into a high traffic WWW site.  We launch PJP Online with coverage of The Fred Meyer Challenge, the most popular non-PGA golf event in the world.
<P>
<li><a href="sponsor.html#corporate">Information on Corporate Sponsorships</a>
<li><a href="sponsor.html#golf">Information on Golf Course Directory Sponsorships</a>

</pre>
Benefits of PJP Online Sponsorship include:
<ul>
<b><li>High Viewership & Visibility</b><br>
We continue our relationship with ESPN this year, as they once again televise The Challenge.  Our online presence benefits from this relationship, as ESPN's WWW site, called <a href="http://ESPNET.SportsZone.com">ESPNet Sports Zone</a>, is the third most popular Web site, averaging 8,500,000 visits per week by 2,000,000 viewers.  ESPN will provide a hyperlink from their popular site to The Challenge Online, assuring that your message will be seen by <b>hundreds of thousands of viewers from around the world</b>.
<P>
<b><LI>Choice Demographics<br></b>
Consider these Internet demographics from the 3rd WWW User Survey, conducted in April-May of this year by the Graphic, Visualization & Usability Center at the Georgia Institute of Technology:
<UL>
<LI>average age of Internet users  --  35
<LI>80.3% male, 20% female
<LI>average income --  $70,000
<LI>50% are online between 2-6 hours/week; 45.3% are online more; 4.8% are online less
<LI>typically college educated
<LI>typically interested in technology, electronics, movies, automobiles, books  
</ul>
<p>
<b><LI>Support <a href="charity.html">Children's Charities</a></b><br>
The Fred Meyer Challenge is proud to have contributed over $3,700,000 to Portland Area Children's Charities over the last nine years.  Your online sponsorship will help us continue this proud tradition.
<p>
<b><LI>Option on Online Sponsorship Next Year<br></b>
As the Internet and the World Wide Web continue their explosive growth, it's becoming increasingly important to have an online presence.  Your participation this year reserves your spot for next year at guaranteed rates.
