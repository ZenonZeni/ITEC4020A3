<h4 align="center">Helpline Fact Sheet </h4>

<h4 align="center"><font color="#000080"><b>Paxil--Treatment for </b></font><a
href="depress.htm"><font color="#000080"><b>Depression</b></font></a></h4>

<hr>

<p><font color="#FF0000">What is Paxil? </font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - paroxetine hydrochloride</p>
    <p>2 - a new antidepressant medication</p>
    <p>3 - introduced to the United States in 1993</p>
    <p>4 - manufactured by SmithKline Beecham Pharmaceuticals</p>
    <p>5 - a selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI)</p>
    <p>6 - blocks the reabsorption of serotonin &amp; normalizes
    the brain&#146;s chemical supply</p>
    <p>7 - alleviates the symptoms of major depression</p>
    <p>8 - fewer side effects than the older tricyclic
    antidepressants (TCAs)</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000">Dosage </font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - available in pink 20mg tablets or in blue 30mg tablets</p>
    <p>2 - recommended dosage for average patients is 20 mg once
    a day in the morning</p>
    <p>3 - recommended dosage for depressed elderly is 10 mg once
    a day in the morning</p>
    <p>4 - one should never take more than 50 mg in one day</p>
    <p>5 - geriatric patients should never take more than 40 mg
    in one day</p>
    <p>6 - dosage adjustments should be made in increments of 10
    mg &amp; by a doctor</p>
    <p>7 - do not mix paxil with any monoamine oxidase inhibitors
    (MAOIs)/doing so </p>
    <p>could have fatal results/there must be a 14 day interval
    between them</p>
    <p>8 - use cautiously if you suffer kidney or liver
    dysfunction or if you take lithium</p>
    <p>9 - do not consume alcohol while on paxil</p>
    <p>10 - consult a doctor before using other medications
    simultaneously</p>
    <p>11 - easily tolerated for up to 4 years</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000">Benefits </font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - causes fewer adverse cardiovascular &amp;
    anticholinergic effects (dry mouth,</p>
    <p>constipation, blurred vision, urinary retention) than
    older tricyclic antidepressants</p>
    <p>2 - does not cause drowsiness, low blood pressure, or
    weight change</p>
    <p>3 - no active metabolites</p>
    <p>4 - leaves one&#146;s system within 24 hours after it
    enters</p>
    <p>5 - extremely selective, thus causing serotonin levels in
    the brain to normalize </p>
    <p>quickly &amp; relieving depression with relatively few
    side effects</p>
    <p>6 - good for the elderly because it causes no significant
    changes in blood pressure</p>
    <p>7 - effectively treats anxiety which often accompanies
    depression</p>
    <p>8 - does not impair psychomotor function or cause lethargy</p>
    <p>9 - begins to take effect within a week or two/fully
    effective within 6 weeks </p>
    <p>10 - effective in treating both short-term &amp; long-term
    depression</p>
    <p>11 - helps to prevent relapse after recovery</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000">Risks and Side Effects</font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - risks are usually mild, brief</p>
    <p>2 - nausea is the major side effect (risk reduced by
    taking paxil with food)</p>
    <p>3 - drowsiness</p>
    <p>4 - asthenia (lack of physical strength) tremors</p>
    <p>5 - nervousness</p>
    <p>6 - dizziness</p>
    <p>7 - insomnia</p>
    <p>8 - sweating</p>
    <p>9 - ejaculatory delay &amp; other male genital dysfunction</p>
    <p>10 - slight weight loss</p>
    <p>11- especially risky for pregnant or nursing women </p>
    <p>12 - especially risky for people with kidney or liver
    dysfunction</p>
</blockquote>

<p><font color="#FF0000">Financial assistance/Paxil Access to
Care program </font></p>

<blockquote>
    <p>1 - a physician can arrange no-cost supplies of paxil for
    patients who meet specific requirements</p>
    <blockquote>
        <p>a. less than $25,000 annual income</p>
        <p>b. no medical insurance</p>
        <p>c. ineligible for government or private programs
        covering prescriptions</p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>2 - physicians who wish to obtain supplies can call
    1-800-729-4544</p>
</blockquote>
