\#G 1994.61 Labelled &quot;ATC 33.61.20.05.87&quot;. Details of Polarization
and Directionality of Earth Reflectances sensor developed by CNES for flight
on the NASDA Advanced Earth Observation satellite. POLDER is a wide field,
moderate resolution multi spectral imaging radiometer/polarimeter for repetitive
observation of solar radiation reflected by the earth. Applications include
ocean colour, vegetation indices, and multi-spectral properties of clouds
and aerosols. Products should include Bidirectional Reflectance Distribution
Function and Bidirectional Polarization Distribution Function. mvk 30.4.94<BR>
