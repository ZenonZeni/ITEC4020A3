<H1 ALIGN=CENTER>Welcome to the<BR>
National Clearinghouse for<BR>
Professions in Special Education<BR>
Web Page !</H1>

<P>
<HR></P>

<BLOCKQUOTE>
<P>The National Clearinghouse for Professions in Special Education (NCPSE)
is operated by the <A HREF="http://www.cec.sped.org/">Council for Exceptional
Children (CEC)</A> under Cooperative Agreement number H030E30002 with the
Office of Special Education Programs (OSEP), U.S. <A HREF="http://www.ed.gov">Department
of Education.</A> We are an information resource for professionals and
potential students in the fields of special education and the related services
professions. As the only national information center of its type, the Clearinghouse
gathers, develops, and disseminates information on recuritment, preservice
preparation, employment opportunities, and attrition and retention issues.
We also maintain the most current data on personnel supply and demand.<BR>
We are easy to reach:<BR>
<BR>

703-620-2521(Fax); ncpse@cec.sped.org(E-mail)</I></P>
</BLOCKQUOTE>

</P>

<LI><I>job seekers</I></LI>

<LI><I>state education agency staff and Comprehensive System of Personnel

<LI><I>professional association staff</I></LI>
</UL>
</BLOCKQUOTE>
