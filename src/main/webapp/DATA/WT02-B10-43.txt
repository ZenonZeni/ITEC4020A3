<H4>Welcome to browse the various sections of the KFB EV & Hybrid program web pages! </H4>


<P>The pages are set up as follows: <B><!-- Font Tag --><FONT COLOR="#BF361C">R, D & D program</FONT></B> contains a description of the assignment to KFB and a description of strategies, methods and principles employed. By clicking on one of the five <B><!-- Font Tag --><FONT COLOR="#BF361C">topics</FONT></B> (infrastructure, energy and emissions etc.) you reach scores of assessment areas associated to large scale EV and Hybrid introduction. <B><!-- Font Tag --><FONT COLOR="#BF361C">what is under the hood</FONT></B> contains a technical account of EVs and Hybrids of today and tomorrow. <B><!-- Font Tag --><FONT COLOR="#BF361C">projects</FONT></B> takes you to an overview of major associated demonstration and research projects. Demo program pages have links to vehicle data. </P>





<P>The web pages which you have accessed reside on the KFB server in Stockholm regarding program issues and project information. All vehicle related material is located on the Data Base server at Lund University of Technology, Department of Industrial Engineering and Automation. Our ambition is to offer a consistent graphics interface as to leave you undisturbed by which server you are currently visiting.</P>





<P>Please don&#180;t hesitate to forward your comments on the web pages and related questions to <!-- MailTo Tag --><A HREF="MAILTO:EV@kfb.se">EV@kfb.se</A>.

<BR>
