<BODY><H1><B>THE NUCLEAR WASTE POLICY ACT OF 1982</B><A HREF="#footnotes">*</A></H1> <HR>  

 An Act to provide for the development of repositories for the disposal of high-level radioactive waste and spent nuclear fuel, to 
establish a program of research,  development, and    demonstration regarding the disposal of high-level radioactive  waste and spent 
nuclear fuel, and   for other purposes.  <P>
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America  in    Congress assembled,<P>
