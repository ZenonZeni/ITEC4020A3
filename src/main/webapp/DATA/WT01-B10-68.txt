<h1 align=center>&quot;<B>INFERTILITY -- Exploring The Male Factor</B>&quot;</h1><hr>
<img align=right src="levine.jpg"><b>Ones out of six couples are presently experiencing an infertility
problems. While it's often assumed the problem stems with the
woman, the fact is that up to 50% of these cases involve what
is called a &quot;male factor problem.&quot; 
<P>
When seeking information on infertility couples often misunderstand the current literature and are often confused at the various lectures they attend. When in the doctor's office, many get too emotional to focus in on the proper questions to ask within the time frame they have with the physician.
<P>
Only with the proper understanding of this complex subject can the couple make informed decisions and properly communicate with their physician.
<P>
<font size="+1"><em>INFERTILITY -- Exploring The Male Factor</em></font> is geared toward those couples who are just starting there fertility evaluation and hope to understand the basics of male factor fertility. It is aimed at couples who desperately seek concise no nonsense information; information vital to the couples success! Now couples can learn in the privacy of their own home, and review the tape as often as needed to learn the material.
<P>
Starting with determining the proper timing of intercourse, the video is an in depth analysis of the history, physical exam, semen analysis, endocrine testing, urinalysis and the various surgical options presently available to correct male factor problems. 
<P>
Filmed primarily at The University of Connecticut Health Center in Farmington, Connecticut, the video features Jeffrey P. Buch MD, and John Nulsen M.D.
