<H4><CENTER>Advocacy Group and<BR>
Government Relations</CENTER>
</H4>
Defining and defending corporate environmental policies and practices to
environmental advocacy groups and governmental agencies is a sensitive and
often frustrating experience. <BR>
<BR>
Coddington Environmental serves as an intermediary in such conversations.
Coddington Environmental does not engage in green-washing. Instead, we recognize
the need for compromise and creativity in environmental issue-related discussions
and negotiations. <BR>
<BR>
Coddington Environmental is respected by environmental organizations, federal
environmental agencies and corporate clients alike for its integrity and
its ability to find common ground among disparate groups.
