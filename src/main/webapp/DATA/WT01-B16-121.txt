<BODY BGCOLOR="#ffffcc"><FONT SIZE=6><B>Biography of Mr Michel B E Poissonnet </B></FONT>
<IMG WIDTH=100% SRC="http://www.uilondon.org/bluline.gif">
<P>
Mr Michel B E Poissonnet
is President of COGEMA Resources Inc and of its affiliated mining companies in North America, among which are Minatco in Canada, and Pathfinder and COMIN in the United States. He joined the French Atomic Energy Commission in 1972, and then COGEMA when it was created in 1976. He held several positions in uranium exploration, and after 1979 he managed the mining operations in Vend&eacute;e and La Crouzille, two of COGEMA's former mining districts in France. He assumed his present positions in Canada in 1989, and took responsibility for COGEMA's interests in the United States in 1993. Mr Poissonnet received a degree from the Mining School of Nancy, France, in 1970. He is a Director of the Canadian Nuclear Association and a Director and the past Chairman of Uranium Saskatchewan Association.
