<H3>Leaf Collection <BR>
November 4 through January 3</H3>
Corvallis Disposal will collect leaves in Corvallis, Philomath, and Adair
if swept into the street. These are picked up on the same day as your garbage
day. These leaves are made into compost at the Processing and Recovery Center
(PRC) 745-5831.<BR>
<HR>
<H3>Christmas Tree Collection <BR>
December 30 through January 10 </H3>
Christmas trees will be collected curbside in Corvallis, Philomath, and
Adair and turned into compost at the Processing and Recovery Center (PRC)
745-5831. Trees should be free of tinsel and flocking. Customers outside
of these areas can have their trees picked for no charge by their garbage
route. You can also drop off your tree for recycling at Corvallis Disposal
or the Philomath Fire Department.<BR><HR><BR>
