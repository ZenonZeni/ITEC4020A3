Transportation Costing for Decision Making.<BR>

Transportation Industry Market Structure in Sweden and its Implications for Pricing and Costing<BR>
<BR>

<B>PUBLICERINGSDATUM/DATE OF PUBLISHING</B> <BR>

January 1995<BR>
<BR>

<B>UTGIVARE/PUBLISHER</B><BR>


KFBs DNR 92-126-43<BR>

<B>REFERAT</B> (Syfte, Metod, Resultat)<BR>

Syftena med f&ouml;religgande studie &auml;r dels att (1) analysera strukturen p&aring; den svenska transport- marknaden och dess konsekvenser f&ouml;r priss&auml;ttning och kalkylering, dels att (2) analysera olika problem vid kalkylering av transporter och utveckla l&auml;mpliga principer f&ouml;r kalkylering. Modellerna som utvecklas i part IV visar olika f&ouml;rflyttningar av transporter och bygger p&aring; sambanden mellan (i) olika kostnader f&ouml;r transport av godset (ii) kostnader f&ouml;r att hantera godset p&aring; terminal och (iii) allokering av gemensamma kostnader. Det grundl&auml;ggande angreppss&auml;ttet  bygger p&aring; analys av vilka olika produktionsresurser  som &auml;r n&ouml;dv&auml;ndiga f&ouml;r olika steg. Det kan konstateras att aktivitetsbaserad kalkylering inte &auml;r l&auml;mplig f&ouml;r beslutssituationer som pr&auml;glas av kapacitets&ouml;verv&auml;ganden och alternativkostnads- resonemang. ABC &auml;r d&auml;remot l&auml;mplig som kalkylmetod  vid terminaler d&auml;r kapacitets&ouml;verv&auml;ganden &auml;r av mindre betydelse och d&auml;r aktiviteterna &auml;r samma &ouml;ver tiden.



<P>Mikroekonomisk analys anv&auml;nds f&ouml;r att studera marknadsstrukturen. I Sverige &auml;r koncentrationen av utbudssidan mycket kraftig och vi har n&auml;stan en marknad som utm&auml;rks av duopol. Det existerar en sorts priskartell, d&auml;r det f&ouml;rsta steget mot en kartellbindning &auml;r h&ouml;ga priser och kraftiga priss&auml;nkningar, s k kvasipermanenta priser.     
</P>



<B>ABSTRACT (Aim, Method, Results)</B><BR>


The aims of this study were (1) to analyse the Swedish transportation industry market structure and explain its consequences to transport carrier costing and pricing and (2) to analyse the transportation costing problem area and develop appropriate costing principles. The models developed in part IV represent the operation of  transportation movements by a carrier, (ii) terminal handling costs of the goods and (iii) allocation of the overhead costs. The basic approach is the analysis of a task in terms of the production resources necessary for each stage. Fullcosting methods as activity-based costing is not appropriate for capacity considerations and considerations about opportunity costs is missing. ABC is on the contrary appropriate for cost allocation at the terminals because there is usually no capacity problems and the activities are the same over the time.<BR>
</B>



<P>Microeconomic analysis is used for explicitly consider market structure effects. In Sweden there is a very strong concentration  of supply and we nearly have a dupoly-case. In this country exists a sort of price-fixing (a pricing-cartel) agreement. The first step toward the establishment of a cartel and to inefficiency are higher prices and large price reductions, i e quasipermanent prices. 

</P>

Combi-Terminal and Intermodal Freight Centre development - an assessment<BR>
<BR>


<B>PUBLICERINGSDATUM/DATE OF PUBLISHING </B><BR>

August 1995<BR><BR>


<B>UTGIVARE/PUBLISHER</B><BR>



KFBs DNR 93-122-43<BR>

<B>
REFERAT (Syfte, Metod, Resultat)</B><BR>

Syftet med denna studie har varit att unders&ouml;ka och analysera utvecklingen av kombi-terminaler och integrerade godstransportcenter p&aring; kontinenten och dess m&ouml;jliga p&aring;verkan p&aring; utvecklingen i Sverige. P&aring; kontinenten har l&auml;nderna Holland, Italien, Tyskland och Polen analyserats d&aring; de utg&ouml;r viktiga handelspartner f&ouml;r Sverige samt driver delvis olika terminal-strategier. Den existerande situationen och aktuella utvecklingsplaner har unders&ouml;kts med en koncentration p&aring; viktiga marknadsakt&ouml;rer, terminal-&auml;gar-struktur och de f&ouml;r&auml;ndringar som kan f&ouml;rv&auml;ntas inom den n&auml;rmaste framtiden. Studien visar att utvecklingen av intermodala terminaler &auml;r en nationell fr&aring;ga vilken ofta drivs av de nationella j&auml;rn-v&auml;garna. N&auml;tverket av intermodala terminaler &auml;r generellt sett bra utvecklat i de unders&ouml;kta l&auml;nderna (utom f&ouml;r Polen d&auml;r en utbyggnad planeras), men kapacitets- och effektivitetsproblem finns. Vad g&auml;ller utvecklingen av integrerade godstransportcenter s&aring; p&aring;g&aring;r en dynamisk utveckling i b&aring;de Tyskland och i Italien vilken ofta drivs av lokala/regionala intressegrupper och j&auml;rnv&auml;garna. I Sverige och i Holland &aring;terfinns terminalstrukturer/omr&aring;den (liknande IFC) av samma typ vilka inte har utvecklats enligt en medveten terminalstrategi. Ansvariga f&ouml;r planering &auml;r lokala och regionala myndigheter. I Polen b&ouml;rjar olika akt&ouml;rer att intressera sig f&ouml;r en m&ouml;jlig utveckling av godstransportcenter.<BR>
<BR>


<B>ABSTRACT (Aim, Method, Results)</B><BR>

The purpose of the study is to analyze the Combi-terminal and Intermodal Freight Centre development (IFC) on the continent and it&#146;s possible influence on the development in Sweden. On the continent four countries, Italy, the Netherlands, Germany and Poland, have been investigated because they all are important trade partners to Sweden and pursue some-what different terminal strategies. For these countries the present situation and existing deve-lopment plans have been analysed with a focus on the important market actors, the structure of the terminal ownership and the changes which can be expected during the  next years. The study shows that the development of intermodal terminals is a national question which is usually driven by the national railways. The network of intermodal terminals is in general well developed in the five contries (except for Poland were an extension is planned), but capacity and efficiency problems exist. For the development of Intermodal Freight Centres (IFC) a pronounced development takes place in Italy and Germany which is driven by local/regional interest groups and by the  railways. In Sweden and the Netherlands similar structures/areas (like IFC) can be found, but have not been developed specifically. Responsible for the planning are local and regional authorities. In Poland first thoughts are put to the development of Intermodal Freight Centres (IFC).<BR>

Modeller f&ouml;r samordning av samh&auml;lls-betalda resor
Models for coordination of special transport services and other transports paid by authorities<BR><BR>


<B>PUBLICERINGSDATUM/DATE OF PUBLISHING </B><BR>

Maj 1995<BR>
<BR>

<B>UTGIVARE/PUBLISHER
</B><BR>

KFBs DNR 94-40-732<BR>

<B>
REFERAT (Syfte, Metod, Resultat)</B><BR>

Studien j&auml;mf&ouml;r samarbetet mellan landstinget i S&ouml;rmland och kommunerna i &ouml;stra l&auml;nsdelen med olika samarbetsformer i Hallands, V&auml;rmlands, &Ouml;rebro och G&auml;vleborgs l&auml;n. F&ouml;r vissa typer av rationaliseringar och samarbete har j&auml;mf&ouml;relser gjorts f&ouml;r hela landet.<BR>


<BR>
Erfarenheten &auml;r att den effektivaste l&ouml;sningen f&ouml;r samh&auml;llsbetalda transporter &auml;r samverkan mellan kommuner och landsting av b&aring;de upphandling och resor genom en best&auml;llnings-central. Samordningen skall ske genom att resen&auml;rerna f&ouml;rbest&auml;ller resan (20 - 60 min) och att best&auml;llningscentralen har r&auml;tt att justera avresetiden (10-30 min). Transport&ouml;rer skall ers&auml;ttas enligt en taxa som medger att best&auml;llningscentralen kan ber&auml;kna ers&auml;ttningen i f&ouml;rv&auml;g.<BR>
<BR>











<B>ABSTRACT (Aim, Method, Results)</B><BR>

The aim of the project is to compile experiences of different ways to organize coordination measurements for personal transports paid by local authorities and county councils.<BR>




<P>The most efficient organization is a cooperation between local authorities and county councils concerning purchasing and coordination of trips in a booking office. The coordination will be more effective if the trips are booked 20-60 min in advance and if the booking office is allowed to change the time of departure 10-30 min.
</P>




<P>The control of charges and the administration of invoices can be reduced if the compensation to transport operators are calculated in advance instead of calculated by taximeter.

</P>

Motorgasdrivna bussar i Sundsvall
LPG propelled buses in the city of Sundsvall, Sweden<BR>




<B>PUBLICERINGSDATUM/DATE OF PUBLISHING </B><BR>

Maj 1995<BR>
<BR>

<B>UTGIVARE/PUBLISHER
</B><BR>

KFBs DNR 90-46-732<BR>

KFB - Kommunikationsforskningsberedningen, Stockholm<BR><BR>



<B>REFERAT (Syfte, Metod, Resultat)</B><BR>

Prov med motorgasdrivna stadsbussar har gjorts f&ouml;rut. Provet med tv&aring; &auml;ldre Volvobussar i trafik i Sundsvall, gjordes med syftet att prova om avgasutsl&auml;ppen gick att s&auml;nka dramatiskt och om tillg&auml;ngligheten kunde h&aring;llas uppe med nyutvecklad teknik. Motorgas &auml;r l&auml;tt till-g&auml;nglig i Sundsvall och proven skulle utf&ouml;ras med motorerna optimerade f&ouml;r mager resp idealisk br&auml;nsle-luftblandning. V&auml;rmeutvecklingen gjorde dock att b&aring;da motorerna an-passades enligt magerbr&auml;nsleprincipen.<H6></H6>



<P>Resultatet blev att NOX-, HC- och CO-utsl&auml;ppen reducerades mer &auml;n v&auml;ntat. Koldioxidutsl&auml;ppen (CO2) s&auml;nktes med 8 % j&auml;mf&ouml;rt med motsvarande dieseldrift. V&auml;rmeutvecklingen orsakade skador i motorerna och motorhaverier &auml;ven i magerbr&auml;nsleutf&ouml;randet. Allt eftersom erfarenheterna har kommit, har dock denna sv&aring;righet kunnat minskas. Bussen med den nyare motorn k&ouml;rdes utan st&ouml;rningar under de sista 12 av de 18 m&aring;nadernas provdrift. Vid projektets genomf&ouml;rande var denna buss sannolikt den renaste bussen i drift i v&auml;rlden enligt ECE R49 m&auml;tmetod.
</P>


<B>ABSTRACT (Aim, Method, Results)</B><BR>

Trying LPG in heavy duty buses has been done before. This test, with two Volvo buses in operation in the city of Sundsvall, Sweden, had the objective to show how low the exhaust emissions and how high accessibility could be with engines optimised for LPG-operation. LPG is also readily available in the Sundsvall area. Lean Burn and stoichiometric optimi-sation was tested, but the stoichiometric technique had to be eliminated due to a heat release that was too high. Both buses thereby became Lean Burn operated.<BR>




<P>The results shoved that the NOX, HC and CO exhausts volumes were more reduced than anticipated. Carbon Dioxide (CO2) exhaust was also reduced by over all 8 % compared with diesel operation under identical conditions. Heat release has been a concern and a source of trouble, both engines have suffered break downs. As time went by and experiences accumulated, the issue became of less significance. The newer of the two buses operated without interruption for the last 12 months of the project. At the time for the project, this newer bus was maybe the cleanest operating bus in the world, according to the ECE R49 test method.</P>

Modern sp&aring;rv&auml;gstrafik. En inventering av l&auml;get i Sverige, Europa och USA
Modern Light Rail systems in Sweden, Europe and the United States<BR>
<BR>

<B>PUBLICERINGSDATUM/DATE OF PUBLISHING </B><BR>

Augusti 1995<BR><BR>



<B>UTGIVARE/PUBLISHER
</B>
<BR>
KFBs dnr 91-97-732 <BR>

KFB -  Kommunikationsforskningsberedningen, Stockholm<BR>
<BR>


<B>REFERAT (Syfte, Metod, Resultat)</B><BR>

Syftet med projektet har varit att klarl&auml;gga l&auml;get vad g&auml;ller utvecklingen av modern sp&aring;rv&auml;gstrafik. Flera utredningar har gjorts om sp&aring;rv&auml;gstrafik i  Sverige. I Europa f&ouml;rnyas sp&aring;rv&auml;gstekniken vilket &auml;r av intresse f&ouml;r Sverige.<BR>




<P>Projektet har genomf&ouml;rts som en litteraturstudie samt intervjuer och diskussioner med nyckelpersoner inom utredningar som har p&aring;g&aring;tt och p&aring;g&aring;r i Sverige.
</P>




<P>Rapporten redovisar en genomg&aring;ng av sp&aring;rv&auml;gen och dess utveckling de senaste 15 &aring;ren. I rapporten beskrivs befintliga och planerade system i  Sverige och j&auml;mf&ouml;relser har gjorts med &ouml;vriga Europa och USA. Det &auml;r endast system som kan vara av intresse f&ouml;r svenska  f&ouml;rh&aring;llanden som har behandlats. Sp&aring;rv&auml;gen har &auml;ven j&auml;mf&ouml;rts med andra trafikslag som bussar, automatbanor och tunnelbanor. En genomg&aring;ng redovisar finansieringssystem i Sverige, Tyskland, Frankrike och USA.
</P>
<BR>




<B>ABSTRACT (Aim, Method, Results)</B><BR>

The aim of this project is to describe the situation concerning the development of Light Rail systems. A number of studies has been done on Light Rail systems in Sweden. In Europe there are a lot of new ideas in Light Rail transit which can be of great interest for planners in Sweden.<BR>




<P>The project is realized as a literature study and interviews with key persons in the Swedish Light Rail studies.</P>





<P>This report is an examination of Light Rail systems and its development during the last 15 years. In the report, existing and planned Swedish systems are described. Comparisons have been made between cities in Europe and USA. Only systems of interest for Swedish conditions are  discussed. Light Rail is also compared with other modes of transport such as buses, people movers and underground systems. Different forms of financing Light Rail in Sweden, Germany, France and the United States are described.</P>

 Tillg&auml;nglig kollektivtrafik f&ouml;r &auml;ldre och handikappade. Betydelsen av reskedjans olika delar.
Accessible public transport for elderly and physically disabled. Significance of the various links in the transportation chain<BR>
<BR>

<B>PUBLICERINGSDATUM/DATE PUBLISHED</B><BR>

Maj 1996<BR>
<BR>

<B>UTGIVARE/PUBLISHER </B><BR>

KFB - Kommunikationsforskningsberedningen<BR>


KFBs DNR  88-251-731<BR>

<BR>


<B>REFERAT (Syfte, Metod, Resultat)</B><BR>

<B>Syftet</B> med projektet var att utv&auml;rdera effekterna (r&ouml;rlighet, s&auml;kerhet, livskvalitet) bland de &auml;ldre i tv&aring; olika bostadsomr&aring;den i Bor&aring;s. Likv&auml;rdiga &aring;tg&auml;rder genomf&ouml;rdes i den fysiska milj&ouml;n. I ett av omr&aring;dena inf&ouml;rdes Servicelinjetrafik, i det andra genomf&ouml;rdes sm&auml;rre &aring;tg&auml;rder p&aring; befintliga 12-meters bussar. &Auml;ldres attityder till och v&auml;rderingar av olika &aring;tg&auml;rder studerades.
      Ett flertal <B>studier</B> genomf&ouml;rdes; postenk&auml;t (f&ouml;re-efterstudie) till samtliga personer &ouml;ver 65 &aring;r i de b&aring;da omr&aring;dena, intervjuer med fotg&auml;ngare och bussresen&auml;rer, r&auml;kningar av fotg&auml;ngare och busspassagerare, f&auml;rdtj&auml;nstm&auml;tningar, intervjuer med personer med synneds&auml;ttningar samt en s&auml;rskild stated-preference studie. <BR>
<BR>

    <B> De sammanfattande resultaten</B> av projektet &auml;r att antalet resen&auml;rer i kollektivtrafiken &ouml;kar i b&aring;da omr&aring;dena men &ouml;kningen &auml;r betydligt st&ouml;rre i det omr&aring;de d&auml;r Servicelinjetrafik introducerats. Antalet fotg&auml;ngare i bostadsomr&aring;dena &ouml;kar. Attityderna &auml;r positivare till f&ouml;r&auml;ndringarna i kollektivtrafiken &auml;n till f&ouml;r&auml;ndringar i den fysiska milj&ouml;n. De boende i omr&aring;det d&auml;r Servicelinjetrafik introducerats &auml;r emellertid mer positiva till &aring;tg&auml;rderna i den fysiska milj&ouml;n &auml;n de boende i omr&aring;det d&auml;r &aring;tg&auml;rder inom den traditionella kollektivtrafiken genomf&ouml;rts. Effekterna och attityderna &auml;r mest positiva bland de &auml;ldsta (75+) och bland de mer funktionsnedsatta.<BR>
<BR>

     <B> Slutsatserna</B> av projektet &auml;r att det &auml;r m&ouml;jligt att attrahera nya &auml;ldre resen&auml;rer till kollektivtrafiken och att &ouml;ka resandet bland tidigare resen&auml;rer genom att &ouml;ka kvaliteten i kollektivresan. Detta har i sin tur positiva effekter p&aring; f&auml;rdtj&auml;nstresandet. &Aring;tg&auml;rderna i den fysiska milj&ouml;n har &auml;ven positiv effekt p&aring; resandet, s&auml;rskilt i de omr&aring;den som erbjuder h&ouml;gst kvalitet i kollektivresan, d v s d&auml;r Servicelinjetrafik inf&ouml;rts.<BR>
<BR>


<B>ABSTRACT (Aim, Method, Results)</B><BR>

<B>The aim</B> of the project was to evaluate the overall benefit (mobility, safety, quality of life etc) for the elderly population in two different residential areas in Bor&aring;s. In both areas similar measures were undertaken in the physical environment. In the public transport system, a service route was introduced in one of the areas, and measures on the buses were undertaken in the other area. The elderly&#146;s attitudes towards and values of different measures were studied.<BR>
<BR>

     Many <B>studies</B> were undertaken; a questionnaire (before-after-study) to all people over the age of 65 in both the residential areas, interviews with pedestrians and bus-passengers, counting of passengers on the buses and pedestrians in the areas, counting of passengers in the special transport system (STS), interviews with a group of vision impaired people and a special stated preference study.<BR>
<BR>

    <B>The summarized resut</B> of the project is that the number of passengers in the public transport system increases in both area but the increase is bigger where the service route traffic were introduced; more pedestrians is out in both neighborhoods; measures in both the public transport system and the physical environment are appreciated by the elderly; there is a more positive attitude to the changes in the public transport system than in the physical environment; the residents in the area where the service route was introduced value however the measures in the physical environment higher than do the residents where changes on the buses in the mainstream service were made. Furthermore the effect and the attitudes are more positive among the oldest (75+) and the group of people reporting more severe impairments. <BR>
<BR>

     <B>The conclusion</B> of the project is that it is possible to attract new passengers and to increase the use of the public transport system by offering a better quality and a higher adaptation level in the system. This, in turn, has a positive effect on the use of STS. The effect of measures in the physical environment is also positive, but such measures are likely to be more appreciated when the public transport system also offers a higher adaptation level (i.e. service routes).<BR>
