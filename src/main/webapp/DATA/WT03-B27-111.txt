<H3>ASSOCIATED PRESS (AP)</H3>

<B>Associated Press</B><BR>
$1.50/article<BR>
Continuous wire feed with immediate updating; 10-week retention of articles<BR>
The Associated Press, the world's largest supplier of general interest news to the
media, provides two real-time newswire feeds to NewsNet. These two feeds, AP
DataStream Business News and AP DataStream General News, are divided into four
separate newswire services on NewsNet, all monitored on a 24-hour basis.
Information from these four services is accessible immediately through the DISPLAY
and SCAN commands, and is processed continuously for NewsFlash delivery to users
who have established NewsFlash profiles. Full-text searching of all articles is
available within one hour of release.<P>


<B>AP DataStream Business News Wire--AP01W</B><BR>
(Also in FI, GB, Wires, BWires)<BR>
Continuous reporting on business and financial news.<P>


<B>AP DataStream National News Wire--AP02W</B><BR>
(Also in Wires)<BR>
Continuous reporting on all domestic news other than financial and political.<P>


<B>AP DataStream Washington News Wire--AP03W</B><BR>
(Also in GT, PO, WashDC, Wires)<BR>
Continuous reporting on political news from the U.S. capital.<P>


<B>AP DataStream International News Wire--AP04W</B><BR>
(Also in IT, PO, IWires, Wires)<BR>
Continuous reporting on international news, including the United Nations.<P>
