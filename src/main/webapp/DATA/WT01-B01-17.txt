<H1>Company Profile</H1>
<P>Environ Technologies Pte Ltd is the regional administrator of the Danduct Duct Cleaning System. Environ Technologies is a subsidiary of <B>Pace Air-conditioning and Engineering Pte Ltd (PACE)</B>. PACE is a member of the Sembawang Group of Companies.

<P>At Environ Technologies, we are fully equipped with the latest technologies to help you with your Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) problems. Our committed service personnel are trained to analyse the unique IAQ problems of each client, since the conditions and ducting configuration in each building is different. We are the solution to your IAQ problems.<BR>
