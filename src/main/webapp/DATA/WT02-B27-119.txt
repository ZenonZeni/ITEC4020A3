<H3>Departing Drugs</H3>

<P>See also <A HREF="sd.html">Self-deliverance</A> </P>

<P>Applicants must be over 21 yrs. (We also reserve the right to refuse
purchase.)<BR>
<I>Departing Drugs</I> is the first international syllabus of drugs for
self-deliverance, compiled by an international team using thousands of
pages of collated data and the latest clinical evidence from the Netherlands.
Scrutinized, by experts in several fields, <I>Departing Drugs</I> presents
methods with illustrations and simple, thorough explanations. The book
explodes myths about previously accepted self-deliverance drugs and sheds
new light on readily available drugs. It includes a country-by-country
index providing brand names for over twenty countries, and an extensive
glossary. Proof of three months' membership of a right-to-die society is
required. Non-profit venture. <BR>
Price including postage: <BR>
&pound;6GBP UK; &pound;8GBP overseas <BR>
British currency only. <A HREF="http://www.netlink.co.uk/users/vess/dd.html">Order
form &amp; contents</A> 
<HR><A NAME="bfe"></A></P>

<H3>Beyond Final Exit</H3>

<P>See also <A HREF="sd.html">Self-deliverance</A> </P>

<P>Academic articles on methods of self-deliverance (rational suicide).
Includes a history of books on self-deliverance and one of the most comprehensively
referenced articles on fasting currently available. The title is based
on the series of articles in the Canadian journal &quot;Last Rights&quot;
in which some of the chapters initially appeared. <BR>
Price including postage: <BR>
&pound;8GBP UK; &pound;10GBP overseas <BR>
British currency only. <A HREF="http://www.netlink.co.uk/users/vess/bfe.html">Order
form</A> <BR>
North American (US$)orders: cksmith@rio.com. Canadian (Can$)orders: jh@islandnet.com

<HR><A NAME="lw"></A></P>

<H3>Living Will Info Pack</H3>

<P>Includes:</P>

<LI>&quot;A Living Will - Making it Work&quot; - a 32-page booklet on choosing
and making a living will, legal info, info for your doctor and further
references </LI>

<LI>Alternative living will documents including </LI>

<LI>The latest living will document and a guide to customising it to your
individual needs </LI>

<LI>A &quot;values history&quot; to guide you when articulating personal
values, and to guide the health care team and family when trying to assess
the patient's wishes in areas that cannot be anticipated by a living will
<A NAME="alert"></A></LI>

<LI>A medical &quot;alert card&quot; - a credit-card sized plastic card
for carrying in the wallet and alerting health care staff to the existence
of your document in an emergency <A NAME="stick"></A></LI>

<LI>Attention stickers for the patient's medical files <A NAME="wallet"></A></LI>

<LI>A carrying wallet. </LI>

<P>Some features of the main living will document in this pack: </P>

<LI>escape clauses to prevent non-treatment that would accord with the
letter but not the spirit of the document </LI>

<LI>explanation of all medical terms in non-medical language </LI>

<LI>option directing a proxy to use either substituted judgement or own
judgement </LI>

<LI>formal encouragement of dialogue with the doctor </LI>

<LI>eye-catching use of colour and different font sizes/styles </LI>

<LI>avoidance of any mention of the &quot;voluntary euthanasia&quot; (such
as in the name of VESS) </LI>

<LI>inclusion of a short values-history </LI>

<P><BR>
Price including postage: <BR>
&pound;15GBP (Orders within the UK) or &pound;30 (Outside UK). The pack
is included free with membership subscription to the Scottish Voluntary
Euthanasia Society &amp; Newsletter. <BR>
British currency only. </P>
