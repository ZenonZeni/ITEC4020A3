<strong>Regional Ballpark in Sacramento <i>(RBIS)</i></strong>
is a partnership between the
<a href="http://www.sacsports.com/">Sacramento Sports Commission</a>
and the
<a href="http://www.metrochamber.org/">Sacramento Metropolitan Chamber of Commerce</a>
whose goal is to help bring
<a href="http://www.majorleaguebaseball.com/">major league baseball</a>
to Sacramento and to lobby
for a major ballpark, preferably in 
<a href="http://www.pageweavers.com/pageweavers_downtown.html">downtown</a>.
RBIS will work with community leaders, sports fans,
<a href=goverment.html>regional elected officials</a>
and other business groups to support efforts that move the
<a href="http://www.pageweavers.com/pageweavers_community.html">Sacramento region</a>
closer to this goal.
