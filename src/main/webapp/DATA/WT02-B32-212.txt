A monthly magazine directed toward those in the design and construction

features as book reviews, editorials, and design news.<P>


<B>The Construction Claims Citator--BC12</B>  &#183; <A HREF="../libiss/bc12.html">Sample Issue</a><BR>
Construction Industry Press<BR>
$4.00/article; $40.00/issue<BR>

research, it is a concise source of information on pertinent cases from all
jurisdictions.<P>

projects, technological achievements, business conditions, legislation,
construction methods, equipment, materials, and more.<P>
