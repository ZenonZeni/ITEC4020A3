<H2 ALIGN="center"><B>WISCONSIN ASSOCIATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT ADMINISTRATORS</B></H2>
<H3 ALIGN="center"><B>Mission Statement</B></H3>

<OL>
<LI>
<P ALIGN="left"> To promote the education of Wisconsin youth;</P>
</LI>
<LI>
<P ALIGN="left"> To serve as advocates for the welfare of Wisconsin youth;</P>
</LI>
<LI>
<P ALIGN="left">To promote the professional growth and welfare of administrative, teaching and other school   district personnel;</P>
</LI>
<LI>
<P ALIGN="left">To place before the public the needs, problems and achievements of the public schools;</P>
</LI>
<LI>
<P ALIGN="left">To promote area meetings of administrators for the discussion of common interests; and</P>
</LI>
<LI>
<P ALIGN="left">To cooperate with other professional organizations and other organizations interested in improving education.</P>
</LI>
</OL>





<P ALIGN="left">This association was not organized for profit and no part of the net earnings will inure to the benefit of any private individual.</P>
