<h5> Area of Research </h5>
Analytic and experimental work on resonant frequencies and 
mechanical wave propagation characteristics of engineering
structures; Non-destructive material characterization by means
of ultrasound; Flaw detection and measurement as part of the quality control
requirement for manufacturing in transportation, power generation,
and military fields; Fracture studies; Elastic plastic fracture
mechanics and leak-before-break analysis.<p>
